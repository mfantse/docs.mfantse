# Nkanee

## Tsir 1

1. Na Israelfo fii Egypt asaase do firii no n'ekyir afe a otsia ebien no mu bosoom a otsia ebien no ne da a odzi kan no, EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses wɔ Sinai sar do wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu dɛ,
NUM.1.2, Ɔwo na Aaron mbu Israelmba mboanofo no nyinara ano, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee, na mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, na dɛ mbrɛ hɔn banyin biara ne dzin tse nkorkor.
3. Israel mu hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa nyinara no hom nkan hɔn dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse.
4. Na ebusuakuw biara mu banyin bi nka hom ho; kor biara a ɔyɛ n'egyanom fi mu otsitsir.
5. Na mbanyin a womfo a ebusuakuw no mu mboa hom no hɔn edzin nye yi: Reuben dze; Sedeur ne babanyin Elizur.
6. Simeon dze; Zurisadai ne babanyin Selumiel.
7. Juda dze; Amminadab ne babanyin Nahson.
8. Isakar dze; Zuar ne babanyin Netanel.
9. Zebulun dze; Helon ne babanyin Eliab.
10. Josef ne mba no dze; Efraim dze; Ammihud ne babanyin Elisama: Manaasɛ dze; Pedazur ne babanyin Gamaliel.
11. Benjamin dze; Gideoni ne babanyin Abidan.
12. Dan dze; Ammisaddai ne babanyin Ahiezer.
13. Aser dze; Okran ne babanyin Pagiel.
14. Gad dze; Deutel ne babanyin Eliasaf.
15. Naftali dze; Enan ne babanyin Ahira.
16. Iyinom nye hɔn a wɔfrɛɛ hɔn wɔ mboanofo no mu, hɔn egyanom ebusuakuw mu mbapɔmba no; wɔyɛ Israel mpem mpem mu etsitsir.
17. Na Moses nye Aaron faa mbanyin a wɔabobɔ hɔn edzin yi:
18. na wohyiaa mboanofo no nyinara boaa ano wɔ bosoom a otsia ebien no ne da a odzi kan no mu, na wɔkyerɛɛ hɔn asefo dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse, hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir, dɛ mbrɛ wɔtse nkorkor.
19. Dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no, dɛmara na ɔkaan hɔn wɔ Sinai sar no do.
20. Na Israel n'abakan Reuben, ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse no, ɔbanyin biara a oedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir, na hɔn a wotum kɔ ɔsa
21. a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Reuben ebusuakuw mu, nyinara yɛ mpemeduanan esia na ahaenum.
22. Simeon ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse no, ɔbanyin biara a oedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir, na hɔn a wotum kɔ ɔsa nyinara;
23. a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Simeon ebusuakuw mu no, nyinara mpemeduonum akron na ahaasa.
24. Gad ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse, hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa
25. a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Gad ebusuakuw mu, nyinara yɛ mpemeduanan enum ahaesia na eduonum.
26. Juda ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse, hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir na hɔn a wotum kɔ ɔsa nyinara
27. a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Juda ebusuakuw mu no nyinara mpemeduosuon anan na ahaesia.
28. Isakar ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse no hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir, hɔn a wotum kɔ ɔsa nyinara
29. a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Isakar ebusuakuw mu no yɛ mpemeduonum anan na ahaanan.
30. Zebulun ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse no, hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa
31. a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Zebulun ebusuakuw mu nyinara, wɔyɛ mpemeduonum esuon na ahaanan.
32. Josef n'asefo a wɔyɛ, Efraim ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse no hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa
33. a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Efraim ebusuakuw mu no yɛ mpemeduanan na ahaenum.
34. Manaasɛ ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse no, hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa
35. a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Manaasɛ ebusuakuw mu nyinara, yɛ mpemeduasa ebien na ahaenu.
36. Benjamin ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse, hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa nyinara;
37. hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Benjamin ebusuakuw mu no, yɛ mpemeduasa enum na ahaanan.
38. Dan ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse no, hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa
39. a wɔkaan hɔn, wɔ Dan ebusuakuw mu no nyinara yɛ mpemeduosia ebien na ahaesuon.
40. Aser ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse no hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa
41. wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Aser ebusuakuw mu no nyinara, wɔyɛ mpemeduanan biako na ahaenum.
42. Naftali ne mba, hɔn ebusuasantsen, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse, hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa nyinara
43. a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Naftali ebusuakuw mu no nyinara, wɔyɛ mpemeduonum ebiasa na ahaanan.
44. Iyinom nye hɔn a Moses na Aaron nye Israel mbapɔmba no a wɔyɛ mbanyin duebien no kanee no: wogyinagyinaa hɔn egyanom efi ananmu nkorkor.
45. Dɛm ntsi hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Israelmba mu dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee nyinara, hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu na ekyir a wotum kɔ ɔsa wɔ Israel mu
46. a wɔkaan hɔn nyinara no yɛ mpemahaesia na ebiasa na ahaenum eduonum.
47. Levifo no dze, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom ebusuakuw tse no, wɔannkan hɔn ammfora hɔn.
48. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.1.49, Levi ebusuakuw nkotsee na mma nnkan, nkyii so mma mmbu hɔn dodow ano mmfora Israelmba:
50. mbom woara fa Levifo no si dase ntamadan no na no ho ndzɛmba nyinara na dza odzi wɔ no nyinara do: hɔn na wɔnsoa ntamadan no, na no ho ndzɛmba nyinara; na wɔnyɛ ho edwuma, na wɔmbɔ nseraban nhyia ntamadan no ho.
51. Na sɛ worutu ntamadan no akɔ kan a, Levifo no na wontutu: na sɛ wɔrekyer ntamadan no nsera a, Levifo no na wonsi: na sɛ ɔhɔho ba ho a wonku no.
52. Na Israelmba no mbɔ hɔn asesee, nyimpa biara wɔ nankasa ne nseraban do, na nyimpa biara wɔ nankasa ne frankaa ho, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse.
53. Levifo no dze wɔnkyer nsera wɔ dase ntamadan no ho nhyia, amma ebufuw bi aammba Israelmba mboanofo no do: na Levifo no nhwɛ dase ntamadan ahwɛdze no do.
54. Na Israelmba no yɛe; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no nyinara, dɛmara na wɔyɛe.

## Tsir 2

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses na Aaron dɛ,
NUM.2.2, Israelmba no nkyer nsera wɔ nyimpa biara nankasa ne frankaa ho, wɔ hɔn egyanom efi nsɛnkyerɛdze do: ehyiadzibuw no ne tsentsendo na wɔnkyer hɔn nsera no nhyia.
3. Na hɔn a wɔnkyer nsera wɔ boka afamu a odzi kɔ wipuei no nye hɔn a wɔwɔ Juda nseraban do frankaa ho no dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse: na Amminadab ne babanyin Nason na ɔnyɛ Judamba no hɔn safohen panyin.
4. Na no dɔm, na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no, wɔyɛ mpemeduosuon anan na ahaesia.
5. Na hɔn a wɔnkyer nsera ntoa no do nye Isakar ebusuakuw. Zuar ne babanyin Netanel na ɔnyɛ Isakarmba no hɔn safohen panyin:
6. na no dɔm, na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no, wɔyɛ mpemeduonum anan na ahaanan.
7. Zebulun ebusuakuw ntoa do: na Helon ne babanyin Eliab na ɔnyɛ Zebulunmba no hɔn safohen panyin:
8. na no dɔm, na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no, wɔyɛ mpemeduonum esuon na ahaanan.
9. Hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Juda nseraban do nyinara yɛ mpem ɔha eduowɔtwe esia na ahaanan, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse. Hɔn na wondzi kan ntu.
10. Pomu afamu na Reuben nseraban do frankaa ndzi dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse: na Sedeur ne babanyin Elizur na ɔnyɛ Reubenmba ɔsafohen panyin.
11. Na no dɔm, na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no yɛ mpemeduanan esia na ahaenum.
12. Hɔn a wɔnkyer nsera ntoa no do nye Simeon ebusuakuw: na Zurisadai ne babanyin Selumiel na ɔnyɛ Simeonmba no hɔn safohen panyin:
13. na no dɔm a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no, wɔyɛ mpemeduonum akron na ahaasa.
14. Gad ebusuakuw no ntoa hɔn do: na Reuel ne babanyin Eliasaf na ɔnyɛ Gadmba no hɔn safohen panyin:
15. na no dɔm a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no yɛ mpemeduanan enum na ahaesia eduonum.
16. Hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Reuben nseraban do nyinara wɔyɛ mpem ɔha eduonum biako na ahaanan eduonum, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse. Na hɔn na wɔntɔ do ebien ntu.
17. Nkyii so ehyiadzibuw no, nye Levifo nseraban no ntu ndzi nseraban nseraban no ne finimfin: dɛ mbrɛ wɔbɔ nseraban no, dɛmara na wontu, nyimpa biara wɔ ne dzibew, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn mfrankaa no tse.
18. Efraim ne nseraban do frankaa ndzi anee afamu dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse: na Ammihud ne babanyin Elisama na ɔnyɛ Efraimmba no hɔn safohen panyin.
19. Na no dɔm, na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no, wɔyɛ mpemeduanan na ahaenum.
20. Na Manaasɛ ebusuakuw ntoa no do: na Pedazur ne babanyin Gamaliel na ɔnyɛ Manaasɛmba no hɔn safohen panyin.
21. Na no dɔm, na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no yɛ mpemeduasa ebien na ahaenu.
22. Benjamin ebusuakuw no, Gideoni ne babanyin Abidan na ɔnyɛ hɔn safohen panyin:
23. na no dɔm, na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no yɛ mpemeduasa enum na ahaanan.
24. Hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Efraim nseraban do nyinara yɛ mpem ɔha nye awɔtwe na ɔha, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse. Na hɔn na wɔntɔ do ebiasa ntu.
25. Dan ne nseraban do frankaa ndzi kwaamu afamu dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse: na Ammisaddai ne babanyin Ahiezer na ɔnyɛ Danmba no hɔn safohen panyin.
26. Na no dɔm a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no yɛ mpemeduosia ebien na ahaesuon.
27. Na hɔn a wɔnkyer nsera ntoa no do nye Aser ebusuakuw: na Okran ne babanyin Pagiel na ɔnyɛ Asermba no hɔn safohen panyin.
28. No dɔm na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no, wɔyɛ mpemeduanan biako na ahaenum.
29. Naftali ebusuakuw no, Enan ne babanyin Ahira na ɔnyɛ Naftalimba no hɔn safohen panyin:
30. na no dɔm a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no, yɛ mpemeduonum ebiasa na ahaanan.
31. Hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Dan ne nseraban do nyinara yɛ mpem ɔha na eduonum esuon na ahaesia. Hɔn na wondzi ekyir ntu dɛ mbrɛ hɔn mfrankaa no tse.
32. Iyinom nye Israelmba mu hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee: hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ nseraban nseraban do dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tse nyinara yɛ mpemahaesia nye ebiasa na ahaenum eduonum.
33. Levifo no dze, wɔannkan hɔn ammfora Israelmba no; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
34. Na Israelmba no yɛɛ dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no nyinara, na wɔkyeer nsera dɛ mbrɛ hɔn mfrankaa no tse, na dɛmara so na wotui, obiara dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua na hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee.

## Tsir 3

1. Afei iyinom nye Aaron nye Moses hɔn ebusuasantsen wɔ da a EWURADZE nye Moses kasae, wɔ kokwaa Sinai do no.
2. Na iyinom nye Aaron ne mbabanyin no hɔn edzin; Nadab a ɔyɛ abakan no, nye Abihu, Eleazar, nye Itamar.
3. Iyinom nye Aaron ne mbabanyin, asɔfo a wɔseraa hɔn ngo a wotuu hɔn sii nkyɛn dɛ wondzi asɔfo no, hɔn edzin.
4. Na Nadab nye Abihu wuwui wɔ EWURADZE enyim, aber a wɔdze ananagya baa EWURADZE enyim, wɔ Sinai sar do no, na woennya mba ntsi Eleazar nye Itamar nko na wodzii asɔfo wɔ hɔn egya Aaron enyim.
5. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.3.6, Fa Levi ebusuakuw no bɛn, na fa hɔn begyina ɔsɔfo Aaron enyim ma wɔnsom me.
7. Na wɔnhwɛ n'ahwɛdze do, nye mboanofo no nyinara ahwɛdze no do wɔ ehyiadzibuw no enyim, mfa nyɛ ntamadan no ho edwuma no.
8. Na wɔnhwɛ ehyiadzibuw no ne ndzɛmba no nyinara, nye Israelmba ahwɛdze no do, mfa nyɛ ntamadan no ho edwuma no.
9. Na fa Levifo no ma Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no: wɔdze hɔn ama no koraa wɔ Israelmba no afamu.
10. Na yi Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no si hɔ, na wɔnhwɛ hɔn asɔfodzi no do: na sɛ ɔhɔho ba ho a wonku no.
11. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.3.12, Hwɛ, emi, mafa Levifo no efi Israelmba mu ahyɛ Israelmba mu mbakan a wobue yafun mu no nyinara no ananmu; na Levifo no nyɛ medze:
13. no mbakan nyinara yɛ medze. Da a mobobɔɔ Egypt asaase do mbakan nyinara no na metseew Israel mu mbakan nyinara ho maa moho, nyimpa nye abowa nyina: wɔyɛ medze; emi nye EWURADZE.
14. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses wɔ Sinai sar do dɛ,
NUM.3.15, Kan Levimba no dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi, nye hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: banyin biara a oedzi bosoom nye ekyir no kan no.
16. Na Moses kaan hɔn dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE n'asɛm a ɔhyɛɛ no no tse.
17. Na iyinom nye Levi mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn edzin no tse; Gerson, Kohat, nye Merari.
18. Na iyinom nye Gerson ne mbabanyin edzin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee; Libni nye Simei.
19. Na Kohat mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee; Amram, Izhar, Hebron, nye Uziel.
20. Na Merari mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee; Mahili nye Musi. Iyinom nye Levifo ebusua ebusua no dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi no tsetsee.
21. Gerson dze nye Libnifo, nye Simeifo ebusua: iyinom nye Gersonfo ebusua ebusua no.
22. Hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no, dɛ mbrɛ mbanyin no nyinara a woedzi bosoom nye ekyir no hɔn dodow tse no, wɔyɛ mpemesuon nye ahaenum.
23. Gersonfo ebusua ebusua no nkyer nsera wɔ ntamadan no ekyir anee afamu.
24. Na Lael ne babanyin Eliasaf na ɔnyɛ Gersonfo egyanom efi mu safohen panyin.
25. Na Gerson ne mbabanyin no hɔn ahwɛdze wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu nye ntamadan no, nye Buw no, n'akatado, nye ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano nkataano,
NUM.3.26, nye dampan no ne nsensɛnho, nye dampan no n'abowano nkataano no, dza ɔwɔ ntamadan no ho, na otwa afɔrmbukyia no ho hyia no, nye ne ntampehoma wɔ no ho edwuma nyinara ho.
27. Na Kohat dze nye Amramfo ebusua, Izharfo ebusua, Hebronfo ebusua, nye Uzielfo ebusua: iyinom nye Kohatfo ebusua ebusua no.
28. Dɛ mbrɛ mbanyin nyinara a woedzi bosoom nye ekyir no hɔn dodow tse no, wɔyɛ mpemawɔtwe nye ahaesia a wɔhwɛ ahotseweebea ahwɛdodze no do.
29. Kohat ne mbabanyin ebusua ebusua no nkyer nsera wɔ ntamadan no nkyɛn wɔ pomu afamu.
30. Na Uziel ne mbabanyin Elizafan na ɔnyɛ ɔsafohen panyin wɔ Kohatfo ebusua ebusua no hɔn egyanom fie mu.
31. Na ahwɛdze nye adaka no, nye pon no, nye kandzeadua no, nye afɔrmbukyia no, nye ahotseweebea ndzɛmba no a wɔdze som no, nye nkataano no, nye no ho edwuma nyinara.
32. Na ɔsɔfo Aaron ne babanyin Eleazar na nyɛ ɔsafohen panyin wɔ Levifo asafohemfo mu, na ɔnhwɛ hɔn a wɔhwɛ ahotseweebea no do ahwɛdodze do no do.
33. Merari dze nye Mahilifo ebusua, nye Musfo ebusua: iyinom nye Merari ebusua ebusua no.
34. Na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no, dɛ mbrɛ mbanyin no nyinara a woedzi bosoom nye ekyir no hɔn dodow tse no, wɔyɛ mpemesia nye ahaenu.
35. Abihail ne babanyin Zuriel nye Merari ebusua ebusua no mu egyanom fi mu safohen panyin: hɔn na wɔnkyer nsera wɔ ntamadan no nkyɛn wɔ kwaamu afamu.
36. Na ahwɛdodze a wɔahyɛ ama Merari ne mbabanyin no nye ntamadan no ne ntaabow, nye ne ndaban, nye n'edum, nye ne ntaataase, nye no ho ndzɛmba nyinara, nye no ho edwuma nyinara;
37. na dampan no n'edum a ehyia ho no, nye ne ntaease no, nye no mbɔfondua, nye ne ntampehoma.
38. Na hɔn a wɔnkyer nsera wɔ ntamadan no enyim wɔ boka afamu, wɔ ehyiadzibuw no enyim a odzi kɔ wipuei no, nye Moses, nye Aaron nye ne mbabanyin a wɔhwɛ ahotseweebea ahwɛdodze no ma Israelmba no; na ɔhɔho biara a ɔbɛba ho no wonku no.
39. Hɔn a wofi Levifo mu a Moses nye Aaron nam EWURADZE ne mbrasɛm do kaan hɔn nyinara, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua no tse no, mbanyin a woedzi bosoom nye ekyir no nyinara, wɔyɛ mpemeduonu ebien.
40. Na EWURADZE see Moses dɛ, Kan Israelmba mu mbakan mbanyin a woedzi bosoom nye ekyir no nyinara, na bu hɔn edzin no dodow ano.
41. Na fa Levifo no ma me (emi nye EWURADZE) fa hyɛ Israelmba mu mbakan no nyinara ananmu; nye Levifo fiembowa no hyɛ Israelmba fiembowa mu mbakan no nyinara ananmu.
42. Na Moses kaan Israelmba mu mbakan no nyinara, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ no no.
43. Na mbakan mbanyin a woedzidzi bosoom nye ekyir, dɛ mbrɛ edzin dodow no tse wɔ hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu no mu no, wɔyɛ mpemeduonu ebien ahaenu eduosuon ebiasa.
44. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛ, Moses dɛ,
NUM.3.45, Fa Levifo no hyɛ Israelmba mu mbakan no nyinara ananmu, na fa Levifo fiembowa no hyɛ hɔn fiembowa no ananmu: na Levifo no nyɛ medze; emi nye EWURADZE.
46. Na Israelmba mbakan ahaenu eduosuon ebiasa a wɔbor Levifo no hɔn dodow do no hɔn apondze no dze,
NUM.3.47, gyegye hɔn tsir sika dwetɛbona enum enum dɛ mbrɛ wɔtse nkorkor; mbrɛ ahotseweebea nhyehyɛɛ tse no na gye hɔn (dwetɛbona yɛ  gera   enum):
48. na fa sika no a wɔdze poon hɔn a wɔbor hɔn do no ma Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no.
49. Na Moses gyegyee apondze sika no wɔ hɔn a wɔbor hɔn a Levifo poon hɔn no hɔ:
50. Israelmba mbakan hɔ na ɔgyegyee sika no; dwetɛbona apem ahaasa eduosia enum, dɛ mbrɛ ahotseweebea dwetɛbona tse:
51. na Moses dze apondze sika no maa Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE n'asɛm a ɔhyɛɛ Moses no tse no.

## Tsir 4

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses nye Aaron dɛ.
2. Bu Kohat ne mbabanyin no ano fa fi Levi mbabanyin no mu, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, nye hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee,
NUM.4.3, ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduasa nye ekyir do kodu hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonum do, hɔn a wɔhɛn ɔsom no mu kɛyɛ edwuma wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu no nyinara.
4. Iyi nye Kohat ne mbabanyin no hɔn edwuma wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu, wɔ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn ho:
5. sɛ nseraban no rubotu a, Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no na wɔnkɔ mu, na wonkeyi ntwamutam nkataano no, na wɔmfa nkata dasedaka no do:
6. na wɔmfa esukraman nwoma akatado nto do, na wɔmfa tam a ne nyinara yɛ bibir nkata do, na wɔmfa no mpoma no nhyehyɛ mu.
7. Na wɔmfa tam bibir nkata ɔkyerɛpaanoo pon no do, na wɔmfa nkwansɛn no, nye ntser no, nye nkora, nye nkɔɔpow a wɔdze hue no nsisi do: na daa daa paanoo no nsi do:
8. na wɔmfa tam memen nkata do, na wɔmfa esukraman nwoma akatado nkata ho, na wɔmfa no mpoma no nhyehyɛ mu.
9. Na wɔmfa tam bibir, nkata kandzea no ne kandzeadua nye ne nkandzea, nye n'adabaa, nye no nsompokuwa no, nye no ngo ntoaba nyinara a wɔdze som no ho:
10. na wɔmfa ɔno nye no ho adze nyinara nhyɛ esukraman nwoma akatado mu, na wɔmfa nto pata no do.
11. Wɔmfa tam bibir nkata sika afɔrmbukyia no do, na wɔmfa esukraman nwoma akatado nkata do, na wɔmfa no mpoma no nhyehyɛ mu:
12. na wɔmfa ɔsom ho ndzɛmba nyinara a wɔdze som wɔ ahotseweebea no nhyɛ tam bibir mu, na wɔmfa esukraman nwoma akatado nkata ho, na wɔmfa nto pata no do.
13. Na wɔnsesaw nso no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, na wɔmfa tam ɔberdum nkata do:
14. na wɔmfa no ho ndzɛmba nyinara a wɔdze som wɔ ho no ngu do, ogyasɛn, edzinam, nye nsofi, nye mbɛɛsen, afɔrmbukyia no ho ndzɛmba no nyinara; na wɔmfa esukraman nwoma akatado nkata do, na wɔmfa no mpoma no nhyehyɛ mu.
15. Na sɛ nseraban no rubotu, na Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no wie ahotseweebea no, nye ahotseweebea no ho ndzɛmba no nyinara do kata a; nna nkyii Kohat ne mbabanyin no mbra mbɔsoa: ahotseweebea no dze mma wɔnnka, amma woeennwuwu. Ndzɛmba yi nye Kohat ne mbabanyin no hɔn adzesoa wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu.
16. Na ɔsɔfo Aaron ne babanyin Eleazar n'ahwɛdodze nye kandzea no ho ngo no, nduhuanhuam no, daa daa edziban afɔr no, ɔserango no, ntamadan no nye dza ɔayɛ no krɔnkrɔn a ɔwɔ mu no nyinara.
17. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses nye Aaron dɛ,
NUM.4.18, Hom nhwɛ ma wontwa Kohatfo ebusua ebusua mu ebusuakuw no mmfi Levifo no mu:
19. na dza hom nyɛ mma hɔn ama woeenya nkwa na woeennwuwu, sɛ wɔbɛn krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn no a: Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no nhɛn mu, na wɔmfa hɔn nsisi obiara n'edwuma nye n'adzesoa ho:
20. na hɔn dze, mma wɔnnhɛn mu nnkɔhwɛ ahotseweebea no dɛ enyi robɔ do mpo, amma woeennwuwu.
21. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.4.22, Bu Gerson mbabanyin no so ano, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi, nye hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee;
23. ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduasa nye ekyir do kodu hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonum do na kan hɔn; hɔn a wɔhɛn mu kɔsom dɛ wɔkɛyɛ edwuma wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu no nyinara.
24. Iyi nye Gersonfo ebusua ebusua hɔn som wɔ ɔsom nye ndzesoa soa mu.
25. Hɔn na wɔnsoa ntamadan no ne nsensɛnho atam no, nye ehyiadzibuw no, ne nkatado, nye esukraman nwoma akatado no a ɔwɔ do wɔ sor no, nye ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano nkataano;
26. nye pampan no ne nsensɛnho no, nye pampan no n'abotsen abowano nkataano, dza etwa ntamadan no nye afɔrmbukyia no ho ehyia no, nye ne ntampehoma, nye no ho edwuma ho ndzɛmba nyinara, nye biribiara a wɔdze bɛyɛ no, no mu na wɔnsom.
27. Aaron nye ne mbabanyin mbrasɛm do na Gersonfo mbabanyin no nyɛ hɔn edwuma nyinara, wɔ hɔn adzesoa nyinara, nye hɔn edwuma nyinara mu: na hom mfa hɔn adzesoa nyinara nhyɛ hɔn nsa ma wɔnhwɛ do.
28. Iyi nye Gersonfo mbabanyin ebusua ebusua no hɔn edwuma wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu: na hɔn ahwɛdodze wɔ ɔsɔfo Aaron ne babanyin Itamar nsamu.
29. Merari ne mbabanyin no dze, kan hɔn dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, nye hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee;
30. ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduasa nye ekyir do kodu hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonum do no mu, obiara a ɔhɛn ɔsom no mu, dɛ ɔkɛyɛ edwuma wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu no.
31. Na iyi nye hɔn adzesoa ahwɛdodze, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn edwuma nyinara tse wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu; ntamadan no ne ntaabow, nye n'adaban, nye n'edum, nye ne ngyinado na kan hɔn;
32. na pampan no n'edum a etwa ho ehyia no, nye ne ngyinado, nye no mbɔfondua, nye ne ntampehoma, ɔnye no ho ndzɛmba nyinara, na ɔnye n'edwuma nyinara: na hom mfa hɔn adzesoa ahwɛdodze ndzɛmba no mbɔ dzin nhyɛ hɔn nsa.
33. Iyi nye Merari mbabanyin ebusua ebusua edwuma, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn edwuma nyinara tse, wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu, wɔ ɔsɔfo Aaron ne babanyin Itamar nsamu.
34. Na Moses nye Aaron nye mboanofo no mu mbapɔmba no kaan Kohatfo mbabanyin no dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, nye hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee,
NUM.4.35, ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduasa nye ekyir do kodu hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonum do, dɛ ɔkɛyɛ edwuma wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu no:
36. na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee no wɔyɛ mpemebien ahaesuon eduonum.
37. Iyinom nye hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Kohatfo ebusua ebusua mu, hɔn a wɔsoom wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu nyinara a Moses nye Aaron nam EWURADZE mbrasɛm a ɔwɔ Moses nsamu no do kanee no.
38. Na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Gerson mbabanyin mu, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, nye hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee,
NUM.4.39, ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduasa nye ekyir do kodu hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonum do, obiara a ɔhɛn ɔsom no mu, dɛ ɔkɛyɛ edwuma wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu no,
NUM.4.40, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua, nye hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee no, wɔyɛ mpemebien nye ahaesia eduasa.
41. Iyinom nye hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Gerson ne mbabanyin ebusua ebusua mu, hɔn a wɔsomee wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu nyinara a Moses nye Aaron nam EWURADZE mbrasɛm do kanee no.
42. Na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Merari mbabanyin ebusua ebusua mu, dɛ mbrɛ ebusua ebusua nye hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee,
NUM.4.43, ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduasa nye ekyir kodu hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonum do, obiara a ɔhɛn ɔsom no mu, dɛ ɔkɛyɛ edwuma wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu no,
NUM.4.44, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tse a wɔkaan hɔn no, wɔyɛ mpemebiasa nye ahaenu.
45. Iyinom nye hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Merari ne mbabanyin ebusua ebusua a Moses nye Aaron nam EWURADZE mbrasɛm a ɔwɔ Moses nsamu no do kanee no.
46. Hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Levifo mu nyinara a Moses nye Aaron nye Israel mbapɔmba no kanee, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua, nye hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee,
NUM.4.47, ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduasa nye ekyir do kodu hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonum do, obiara a ɔhɛn mu dɛ ɔkɛyɛ ɔsom edwuma, nye ndzesoa soa mu edwuma wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu
48. a wɔkaan hɔn no, wɔyɛ mpemawɔtwe ahaenum eduawɔtwe.
49. Dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE mbrasɛm tse na wɔnam Moses nsa do kaan hɔn, obiara dɛ mbrɛ no som tse, nye mbrɛ n'adzesoa tse no ara: dɛm na kaan hɔn, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ no no.

## Tsir 5

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.5.2, Hyɛ Israelmba ma wontu ɔkwatanyi biara, nye obiara a ɔwɔ ahosen, nye onyia biara a ɔdze fun egu noho fi, mfi nseraban no mu:
3. ɔbanyin o ɔbaa o hom ntu no. Nseraban no ekyir na hom ntu hɔn nkogu amma woeenngu hɔn nseraban a metse ne finimfin no ho fi.
4. Na Israelmba no yɛɛ dɛmara, na wotuu hɔn koguu nseraban ekyir: na dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses no, dɛmara na Israelmba no yɛe.
5. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.5.6, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba dɛ, Sɛ ɔbanyin anaa ɔbaa yɛ bɔn tsia nyimpa bi, dze fom EWURADZE, na ɔkra no dzi so a;
7. ɔno ɔmpaa mu nka hɔn bɔn a wɔayɛ no: na ontua n'asodzidze no imu, na ɔmfa no mu ebupɛn enum mu kor nto do, na ɔmfa mma nyia ɔafom no edzi so no.
8. Mbom sɛ nyimpa no ewu na onnyi bɔgya bi a ɔbɛn no a wɔdze asodzidze bɛma no wɔ asodzi no ntsi a, asodzi no ho asodzidze no yɛ EWURADZE dze na ɔnyɛ ɔsɔfo no dze; nka mpata oguannyin no a wɔdze rebɛpata ama no no ho.
9. Na ɔtow afɔr biara a ɔwɔ Israelmba adzekrɔnkrɔn nyinara mu a wɔdze brɛ ɔsɔfo no, nyɛ nedze.
10. Na nyimpa biara n'adzekrɔnkrɔn a ɔdze ba no yɛ ɔsɔfo no dze: biribiara a nyimpa biara dze ma ɔsɔfo no, nedze ara na ɔnyɛ.
11. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.5.12, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ ɔbanyin bi ne yer fi ne nkyɛn, na ɔkɔfom no,
NUM.5.13, na ɔbanyin bi nye no da dze no ho nsu ma no, na wɔdze suma no kun, na wosiw do, na ogu noho fi, na no ho ɔdasefo bi nnyi hɔ, nkyii so wɔannkyer no wɔ do;
14. na nyinkun sunsum ba no do, na ɔtwe ne yer no ho nyinkun, na sɛ basia no egu noho fi ampa: anaa sɛ nyinkun sunsum ba no do, na ɔtwe ne yer no ho nyinkun, na sɛ basia no nguu noho fi a:
15. ɔno ɔbanyin no mfa ne yer no mbrɛ ɔsɔfo no, na ɔmfa no ho ayɛyɛdze mbra, atoko isiam efa ebupɛn du mu kor; mma ɔmmfa ngo nngu do, na ɔmmfa krɔbɔw so nnto do; osiandɛ ɔyɛ nyinkun ho edziban afɔr na nkaa ho edziban afɔr, a wɔdze kaa emumuyɛ.
16. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa no mbɛn, na ɔmfa no mbegyina EWURADZE enyim:
17. na ɔsɔfo no mfa nsu krɔnkrɔn ngu dɛtse kɔɔpow bi mu; na ɔsɔfo no mfa ntamadan no ne daadze huntuma no bi, ngu nsu no mu:
18. na ɔsɔfo no mfa ɔbaa no ngyina EWURADZE enyim, na ontutu ɔbaa no ne tsinhwi ngu do, na ɔmfa nkaa edziban afɔr no a ɔyɛ nyinkun edziban afɔr no nhyɛ ne nsamu: na ɔsɔfo no nkitsa nsu wenwen a ɔdze ndom ba no wɔ ne nsamu.
19. Na ɔsɔfo no mma ondzi nsew, na ɔnse ɔbaa no dɛ, Sɛ banyin bi nnye wo nndae, na sɛ immfii wo kun nkyɛn nnkoguu woho fi a, wo ho nyɛ tɔtrɔtɔ wɔ nsu wenwen a ɔdze ndom ba yi ho:
20. mbom sɛ efi wo kun nkyɛn, ber a ewar wo kun yi na sɛ ekogu woho fi, na banyin bi nye wo ada ka wo kun ho a:
21. ɔno ɔsɔfo no mma ɔbaa no mfa ndom ntam ndzi nsew, na ɔsɔfo no nse ɔbaa no dɛ, EWURADZE nyɛ wo dzin ndom nye ntam wɔ wo man mu, aber a EWURADZE bɛma w'aserɛ atotɔ mu, na wo yafun ahon.
22. Na nsu a ɔdze ndom ba yi akɛhɛn wo nson mu, na ɔama wo yafun ahon, na w'aserɛ atotɔ mu: na ɔbaa no nse dɛ, Amen, Amen.
23. Na ɔsɔfo no nkyerɛw ndom yi wɔ nwoma mu, na ɔmpepa ngu nsu wenwen no mu:
24. na ɔmma ɔbaa no nnom nsu wenwen a ɔdze ndom ba no: na nsu a ɔdze ndom ba no nkɔ no mu nkɛyɛ wenwen.
25. Na ɔsɔfo no ngye nyinkun ho edziban afɔr no wɔ ɔbaa no nsamu, na ɔntow edziban afɔr no wɔ EWURADZE enyim, na ɔmfa mbra afɔrmbukyia no ho;
26. na ɔsɔfo no mfa edziban afɔr no mu nsapuw, dɛ ne nkaadze, na ɔnhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, na n'ekyir no ɔmma ɔbaa no nnom nsu no.
27. Na ɔbɛba dɛ aber a ɔama ɔanom nsu no, sɛ oegu noho fi na ɔayɛ bɔn etsia no kun a, nsu no a ɔdze ndom ba no bɛhɛn no mu akɛyɛ wenwen, na ne yafun ahon, na n'aserɛ atotɔ mu: na ɔbaa no ayɛ ndom wɔ ne man mu.
28. Na sɛ ɔbaa no ho enngu fi, na no ho tsew dze a; ɔno no ho bɛyɛ tɔtrɔtɔ, na oenyinsen ɔba.
29. Iyi nye nyinkun ho mbra, sɛ ɔyer bi fi no kun nkyɛn na okogu noho fi;
30. anaa sɛ nyinkun sunsum ba banyin bi do, na ɔtwe ne yer ho nyinkun a; ɔno ɔmfa ɔbaa no mbegyina EWURADZE enyim, na ɔsɔfo no nyɛ no mbra yi mu adze nyinara.
31. Na ɔbanyin no ho bɛyɛ tɔtrɔtɔ wɔ emumuyɛ ho, na ɔbaa no asoa n'emumuyɛ.

## Tsir 6

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.6.2, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ ɔbanyin anaa ɔbaa bi bɔ anohoba soronko, Nazirinyi anohoba, dɛ orutu noho dze ama EWURADZE a:
3. ɔnkam noho wɔ wenyin nye nsa dzendzen ho: mma ɔnnom wenyin kawee, anaa nsa dzendzen kawee, nkyii so mma ɔnnom esikyireamba nsa biara, na onndzi esikyireamba momon anaa guanee so.
4. Noho nkamee nda nyinara mu mma onndzi biribiara a wɔdze wenyindua ho biribi ayɛ, no nsowee mu aba o, no ho ahon o.
5. Noho nkamee anohoba nda nyinara mu no mma oyindam bi nnka ne tsir: ɔnyɛ krɔnkrɔn nkodu dɛ nda a ɔkaam noho maa EWURADZE wɔ mu no bɔso, onsie etsimum.
6. Nda a ɔakam noho ama EWURADZE no nyinara mma ɔnnkɔ nyimpa bi fun ho.
7. Sɛ n'egya, anaa ne na, no nua anaa n'akyerɛbaa mpo wuwu a, mma onnkogu noho fi: osiandɛ noho nkamee a ɔdze ma Nyankopɔn no wɔ n'etsifi.
8. Noho nkamee nda nyinara ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn ma EWURADZE.
9. Na sɛ nyimpa bi wu awerɛfir mu wɔ ne nkyɛn, na ogu noho nkamee tsir ho fi a; ɔno da a otsia esuon no onyi ne tsir wɔ n'ahoguar da no mu.
10. Na da a otsia awɔtwe no ɔmfa ntamram ebien, anaa mborɔnomamba ebien, mbrɛ ɔsɔfo no, wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano:
11. na ɔsɔfo no mfa kor mbɔ bɔn ho afɔr, na kor no so ɔmfa nyɛ ɔhyew afɔr, na ɔmpata mma no, dɛ ɔafom wɔ fun no ho, na ɔntsew ne tsir ho da no ara.
12. Na ɔnsan nkam noho mma EWURADZE, na ɔmfa eguambaanyin a oedzi afrenhyia mbra mbɔbɔ asodzi ho afɔr: mbom nkan no nda no ayɛ kwa, osiandɛ noho nkamee no ho egu fi.
13. Na iyi nye Nazirinyi ho mbra, aber a noho nkamee nda ewie du no: wɔmfa no mbra ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano:
14. na ɔmfa ayɛyɛdze mbrɛ EWURADZE, eguambaanyin a oedzi afrenhyia a onndzii dzɛm kor mbɔbɔ ɔhyew afɔr, na eguambaaber a oedzi afrenhyia a onndzii dzɛm kor mbɔbɔ bɔn ho afɔr, nye oguannyin a onndzii dzɛm kor mbɔbɔ asomdwee afɔr,
NUM.6.15, nye epitsi boodoo kɛntsɛn no, nye isiam pa tatar a wɔdze ngo afrɔw, nye epitsi tatar ntrɛtraba a wɔdze ngo egu do, nye n'edziban afɔr, nye no nom afɔr.
16. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa mbra EWURADZE enyim, na ɔmbɔ no bɔn ho afɔr, nye ne hyew afɔr:
17. na ɔmfa oguannyin no mbɔ asomdwee afɔr mma EWURADZE, nka epitsi boodoo kɛntsɛn no ho: ɔsɔfo no mbɔ n'edziban afɔr, nye no nom afɔr no so.
18. Na Nazirinyi no nyi ne tsir no wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano, na ɔmfa ne tsinhwi no ngu ogya no a ɔwɔ asomdwee afɔr ase no mu.

NUM.8.24, Dza ɔyɛ Levifo dze no nye yi: hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu enum nye ekyir no nkɔ nkɔsom wɔ ehyiadzibuw no edwuma no mu:
25. na ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonum do no ngyaa edwuma yɛ, na mma wɔnnkɔsom bio;
26. mbom wɔnye hɔn nuanom nsom wɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu, nhwɛ ahwɛdodze no, na ɔsom edwuma no na mma wɔnnyɛ. Dɛm na yɛ ma Levifo no wɔ hɔn ahwɛdodze ho afamu.

## Tsir 9

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses wɔ Sinai sar do, wofii Egypt asaase do firii no n'ekyir afe a otsia ebien no no bosoom a odzi kan no mu dɛ,
NUM.9.2, Nkyii so ma Israelmba ndzi apaho no wɔ n'aber a wɔahyɛ mu,
NUM.9.3, bosoom yi ne da a otsia duanan no mu ewimbir, na hom ndzi: dɛ mbrɛ n'ahyɛdze nyinara tse, na mbrɛ no ho mmom nyinara tse no, na hom ndzi.
4. Na Moses kasaa kyerɛɛ Israelmba no dɛ wondzi apaho no.
5. Na wodzii apaho no wɔ bosoom a odzi kan no ne da a otsia duanan no mu, ewimbir, wɔ Sinai sar do: dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no nyinara, dɛmara na Israelmba no yɛe.
6. Na mbanyin bi wɔ hɔ a hɔn ho egu fi wɔ nyimpa bi no fun ho, ma dɛm ntsi woenntum enndzi apaho no da no ara:
7. na mbanyin no see no dɛ, Hɛn ho egu fi wɔ nyimpa bi no fun ho: ɔyɛ dɛn ntsi na wɔakam hɛn, dɛ mma yɛmmfa EWURADZE ayɛyɛdze mmba wɔ n'aber a wɔahyɛ mu wɔ Israelmba no mu?
8. Na Moses see hɔn dɛ, Hom ngyina hɔ; ma minketsie dza EWURADZE bɛhyɛ wɔ hom ho.
9. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.9.10, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba dɛ, Sɛ nyimpa bi no ho gu fi wɔ fun bi ho, anaa ɔwɔ ekyirekyir akwantu mu, wɔ hom mu, anaa hom ebusuasantsen mu a, ondzi apaho no mma EWURADZE ara:
11. bosoom a otsia ebien no mu da a otsia duanan no ewimbir na wondzi; epitsi boodoo nye fan wenwen na wɔmfa ndzi:
12. mma wonngya mu bi mma onnkodu anapa, na mma wommbu ne biew bi mu so: na dɛ mbrɛ apaho no no ho ahyɛdze no nyinara tse na wondzi.
13. Mbom nyimpa no a no ho tsew, na orunntu kwan, na ogyaa dɛ odzi apaho no dze, ɔkra no wobotwa no efi ne man mu akyen osiandɛ ɔammfa EWURADZE ayɛyɛdze no ammba wɔ n'aber a wɔahyɛ mu, dɛm nyimpa no nsoa no bɔn.
14. Na sɛ ɔhɔho bi bɔsoɛ hom mu na oridzi apaho no ama EWURADZE a ɔno dɛ mbrɛ apaho ahyɛdze no tse, na mbrɛ no ho mbra no tse no, na ɔnyɛ: hom nya ahyɛdze kor mfa mma ɔhɔho nye nyia wɔawo no wɔ asaase no do nyina.
15. Na da a wɔmaa ntamadan no do sii hɔ no mununkum no bɛkataa ntamadan no do, mekyerɛ dasebuw no: na ewimbir no na ɔwɔ ntamadan no do epue tse dɛ ogya dze kodu anapa.
16. Dɛm na ɔtse daa: mununkum no kataa do, na ogya no puee anadwe.
17. Na aber biara a wɔma mununkum no do ma ɔma noho do fi Buw no do no, ɔno n'ekyir no Israelmba tu kwan, na beebi mbrɛ mununkum no gyinae no, hɔ na Israelmba kyeer nsera.
18. EWURADZE mbrasɛm do na Israelmba nam tuu kwan, na EWURADZE mbrasɛm do na wɔnam kyeer nsera: aber tsentsen biara a mununkum no tsena ntamadan no do no nyinara wɔtsena nserakyer mu.
19. Na sɛ mununkum no kyinkyim ntamadan no do nda pii a, ɔno Israelmba hwɛ EWURADZE ahwɛdodze no do, na wonntu kwan.
20. Na ɔtɔfabi a mununkum no dzi nda kakrabaa bi wɔ ntamadan no do; nna dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE mbrasɛm tse no wɔatsena nsera no mu, na dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE mbrasɛm tse no, nna wɔnam do etu.
21. Na ɔtɔfabi so a mununkum no wɔ hɔ araa fi ewimbir kodu anapa; na sɛ mununkum no ma noho do anapa a, na woetu: anaa sɛ ɔwɔw do adzekyee nye adzesaa, na sɛ mununkum no ma no ho do a, na woetu.
22. Sɛ nda ebien o, bosoom o, afrenhyia o, nna mununkum no kyinkyim wɔ ntamadan no do tsena do a, Israelmba no tsena nsera no mu, na wonntu: na sɛ ɔma no ho do a, nna woetu.
23. EWURADZE ne mbrasɛm na wɔnam do kyeer nsera, na EWURADZE ne mbrasɛm na wɔnam do tui: wɔhwɛɛ EWURADZE n'ahwɛdodze do, wɔ EWURADZE ne mbrasɛm a ɔnam Moses ne nsa do hyɛɛ no do.

## Tsir 10

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.10.2, Yɛ dwetɛ ntotorobɛnto ebien fa ma woho: ɔbor edwuma na fa yɛ: na dze aafrɛ mboanofo no aba, na dze ama woeetutu nseraban no akɔ.
3. Na sɛ wɔhɛn n'ebien nyina a, mboanofo no nyinara hɔn ano nhyia wo nkyɛn wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano.
4. Na sɛ wɔhɛn kor a, ɔno mbapɔmba no, Israel mpempem mu etsitsir no, hɔn ano nhyia wo nkyɛn.
5. Na sɛ hom hɛn ewubɔ a, nseraban a ɔdeda boka afamu no ntutu nkɔ.
6. Na sɛ hom hɛn ewubɔ toa do ebien a, nseraban a ɔdeda pomu afamu no ntutu nkɔ: worubotu no, wɔnhɛn ewubɔ.
7. Mbom sɛ bagua no ribehyia a, hom nhɛn, na mma hom nnhɛn ewubɔ.
8. Na Aaron ne mbabanyin asɔfo no, na wɔmfa ntotorobɛnto no nhɛn; na ɔnyɛ daa ahyɛdze mma hom wɔ hom ebusuasantsen nyinara mu.
9. Na sɛ hom rokɔ ɔsa wɔ hom asaase do nye ɔtamfo a ohiahia hom ho akɔko a, ɔno hom mfa ntotorobɛnto no nhɛn ewubɔ; na wɔnkaa hom wɔ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn enyim, na wɔngye hom nkwa wɔ hom atamfo nsamu.
10. Na hom enyigye nda mu, nye hom afahyɛ a wɔahyehyɛ mu, na hom abosoom ahyɛse mu so, hom mfa ntotorobɛnto no nhɛn no wɔ hom ɔhyew afɔr nye hom asomdwee afɔr no do; na ɔnyɛ nkaa mma hom wɔ hom Nyankopɔn enyim: emi nye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn.
11. Na ɔbaa dɛ afe a otsia ebien no, bosoom a otsia ebien no, wɔ bosoom no ne da a otsia eduonu no mu no, mununkum no maa noho do fii dase ntamadan no do.
12. Na Israelmba no sii mu fii Sinai sar do dɛ mbrɛ hɔn akwantu no tse: na mununkum no kegyinaa Paran sar do.
13. Na wodzii kan tuu hɔn akwantu no dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE mbrasɛm a ɔwɔ Moses nsamu no tse.
14. Na Judamba nseraban do frankaa na odzii kan tui dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tsetsee: na Aminadab ne babanyin Nahson na ɔhwɛɛ no dɔm no do.
15. Na Zuar ne babanyin Netanel na ɔhwɛɛ Isakarmba ebusuakuw dɔm no do.
16. Na Helon ne babanyin Eliab na ɔhwɛɛ Zebulunmba ebusuakuw dɔm no do.
17. Na wɔpaa ntamadan no; na Gerson ne mbabanyin nye Merari ne mbabanyin a wɔsoasoaa ntamadan no tui.
18. Na Reuben nseraban do frankaa tui dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tsetsee: na Sedeur ne babanyin Elizur na ɔhwɛɛ no dɔm no do.
19. Na Zurisadai ne babanyin Selumiel na ɔhwɛɛ Simeonmba ebusuakuw dɔm no do.
20. Na Deutel ne babanyin Eliasaf na ɔhwɛɛ Gadmba ebusuakuw dɔm no do.
21. Na Kohatfo no soasoaa ahotseweebea no dze tui: na binom so sii ntamadan no dze kodur dɛ wɔbae.
22. Na Efraimmba nseraban do frankaa tui dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tsetsee: na Ammihud ne babanyin Elisama na ɔhwɛɛ no dɔm no do.
23. Na Pedazur ne babanyin Gamaliel na ɔhwɛɛ Manaasɛmba ebusuakuw dɔm no do.
24. Na Gideoni ne babanyin Abidan na ɔhwɛɛ Benjaminmba ebusuakuw dɔm no do.
25. Na Danmba nseraban do frankaa a ɔyɛ nseraban nseraban nyinara ne nkyidɔm no, tui dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tsetsee: na Ammisaddai ne babanyin Ahiezer na ɔhwɛɛ no dɔm no do.
26. Na Okran ne babanyin Pagiel na ɔhwɛɛ Asermba ebusuakuw dɔm no do.
27. Na Enan ne babanyin Ahira na ɔhwɛɛ Naftalimba ebusuakuw dɔm no do.
28. Israelmba akwantu a wotui dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm no tsetsee no nye yi; na wotui.
29. Na Moses see Hobab, a ɔyɛ n'asew Midiannyi Reuel ne babanyin no, dɛ, Yerutu akɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE kasaa ho dɛ, Medze mebɛma hom no: bra ma yɛnkɔ, na yɛbɛyɛ wo yie: na EWURADZE aka Israel ho asɛm pa.
30. Na ɔsee no dɛ, Moronnkɔ; mbom mobɔkɔ mankasa m'asaase do, nye m'ebusua mu.
31. Na ɔsee dɛ, Mepa wo kyɛw, mma nngya hɛn; susuampa dɛ ɔwo inyim mbrɛ yebesi akyer nsera wɔ sar no do ntsi, ɔwo yɛ enyiwa fa ma hɛn.
32. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, sɛ ɔwo nye hɛn kɔ a, ampa, ɔbɛba dɛ papa kor a EWURADZE dze bɛyɛ hɛn no, ɔnoara na yɛdze bɛyɛ wo.
33. Na wotutui fii EWURADZE bepɔw no ho tuu ndaansa kwan; na EWURADZE ahyɛmudaka no tui dzii hɔn enyim kɔr ndaansa kwan, dɛ ɔrokɔhwehwɛ ahomgyebea ama hɔn.
34. Na sɛ wotu fi nseraban no mu a, nna EWURADZE mununkum no wɔ hɔn do adzekyee.
35. Na ɔbaa dɛ, sɛ adaka no rutu a, Moses ka dɛ, EWURADZE, soɛr ɛ, na ma w'atamfo mbɔ mpetse; na ma hɔn a wokyir wo nguan w'enyim.
36. Na sɛ ɔsoɛ a, ɔka dɛ, EWURADZE, san bra Israel mpempem no nkyɛn.

## Tsir 11

1. Na ɔman no tse dɛ ehwinhwimfo a wɔkasa bɔn wɔ EWURADZE asomu: na EWURADZE tsee no, n'ebufuw dzɛwee; na EWURADZE ogya hyewee wɔ hɔn mu, na ɔmeen nseraban no ano noho.
2. Na ɔman no sui tɔɔ Moses do; na Moses paa EWURADZE kyɛw, na ogya no dumii.
3. Na wɔfrɛɛ bea no dzin dɛ Tabera: osiandɛ EWURADZE ogya hyewee wɔ hɔn mu.
4. Na aforafora nyimpa dodow a wɔwɔ hɔn mu no hɔn kɔn dɔɔ adze: Na Israelmba no so suu bio, na wɔsee dɛ, Woana na ɔbɛma hɛn nam ma yeedzi?
5. Yɛkaakaa esunam a yedzii no wɔ Egypt no kwa; efer, nye anamuna, nye fan, nye anwew, nye galik no:
6. afei dze hɛn kra etwintwam; biribi biribiara nnyi hɔ: yennyi hwee gyedɛ  manna   yi ara na yɛahwɛ.
7. Na  manna   no tse dɛ korianda aba, na no ho tse dɛ bedula hyɛ ho.
8. Ɔman no kekyinii, na wɔtasetasee, na wɔnyaam no wɔ nwuyambo do, anaa wosiiw no nwɔdur mu, na wɔnoaa no wɔ asɛn mu, na wɔyɛɛ no tatar: na ne dɛw tse dɛ ngo momon ne dɛw.
9. Na sɛ ɔbɔsu tɔ gu nseraban no do anadwe a, manna   no so tɔ gu do.
10. Na Moses tsee dɛ ɔman no worususu wɔ hɔn ebusua ebusua mu: nyimpa biara ɔwɔ no buw abowano: na EWURADZE n'ebufuw dzɛɛw kɛsenara; na ɔyɛɛ Moses ahomtsew.
11. Na Moses see EWURADZE dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn ntsi na ɔwo nye w'akowaa edzi no bɔn? Na ɔyɛ dɛn ntsi na mennya w'enyim enyimnyam, dɛ edze ɔman yi nyinara adzesoa ato mo do yi?
12. Ana emi na minyinseen ɔman yi nyinara? Emi na mowoo hɔn, na esee me dɛ Tur hɔn wɔ wo koko do, dɛ mbrɛ egya bagyegyefo tur enumfoanoba, fa kɔ asaase a ekaa ho ntam maa hɔn egyanom no do?
13. Henfa na minya nam mema ɔman yi nyinara? Osiandɛ wosu gu mo do, dɛ, Ma hɛn nam, ma yendzi.
14. Mo nko murunntum nnkora ɔman yi nyinara, osiandɛ ɔyɛ dur dodow ma me.
15. Na sɛ dɛm na enye me bedzi no a, mepa wo kyɛw, sɛ menya w'enyim enyimnyam a, ɔnodze oku mbom na ku me; na mma munnhu m'awerɛhow.
16. Na EWURADZE see Moses dɛ, Boa Israel mpanyimfo mu eduosuon ano ma me hɔn a inyim dɛ wɔyɛ ɔman no mpanyimfo, nye hɔn do asafohemfo; na fa hɔn bra ehyiadzibuw no ho, ma wɔnye wo ngyina hɔ.
17. Na mibesian na menye wo abɛkasa wɔ hɔ: na mebɛfa sunsum a ɔwɔ wo do no bi, na medze egu hɔn do; na wɔnye wo aasoa ɔman no adzesoa no, amma woara nko aannsoa.
18. Na se ɔman no dɛ, Hom ntsew homho nsie ɔkyena, na hom bedzi nam: osiandɛ hom ekosu egu EWURADZE asomu dɛ, Woana na ɔbɛma hɛn nam ma yeedzi? Oye ma hɛn wɔ Egypt mbom: dɛm ntsi EWURADZE bɛma hom nam, na hom eedzi.
19. Nnyɛ da kor na hom bedzi, anaa nda ebien anaa nda enum, nnyɛ dadu, anaa adaduonu.
20. Mbom bosoom ara mu, ekodu dɛ obefifir hom ahwentokura mu, na ayɛ hom mfon: osiandɛ hom apow EWURADZE a ɔwɔ hom mu no, na hom esu wɔ n'enyim aka dɛ, Ebɛn ntsi na yefii Egypt bae?
21. Na Moses see dɛ, Ɔman a mowɔ hɔn mu yi yɛ anamɔnmufo mpemahaesia; na ɔwo ese dɛ, Mebɛma hɔn nam ma woeedzi no bosoom ara mu.
22. Aso wobokuku nguan nye anantwi ama hɔn ma aaso hɔn? Anaa wɔbɔboaboa po mu nam nyinara ano ama hɔn, ma aaso hɔn?
23. Na EWURADZE see Moses dɛ, Ana woetwa EWURADZE nsa do a? Afei na ibohu kyerɛ dɛ m'asɛm bɛba mu ama wo, anaa ɔnnkɛba mu.
24. Na Moses fiir edzi, na ɔkɛkaa EWURADZE n'asɛm no kyerɛɛ ɔman no: na ɔboaa ɔman mpanyimfo no mu mbanyin eduosuon ano, na ɔdze hɔn begyinaa Buw no ho hyiae.
25. Na EWURADZE sianee wɔ mununkum no mu, na ɔkasaa kyerɛɛ no, na ɔfaa sunsum a ɔwɔ no do no bi, na ɔdze guu mpanyimfo eduosuon no do: na ɔbaa dɛ, sunsum no baa hɔn do no, wɔhyɛɛ nkɔm, naaso wɔannyɛ dɛm bio.
26. Mbom ɔkaa mbanyin beenu wɔ nseraban no mu, ɔbaako ne dzin nye Eldad, na kor no so ne dzin dze Medad: na sunsum no baa hɔn do; na wɔfora hɔn a wɔkyerɛɛw hɔn no mu dze, na wonnyae nnkɔr Buw no ho: na wɔhyɛɛ nkɔm wɔ nseraban no mu.
27. Na aberantsɛ bi tutuu mbirika kɛkaa kyerɛɛ Moses, na ɔsee dɛ, Eldad nye Medad rehyɛ nkɔm wɔ nseraban no mu.
28. Na Nun ne babanyin Josua, Moses no somfo no, ɔyɛ ne mbanyin mpamufo mu kor no, buae see dɛ, Mo wura Moses, bra hɔn.
29. Na Moses see no dɛ, Ana emi ntsi erotwe nyinkun a? Ɔka mo nko a nkyɛ Nyankopɔn aama EWURADZE ne man nyinara ayɛ nkɔnhyɛfo, ma EWURADZE dze no sunsum eegu hɔn do.
30. Na Moses sanee baa nseraban no mu, ɔno nye Israel mpanyimfo no.
31. Na mframa fi EWURADZE nkyɛn bɔe, na ɔdze Nkokɔhwerew fi po no ho boguu nseraban no ho, bɛyɛ da kor akwantu wɔ afa ha, nye da kor akwantu wɔ afa ha so, dze hyiaa nseraban no ho, na hɔn krɔn bɛyɛ basafa kor wɔ asaase enyi.
32. Na ɔman no soɛɛr adzekyee no nyinara, nye anadwe no nyinara, na wɔkɔboaboaa Nkokɔhwerew no ano: nyia ɔboaa kumaabi ano mpo, onyaa  homer   du: na wɔhatahatae araa dze twaa nseraban no ho hyiae maa hɔnho.
33. Aber a nam no da ho wowɔ hɔn se mu a wonnyae nnkosae no, EWURADZE ebufuw fraam ɔman no do, na EWURADZE dze ɔyardɔm kɛsenara bobɔɔ ɔman no.
34. Na wɔfrɛɛ bea no dzin dɛ Kibrot-hataava, osiandɛ hɔ na wosiee ɔman a hɔn kɔn dɔɔ adze no.
35. Na ɔman no tui fii Kibrot-hataava kɔr Hazerot; na wɔtsenaa Hazerot.

## Tsir 12

1. Na Miriam nye Aaron kasaa tsiaa Moses wɔ Kusnyi ɔbaa bi a ɔawar no no ntsi: osiandɛ nna ɔawar Kusnyi ɔbaa bi.
2. Na wɔsee dɛ, Ana ampaara dɛ Moses nko na EWURADZE nye no akasa? Ana ɔnnye hɛn so nnkasae? Na EWURADZE tsee.
3. Afei ɔbarimba Moses dze nna ɔdwe dodoodow, ɔbor nyimpa a wɔwɔ asaase do nyinara do.
4. Na EWURADZE kasaa awerɛfir mu kyerɛɛ Moses, nye Aaron, nye Miriam dɛ, Hom baasa hom mfir edzi mbra ehyiadzibuw no ho. Na hɔn baasa no fiir edzi bae.
5. Na EWURADZE sianee wɔ mununkum dum mu, na obegyinaa Buw no n'abowano, na ɔfrɛɛ Aaron nye Miriam: na hɔn beenu no fiir edzi bae.
6. Na ɔsee dɛ, Afei hom ntsie me nsɛm: sɛ nkɔnhyɛfo bi wɔ hom mu a, emi EWURADZE mibeyi moho akyerɛ no wɔ enyido adzehu mu, adaaso mu na menye no bɛkasa.
7. M'akowaa Moses nntse dɛm; ɔno na medze me fie nyinara ahyɛ ne nsa.
8. Ano nye ano na menye no rekasa, mekyerɛ petee mu a, na nnyɛ abɛbu mu a; na ɔbɔhwɛ EWURADZE no su: na ɔyɛ dɛn ntsi na hom ennsuro dɛ hom bɛkasa etsia m'akowaa Moses?
9. Na EWURADZE ebufuw fraam hɔn do; na ofii hɔ kɔree.
10. Na mununkum no twee fii Buw no do; na, hwɛ, Miriam ayar kwata, ɔhoa dɛ esukwankyɛba: na Aaron hwɛɛ Miriam no, hwɛ, nna ɔayar kwata.
11. Na Aaron see Moses dɛ, Ao mo wura, mepa wo kyɛw, mma mmfa bɔn nnto hɛn do, na yɛayɛ nkwaseadze, na ɔno ho yɛayɛ bɔn.
12. Me kyɛw ara nye yi, mma ɔnnyɛ dɛ obi a oewu fi ne na yamu aba a woedzi no honam fa dada.
13. Na Moses sui frɛɛ EWURADZE dɛ, Ao Nyankopɔn, meserɛ wo, sa no yarba.
14. Na EWURADZE see Moses dɛ, Sɛ n'egya hina n'enyi do ntafi a, ana nkyɛ n'enyi nnwu nda esuon bi a? Ma wɔntow no mu nda esuon wɔ nseraban no ekyir, na n'ekyir no na wɔmfa no mbra mu bio.
15. Na wɔtoow Miriam mu wɔ nseraban no ekyir nda esuon: na ɔman no enntu araa kodur dɛ wɔfaa Miriam baa mu bio.
16. Na n'ekyir no na ɔman no tui fii Hazerot, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Paran sar do.

## Tsir 13

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.13.2, Soma mbanyin ma wɔnkɛsera Keenan asaase a medze rema Israelmba no: hɔn egyanom ebusuakuw biara mu no hom nsoma mu banyin kor, na hɔn nyina nyɛ hɔn mu mbapɔmba no bi.
3. Na Moses somaa hɔn fi Paran sar do dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE mbrasɛm tse: hɔn nyinara wɔyɛ mbanyin a wɔyɛ Israelmba mu etsitsir.
4. Na hɔn edzin nye yi: Reuben ebusuakuw dze, Zakur ne babanyin Sammua.
5. Simeon ebusuakuw dze, Hori ne babanyin Safat.
6. Juda ebusuakuw dze, Jefune ne babanyin Kaleb.
7. Isakar ebusuakuw dze, Josef ne babanyin Igal.
8. Efraim ebusuakuw dze, Nun ne babanyin Hosea.
9. Benjamin ebusuakuw dze, Rafu ne babanyin Palti.
10. Zebulun ebusuakuw dze, Sodi ne babanyin Gaddiel.
11. Josef ebusuakuw dze, mekyerɛ Manaasɛ ebusuakuw dze, Susi ne babanyin Gaddi.
12. Dan ebusuakuw dze, Gemali ne babanyin Ammiel.
13. Aser ebusuakuw dze, Maekɛl ne babanyin Setur.
14. Naftali ebusuakuw dze, Vofsi ne babanyin Nahibi.
15. Gad ebusuakuw dze, Maki ne babanyin Geuel.
16. Iyinom nye mbanyin a Moses somaa hɔn dɛ wɔnkɛsera asaase no hɔn edzin. Na Moses frɛɛ Nun ne babanyin Hosea no dɛ Josua.
17. Na Moses somaa hɔn dɛ wɔnkɛsera Keenan asaase no, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom mfa pomu afamu ɔkwan yi do, na hom nkɔfow mbepɔw no:
18. na wɔnhwɛ asaase no, mbrɛ ɔtse; nye ɔman a wɔtse do no, kyerɛ dɛ wɔyɛ dzen anaa wɔyɛ mberɛw, dɛ wosuar anaa wɔdɔɔ so;
19. nye mbrɛ asaase a wɔtse do no tse, dɛ oye anaa omuo; na mbrɛ nkurow a wɔtsetse mu no tsetsee, dɛ wɔtsetse nseraban mu, anaa akoban mu;
20. na mbrɛ asaase no tse, dɛ ɔyɛ sɔɔsɔɔ anaa etwee, dɛ ndua wɔ do anaa ɔnntse dɛm. Na hom nnya akokodur, na wɔmfa asaase no do aba bi mbra. Nna aber no yɛ esikyireamba ababer a odzi kan n'aber.
21. Nna wosii mu kɔree, na wɔkɛseraa asaase no fi Zin sar do kodur Rehob, wɔ mbrɛ wɔnam kɔ Hamat no.
22. Na wɔfowee faa Pomu afamu, na wodur Hebron; na hɔ na Anakmba no, Ahiman, Sesai, nye Talmai, wɔwɔ. (Na Hebron yi wɔkyekyeer no mfe esuon ansaana wɔrekyekyer Zoan a ɔwɔ Egypt.)
23. Na wodur Eskol ɔbɔn no mu, na wotwaa abaw a esikyireamba siaw kor wɔ do wɔ hɔ, na beenu wuraa no dua soae; na wɔdze pɔmegranate eduaba, nye adɔmba so bi bae.
24. Wɔfrɛɛ bea no Eskol ɔbɔn no mu, osian siaw no a Israelmba twaa no wɔ hɔ no ntsi.
25. Na adaduanan soe no, wɔsanee fii asaase no ne nserae mu bae.
26. Na wosii mu na wɔbaa Moses, nye Aaron, nye Israelmba mboanofo no nyinara nkyɛn, wɔ Paran sar do, wɔ Kades; na wɔbɔɔ hɔn amandzɛɛ kyerɛɛ hɔn, nye mboanofo no nyinara, na wɔdze asaase no do aba no kyerɛɛ hɔn.
27. Na wɔkaa kyerɛɛ no, na wɔsee dɛ, Yɛkɔr asaase a esomaa hɛn do no do, na numfonsu nye ewo pem wɔ do ampa; na no do aba nye yi.
28. Naaso ɔman a wɔtse asaase no do no na wɔyɛ dzen, na nkurow no wɔabɔ ho ban, ɔsoso dodow: na nkyii so yehun Anakmba wɔ hɔ.
29. Amalek tse Pomu afamu asaase no do: na Hitifo, nye Jebusfo, nye Amorifo, tsetse mbepɔw no do: na Keenanfo tse po no ho, nye Jordan nkyɛn.
30. Na Kaleb dwedwee ɔman no wɔ Moses enyim, na ɔsee dɛ, Hom mma yɛnkɔfow nkɔ prɛko, na yɛnkɛfa, na yebotum edzi hɔn do nyim.
31. Na mbanyin a wɔnye no kɔree no dze see dɛ, Yerunntum nnkɔfow nnkɔ nnkɛhyɛ ɔman no do; osiandɛ wɔyɛ dzen sen hɛn.
32. Na wɔdze asaase a wɔkɛserae no ho atsenka bɔn brɛɛ Israelmba no dɛ, Asaase a yɛakɛfa mu akɛsera no, ɔyɛ asaase a odzi no do atsefo no; na nkorɔfo a yehun hɔn wɔ do no nyinara wɔyɛ nyimpa a wɔyɛ akraban.
33. Na hɔ na yehun abran akɛse no, Anak ne mbabanyin no a wofi Nefilfo mu no: na yɛyɛɛ dɛ mbɛbɛw wɔ hɛnankasa hɛn enyi do, na dɛmara na yɛyɛe wɔ hɔn enyi do.

## Tsir 14

1. Na mboanofo no nyinara memaa hɔn ndze do, na wɔkekaa mu; na ɔman no suu anadwe no.
2. Na Israelmba no nyinara hwinhwiin Moses nye Aaron ho: na mboanofo no nyinara see hɔn dɛ, Ka hɛn nko a nkyɛ Nyankopɔn maa yewuwui wɔ Egypt asaase do! Anaa sar yi do ha ara mpo, ka hɛn nko a nkyɛ Nyankopɔn maa yewuwui!
3. Na ebɛn ntsi na EWURADZE dze hɛn reba asaase yi do, dɛ yɛmbɔtotɔr nkrantsee ano? Hɛn yernom nye hɛn mbofraba bɛyɛ afonam: ana onnye mma hɛn dɛ yɛbɛsan akɔ Egypt bi a?
4. Na wɔsesee hɔnho dɛ, Wɔmma yɛnhyɛ ɔpanyin, na yɛnsan nkɔ Egypt.
5. Nna Moses nye Aaron dze hɔn enyim butubutuuw famu wɔ Israelmba mboanofo bagua no nyinara enyim.
6. Na Nun ne babanyin Josua nye Jefune ne babanyin Kaleb a wofi hɔn a wɔkɛseraa asaase no mu no, sunsuaan hɔn ntar mu:
7. na wɔkasaa kyerɛɛ Israelmba mboanofo no nyinara dɛ, Asaase a yetwaa mu kɔree kɛserae no, ɔyɛ asaase pa mbordo.
8. Sɛ yɛsɔ EWURADZE enyi a, ɔno ɔdze hɛn bodu asaase no do, na ɔdze ama hɛn; asaase a numfonsu nye ewo pem do.
9. EWURADZE do etua ara nko na mma hom nntsew, nkyii so mma hom nnsuro asaase no do nkorɔfo no; na wɔyɛ edziban ma hɛn: hɔn akataman efi hɔn do, na hɛn dze EWURADZE nye hɛn wɔ hɔ: mma wonnsuro hɔn.
10. Na mboanofo no nyinara kaa dɛ wonsiw Josua na Kaleb abo. Na EWURADZE enyimnyam puee ehyiadzibuw no mu kyerɛɛ Israelmba no nyinara.
11. Na EWURADZE see Moses dɛ, Ɔman yi rubu me enyimtsia ekodu dabɛn? Na dabɛn na wɔrenngye me nndzi, wɔ nsɛnkyerɛdze a mayɛ wɔ hɔn mu nyinara mu no ekodu?
12. Medze owuyarba mobɔbɔ hɔn, na masɛɛ hɔn, na medze wo mayɛ ɔman kɛse a ɔwɔ dzin sen hɔn.
13. Na Moses see dɛ, Ɔno Egyptfo bɛtse; na ɔwo dze wo tum na edze eyi ɔman yi efi hɔn mu;
14. na wɔbɛka akyerɛ asaase yi do atsefo: wɔatse dɛ ɔwo EWURADZE ewɔ ɔman yi mu; na ɔwo EWURADZE wohu wo enyim nye enyim, na wo mununkum gyina hɔn do, na ɔwo na idzi hɔn enyim, wɔ mununkum dum mu adzekyee, nye ogya dum mu adzesaa.
15. Afei sɛ iku ɔman yi dɛ nyimpa kor a, ɔno amanaman a wɔatse wo nka no bɛka dɛ,
NUM.14.16, Osiandɛ EWURADZE enntum dɛ ɔdze ɔman yi kɔ asaase a ɔkaa hɔn ho ntam no do no, ntsi na oeku hɔn wɔ sar no do no.
17. Na afei, mepa wo kyɛw, ma EWURADZE no tum nyɛ kɛse, dɛ mbrɛ ɔwo aka dɛ,
NUM.14.18, EWURADZE fa ebufuw nyaa, na n'adɔyɛ so, ɔdze emumuyɛ nye mbrato kyɛ, na bem bi na onnkobu ɔfɔdzinyi no; ɔdze egyanom hɔn emumuyɛ hwɛ mba, kodu ebusuasantsen ebiasa nye anan do.
19. Mepa wo kyɛw, fa ɔman yi hɔn emumuyɛ fir hɔn dɛ mbrɛ w'adɔyɛ ne kɛse tse, na dɛ mbrɛ ɔwo dze efir ɔman yi, efi Egypt araa ebodu sesei.
20. Na EWURADZE see dɛ, Medze akyɛ dɛ mbrɛ w'asɛm tse no:
21. mbom nokwar ara, dɛ metse ase, na dɛ EWURADZE enyimnyam bɛhyɛ asaase nyinara ma yi;
22. osiandɛ nyimpa nyina a woehu m'enyimnyam, nye me nsɛnkyerɛdze a meyɛe wɔ Egypt nye sar no do no, naaso wɔasɔ me ahwɛ mpɛn du nye yi, na woenntsie me ndze no;
23. ampaara wonnkohu asaase a mekaa ho ntam mekyerɛɛ hɔn egyanom no, nkyii so hɔn mu biara a obu me enyimtsia no runnhu:
24. na m'akowaa Kaleb dze, osiandɛ ɔwɔ sunsum bi so wɔ no mu, na oedzi m'ekyir koraa no ntsi, medze no mubodu asaase a ɔkɔr do no do; na n'asefo afa.
25. Afei Amalekfo nye Keenanfo tse ɔbɔn no mu: ɔkyena hom ndan, na hom ntu nkɔ sar no do wɔ Mbɛw Po kwan do.
26. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses nye Aaron dɛ,
NUM.14.27, Dabɛn na memma mboanofo bɔn yi a wohwinhwin tsia me yi kwan ma onkodu? Israelmba hɔn ehwinhwin a wohwinhwin tsia me no matse.
28. Se hɔn dɛ, Metse ase yi, EWURADZE na ɔse, ampaara dɛ mbrɛ hom akasa wɔ m'asomu no, dɛmara so na mebɛyɛ hom:
29. sar yi do ara na hom efun bɛdeda; nye hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ hom mu no nyinara, dɛ mbrɛ hom dodow nyina tse, ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu nye ekyir do no a woehwinhwin etsia me no,
NUM.14.30, ampaara hom runndu asaase a memaa me nsa do mekyerɛɛ dɛ mebɛma hom atsena do no do, gyedɛ Jefune ne babanyin Kaleb, nye Nun ne babanyin Josua.
31. Mbom hom mbofraba a hom see dɛ wɔbɛyɛ afonam no, hɔn na medze hɔn bɔkɔ do, na wobohu asaase a hom apow no.
32. Na hom dze, sar yi do ara na hom efun bɛdeda.
33. Na hom mba ayɛ aworamafo wɔ sar no do mfe eduanan, na wɔasoa hom ndwamanyɛ, ekodu dɛ hom efun bɛsa wɔ sar no do.
34. Nda a hom dze kɛseraa asaase no, no dodow mbrɛ ɔtse no, mekyerɛ adaduanan na da kor nye afe kor, nna hom nsoa hom emumuyɛ, mekyerɛ mfe eduanan, na hom ehu mbrɛ me pa tse.
35. Emi EWURADZE na maka, ampaara iyi na medze bɛyɛ mboanofo bɔn yi nyinara a woedzi ehyia etsia me yi: sar yi do ara na wɔbɛsa, na hɔ na wobowuwu.
36. Na mbanyin no a Moses somaa hɔn ma wɔkɛseraa asaase no a wɔsanee bae, na wɔmaa mboanofo no nyinara hwinhwinii tsiaa me no, dɛ wɔdze asaase no ho atsenka bɔn bae no,
NUM.14.37, mekyerɛ mbanyin no a wɔdze asaase no ho atsenka bɔn bae no, wowuu owuyar wu wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
38. Mbom Nun ne babanyin Josua, nye Jefune ne babanyin Kaleb, na wonyaa nkwa wɔ mbanyin a wɔkɔree kɛseraa asaase no mu.
39. Na Moses kaa nsɛm yi kyerɛɛ Israelmba no nyinara: na ɔman no gyaam kɛsenara.
40. Na wɔsoɛɛr ahanamakye, na wɔfowee kɔr bepɔw no etsifi, dɛ, Hwɛ hɛn nye yi, yɛbɔfow akɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE abɔ ho anohoba no: na yɛayɛ bɔn.
41. Na Moses see dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn ntsi na afei hom to EWURADZE mbrasɛm yi, na iyi dze ɔrennyɛ yie?
42. Mma hom mmfow nnkɔ, na EWURADZE nnyi hom mu; mma hom aanntotɔr wɔ hom atamfo enyim.
43. Na Amalekfo nye Keenanfo wɔ hom enyim hɔ, na hom bɔtotɔr wɔ nkrantsee ano: osiandɛ hom asan efi EWURADZE ekyir ntsi EWURADZE so rinndzi hom ekyir.
44. Na wotsitsii hɔn enyiwa ho ara dɛ wɔrofow akɔ bepɔw no etsifi: naaso EWURADZE n'ahyɛmudaka na Moses dze, woemmfi nseraban no mu emmfi.
45. Nna Amalekfo, nye Keenanfo a wɔtse bepɔw no do no sianee bae bokunkum hɔn na wɔbɔɔ hɔn petsee araa kodur Horma.

## Tsir 15

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.15.2, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom du hom tsenabew asaase a medze rema hom no do,
NUM.15.3, na wɔrobɔ ogya afɔr ama EWURADZE, ɔhyew afɔr, anaa okum afɔr, dze ahyɛ anohoba bi ma, anaa dɛ ayamuyi afɔr, anaa hom afahyɛ a wɔahyɛ no mu, dze eyi nka huanhuam ama EWURADZE a ofi anantwi, anaa nguan mu a:
4. ɔno nyia ɔdze n'ayɛyɛdze ba no, ɔmfa edziban afɔr isiam pa efa ebupɛn du mu kor nka ngo  hin   ebupɛn anan mu kor ho mbrɛ EWURADZE:
5. na ɔnom afɔr wenyin, hin   ebupɛn anan mu kor, na fa ka ɔhyew afɔr, anaa okum afɔr eguambaa kor ho siesie.
6. Na oguannyin dze, fa isiam pa efa ebupɛn du mu ebien a wɔdze ngo  hin   ebupɛn ebiasa mu kor afrɔw, siesie yɛ edziban afɔr:
7. na ɔnom afɔr no fa wenyin  hin   ebupɛn ebiasa mu kor bra, ma onyi nka huanhuam mma EWURADZE.
8. Na sɛ ɔnantwisaa na irisiesie abɔ ɔhyew afɔr, anaa okum afɔr, dze ahyɛ anohoba bi ma, anaa edze robɔbɔ asomdwee afɔr ama EWURADZE a:
9. ɔno edziban afɔr a ɔmfa nka ɔnantwisaa no ho nye isiam pa efa ebupɛn du mu ebiasa a wɔdze ngo  hin   ɔfa afrɔw.
10. Na fa wenyin  hin   ɔfa yɛ ɔnom afɔr no bra bɔbɔ ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ma EWURADZE.
11. Dɛm na wɔnyɛ ɔnantwisaa kor, anaa oguannyin kor, anaa nguambaanyin no, anaa mpɔnkyemba no nkorkor.
12. Dɛ mbrɛ dodow a hom besiesie no tse no, dɛmara na hom nyɛ no nkorkor dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dodow tse.
13. Ekuromfo nyinara nyɛ ndzɛmba yi dɛm mfa mbɔ ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam mma EWURADZE.
14. Na sɛ ɔhɔho bi soɛ hom mu, anaa obiara a ɔwɔ hom mu wɔ hom ebusuasantsen nyina mu no, na ɔbɔbɔ ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ama EWURADZE no; dɛ mbrɛ hom yɛ no, dɛmara so na ɔnyɛ.
15. Bagua no, ahyɛdze kor na ɔnda hɔ mma hom na ɔhɔho a ɔbɔsoɛ hom mu no, ɔnyɛ daa ahyɛdze wɔ hom ebusuasantsen nyina mu: dɛ mbrɛ hom tse no, dɛmara so na ɔhɔho no nyɛ wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
16. Mbra kor nye ɔmom kor na ɔnda hɔ mma hom, nye ɔhɔho a ɔabɔsoɛ hom mu nyina.
17. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.15.18, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom du asaase a medze hom rokɔ do no do a,
NUM.15.19, ɔno ɔba dɛ, sɛ hom ridzi asaase no do edziban no bi a, hom mbɔ ohim afɔr mma EWURADZE.
20. Hom mbɔrkan no bi ne tatar na hom mfa nyɛ ohim afɔr: dɛ mbrɛ hom yɛ ewuporoweebea ohim afɔr no, dɛmara so na hom nhim no.
21. Hom mbɔrkan no bi na hom mfa mma EWURADZE dɛ ohim afɔr wɔ hom ebusuasantsen nyina mu.
22. Na sɛ hom fom, na hom annhyɛ mbransɛm yi a EWURADZE aka akyerɛ Moses no nyinara nsew,
NUM.15.23, mekyerɛ dza EWURADZE nam Moses nsa do ahyɛ hom nyinara, ofi da a EWURADZE hyɛɛ mbra no, na ɔdze ba yi wɔ hom ebusuasantsen nyina mu no a;
24. ɔno ɔba dɛ, sɛ wɔyɛ no ntɔgon mu, dɛ mboanofo no nnyim a, mboanofo no nyinara mfa ɔnantwisaa eburwa kor mbɔ ɔhyew afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ma EWURADZE, nka n'edziban afɔr, nye no nom afɔr no ho dɛ mbrɛ mbra no tse, nye apɔnkyenyin kor na ɔnyɛ bɔn ho afɔr.
25. Na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma Israelmba mboanofo no nyina, na wɔdze bɛkyɛ hɔn; na ɔyɛ mfom, na hɔn mfom no ntsi, wɔdze hɔn ayɛyɛdze aba dɛ EWURADZE ogya afɔr, nye hɔn bɔn ho afɔr wɔ EWURADZE enyim:
26. na wɔdze befir Israelmba mboanofo no nyinara, nye ɔhɔho a ɔabɔsoɛ hɔn mu no; na ɔman no nyinara afamu na wɔyɛɛ no ntɔgon mu.
27. Na sɛ nyimpa kor bi annhyɛ da na ɔyɛ bɔn a, ɔno ɔmfa apɔnkyeber a oedzi afrenhyia mbra mbɔbɔ bɔn ho afɔr.
28. Na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma ɔkra a ɔfom no, sɛ ɔyɛ bɔn no ber a ɔnndze enyidaho wɔ EWURADZE enyim a, ɔmpata mma no; na wɔdze bɛkyɛ no.
29. Hom nnya mbra kor nto hɔ mma nyia ɔyɛ biribi a ɔnndze enyidaho dɛ ɔyɛ okuromnyi wɔ Israelmba mu, anaa ɔhɔho a ɔbɔsoɛ hom mu no.
30. Ɔkra a ɔhyɛ da ara yɛ bɔn no dze, sɛ ɔyɛ okuromnyi bi o, ɔhɔho bi o, oedzi etsia EWURADZE; na dɛm ɔkra no wobotwa no efi ne man mu akyen.
31. Osiandɛ oebu EWURADZE n'asɛm enyimtsia, na ɔato ne mbra no; otwa ara na wobotwa ɔkra no akyen, n'emumuyɛ nda no do.
32. Na aber a Israelmba wɔwɔ sar no do no, wohun ɔbanyin bi a ɔrepɛ nyena ahomgyeda.
33. Na hɔn a wohun no dɛ ɔrepɛ nyena no wɔdze no brɛɛ Moses nye Aaron, nye mboanofo no nyinara.
34. Na wɔbɔɔ no dua mu, osiandɛ wonnyae nnkyerɛɛ dza wɔmfa nyɛ no.
35. Na EWURADZE see Moses dɛ, Oku na wonku banyin no: mboanofo no nyinara nsiw no abo wɔ nseraban no ekyir.
36. Na mboanofo no nyinara dze no baa nseraban no ekyir na wosiiw no abo ma owui; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
37. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.15.38, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na hyɛ hɔn ma wɔnyɛ hɔn ntar ano ndwow wɔ hɔn ebusuasantsen nyina mu, na wɔmfa bibir ahoma nsusuo n'ano n'ano ndwow no mu:
39. na ɔnyɛ ndwow mma hom, ma hom aahwɛ, na wɔaakaa EWURADZE mbrasɛm nyinara, na wɔaayɛ; na hom eennkekyin enndzi hom akoma nye hom enyiwa ekyir, dza hom tɔ dɛ wodzi ekyir ndwamanyɛ mu no:
40. ama hom aakaa na wɔayɛ me mbransɛm nyinara, na hom ayɛ krɔnkrɔn ama hom Nyankopɔn.
41. Emi nye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn a miyii hom fii Egypt asaase do dɛ mebɛyɛ hom Nyankopɔn no: emi nye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn.

## Tsir 16

1. Afei Kora a n'egya nye Izhar a ɔyɛ Kohat a n'egya nye Levi ne babanyin no na Eliab ne mbabanyin Datan na Abiram na Pelet ne babanyin on (Eliab na Pelet yɛ Reuben ne mbabanyin)
2. na wɔsoɛree tsiaa Moses. Wɔnye Israelmba no mu bi, mboanofo no mu mbapɔmba ahaenu eduonum a wɔfrɛ hɔn bagua mu, nyimpa a wɔwɔ dzin:
3. na wɔpoon hɔnho hyiae bɔɔ Moses nye Aaron do ngua, na wɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom yɛ dodow, na mboanofo nyinara yɛ krɔnkrɔn, hɔn mu obiara, na EWURADZE wɔ hɔn mu: ɔyɛ dɛn ntsi mbɔ na hom mema hom ho do wɔ EWURADZE ne baguaa do?
4. Na Moses tsee no, ɔdze n'enyim butuuw famu:
5. na ɔkasaa kyerɛɛ Kora nye no kuw no nyinara dɛ, Anapa EWURADZE bɛkyerɛ hɔn a wɔyɛ nedze, nye nyia ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn, na ɔbɛma ɔaba ne nkyɛn: mekyerɛ nyia ɔbɛsan mu eyi no no nna ɔbɛma ɔaba ne nkyɛn.
6. Iyi na hom nyɛ; hom mfa nduhuam asɛn, Kora nye no kuw nyinara;
7. na wɔmfa ogya ngu mu, na wɔmfa eduhuam ngu do wɔ EWURADZE enyim ɔkyena: na ɔbɛba dɛ nyimpa a EWURADZE bɛfa no no, ɔbɛyɛ krɔnkrɔn: hom yɛ dodow, hom Levi mbabanyin.
8. Na Moses see Kora dɛ, Afei hom ntsie, hom Levi mbabanyin:
9. ana ɔyɛ hom adze ketseaba, dɛ Israel Nyankopɔn no atsew hom efi Israel mboanofo mu dɛ ɔdze hom rebɛn noho; ma hom aayɛ EWURADZE ntamadan no ho edwuma, na hom egyina mboanofo no enyim asom hɔn;
10. Na ɔdze nuanom, Levi ne mbabanyin no nyinara ho abɛn, na hom rohwehwɛ asɔfodzi so yi?
11. Dɛm ntsi na ɔwo nye wo kuw nyinara ehyia wɔ EWURADZE do: na Aaron dze, woana nye no na hom hwinhwin tsia no?
12. Na Moses somae ma wɔkɛfrɛɛ Datan nye Abiram, Eliab ne mbabanyin no: na wɔsee dɛ, Yɛremmba:
13. ana ɔyɛ adze ketseaba dɛ eyi hɛn efi asaase a numfonsu nye ewo pem wɔ do do, ruboku hɛn wɔ sar do, na kyenkyennkyen iridzi hɛn do ahenmbasɛm so yi?
14. Nkyii so emmfaa hɛn mmbaa asaase a numfonsu nye ewo pem wɔ do no do, na emmfaa haban nye wenyin nture enyadze no so mmaa hɛn; ana mbanyin yi enyiwa na irubotutu? Yɛremmba.
15. Na Moses no bo fuuw kɛsenara, na ɔsee EWURADZE dɛ, Mma w'enyi nnsɔ hɔn afɔrbɔ: menngyee hɔn nsamu asoaso kor, nkyii so mennyɛɛ hɔn mu kor biara bɔn.
16. Na Moses see Kora dɛ, Ɔwo nye wo mboanofo no nyinara mbra EWURADZE enyim, ɔwo, nye hɔn, nye Aaron, mbra ɔkyena:
17. na hom mu nyimpa biara mfa n'eduhuamsɛn, na hom mfa eduhuam ngugu mu, na hom mu nyimpa biara mfa n'eduhuamsɛn mbra EWURADZE enyim, nduhuamsɛn ahaenu eduonum; ɔwo so, nye Aaron, obiara mfa n'eduhuamsɛn.
18. Na wɔfefae, nyimpa biara nye n'eduhuamsɛn, na wɔdze ogya guguu mu, na wɔnye Moses nye Aaron kegyinagyinaa ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano.
19. Na Kora poon mboanofo no nyinara hyiae bɔɔ hɔn do ngua wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano: na EWURADZE enyimnyam yii noho edzi kyerɛɛ mboanofo no nyinara.
20. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses nye Aaron dɛ,
NUM.16.21, Hom ntsew homho mfi mboanofo yi mu, ma menhyew hɔn amon mu.
22. Na wɔdze hɔn enyim butubutuuw famu, na wɔsee dɛ, Ao Nyankopɔn, honam nyinara hɔn esunsum Nyankopɔn, ana nyimpa kor bɛyɛ bɔn, na wo bo efuw mboanofo no nyinara?
23. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.16.24, Kasa kyerɛ mboanofo no dɛ, Hom ntwe hom ho do mfi Kora, Datan, nye Abiram ntamadan ho.
25. Na Moses soɛree na ɔkɔr Datan nye Abiram nkyɛn; na Israel mpanyimfo no dzii n'ekyir kɔree.
26. Na ɔkasaa kyerɛɛ ɔman no dɛ, Mepa hom kyɛw, hom ntwe hom ho mfi nyimpa bɔn yi hɔn asesee ho, na mma hom nnka hɔn biribiara, bɛyɛ na hom ahyew wɔ hɔn bɔn nyinara mu.
27. Nna wɔtwee hɔnho fii Kora, Datan, nye Abiram ntamadan ho wɔ afa nyina: na Datan nye Abiram fiir edzi, na wobegyinagyinaa hɔn asesee abowano, hɔn yernom, nye hɔn mbabanyin, nye hɔn mbɔtoaba so.
28. Na Moses see dɛ, Iyi na hom dze bohu dɛ EWURADZE na ɔasoma me dɛ mindzi dwuma yi nyinara; na mommbɔɔ mankasa me tsir mu yɛ.
29. Sɛ nyimpa nyinara wu ara na iyinom bowuwu, anaa sɛ dza ɔto nyimpa nyinara na ɔbɔtɔ hɔn a, ɔnodze na nnyɛ EWURADZE na ɔasoma me.
30. Na sɛ adze fofor bi dze na EWURADZE bɛyɛ, na asaase bobue n'ano, na ɔamen hɔn, nye dza odzi wɔ hɔn nyinara, na wɔnam enyikan akɔ samanadze a; ɔnodze hom ntse ase dɛ mbanyin yi ebu EWURADZE enyimtsia.
31. Na ɔbaa dɛ owiee nsɛm yi nyinara ka no, asaase a ɔwɔ hɔn ase no paae:
32. na asaase buee n'ano, na ɔmeen hɔn, nye hɔn efifo, nye nyimpa a wodzi wɔ Kora nyinara, nye hɔn adze nyinara.
33. Nna hɔn, nye dza odzi wɔ hɔn nyinara, nam enyikan sianee kɔr samanadze: na asaase no seraam hɔn do, na wɔherae wɔ bagua no mu.
34. Na Israelfo a woetwa hɔnho ehyia no nyinara guanee wɔ hɔn nkekamu no ntsi: na wɔsee dɛ, Bɛyɛ na asaase amen hɛn.
35. Na ogya fii EWURADZE hɔ bae, na ɔbɛhyeew mbanyin ahaenu eduonum a wɔdze eduhuam bae no.
36. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.16.37, Kasa kyerɛ ɔsɔfo Aaron ne babanyin Eleazar dɛ, ɔntase nduhuam asɛn no mfi ogyahyewee no mu, na fa ogya no petsepetse enyim noho; na ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn:
38. mekyerɛ ndzebɔnyɛfo yi a wɔayɛ hɔnankasa hɔn nkwa ho bɔn nduhuam asɛn no, na ma wɔnyɛ no ɔborntɛrba mfa nyɛ afɔrmbukyia no ho nkataho: osiandɛ wɔdze baa EWURADZE enyim, dɛm ntsi ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn: na ɔnyɛ asɛnkyerɛdze mma Israelmba.
39. Na ɔsɔfo Eleazar faa boran nduhuam asɛn no a hɔn a wɔhyewee no dze bae no; na wɔboree dze yɛɛ afɔrmbukyia no ho nkataho:
40. dɛ ɔnyɛ nkaadze mma Israelmba, amma ɔhɔho bi a ommfi Aaron n'asefo mu aammba ho ammbɛhyew eduhuam wɔ EWURADZE enyim; mma ɔaannyɛ dɛ Kora, nye no kuw no: dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE nam Moses do kaa kyerɛɛ no no.
41. Na n'adzekyee no Israelmba mboanofo no nyinara hwinhwinii tsiaa Moses nye Aaron dɛ, Hom na hom eku EWURADZE ne man.
42. Na ɔbaa dɛ, aber a mboanofo no boaboaa hɔn ano tsiaa Moses nye Aaron no, wɔdandaan hɔn enyi wɔ ehyiadzibuw no do: na, hwɛ, mununkum no akata do, na EWURADZE enyimnyam no yii noho edzi.
43. Na Moses nye Aaron baa ehyiadzibuw no enyim.
44. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.16.45, Hom mma hom ho do mfi mboanofo yi mu, ma menhyew hɔn amon mu. Na wɔdze hɔn enyim butubutuuw famu.
46. Na Moses see Aaron dɛ, Fa w'eduhuamsɛn, na sɔ afɔrmbukyia no do ogya gu mu, na fa eduhuam to do, na suo mu nantsew ntsɛmara kɔ mboanofo no nkyɛn, na kɛpata ma hɔn: na ebufuhyew efi EWURADZE hɔ efir; ɔyardɔm no ahyɛ ase.
47. Na Aaron fae dɛ mbrɛ Moses kae no, na otuu mbirika kɔr bagua no finimfin; na, hwɛ, ɔyardɔm no ahyɛ ase wɔ ɔman no mu: na ɔdze eduhuam no gui, na ɔpatae maa ɔman no.
48. Na ogyinaa ewufo nye enyikamfo no ntamu; na wosiankaa ɔyardɔm no.
49. Afei hɔn a wowuwui wɔ ɔyardɔm no mu wɔyɛ mpemduanan nye ahaesuon, ɔsor hɔn a Kora asɛm no ntsi wowuwui no.
50. Na ɔyardɔm no siankaa no, Aaron sanee baa Moses nkyɛn wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano.

## Tsir 17

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.17.2, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na gyegye hɔn hɔ mpoma, egyanom ofi kor mu kor, wɔ hɔn mbapɔmba no nyinara hɔ dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, mpoma duebien: kyerɛw nyimpa biara ne dzin wɔ no poma do.
3. Na kyerɛw Aaron ne dzin wɔ Levi poma do: na poma kor nyɛ hɔn egyanom efi do tsitsir kor dze.
4. Na gyaa mu gu ehyiadzibuw no mu wɔ Dase no enyim, mbrɛ menye hom hyia no.
5. Na ɔbɛba dɛ banyin a mibeyi no no, no poma bɛfefɛw: na mebɛma mo ho ehwinhwin a Israelmba hwinhwin tsia hom no egyaa.
6. Na Moses kasaa kyerɛɛ Israelmba, na hɔn mbapɔmba no nyinara memaa no mpoma, ɔbapɔmba kor nedze kor, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee no, mekyerɛ mpoma duebien: na Aaron no poma wɔ hɔn mpoma no mu.
7. Na Moses gyaa mpoma no mu guu EWURADZE enyim wɔ Dasebuw no mu.
8. Na ɔbaa dɛ adze kyee no, Moses kɔr Dasebuw no mu no; na, hwɛ, Aaron no poma no a ɔyɛ Levi ofi dze no afefɛw, ayɛ frɔmfrɔm, na egu nhyiren, na asow almɔnd aba beree.
9. Na Moses dze mpoma no nyinara fi EWURADZE enyim brɛɛ Israelmba no nyinara: na wɔhwɛe, na nyimpa biara faa no poma.
10. Na EWURADZE see Moses dɛ, Fa Aaron no poma no san to Dase no enyim, ma wɔmfa nsie nyɛ asɛnkyerɛdze mma mba asoɔdzenfo no; ama eetwa hɔn ehwinhwin a wohwinhwin tsia me no do, amma woeennwuwu.
11. Dɛm na Moses yɛe: dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ no no, dɛmara na ɔyɛe.
12. Na Israelmba no kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, Hwɛ yeruwu, yɛreyera, hɛn nyinara rehera.
13. Obiara a ɔba ho, nyia ɔba EWURADZE ntamadan no ho, owu: ana hɛn nyinara yenwuwu?

## Tsir 18

1. Na EWURADZE see Aaron dɛ, Ɔwo nye wo mbabanyin nye w'egyanom fi a wɔka wo ho no nsoa ahotseweebea no ho emumuyɛ: na ɔwo nye wo mbabanyin a wɔka wo ho no nsoa hom asɔfodzi ho emumuyɛ.
2. Na wo nuanom no so, Levi ebusuakuw no, w'egya n'ebusuakuw no, fa hɔn ka woho bra ho, ma wɔmboa wo wɔ ɔsom no mu: mbom ɔwo nye wo mbabanyin a wɔka wo ho no nsom wɔ dasebuw no enyim.
3. Na wɔnhwɛ w'ahwɛdodze, nye Buw no nyinara ahwɛdodze no: ahotseweebea ndzɛmba ho nye afɔrmbukyia no ho nko ara na mma wɔmmba, amma woeennwuwu, hɔn o, hom o.
4. Na wɔnka wo ho, na wɔnhwɛ ehyiadzibuw no n'ahwɛdodze, dɛ mbrɛ Buw no no ho edwuma nyinara tse: na mma ɔhɔho bi mmba hom nkyɛn.
5. Na hom nhwɛ ahotseweebea ahwɛdodze no, nye afɔrmbukyia no ahwɛdodze no: amma ebufuhyew bi aammba Israelmba do bio.
6. Na mara, hwɛ, mafa wo nuanom Levifo no mefi Israelmba no mu: mama hom dɛ wɔyɛ akyɛdze a wɔdze ama EWURADZE, dɛ wɔnyɛ ehyiadzibuw no ho edwuma.
7. Na ɔwo nye wo mbabanyin a wɔka wo ho no nhwɛ hom asɔfodzi no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no n'adze biara mu, na ntwamutam no mu dze no mu; na hom mfa nsom: medze asɔfodzi no mema hom dɛ akyɛdze dwuma: na ɔhɔho a ɔba ho no wonku no.
8. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Aaron dɛ, Hwɛ, emi dze, medze me mado afɔr ahwɛdodze ama wo, mekyerɛ Israelmba adzekrɔnkrɔn nyinara, medze ama ɔwo nye wo mbabanyin no osian ngosera no ntsi, dɛ daa ahyɛdze.
9. Iyi na ɔnyɛ wodze wɔ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn a wɔdze esie efi ogya no mu no mu: hɔn ayɛyɛdze biara, mekyerɛ hɔn edziban afɔr biara, nye hɔn bɔn ho afɔr biara, nye hɔn asodzi ho afɔr biara a wɔdze brɛ me no, ɔnyɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn mma ɔwo nye wo mbabanyin no.
10. Dzi no wɔ bea a hɔ yɛ krɔnkrɔn: ɔbanyin biara ndzi bi; ɔnyɛ krɔnkrɔn mma wo.
11. Na iyi nyɛ wodze; hɔn ayɛyɛdze mu ɔmado afɔr, mekyerɛ Israelmba hɔn ohim afɔr nyinara; medze ama ɔwo, nye wo mbabanyin, nye wo mbabaa a wɔka wo ho, dɛ daa ahyɛdze: obiara a no ho tsew wɔ wo fi mu no ndzi bi.
12. Ngo mu papa nyinara, nye wenyin nye eburow mu papa nyinara no mu nduadzewakan a wɔdze ma EWURADZE no, ɔwo na medze ama wo.
13. Hɔn asaase do nduadzewa nyinara mu edziban beree a wɔdze brɛ EWURADZE no, ɔnyɛ wodze; obiara a no ho tsew wɔ wo fi mu no ndzi bi.
14. Israel mu adze biara a woehyira do ama nyɛ wodze.
15. Adze biara a obue ɔyafun, wɔ honam a wɔdze brɛ EWURADZE no nyinara mu, nyimpa nye abowa nyina, nyɛ wodze: naaso ɔpon na pon nyimpa mbakan, nye abowa a hɔn ho nntsew mbakan so pon hɔn.
16. Na hɔn a wɔrobɔpon hɔn a woedzi bosoom no, pon hɔn, dɛ mbrɛ w'abotwa tse, sika dwetɛbona enum, dɛ mbrɛ ahotseweebea dwetɛbona a ɔnoara nye  gera   eduonu.
17. Na ɔnantwi abakan, anaa apɔnkye abakan dze, mma mmpon; wɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn: fa hɔn bɔgyaa no petse afɔrmbukyia no do, na hyew hɔn seradze yɛ ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ma EWURADZE.
18. Na hɔn nam nyɛ wodze, dɛ mbrɛ ohim nyan nye aserɛ nyimfa no yɛ wodze no.
19. Ndzɛmba krɔnkrɔn mu ɔmado afɔr nyinara a Israelmba dze brɛ EWURADZE no, medze mama ɔwo, nye wo mbabanyin, nye wo mbabaa a wɔka wo ho no, dɛ daa ahyɛdze: ɔyɛ EWURADZE enyim daa nkyen ahyɛmu dze ma ɔwo nye w'asefo a wɔka wo ho no.
20. Na EWURADZE see Aaron dɛ, Mma nnya egyapadze wɔ hɔn asaase mu, nkyii so mma nnya kyɛfa bi wɔ hɔn mu: emi nye wo kyɛfa nye w'egyapadze wɔ Israelmba mu.
21. Na Levimba no dze, hwɛ, medze Israel mu ebupɛn du du nyinara ama hɔn dɛ egyapadze, ahyɛ hɔn som a wɔsom no ananmu, mekyerɛ ehyiadzibuw no ho ɔsom no a.
22. Na ɔdzekɔr yi mma Israelmba no mmba ehyiadzibuw no ho, bɛyɛ na wɔasoa bɔn, na woewuwu.
23. Na Levifo no dze wɔnyɛ ehyiadzibuw no ho ɔsom edwuma, na wɔnsoa no ho mbusu: ɔnyɛ daa ahyɛdze wɔ hom ebusuasantsen nyina mu, na Israelmba mu dze worinnya egyapadze.
24. Na Israelmba ebupɛn du du a wɔdze ma dɛ ɔmado afɔr ma EWURADZE no, medze ama Levifo no dɛ egyapadze: dɛm ntsi na mase hɔn dɛ, Israelmba mu dze worinnya egyapadze no.
25. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.18.26, Afei so kasa kyerɛ Levifo no, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom regyegye Israelmba no hɔ ebupɛn du du no a medze fi hɔn hɔ ama hom dɛ hom egyapadze no a, ɔno hom mfa no mu ebupɛn du du mu ebupɛn du nyɛ no ɔmado afɔr mma EWURADZE.
27. Na wombu hom ɔmado afɔr mma hom dɛ ewuporoweebea eburow, nye wenyintwer mayɛ.
28. Dɛm na hom so mbɔ ɔmado afɔr mma EWURADZE wɔ hom ebupɛn du du a, hom gyegye no wɔ Israelmba hɔ nyinara mu; na hom mfa no mu EWURADZE ɔmado afɔr no mma ɔsɔfo Aaron.
29. Hom akyɛdze nyinara mu na hom mfa mu EWURADZE ɔmado afɔr biara mbra, no mu papa nyinara, mekyerɛ no mu adzekrɔnkrɔn no.
30. Dɛm ntsi se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom rema no mu papa no do a, ɔno wombu mma Levifo no dɛ ewuporoweebea ndɔree, nye wenyintwer ndɔree.
31. Na hom ndzi no beebiara, hom nye hom efifo: na ɔyɛ hom akatua a ɔhyɛ hom ehyiadzibuw ho ɔsom edwuma ananmu.
32. Na sɛ hom ma no mu papa no do a, hom ronnsoa bɔn bi wɔ ne ntsi: na mma hom nnhura Israelmba adzekrɔnkrɔn ho, mma hom eennwuwu.

## Tsir 19

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses nye Aaron dɛ,
NUM.19.2, Iyi nye mbra ahyɛdze a EWURADZE ahyɛ, dɛ, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba no, ma wɔmfa ɔnantwiber memen a nkekaa nnyi ho a onndzi dzɛm a kɔndua nnkɔr no do pɛn:
3. na hom mfa no mma ɔsɔfo Eleazar, na ɔmfa no mpue mbra nseraban no ekyir, na obi nku no wɔ n'enyim:
4. na ɔsɔfo Eleazar mfa ne nsatseaba mfa no bɔgyaa bi, na ɔmpetse no bɔgyaa no bi ngu ehyiadzibuw no enyim afamu mpɛn esuon:
5. na obi nhyew ɔnantwiber no wɔ n'enyim; no nwoma, nye ne nam, nye no bɔgyaa, nye n'egyanan no:
6. na ɔsɔfo no mfa kyindur dua, nye odwonhataw, nye serdaa, na ɔntow ngu ɔnantwiber a ɔrehyew no do.
7. Na afei ɔsɔfo no nhor ne ntar, na onguar wɔ nsu mu, na n'ekyir no na ɔmbra nseraban no mu, na ɔsɔfo no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir.
8. Na nyia ɔhyew no no nhor ne ntar wɔ nsu mu, na onguar no honam wɔ nsu mu, na no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir.
9. Na ɔbanyin bi a no ho tsew nsesaw ɔnantwiber no nso no, na ɔmfa nkogu nseraban no ekyir beebi a ɔtsew, na wɔmfa nsie nyɛ Israelmba mboanofo ahoguar nsu: ɔyɛ bɔn ho afɔr.
10. Na nyia ɔsesaw ɔnantwiber no nso no ɔnhor ne ntar, na no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir: na ɔnyɛ Israelmba no, nye ɔhɔho a ɔsoɛ hɔn mu no, daa ahyɛdze.
11. Nyia ɔka nyimpa biara no fun no no ho nntsew araa nda esuon:
12. ɔnoara na ɔmfa iyi nguar noho da a otsia ebiasa no, na da a otsia esuon no na no ho atsew: na sɛ oennguar noho da a otsia ebiasa no, nye da a otsia esuon no a, no ho renntsew.
13. Obiara a ɔka nyimpa biara a oewu no fun no, na onnguar noho no, ogu EWURADZE ntamadan no ho fi; na ɔkra no wobotwa no efi Israel akyen: osiandɛ wɔammpetse ahoguar nsu no enngu no do ntsi no ho nntsew; n'ahoɔfi da ho wɔ no do.
14. Sɛ nyimpa wu asese mu a no ho mbra nye yi: obiara a ɔba asese no mu, na obiara a ɔwɔ asese no mu no, no ho nntsew araa nda esuon.
15. Na kɔɔpow biara a n'ano da hɔ a onnyi ebuado wɔ do no, no ho nntsew.
16. Na obiara a ɔka obi a ɔatɔr nkrantsee ano, anaa fun bi, anaa nyimpa biew bi, anaa nda bi wɔ haban mu no, no ho nntsew araa nda esuon.
17. Na nyia no ho nntsew no wɔmfa bɔn ho afɔr nhyewee no no nso no bi, na wɔmfa mbasu ngu do wɔ kɔɔpow mu mma no:

## Tsir 22

16. Na wɔbaa Balaam nkyɛn, na wɔsee no dɛ, Mbrɛ Zipor babanyin Balak se nye yi, Mepa wo kyɛw, mma biribi nnsiw wo me nkyɛn ba ho kwan:

19. Afei dɛm ntsi, mɛserɛ hom, hom so ntsintsim ha anadwe yi, ma munhu dza EWURADZE bɛka akyerɛ me bio.
20. Na Nyankopɔn baa Balaam nkyɛn anadwe, na ɔsee no dɛ, Sɛ mbanyin no ba behyia wo a, Soɛr, nye hɔn nkɔ; mbom dza meka mekyerɛ wo no nko na yɛ.
21. Na Balaam soɛɛr anapa no, na ɔhyehyɛɛ n'asoasopɔnkɔ, na ɔnye Moab mbapɔmba no kɔree.
22. Na Nyankopɔn n'ebufuw dzɛwee dɛ ɔkɔree: na EWURADZE bɔfoe begyinaa ɔkwan no do dɛ ɔyɛfo bi a otsia no. Na ɔnodze ɔtse n'asoaso do reba, na no nkowaa beenu ka no ho.

27. Na asoaso no hun EWURADZE no bɔfoe no, na obutuuw famu wɔ Balaam n'ase: na Balaam n'ebufuw dzɛwee, na ɔdze n'abaa bɔɔ asoaso no.

31. Nna EWURADZE buee Balaam n'enyiwa, na ohun EWURADZE no bɔfoe no dɛ ogyina ɔkwan no mu, wɔatwe ne nkrantsee kitsa ne nsamu: na ɔbɔɔ ne tsir adze, na ɔdze n'enyim butuuw famu.
32. Na EWURADZE no bɔfoe no see no dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn ntsi na abɔ w'asoaso mpɛn ebiasa yi? Hwɛ, emi na mefir edzi abɛyɛ wo ɔyɛfo osiandɛ wo kwan no akyea wɔ m'enyim:
33. na asoaso no hun me, na ɔdanee fii m'enyim mpɛn ebiasa yi: sɛ wɔanndan emmfi m'enyim dze a, nkyɛ wo so sisei meku wo, na magye no nkwa.
34. Na Balaam see EWURADZE no bɔfoe no dɛ, Mayɛ bɔn; na minnyim dɛ ɔwo na igyinaa ɔkwan no mu tsiaa me: dɛm ntsi afei, sɛ emmpɛ a, mebɛsan m'ekyir bio.
35. Na EWURADZE no bɔfoe no see Balaam dɛ, Nye mbanyin no nkɔ: na mbom asɛm a mebɛka makyerɛ wo no nko na ka. Nna Balaam nye Balak mbapɔmba no kɔree.
36. Na aber a Balak tsee dɛ Balaam reba no, ofiir edzi kehyiaa no wɔ Moab Kurow a ɔda Arnon ntoaado a ɔwɔ ntoaado ano no.
37. Na Balak see Balaam dɛ, Ana nnyɛ enyiberdo ara na mosomaa dɛ wombehyia wo ma bra? Ɔyɛ dɛn ntsi na ammba me nkyɛn? Egye dzi dɛ murunntum mennhyɛ wo enyimnyam a?
38. Na Balaam see Balak dɛ, Hwɛ, maba wo nkyɛn: ana afei mowɔ tum biara a medze meka asɛm bi? Asɛm a Nyankopɔn dze bɛhyɛ m'anomu no, ɔno na mebɛka.
39. Na Balaam nye Balak kɔree, na wɔbaa Kiriat-huzoth.
40. Na Balak dze anantwi nye nguan bɔɔ okum afɔr, na ɔdze kɛmaa Balaam nye mbapɔmba a wɔnye no wɔ hɔ no.
41. Na ɔbaa dɛ anapa no, Balak faa Balaam, na ɔdze no fowee kɔr Baal ne mbea sorɔnsorɔm no do, na ofi hɔ hun ɔman no ano.

## Tsir 23

1. Na Balaam see Balak dɛ, Sisi afɔrmbukyia esuon ma me wɔ ha, na siesie anantwisaa esuon nye nguannyin esuon ma me wɔ ha.
2. Na Balak yɛɛ dɛ mbrɛ Balaam kae no; na Balak nye Balaam dze ɔnantwisaa kor nye oguannyin kor bobɔɔ afɔr wɔ afɔrmbukyia biara do.
3. Na Balaam see Balak dɛ, Gyina wo hyew afɔr no ho, na monkɔ, anyanyanbiara EWURADZE bɛba ebehyia me a: na biribiara a ɔdze bɛkyerɛ me no mebɛka makyerɛ wo. Na osii mu kɔr sorɔnsorɔm pataa bi do.
4. Na Nyankopɔn behyiaa Balaam: na Balaam see no dɛ, Mesiesie afɔrmbukyia esuon no, na medze ɔnantwisaa nye oguannyin nkorkor abobɔ afɔr wɔ afɔrmbukyia biara do.
5. Na EWURADZE dze asɛm hyɛɛ Balaam anomu, see dɛ, San kɔ Balak nkyɛn, na dza ka nye yi.
6. Na ɔsanee kɔr ne nkyɛn, na, hwɛ, ogyina ne hyew afɔr no ho, ɔno nye Moab mbapɔmba no nyinara.
7. Na ɔkasaa no abɛbu mu see dɛ, Aram na Balak kɛfaa me bae, Moabhen no faa me fi Boka mbepɔw no mu dɛ: Bra, bɛpaa Jakob ma me, Na bra, bɛhyɛ Israel enyimguase.
8. Meyɛ dɛn na mepaa nyia Nyame mmpaa no? Na meyɛ dɛn na mehyɛ nyia EWURADZE nnhyɛɛ no enyimguase?
9. Abotan etsifi na mifi muhun no, Na akoko do na mifi megyeen no: Hwɛ, ɔman a no nko bɛtsena a, Na worummbu no mmfora amanaman.
10. Woana botum akan Jakob huntuma, Anaa Israel ebupɛn anan mu kor no dodow? Ma munwu ɔtseneneenyi wu, Na ma m'ewiei nyɛ dɛ nedze!
11. Na Balak see Balaam dɛ, Ebɛnadze na edze ayɛ me yi? Mekɛfaa wo dɛ bɔdom m'atamfo, na, hwɛ, ehyira hɔn koraa.
12. Na obuae see dɛ, Ana onnhia dɛ mohwɛ yie na meka dza EWURADZE dze hyɛɛ m'anomu no bi a?
13. Na Balak see no dɛ, Mepa wo kyɛw, bra ma yɛnkɔ beebi fofor, mbrɛ ifi bohu hɔn; ibohu hɔn ano nko, na innkohu hɔn nyinara: na paa hɔn ma me wɔ hɔ.
14. Na ɔfaa no kɔr Zofim haban mu, wɔ Pisga etsifi, na osisii mbukyia esuon, na ɔdze ɔnantwisaa, nye oguannyin bobɔɔ ɔhyew afɔr wɔ afɔrmbukyia biara do.
15. Na ɔsee Balak dɛ, Gyina wo hyew afɔr ho ha, ber a mirikehyia EWURADZE wɔ enyim noho yi.
16. Na EWURADZE behyiaa Balaam, na ɔdze asɛm hyɛɛ n'anomu, see dɛ, San kɔ Balak nkyɛn, na mbrɛ ka nye yi.
17. Na ɔbaa ne nkyɛn, na, hwɛ, ogyina ne hyew afɔr no ho, na Moab mbapɔmba ka no ho. Na Balak see no dɛ, EWURADZE aka dɛn?
18. Na ɔkasaa abɛbu mu see dɛ, Soɛr, Balak, na tsie; Yɛ aso ma me, Zipor babanyin:
19. Nyankopɔn nnyɛ nyimpa, na oeedzi ator; Anaaso nyimpa ba, na oeenu noho: Ana ɔase, na ɔrennyɛ? Ana ɔakasa, na ɔremma onntsim?
20. Hwɛ, nhyira ho mbra na menya: Na oehyira, na murunntum menndan enyi.
21. Onnhun emumuyɛ wɔ Jakob mu, Nkyii so onnhun nkontompɔ wɔ Israel mu: EWURADZE ne Nyankopɔn nye no wɔ hɔ, Na ɔhen ho esebɔ wɔ hɔn mu.
22. Nyame eyi hɔn wɔ Egypt; Ɔwɔ ahoɔdzen dɛ ɔnantwi bɔyafo.
23. Ampaara nkaber bi nnyi Jakob mu, Nkyii so ebisa bi nnyi Israel mu: Afei na wɔbɛka no wɔ Jakob nye Israel ho dɛ, Ebɛnadze na Nyame ayɛ yi!
24. Hwɛ, ɔman no soɛr dɛ ogyataber, Na ɔma noho do dɛ ogyata: Orummbutuw gyedɛ oedzi afonam, Na ɔanom ɔtɔfo bɔgyaa ana.
25. Na Balak see Balaam dɛ, Mpaa na eremmpaa hɔn a, na nhyira so innhyira hɔn.
26. Na Balaam buae na ɔsee Balak dɛ, Ana mannka mannkyerɛ wo dɛ, dza EWURADZE bɛka no nyinara, nna mebɛyɛ?
27. Na Balak see Balaam dɛ, Afei bra, ma memfa wo monkɔ beebi fofor; anyanyanbiara ɔbɔsɔ Nyankopɔn enyi ma efi hɔ adom hɔn ama me.
28. Na Balak faa Balaam kɔr Peor etsifi a ɔhwɛ sar pradada no enyi no.
29. Na Balaam see Balak dɛ, Sisi afɔrmbukyia esuon ma me wɔ ha, na siesie anantwisaa esuon nye nguannyin esuon ma me wɔ ha.
30. Na Balak yɛɛ dɛ mbrɛ Balaam see no, na ɔdze ɔnantwisaa nye oguannyin bobɔɔ ɔhyew afɔr wɔ afɔrmbukyia biara do.

## Tsir 24

1. Na aber a Balaam hun dɛ asɔ EWURADZE enyi dɛ ohyira Israel no, ɔannkɔ ebisa bio, dɛ nkan no, mbom ɔdze n'enyi kedzii sar no do.
2. Na Balaam maa n'enyi do, na ohun Israel dɛ wogu hɔ dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusuakuw ebusuakuw tsetsee; na Nyankopɔn sunsum baa no do.
3. Na ɔkasae see dɛ, Beor babanyin Balaam se dɛ, Ɔbanyin a n'enyi hu adze yie no se dɛ:
4. Nyia otsie Nyame ne nsɛm no, Nyia ohu Twerammpon enyido adze no, Ɔrohwe ase a, wɔtseew n'enyi do no, ɔse dɛ:
5. Ao Jakob, mbrɛ wo tsenabea dua yɛ fɛw a, Wo ntamadan no, Ao Israel!
6. Wɔtserɛw dɛ mbɔnsa, Dɛ esutsen ho nture, Dɛ  alɔe  ndua a EWURADZE atsetsɛw, Dɛ nsu ho kyindur ndua.
7. No mbokitsi mu nsu bobu do, Na n'asefo bɛtsena nsu pii mu, Na ne hen bɔkrɔn asen Agag, Na wɔbɛma n'ahendzi do.
8. Nyame eyi no efi Egypt; Ɔwɔ ahoɔdzen dɛ ɔnantwi bɔyafo: Ɔbɛmen amanaman a wɔyɛ n'atamfo, Na woebubu hɔn mbiew esinesin, Na ɔdze n'egyen menaba ehwirahwira hɔn mu.
9. Ɔkotowee, ɔdaa famu dɛ ogyata, Na dɛ ogyataber; woana na obopusuw no? Nhyira nka obiara a ohyira wo, Na mpaa nka obiara a ɔpaa wo.
10. Na Balak n'ebufuw dzɛɛw Balaam do, na ɔbɔɔ ne nsamu: na Balak see Balaam dɛ, Mibehyiaa wo dɛ bɛpaa m'atamfo, na, hwɛ, ɔwo ehyira hɔn mpɛn ebiasa nye yi.
11. Dɛm ntsi afei guan kɔ mbrɛ ifi: modween dɛ nkyɛ mebɛhyɛ wo enyimnyam kɛse; na, hwɛ, EWURADZE esiw w'enyimnyam ho kwan.
12. Na Balaam see Balak dɛ, Ana mannka annkyerɛ w'abɔfoe a esomaa hɔn me nkyɛn no so dɛ,
NUM.24.13, Sɛ Balak dze dwetɛ nye sika ne fi ma mpo bɛma me a, murunntum mummbu EWURADZE n'asɛm do mommbɔ me tsir mu nnkɛyɛ papa anaa bɔn: dza EWURADZE bɛka no, ɔno na mebɛka?
14. Na afei, hwɛ, morokɔ me man mu: bra, na memma hu dza ɔman yi dze bɛyɛ wo man wɔ nda a odzi ekyir no mu.
15. Na obuu no bɛ see dɛ, Beor babanyin Balaam se dɛ, Ɔbanyin a n'enyi emua no se dɛ:
16. Nyia otsie Nyame ne nsɛm no, Na onyim Abɔso no ho nyimdzee no, Nyia ohu Twerammpon enyido adze no, Ɔrohwe ase a, wɔtseew n'enyi do no ɔse dɛ:
17. Muhu no, nnyɛ sesei dze a: Mohwɛ no, ɔmmbɛn dze: Nworaba bi fi Jakob mu bopue, Na ahempoma befi Israel mu asoɛr, Na wɔabɔ afa Moab nkukuwa mu, Na wɔadwerew ɔko mba gyigyeegye no nyinara.
18. Na Edom bɛyɛ adzenyadze, Seir so a wɔyɛ n'atamfo bɛyɛ n'adzenyadze; Na Israel bɛyɛ mbanyindze ara.
19. Na obi fi Jakob mu bedzi tum, Na ɔasɛɛ kurow no mu dza aka no.
20. Na ɔhwɛɛ Amalek, na ɔsɔɔw ne kasaa no do see dɛ Amalek nye amanaman mu odzikamfo; Na n'ewiei bowie no ɔsɛɛ mu.
21. Na ɔhwɛɛ Kennyi, na ɔkasaa bio see dɛ, Wo tsenabew si hɔ pintsinn, Na wɔdze wo berɛbuw ahyɛ ɔbotan mu.
22. Naaso Kain bɔhwer, Ekodu dɛ Assur bɛfa wo ndɔmmum.
23. Na owiee ne kasaa no obuu no bɛ see dɛ, Ebei, Nyame reyɛ iyi no woana bɛtsena ase?
24. Na ahɛn befi Kittim mbew do aba, Na wɔabɛhaw Assur, na wɔahaw Eber, Na ɔnoara so ewie no ɔsɛɛ mu.
25. Na Balaam soɛree, na osii mu sanee kɔr ne tsenabew: na Balak so faa no kwan kɔree.

## Tsir 25

1. Na Israel tsenaa Sittim, na ɔman no hyɛɛ ase nye Moab mbabaa no yɛɛ ehuhudze:
2. na wohyiahyiaa ɔman no kɔr hɔn enyame okum afɔr ho; na ɔman no dzidzii, na wɔbɔɔ hɔn mu adze maa hɔn enyame no.
3. Na Israel dze noho bɔɔ Baal-peor ho: na EWURADZE ebufuw dzɛɛw Israel do.
4. Na EWURADZE see Moses dɛ, Fa ɔman no mu etsitsir nyinara, na sensɛn hɔn ma EWURADZE wɔ ewia do, ma EWURADZE ebufuhyew no ndan mfi Israel do.
5. Na Moses see Israel atsɛmbuafo no dɛ, Hom mu obiara nkuku ne mbanyin a wɔdze hɔnho akɔbɔ Baal-peor ho no.
6. Na, hwɛ, Israelmba no mu kor dze Midiannyi ɔbaa bi baa no nuanom no nkyɛn wɔ Moses n'enyim, nye Israelmba mboanofo no nyinara enyim, aber a worususu wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano.
7. Na aber a ɔsɔfo Aaron ne babanyin Eleazar, ne babanyin Finehas, hunii no, ɔsoɛree fii mboanofo no mu, na ɔfaa nkambaa kitsaa ne nsamu;
8. na odzii Israelnyi no ekyir kɔr pia no mu, na ɔdze wowɔɔ hɔn beenu nyina hwira hwiraa hɔn mu, Israelnyi no, nye ɔbaa no ohwiraa no fur mu. Nna ɔyardɔm no siankae fii Israelmba no do.
9. Na hɔn a wowuwui wɔ ɔyardɔm no mu no wɔyɛ mpemeduonu anan.
10. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.25.11, Ɔsɔfo Aaron ne babanyin Eleazar, ne babanyin Finehas, adan m'ebufuw efi Israelmba do, wɔ dɛ ɔatwe me nyinkuntwe no nyinkun wɔ hɔn mu, ma mannhyew Israelmba no wɔ me nyinkuntwe mu.
12. Dɛm ntsi se dɛ, Hwɛ, medze m'asomdwee ahyɛmu mema no:
13. ɔnyɛ ɔno, nye n'asefo a wobedzi n'ekyir no daa asɔfodzi ahyɛmu; osiandɛ ɔatwe ne Nyankopɔn ho nyinkun, na ɔapata ama Israelmba no.
14. Na Israelnyi no a wokum no no, nyia wokum no kaa Midiannyi ɔbaa no ho no, ne dzin dze Zimri, Salu ne babanyin a, ɔyɛ Simeonfo egyanom ofi mu ɔbapɔmba.
15. Na Midiannyi ɔbaa no a wokum no no, ne dzin dze Kozbi, Zur a ɔyɛ otsitsir wɔ Midian egyanom ofi bi mu nkorɔfo do no, ne babaa a.
16. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.25.17, Hom nhiahia Midianfo no ho, na hom nku hɔn:
18. osiandɛ hɔn so wɔdze hɔn ndaadaa a wɔdze tseetsee hom wɔ Peor ho asɛm no mu, nye hɔn akyerɛbaa Kozbi, Midian ɔsafohen no ne babaa no a wokum no wɔ ɔyardɔm da no mu wɔ Peor ho asɛm no mu no, ehiahia hom ho.

## Tsir 26

1. Na ɔbaa dɛ ɔhaw no ekyir no, EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses nye ɔsɔfo Aaron ne babanyin Eleazar dɛ,
NUM.26.2, Hom nkan Israelmba mboanofo no nyinara, ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu do dze rokɔ no, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, hɔn a wotum ɔsa kɔ wɔ Israel nyinara.
3. Na Moses nye ɔsɔfo Eleazar nye hɔn kasae wɔ Moab tɛtɛr mu wɔ Jeriko Jordan nkyɛn dɛ,
NUM.26.4, Hom nkan ɔman no, ofi hɔn a woedzi mfe eduonu do dze rokɔ no; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses nye Israelmba a wofi Egypt asaase do bae no.
5. Reuben, Israel n'abakan no: Reuben ne mbabanyin; Hanok dze, Hanokfo ebusua: Pallu dze, Pallufo ebusua:
6. Hezron dze, Hezronfo ebusua: Karmi dze, Karmifo ebusua.
7. Iyinom nye Reubenfo ebusua ebusua: na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu wɔyɛ mpemeduanan ebiasa nye ahaesuon eduasa.
8. Na Pallu ne ba; Eliab.
9. Na Eliab ne mbabanyin; Nemuel, nye Datan, nye Abiram. Iyinom nye Datan, nye Abiram no a wɔfrɛɛ hɔn wɔ mboanofo no mu no a wɔhaam Moses nye Aaron ho wɔ Kora no kuw no mu, wɔ aber a wɔhaam EWURADZE ho:
10. na asaase buee n'ano, na ɔmemeen hɔn dze kaa Kora ho, aber a kuw no wuwui no; wɔ aber a ogya memeen nyimpa ahaenu eduonum, dze yɛɛ asɛnkyerɛdze no.
11. Naaso Kora ne mbabanyin no dze, woennwuwu.
12. Simeon ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua tsetsee: Nemuel dze, Nemuelfo ebusua: Jamin dze, Jaminfo ebusua: Jakin dze, Jakinfo ebusua:
13. Zera dze, Zerafo ebusua: Saul dze, Saulfo ebusua.
14. Iyinom nye Simeonfo ebusua, mpemeduonu ebien nye ahaenu.
15. Gad ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua tsetsee: Zefon dze, Zefonfo ebusua: Hagi dze, Hagifo ebusua: Suni dze, Sunifo ebusua:
16. Ozni dze, Oznifo ebusua: Eri dze, Erifo ebusua:
17. Arod dze, Arodfo ebusua: Areli dze, Arelifo ebusua.
18. Iyinom nye Gad ne mbabanyin ebusua ebusua dɛ mbrɛ hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu tse, mpemeduanan nye ahaenum.
19. Juda ne mbabanyin, Er nye Onan: na Er nye Onan wowui wɔ Keenan asaase do.
20. Na Juda ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua tsetsee nye yi; Sela dze, Selafo ebusua: Perez dze, Perezfo ebusua: Zera dze, Zerafo ebusua.
21. Na Perez ne mbabanyin nye yi; Hezron dze, Hezronfo ebusua: Hamul dze, Hamulfo ebusua.
22. Iyinom nye Juda ebusua ebusua dɛ mbrɛ hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu tse, mpemeduosuon esia nye ahaenum.
23. Isakar ne mbabanyin: Tola dze, Tolafo ebusua: Puva dze, Punifo ebusua:
24. Jasub dze, Jasubfo ebusua: Simron dze, Simronfo ebusua.
25. Iyinom nye Isakar ebusua ebusua dɛ mbrɛ hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu tse, mpemeduosia anan nye ahaasa.
26. Zebulun ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: Sered dze, Seredfo ebusua: Elon dze, Elonfo ebusua: Jahleel dze, Jahleelfo ebusua.
27. Iyinom nye Zebulunfo ebusua ebusua dɛ mbrɛ hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu tse, mpemeduosia nye ahaenum.
28. Josef ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: Manaasɛ nye Efraim.
29. Manaasɛ ne mbabanyin: Makir dze, Makirfo ebusua: na Makir woo Gilead: Gilead dze, Gileadfo ebusua.
30. Iyinom nye Gilead ne mbabanyin: Iezer dze, Iezerfo ebusua: Helek dze, Helekfo ebusua:
31. na Asriel dze, Asrielfo ebusua: na Sekem dze, Sekemfo ebusua:
32. na Semida dze, Semidafo ebusua: na Hefer dze, Heferfo ebusua.
33. Na Hefer ne babanyin Zelofehad ennya mbabanyin, na onyaa mbabaa: na Zelofehad ne mbabaa edzin nye Mahala, nye Noa, Hogla, Milka, nye Tirza.
34. Iyinom nye Manaasɛ ebusua: na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu wɔyɛ mpemeduonum ebien nye ahaesuon.
35. Iyinom nye Efraim ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: Sutela dze, Sutelafo ebusua: Beker dze, Bekerfo ebusua: Tahan dze, Tahanfo ebusua.
36. Na iyinom nye Sutela ne mbabanyin: Eran dze, Eranfo ebusua.
37. Iyinom nye Efraim ne mbabanyin ebusua ebusua dɛ mbrɛ hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu tse, mpemeduasa ebien nye ahaenum. Iyinom nye Josef ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua tsetsee.
38. Benjamin ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: Bela dze, Belafo ebusua: Asbel dze, Asbelfo ebusua: Ahiram dze, Ahiramfo ebusua:
39. Sefufam dze, Sufafo ebusua: Hufam dze, Hufamfo ebusua.
40. Na Bela ne mbabanyin nye Ard nye Naaman: Ard dze, Ardfo ebusua: Naaman dze, Naamanfo ebusua.
41. Iyinom nye Benjamin ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu wɔyɛ mpemeduanan enum nye ahaesia.
42. Iyinom nye Dan ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: Suham dze, Suhamfo ebusua. Iyinom nye Dan ebusua dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee.
43. Suhamfo ebusua nyinara, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu tse, wɔyɛ mpemeduosia anan nye ahaanan.
44. Aser ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: Imna dze, Imnafo ebusua: Isvi dze, Isvifo ebusua: Beria dze, Beriafo ebusua.
45. Beria ne mbabanyin dze: Heber dze, Heberfo ebusua: Malkiel dze, Malkielfo ebusua.
46. Na Aser ne babaa ne dzin dze Sera.
47. Iyinom nye Aser ne mbabanyin ebusua ebusua dɛ mbrɛ hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu tse, mpemeduonum ebiasa nye ahaanan.
48. Naftali ne mbabanyin dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: Jahzeel dze, Jahzeelfo ebusua: Guni dze, Gunifo ebusua:
49. Jezer dze, Jezerfo ebusua: Sillem dze, Sillemfo ebusua.
50. Iyinom nye Naftali ebusua dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu wɔyɛ mpemeduanan enum nye ahaanan.
51. Iyinom nye hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Israelmba mu, mpemahaesia nye kor ahaesuon eduasa.
52. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.26.53, Iyinom na wɔnkyɛ asaase no mma hɔn dɛ egyapadze dɛ mbrɛ edzin no no dodow tse.
54. Nyia ɔso no ma no egyapadze a ɔso, na nyia osuar no ma no egyapadze a osuar: obiara dɛ mbrɛ hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn no mu tse no na wɔmfa n'egyapadze mma no.
55. Naaso asaase no wɔmfa tonto nkyɛ: dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom ebusuakuw ebusuakuw edzin tsetsee no na wonnya egyapadze no.
56. Dɛ mbrɛ tonto no tse no na wɔmfa nkyɛ hɔn egyapadze no mma hɔn a wɔso nye hɔn a wosuar.
57. Na iyinom nye hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ Levifo mu dɛ mbrɛ hɔn ebusua ebusua tsetsee: Gerson dze, Gersonfo ebusua: Kohat dze, Kohatfo ebusua: Merari dze, Merarifo ebusua.
58. Iyinom nye Levi ebusua ebusua: Libnifo ebusua, Hebronfo ebusua, Mahilifo ebusua, Korafo ebusua. Na Kohat woo Amram.
59. Na Amram ne yer dzin dze Jokebed, Levi ne babaa a wɔwoo no ma Levi wɔ Egypt no a: na ɔwoo Aaron nye Moses nye hɔn akyerɛbaa Miriam maa Amram.
60. Na Aaron so wɔwoo Nadab nye Abihu, Eleazar nye Itamar maa no.
61. Na Nadab nye Abihu wuwui, wɔ aber a wɔdze ananagya baa EWURADZE enyim no.
62. Na hɔn a wɔkaan hɔn wɔ mu wɔyɛ mpemeduonu ebiasa, banyin biara a oedzi bosoom nye ekyir no: na wɔannkan hɔn ammfora Israelmba, osiandɛ wɔamma hɔn egyapadze wɔ Israelmba no mu.

## Tsir 28

## Tsir 30

1. Na Moses kasaa kyerɛɛ Israelmba ebusuakuw ebusuakuw mu etsitsir no dɛ, Iyi nye adze a EWURADZE ahyɛ.
2. Sɛ nyimpa bi bɔ EWURADZE anohoba, anaa ɔka ntam dze nkyehoma kyekyer ne kra a, mma ommbu n'asɛm do; ɔnyɛ dɛ mbrɛ dza ofi n'anomu nyinara tse.
3. Eso sɛ ɔbaa bi bɔ anohoba ma EWURADZE, na ɔdze nkyehoma kyekyer noho wɔ n'egya fie, ne nkataasiaber mu;
4. na n'egya tse n'anohoba, nye ne nkyehoma a ɔdze akyekyer ne kra, na n'egya ennsiw no kwam a: ɔno n'anohoba nyinara ntsim hɔ, na nkyehoma biara a ɔdze akyekyer ne kra no ntsim hɔ.
5. Sɛ da a n'egya bɛtse no osiw no kwan dze a; ɔno n'anohoba, anaa ne nkyehoma a ɔdze akyekyer ne kra no mu biara rinntsim hɔ: na EWURADZE dze bɛkyɛ no, osiandɛ n'egya siiw no kwan.
6. Na sɛ ɔwɔ kun, wɔ aber a n'anohoba, anaa n'anosɛm hurutuhurutu a ɔdze akyekyer ne kra no, wɔ no do no;
7. na no kun tse, na da a ɔtsee no oennsiw no kwan a: ɔno n'anohoba no ntsim hɔ na ne nkyehoma a ɔdze akyekyer ne kra no ntsim hɔ.
8. Sɛ no kun siw no kwan da a ɔtsee no dze a; ɔno ɔmma n'anohoba a ɔwɔ no do, nye n'anosɛm hurutuhurutu a ɔdze akyekyer ne kra no nyɛ kwa: na EWURADZE dze bɛkyɛr no.
9. Na okunafo, anaa nyia woegyaa no awar no n'anohoba dze, mekyerɛ adze biara a ɔdze kyekyer ne kra no, otsim no do.
10. Na sɛ ɔwɔ no kun fie na ɔbɔɔ anohoba, anaa ɔdze ntam dze nkyehoma kyekyeer ne kra,
NUM.30.11, na no kun tsee, na ɔyɛɛ no komm, na oennsiw no kwan a; ɔno n'anohoba nyinara tsim hɔ, na nkyehoma biara a ɔdze akyekyer ne kra no tsim hɔ.
12. Sɛ no kun no ma ɔyɛ kwa wɔ da a ɔtsee no dze a; ɔno biribiara a ofii n'anomu do wɔ n'anohoba ho, anaa ne kra nkyehoma ho no, rinntsim hɔ: no kun no ama ayɛ kwa; na EWURADZE dze bɛkyɛ no.
13. Anohoba biara, nye ntam biara a wɔdze brɛ ɔkra adze no, no kun na ɔma otsintsim, anaa no kun na ɔma ɔyɛ kwa.
14. Na sɛ no kun yɛ no komm koraa fi da to da a; ɔno otsintsim n'anohoba nyinara, anaa ne nkyehoma nyinara a ɔwɔ no do no: oetsintsim no, osiandɛ ɔyɛɛ no komm wɔ da a ɔtsee no.
15. Sɛ ɔatse no ɔkyɛr ana ekyir no ɔama ayɛ kwa dze a; ɔno kun no nsoa yer no n'emumuyɛ.
16. Iyinom nye ahyɛdze a EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses dɛ ɔnda banyin nye ne yer ntamu, ɔnda egya nye ne babaa a ɔwɔ ne nkataasiaber mu, wɔ n'egya fie ntamu.

## Tsir 31

7. Na wɔnye Midian kɔkoe dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no; na wokunkum mbanyin no nyinara.
8. Na wokunkum Midian ahemfo no kaa hɔn atɔfo nkaa no ho. Evi, Rekem, Zur, Hur, nye Reba Midian ahemfo beenum no; na wɔdze nkrantsee kum Beor ne babanyin Balaam so.
9. Na Israelmba no faa Midian mbaa no nye hɔn mbofraba no ndɔmmum; na wɔfoom hɔn ofiembowa nyinara, nye hɔn nguankuw nyinara, nye hɔn ahodze nyinara.
10. Na wɔdze ogya hyehyeew hɔn nkurow nyinara wɔ mbeambea a wɔtsetsee no, nye hɔn nseraban nyinara.
11. Na wɔfaa asadze no nyinara, nye afonam no nyinara, nyimpa nye abowa nyina.
12. Na wɔdze ndɔmmum no, nye afonam no, nye asadze no, brɛɛ Moses, nye ɔsɔfo Eleazar, nye Israel mboanofo no; wɔ nseraban no mu wɔ Moab tɛtɛr a ɔda Jeriko Jordan ho no do.
13. Na Moses, nye ɔsɔfo Eleazar, nye mboanofo no mu mbapɔmba no nyinara fiir edzi kehyiaa hɔn wɔ nseraban no ekyir.
14. Na Moses no bo fuuw dɔm no hɔn asafohemfo no, apem apem do asafohemfo nye ɔha ɔha do asafohemfo a wofi asayɛ bae no.
15. Na Moses see hɔn dɛ, Ana hom agye mbaa no nyinara nkwa?
16. Hwɛ, iyinom, na ɔnam Balaam apam do, wɔmaa Israel yɛɛ bɔn tsiaa EWURADZE wɔ Peor asɛm no mu, na ɔmaa ɔyardɔm baa EWURADZE no mboanofo mu no.
17. Afei dɛm ntsi hom nkuku mbofraba no mu mbanyin nyinara, na wonku ɔbaa biara a oehu ɔbanyin dɛ ɔnye no ada.
18. Na mbaa mbofraba a wonnhun ɔbanyin dɛ wɔnye no ada no dze, hom ngye hɔn nkwa mma homho.
19. Na hom nkyer nsera wɔ nseraban no ekyir nda esuon: obiara a oeku nyimpa bi no, nye obiara a ɔaka ɔtɔfo bi no, hom ntsew homho da a otsia ebiasa no, nye da a otsia esuon no, hom nye hom ndɔmmumfo no.
20. Na atar biara dze, nye adze a wɔdze nwoma ayɛ nyinara, nye mpɔnkyenhwi edwuma nyinara, nye adze a wɔdze dua ayɛ nyinara, homara hom ntsew ho.
21. Na ɔsɔfo Eleazar see mbanyin akofo a wɔkɔr akono no dɛ, Iyi nye mbra a EWURADZE dze ahyɛ Moses no mu ahyɛdze:
22. naaso sika nye dwetɛ, boran, dadze, seenyaa, nye sumbui no,
NUM.31.23, adze biara a obotum ogya no, hom mma ɔnkɛfa ogya mu, na no ho ntsew; naaso hom mfa ahoguar nsu no ntsew ho: na adze a onnkotum ogya no nyinara, hom mma ɔnkɛfa nsu mu.
24. Na hom nhor hom ntar da a otsia esuon no, na hom ho ntsew, na n'ekyir no na hom mbra nseraban no mu.
25. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ mbu,
NUM.31.26, Ɔwo nye ɔsɔfo Eleazar nye mboanofo no egyanom efi mu etsitsir no, afonam a wɔfae no ano, nyimpa nye mbowa nyina,
NUM.31.27, na kyɛ afonam no mu akyɛkyɛ ebien; ma mbanyin akofo a wɔkɔr akono no, nye mboanofo no nyinara:
28. na gye mbanyin asayɛfo a wɔkɔr akono no hɔ tow ma EWURADZE: nyimpa, nye anantwi, nye nsoasopɔnkɔ, nye nguankuw mu ahaenum mu nkorkor:
29. gye no wɔ hɔn ɔfa no mu, na fa ma ɔsɔfo Eleazar, dɛ EWURADZE ɔmado afɔr.
30. Na Israelmba ɔfa mu dze, gye nyimpa, anantwi, nsaso, nye nguankuw no, mekyerɛ ofiembowa nyinara mu eduonum eduonum mu nkorkor, na fa ma Levifo no a wɔhwɛ EWURADZE ntamadan ahwɛdodze do no.
31. Na Moses nye ɔsɔfo Eleazar yɛɛ dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
32. Afei afonam a ɔbor asadze a mbanyin asayɛfo no fae no do no, ɔyɛ nguan mpemahaesia eduosuon enum,
NUM.31.33, nye anantwi mpemeduosuon ebien,
NUM.31.34, nye nsoasopɔnkɔ mpemeduosia biako,
NUM.31.35, nye nyimpa, mbaa a wonnhun ɔbanyin dɛ wɔnye no ada, nyinara wɔyɛ mpemeduasa ebien.
36. Na ɔfa no a ɔyɛ hɔn a wotui kɔr ɔsa no hɔn kyɛfa no, no dodow yɛ nguan mpemahaebiasa eduasa esuon nye ahaenum:
37. na nguan no mu ɔtow a woyiyii maa EWURADZE no yɛ ahaesia eduosuon enum.
38. Na anantwi no yɛ mpemeduasa esia; hɔn mu ɔtow a woyiyii maa EWURADZE yɛ eduosuon ebien.
39. Na nsoasopɔnkɔ no yɛ mpemeduasa nye ahaenum; hɔn mu ɔtow a woyiyii maa EWURADZE yɛ eduosia biako.
40. Na nyimpa no yɛ mpemduesia; hɔn mu ɔtow a woyiyii maa EWURADZE yɛ nyimpa eduasa ebien.
41. Na Moses dze ɔtow a ɔyɛ EWURADZE ɔmado afɔr no maa ɔsɔfo Eleazar, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
42. Na Israelmba hɔn ɔfa no a Moses gyee no wɔ akofo no hɔ no mu dze,
NUM.31.43, (afei mboanofo no hɔn ɔfa no yɛ nguan mpemahaebiasa eduasa, esuon nye ahaenum,
NUM.31.44, nye anantwi mpemeduasa esia,
NUM.31.45, nye nsoasopɔnkɔ mpemeduasa nye ahaenum,
NUM.31.46, nye nyimpa mpemduesia;)
47. nye Israelmba hɔn ɔfa no mu dze, Moses faa eduonum eduonum mu nkorkor, nyimpa nye mbowa nyina, na ɔdze maa Levifo no a wɔhwɛ EWURADZE ntamadan ahwɛdodze do no; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
48. Na asafohemfo a wɔhwɛ dɔm mpempem do no, nye apem apem do asafohemfo, nye ɔha ɔha do asafohemfo no, baa Moses nkyɛn:
49. na wɔbɛsee no dɛ, Wo nkowaa yeebu akofo a wɔwɔ hɛn nsamu no ano, na hɛn mu nyimpa kor mpo annyew.
50. Na yɛdze EWURADZE n'ayɛyɛdze aba, dza nyimpa biara enya no a, sika ndwindze, ananadze ntweaban, nye nsakɔnadze, nsɔwanonkaba, nsowamuadze, nye nkɔnmuadze, dɛ wɔmfa mpata mma hɛn akra wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
51. Na Moses nye ɔsɔfo Eleazar gyee hɔn hɔ sika no, mekyerɛ edwindze no nyinara.
52. Na ɔmado afɔr sika nyinara a wɔdze brɛ EWURADZE a ofi apem apem do asafohemfo, nye ɔha ɔha do asafohemfo hɔ no, ɔyɛ dwetɛbona mpemduesia ahaesuon eduonum.
53. Na akofo no hɔn afom asadze dze, hɔnankasa na wɔfefae.
54. Na Moses nye ɔsɔfo Eleazar gyee sika no wɔ apem apem nye ɔha ɔha do asafohemfo no hɔ, na wɔdze baa ehyiadzibuw no mu, dɛ ɔnyɛ Israelmba no nkaadze wɔ EWURADZE enyim.

## Tsir 32

1. Afei Reubenmba nye Gadmba no wɔ ofiembowa dodow a wɔdɔɔ so kɛsenara: na aber a wohun Jazer, nye Gilead asaase no, na hwɛ, bea no fata ofiembowa bea no;
2. Wɔbae bɛkaa kyerɛɛ Moses, nye ɔsɔfo Eleazar, nye mboanofo no mu mbapɔmba no dɛ,
NUM.32.3, Atarot, nye Dibon, nye Jazer, nye Nimra, nye Hesbon, nye Eleale, nye Sebam, nye Nebo, nye Beon,
NUM.32.4, asaase a EWURADZE bɔɔ no wɔ Israel mboanofo no enyim no, yɛ asaase a oye ma mbowanyɛn, na wo nkowaa so wɔ ofiembowa.
5. Na wɔsee dɛ, Sɛ yeenya w'enyim enyimnyam a, ma wɔmfa asaase yi mma wo nkowaa ma yɛmfa; mma mmfa hɛn nntwa Jordan.
6. Na Moses see Gadmba nye Reubenmba no dɛ, Aso hom nuanom nkɔ ɔsa, na hom ntsena ha?
7. Na ɔyɛ dɛn ntsi na hom tu Israelmba akoma dɛ mma wonntwa nnkɔ asaase a EWURADZE dze ama hɔn no do yi?
8. Dɛm na hom egyanom yɛe, wɔ aber a mosomaa hɔn fii Kades-barnea dɛ wɔnkɔhwɛ asaase no.
9. Na aber a wɔfowee kɔr Eskol mbɔnsa mu, na wohun asaase no, wobotuu Israelmba no hɔn akoma dɛ mma wɔnnkɔ asaase a EWURADZE dze ama hɔn no do.
10. Na EWURADZE ebufuw dzɛɛw da no, na ɔkaa ntam dɛ,
NUM.32.11, Ampaara mbanyin a wofi Egypt bae no a woedzi mfe eduonu nye ekyir no mu biara runnhu asaase a mekaa ho ntam mekyerɛɛ Abraham, nye Isaak, nye Jakob no; osiandɛ woennwie m'ekyir dzi:
12. gyedɛ Keniznyi Jefune ne babanyin Kaleb, nye Nun ne babanyin Josua: osiandɛ hɔn na woedzi EWURADZE ekyir nokwar mu.
13. Na EWURADZE ebufuw dzɛɛw Israel do, na ɔmaa wokekyiin sar no do mfe eduanan, ma okodur dɛ ebusuasantsen no a wɔyɛɛ bɔn wɔ EWURADZE enyim no nyinara hyewee.
14. Na, hwɛ, hom asoɛr egyina hom egyanom ananmu, ndzebɔnyɛfo asefo, dɛ hom da ho araa dze EWURADZE n'ebufuhyew no reba Israel do.
15. Na sɛ hom dan fi n'ekyir a, ɔbɛda ho ara adan hɔn egya wɔ sar no do; na hom asɛɛ ɔman yi nyinara.
16. Na wɔbaa ne nkyɛn bɛsee dɛ, Nguamban na yɛbɛgyegye ama hɛn ofiembowa wɔ ha, na nkurow so yɛbɛkyekyer ama hɛn mbofraba:
17. na hɛnankasa dze hɛn ho bɛyɛ har yeemia edzi Israelmba enyim, ekodu dɛ yɛdze hɔn bɔkɔ ekodu hɔn tsenabew ana: na hɛn mbofraba no ntsenatsena nkurow a wɔagye ho ban mu osian asaase no do atsefo no ntsi.
18. Yɛrennsan mmba hɛn efiefi, gyedɛ Israelmba ɔbanyin biara enya egyapadze edzi ana.
19. Na yɛnnye hɔn remmfa enyadze wɔ Jordan egya, ɔdze kɔ no; osiandɛ hɛn egyapadze ebefir hɛn Jordan nkyɛn ha boka afamu.
20. Na Moses see hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom bɛyɛ iyi; sɛ hom bemia homho wɔ EWURADZE enyim akɔ ɔsa,
NUM.32.21, na hom mu nyimpa biara a oemia botwa Jordan edzi EWURADZE enyim, ekodu dɛ ɔbɛpam n'atamfo efi n'enyim
22. na wɔabrɛ asaase no adze wɔ EWURADZE enyim a: nna n'ekyir no hom bɛsan na hom edzi bem wɔ EWURADZE nye Israelmba ho; na asaase yi nyɛ hom enyadze wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
23. Na sɛ hom annyɛ dɛm a, hwɛ hom ayɛ bɔn etsia EWURADZE: na hom nhu dɛ hom bɔn beyi hom ama.
24. Hom nkyekyer nkurow mma hom mbofraba, na nguamban mma hom nguan; na hom nyɛ dza efi hom anomu epue no.
25. Na Gadmba nye Reubenmba no kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, Wo nkowaa bɛyɛ dɛ mbrɛ mo wura ahyɛ no.
26. Hɛn mbofraba hɛn yernom hɛn nguankuw, nye hɛn ofiembowa nyinara, bɛtsena Gilead nkurow no mu hɔ:
27. na wo nkowaa dze, ɔbanyin biara a oemia rokɔ ɔsa no, botwa mu wɔ EWURADZE enyim akɔko dɛ mbrɛ mo wura aka no.
28. Nna Moses dze hɔn ho mbra maa ɔsɔfo Eleazar, nye Nun ne babanyin Josua, nye Israelmba ebusuakuw ebusuakuw mu egyanom efi do etsitsir no.
29. Na Moses see hɔn dɛ, Sɛ Gadmba nye Reubenmba nye hom twa Jordan, ɔbanyin biara a oemia dzi EWURADZE enyim rokɔko no, na wɔbrɛ asaase no adze wɔ hom enyim a; ɔno hom mfa Gilead asaase mma hɔn ma wɔmfa:
30. na sɛ woemmia nye hom enntwa a, ɔnodze wɔmma wonnya enyadze wɔ hom mu wɔ Keenan asaase do.
31. Na Gadmba nye Reubenmba no buaa dɛ, Dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE aka akyerɛ wo nkowaa no, dɛmara na yɛbɛyɛ.
32. Yebemia wɔ EWURADZE enyim etwa mu akɔ Keenan asaase do, na hɛn egyapadze a yeenya no wɔ Jordan egya ha no ako hɛn hɔ.
33. Na Moses dze Amorifo hen Sihon n'ahendzi, nye Basanhen Ɔge n'ahendzi, asaase no, dɛ mbrɛ no do nkurow nye no ntoaado tsetsee no, mekyerɛ asaase no do nkurow a ehyia do no, maa hɔn, mekyerɛ Gadmba, nye Reubenmba, nye Josef ne babanyin Manaasɛ n'ebusuakuw ɔfa no a.
34. Na Gadmba no kyekyeer Dibon, nye Atarot, nye Aroer;
35. nye Atroth-sofan, nye Jazer, nye Jogbeha;
36. nye Bɛt-nimra, nye Bɛt-haran: nkurow a wɔagyegye ho ban, nye nguamban.
37. Na Reubenmba no kyekyeer Hesbon, nye Eleale, nye Kiriataim;
38. nye Nebo, nye Baal-meon, (hɔn edzin no sakyesakyeree), nye Sibma: na wɔdze edzin fofor totoo nkurow a wɔkyekyeree no.
39. Na Manaasɛ ne babanyin Makir mba no sii mu Gilead, na wɔkɛfae, na wotuu Amorifo a wɔwɔ mu hɔ no.
40. Na Moses dze Gilead maa Manaasɛ ne babanyin Makir; na ɔtsenaa mu.
41. Na Manaasɛ ne babanyin Jair sii mu kɛfaa no nkurow no na ɔfrɛɛ no Havot-jair.
42. Na Noba sii mu kɛfaa Kenat, nye no nkuroba, na ɔfrɛɛ no Noba, dɛ mbrɛ nankasa ne dzin no tse.

## Tsir 33

1. Iyinom nye Israelmba akwantu a wɔnam Moses nye Aaron nsa do fi Egypt asaase do tutui dɛ mbrɛ hɔn dɔm tsetsee.
2. Na Moses nam EWURADZE mbrasɛm do kyerɛɛw hɔn edzifir dɛ mbrɛ hɔn akwantu mu asoɛe no tse: na iyinom nye hɔn akwantu mu asoɛe dɛ mbrɛ hɔn edzifir tse.
3. Na bosoom a odzi kan no mu wotui fii Rameses, wɔ bosoom a odzi kan no mu da a otsia duenum no; apaho no ekyir adzekyee na Israelmba no dze ahosɛpɛw kɛse fiir edzi wɔ Egyptfo nyinara enyim,
NUM.33.4, wɔ aber a Egyptfo risie sie hɔn mbakan nyinara a EWURADZE kunkum hɔn wɔ hɔn mu no: na hɔn enyame no so EWURADZE buu hɔn ntsɛn.
5. Na Israelmba no tui fii Rameses na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Sukɔt.
6. Na wotui fii Sukɔt, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Etam a ɔwɔ sar no ano no.
7. Na wotui fii Etam, na wɔdanee baa Pi-hahirot a ɔwɔ Baal-zefon enyim no bio: na wɔkyeer nsera wɔ Migidɔl enyim.
8. Na wotui fii Hahirot enyim, na wokotwaa mu faa po no ne finimfin kɔr sar no do: na wɔnantseew ndaansa kwan wɔ Etam sar no do, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Mara.
9. Na wotui fii Mara, na wɔbaa Elim: na Elim hɔ na nsunyiwa duebien, nye nkyiresua ndua eduosuon wɔ; na wɔkyeer hɔ nsera.
10. Na wotui fii Elim, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Mbɛw Po a binom frɛ no Po kɔkɔɔ no ho.
11. Na wotui fii Mbɛw Po no ho, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Sin sar do.
12. Na wotui fii Sin sar do, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Dofka.
13. Na wotui fii Dofka, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Alus.
14. Na wotui fii Alus, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Refidim, mbrɛ ɔman no ennya nsu annom no a.
15. Na wotui fii Refidim, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Sinai sar do.
16. Na wotui fii Sinai sar do, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Kibrot-hataava.
17. Na wotui fii Kibrot-hataava, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Hazerot.
18. Na wotui fii Hazerot, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Ritma.
19. Na wotui fii Ritma, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Rimmon-perez.
20. Na wotui fii Rimmon-perez, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Libna.
21. Na wotui fii Libna, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Rissa.
22. Na wotui fii Rissa, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Kehelata.
23. Na wotui fii Kehelata, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ bepɔw Sefer do.
24. Na wotui fii bepɔw Sefer do, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Harada.
25. Na wotui fii Harada, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Makhelot.
26. Na wotui fii Makhelot na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Tahat.
27. Na wotui fii Tahat, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Tera.
28. Na wotui fii Tera, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Mitka.
29. Na wotui fii Mitka na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Hasmona.
30. Na wotui fii Hasmona na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Maserot.
31. Na wotui fii Maserot, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Bene-jaakan.
32. Na wotui fii Bene-jaakan, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Hor-hagidgad.
33. Na wotui fii Hor-hagidgad, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Jotbata.
34. Na wotui fii Jotbata, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Abrona.
35. Na wotui fii Abrona, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Ezion-geber.
36. Na wotui fii Ezion-geber, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Zin sar do (ɔnoara nye Kades).
37. Na wotui fii Kades, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ bepɔw Hor do, wɔ Edom asaase ano.
38. Na ɔsɔfo Aaron nam EWURADZE mbrasɛm do fowee kɔr bepɔw Hor do na okowui wɔ hɔ, wɔ afe a Israelmba fi Egypt asaase do firii no ekyir dza otsia eduanan no mu, bosoom a otsia enum no mu da a odzi bosoom no ne kan no.
39. Na aber a Aaron wui wɔ bepɔw Hor do no na oedzi mfe ɔha eduonu ebiasa.
40. Na Keenannyi, Aradhen no a ɔtse Keenan asaase do wɔ Pomu afamu no tsee dɛ Israelmba no reba.
41. Na wotui fii bepɔw Hor ho, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Zalmona.
42. Na wotui fii Zalmona, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Punon.
43. Na wotui fii Punon, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Obot.
44. Na wotui fii Obot, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Iye-abarim, wɔ Moab ntoaado.
45. Na wotui fii Iyim, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Dibon-gad.
46. Na wotui fii Dibon-gad, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Almon-diblathaim.
47. Na wotui fii Almon-diblathaim, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Abarim mbepɔw do, wɔ Nebo enyim.
48. Na wotui fii Abarim mbepɔw do, na wɔkɛkyeer nsera wɔ Moab tɛtɛr mu, wɔ Jeriko Jordan ho.
49. Na wɔkyeer Jordan ho nsera, fi Bɛt-jesimot araa kodur Abel-Sittim wɔ Moab tɛtɛr mu.
50. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses wɔ Moab tɛtɛr mu wɔ Jeriko Jordan ho, dɛ,
NUM.33.51, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom twa Jordan kɔ Keenan asaase do a,
NUM.33.52, ɔno hom mpam asaase no do atsefo no nyinara mfi hom enyim, na hom nsɛɛ hɔn abo ehonyi nyinara, na hom nsɛɛ hɔn ehonyi a woegu no nyinara, na hom ndwurudwuruw mbea sorɔnsorɔm nyinara:

54. Na hom ntwe tonto mfa nkyɛ asaase no dɛ mbrɛ hom ebusua ebusua tsetsee; hɔn a wɔso no hom mma hɔn egyapadze a ɔso, na hɔn a wosuar no ma hɔn egyapadze a osuar: beebiara mbrɛ tonto no abɔ nyimpa biara no hɔ na ɔnyɛ nedze; dɛ mbrɛ hom egyanom mbusuakuw tsetsee no na hom nyinya hom egyapadze.
55. Na sɛ hom ammpam asaase no do atsefo no wɔ hom enyim a; ɔno hɔn mu hɔn a hom ama wɔaka no bɛyɛ dɛ awowɔdze wɔ hom enyiwa mu, na ayɛ dɛ nsoɛ, wɔ hom mfe mu, na woehiahia hom ho wɔ asaase a hom tse do no do.
56. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, dɛ mbrɛ modween dɛ mebɛyɛ hɔn no, dɛmara na mebɛyɛ hom.

## Tsir 34

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.34.2, Hyɛ Israelmba no mbra, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom du Keenan asaase no do a, (iyi nye asaase a ɔda hɔ ma hom dɛ egyapadze, mekyerɛ Keenan asaase no dɛ mbrɛ n'ahe tsetsee, )
3. ɔno hom pomu afamu nkyɛn mfi Zin sar do wɔ Edom nkyɛn mu, na hom pomu ɔhe mfi Nkyen Epo ano wɔ boka afamu:
4. na hom ɔhe no ndan mfa Akrabbim mfowee pomu afamu, na ontwa mu nkɔ Zin: na n'edzifir ndu Kades-barnea pomu afamu; na ɔnkɔ Hazar-adar, na ontwa mu nkɛfa Azmon:
5. na ɔhe no mpon mfi Azmon nkɔ Egypt ɔwora ho, na n'edzifir ndu po no ano.
6. Na anee afamu ɔhe no dze, hom wɔ po kɛse no dze yɛ ne he: iyi na ɔnyɛ hom anee ɔhe.
7. Na iyi nyɛ hom kwaamu ɔhe: hom mfi po kɛse no ho nsan bepɔw Hor mfa:
8. bepɔw Hor ho na hom mfi nsan mbrɛ wɔfa kɔ Hamat; na ɔhe no n'edzifir ndu Zedad:
9. na ɔhe no mfir edzi nkɔ Zifron, na n'edzifir ndu Hazar-enan: iyi na ɔnyɛ hom kwaamu ɔhe.
10. Na hom nsan boka he mfa mfi Hazar-enan nkɔ Sefam:
11. na ɔhe no mfi Sefam nsian nkɔ Ribla, wɔ Ain no boka afamu, na ɔhe no nsian araa nkodu Kinneret pomu afamu wɔ boka afamu:
12. na ɔhe no nsian nkodu Jordan do, na n'edzifir ndu Nkyen Po ho: iyi nye hom asaase dɛ mbrɛ no ho ahe tsetsee.
13. Na Moses hyɛɛ Israelmba no mbra dɛ, Iyi nye asaase a hom ntwe ho tonto mfa nkyɛ no a EWURADZE ahyɛ dɛ wɔmfa mma mbusuakuw akron, nye ebusuakuw ɔfa no;
14. nye Reubenmba ebusuakuw, dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee nye Gadmba ebusuakuw dɛ mbrɛ hɔn egyanom efi tsetsee, nye Manaasɛ ebusuakuw ɔfa no, woenya hɔn egyapadze:
15. na mbusuakuw ebien no nye ebusuakuw ɔfa no enya hɔn egyapadze wɔ Jeriko Jordan egya wɔ boka afamu ɔdze kɔ wipuei no.
16. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.34.17, Iyinom nye mbanyin a wɔnkyɛ asaase no mu mma hom no hɔn edzin: ɔsɔfo Eleazar, nye Nun ne babanyin Josua.
18. Na hom nyi ebusuakuw biara mu ɔbapɔmba kor ma wɔnkyɛ asaase no mu.
19. Na mbanyin no hɔn edzin nye yi: Juda n'ebusuakuw mu, Jefune ne babanyin Kaleb.
20. Simeon ne mba ebusuakuw mu, Ammihud ne babanyin Semuel.
21. Benjamin n'ebusuakuw mu, Kislon ne babanyin Elidad.
22. Nye Dan ne mba ebusuakuw mu ɔbapɔmba, Jogli ne babanyin Bukki.
23. Josef ne mba mu: Manaasɛ ne mba ebusuakuw mu ɔbapɔmba, Efod ne babanyin Hanniel:
24. nye Efraim ne mba ebusuakuw mu ɔbapɔmba, Siftan ne babanyin Kemuel.
25. Na Zebulun ne mba ebusuakuw mu ɔbapɔmba, Parnak ne babanyin Elizafan.
26. Na Isakar ne mba ebusuakuw mu ɔbapɔmba, Azzan ne babanyin Paltiel.
27. Na Aser ne mba ebusuakuw mu ɔbapɔmba, Selomi ne babanyin Ahihud.
28. Na Naftali ne mba ebusuakuw mu ɔbapɔmba, Ammihud ne babanyin Pedahel.
29. Iyinom nye hɔn a EWURADZE hyɛɛ hɔn dɛ wɔnkyɛ Keenan asaase no mu mma Israelmba no.

## Tsir 35

1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses wɔ Moab tɛtɛr mu wɔ Jeriko Jordan do dɛ,
NUM.35.2, Hyɛ Israelmba no ma wɔmfa hɔn enyadze egyapadze no mu nkurow bi mma Levifo no ma wɔntsena mu; na nkurow no ho mfikyisaase no so hom mfa mma Levifo no.
3. Na nkurow no na wɔntsena mu: na no ho mfikyisaase no ndeda ho mma hɔn fiembowa, nye hɔn ahodze, nye hɔn mbowa nyinara.
4. Na nkurow no ho mfikyisaase a hom dze bɛma Levifo no, mfi kurow no fasu ekyir nkɔ basafa ahaenum ahaenum ntwa ho nhyia.
5. Na hom nsusu kurow no ekyir boka afamu basafa apem, nye pomu afamu basafa apem, nye anee afamu basafa apem, nye kwaamu afamu basafa apem, na kurow no nda ne finimfin. Iyi na ɔnyɛ nkurow no ho mfikyisaase mma hɔn.
6. Na nkurow no a hom dze bɛma Levifo no nyɛ guansumabew nkurow esia no a, hom dze bɛma dɛ onyimpakumfo nguan nkɔ hɔ no: na hom mfa nkurow eduanan ebien nka ho.
7. Nkurow a hom dze bɛma Levifo no nyinara nyɛ nkurow eduanan awɔtwe: hom mfa nka ne mfikyisaase no ho mma hɔn.
8. Na nkurow a hom bɛgye no wɔ Israelmba enyadze mu ama Levifo no ho dze, hɔn a hɔndze dɔɔ so no hɔ na hom ngye dza ɔdɔɔ so: na hɔn a hɔndze suar no hɔ na hom ngye dza osuar: obiara dɛ mbrɛ n'egyapadze a oenya no tse no na ɔmfa no nkurow no mu bi mma Levifo no.
9. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ,
NUM.35.10, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom twa Jordan kɔ Keenan asaase do a,
NUM.35.11, ɔno hom nyiyi nkurow ma ɔnyɛ guansumabew nkurow mma hom; na onyimpakumfo a oku nyimpa bi wɔ ber a ɔannhyɛ da no oeeguan akɔ hɔ.
12. Na nkurow no nyɛ hom guansumabew wɔ ɔwerdzinyi no nsamu; amma onyimpakumfo no eennwu, ma eekodu dɛ obegyina mboanofo no enyim ma woeebu no atsɛn ana.
13. Na nkurow a hom beyi ama no nyɛ hom guansumabew nkurow esia.
14. Hom nyi nkurow ebiasa mma wɔ Jordan egya, na nkurow ebiasa so na hom nyi mma wɔ Keenan asaase do; ɔnyɛ guansumabew nkurow.
15. Na Israelmba, nye ɔhɔho, nye ɔkwantunyi a ɔwɔ hɔn mu, nye nkurow esia yi nyɛ hɔn guansumabew: na obiara a ne nsa apa no oeeguan akɔ hɔ.
16. Na sɛ obi dze dadze bi bɔ obi, ma owu a, ɔyɛ onyimpakumfo; oku na wonku onyimpakumfo no.
17. Na sɛ obi kitsa ɔbo a obotum eku nyimpa na ɔdze bɔ no, ma owu a, ɔyɛ onyimpakumfo: oku na wonku onyimpakumfo no.
18. Anaa sɛ okitsa dua a obotum eku nyimpa dze bɔ no, ma owu a, ɔyɛ onyimpakumfo: oku na wonku onyimpakumfo no.
19. Bɔgya ho ɔwerdzinyi no noara na onku onyimpakumfo no: sɛ ɔto no a, onku no.
20. Na sɛ ɔtan do na osun no, anaa ɔboapa tow biribi dze bɔ no ma owu;
21. anaa enyitan do na ɔdze ne nsa dze bɔ no ma owu a: oku na wonku nyia ɔbɔ no no; ɔyɛ onyimpakumfo: bɔgya ho ɔwerdzinyi no nku onyimpakumfo no, sɛ ɔto no a.
22. Na sɛ osun no awerɛfir mu na nnyɛ enyitan do, anaa ɔtow adze biara ma ɔbɔ no na ɔannhyɛ da,
NUM.35.23, anaa ɔdze ɔbo biara a obotum eku nyimpa, na oennhu no, na ɔtow bɔ no, ma owu, na nnyɛ ne tamfo nkyii so ɔmmpɛ no bɔn bi a:
24. ɔno mboanofo no mbu nyia ɔbɔe nye no bɔgyaa ho ɔwerdzinyi no ntamu atsɛn dɛ mbrɛ ntsɛmbu yi tsetsee:
25. na mboanofo no nyi onyimpakumfo no mfi bɔgya ho ɔwerdzinyi no nsamu, na mboanofo no mfa no nsan nkɔ no guansumabew kurow no mu, mbrɛ oguanee kɔree no: na ɔnkɛtsena hɔ ma onkodu ɔsɔfopanyin a wɔdze ngo krɔnkrɔn asera no no wu.
26. Na sɛ onyimpakumfo no fir edzi aber biara tra no guansumabew kurow a oguanee kɔr hɔ no ne he do;
27. na bɔgya ho ɔwerdzinyi no hu no wɔ no guansumabew kurow no ne he ekyir, na bɔgya ho ɔwerdzinyi no koku onyimpakumfo no a; onndzi bɔgya bi ho so:
28. osiandɛ no guansumabew kurow no mu na ɔsɛ no dɛ nkyɛ ɔtsena kodu ɔsɔfopanyin no no wu do: na ɔsɔfopanyin no no wu ekyir no dze onyimpakumfo no nsan mbra n'enyadze asaase do bio.
29. Na iyi nyɛ atsɛmbu ahyɛdze mma hom wɔ hom ebusuasantsen nyina mu wɔ hom tsenabew nyinara mu.
30. Obiara a oku nyimpa biara no, adasefo enum na wɔmfa do nku onyimpakumfo no: na ɔdasefo kor dze mma onndzi dase nntsia nyimpa biara mma onnwu.
31. Nkyii so mma hom nngye onyimpakumfo a ɔsɛ owu no nkwa tsir mpata biara: na oku ara na wonku no.
32. Na mma hom nngye nyia oeguan kɔ no guansumabew kurow mu ne tsir mpata biara dɛ ɔnsan mbɛtsena asaase no do bio, nkodu ɔsɔfo no wu do.
33. Dɛm ntsi mma hom nngu asaase no a hom tse do no ho fi: na bɔgya ara na ogu asaase no ho fi: na wɔrennyɛ mpatadze biara mma asaase no wɔ bɔgya a woehue egu do no ntsi, gyedɛ nyia ɔkaa bɔgya gui no ara no bɔgyaa mu.
34. Na mma nngu asaase a hom tse do a mo so metse ne finimfin no do fi: na emi EWURADZE na metse Israelmba finimfin.

## Tsir 36

1. Na Gilead, Makir ne babanyin, Manaasɛ ne babanyin, a wofi Josef ne mbabanyin ebusua ebusua mu egyanom efi do etsitsir no bae, na wɔbɛkasae wɔ Moses, nye mbapɔmba no, Israelmba egyanom efi do etsitsir no enyim:
2. na wɔsee dɛ, EWURADZE hyɛɛ mo wura dɛ ɔmfa tontotwe nkyɛ asaase no mu mma Israelmba ma wɔnyɛ no egyapadze: na mo wura na EWURADZE hyɛɛ no dɛ ɔmfa hɛn nua Zelofehad n'egyapadze mma ne mbabaa.
3. Na sɛ wɔwar hɔn ma Israelmba ebusuakuw bi mu mbabanyin biara a, ɔno wonyi hɔn egyapadze no mfi hɛn egyanom egyapadze mu, na wɔmfa nkɛka ebusuakuw kor a wɔakɛyɛ hɔndze no egyapadze ho: dɛm na wonyi mfi hɛn egyapadze kyɛfa no mu.
4. Na sɛ Israelmba ahosɛpɛw afe no du a, ɔno wɔmfa hɔn egyapadze no nkɛka ebusuakuw kor a wɔakɛyɛ hɔndze no egyapadze ho: ma woeyi hɔn egyapadze efi hɔn egyanom ebusuakuw egyapadze no mu.
5. Na Moses hyɛɛ Israelmba no dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE n'asɛm tse, dɛ, Josef ne mbabanyin ebusuakuw no aka no tsenn.
6. Iyi nye adze a EWURADZE hyɛe wɔ Zelofehad mbabaa no ho no, dɛ, Wɔmma wɔnwarwar hɔn a wɔdwen hɔn dɛ woye no: hɔn egya n'ebusuakuw mu ebusua no ara nko na wɔnwarwar wɔ mu.
7. Amma Israelmba egyapadze biara eemmfi ebusuakuw kor mu annkɔ ebusuakuw fofor bi mu: na Israelmba no ntakyer hɔn egyanom ebusuakuw egyapadze ho.
8. Na ɔbabaa biara a onya egyapadze wɔ Israelmba ebusuakuw biara mu no, ɔnyɛ n'egya ebusuakuw mu ebusua no mu obi ne yer, ama Israelmba no eenyinya nyimpa biara n'egyanom egyapadze edzi.
9. Amma egyapadze biara eemmfi ebusuakuw kor mu annkɔ ebusuakuw kor so mu; na Israelmba ebusuakuw ebusuakuw no ntakyer hɔnankasa hɔn egyapadze ho.
10. Mekyerɛ dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no, dɛmara na Zelofehad ne mbabaa no yɛe:
11. na Mahala, nye Tirza, nye Hogla, nye Milka, nye Noa, Zelofehad mbabaa no warwaar hɔn egya no nuanom mbabanyin no.
12. Wɔwarwaar hɔn kɔr Josef ne babanyin Manaasɛ mbabanyin ebusua ebusua mu, na hɔn egyapadze no kaa hɔn egya n'ebusua ebusuakuw no mu.
13. Iyinom nye mbransɛm nye ntsɛmbu a, EWURADZE nam Moses nsa do hyɛɛ Israelmba wɔ Moab tɛtɛr mu wɔ Jeriko Jordan do.
