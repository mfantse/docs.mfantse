    Levitikɔs

    Tsir 1

    1. Na EWURADZE frɛɛ Moses, na ofii ehyiadzibuw no mu kasaa kyerɛɛ no dɛ, LEV.1.2, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom mu nyimpa biara dze ayɛyɛdze rebrɛ EWURADZE a, hom mfa hom fiembowa, a wɔyɛ anantwi nye nguan mu bi, nyɛ hom ayɛyɛdze mbra.
    2. Sɛ n'ayɛyɛdze yɛ ɔhyew afɔr a ofi anantwi mu a, ɔmfa onyin a onndzii dzɛm mbra ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano, ama aayɛ enyisɔ wɔ EWURADZE enyim ama no.
    3. Na ɔmfa ne nsa nto ɔhyew afɔr no n'etsifi; na ɔbɔsɔ enyi dɛ mpata ama no.
    4. Na onku nantwisaa no wɔ EWURADZE enyim: na Aaron mbabanyin, asɔfo no, mfa bɔgya no mbɛkyerɛ, na wɔmfa bɔgya no mpetse afɔrmbukyia no a ɔwɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano no do nhyia.
    5. Na ɔnyer abowa no, na ontwitwa no esinesin.
    6. Na ɔsɔfo Aaron ne mbabanyin no mfa nyena ngu afɔrmbukyia no do, na wɔnsɔ gya wɔ do.
    7. Na Aaron mbabanyin asɔfo no, mfa esinesin no, tsir no, nye seradze no, ngu yie wɔ nyena a ɔsɛw gya no afɔrmbukyia no do no do;
    8. n'ayamudze nye ne ntwɛr dze ɔmfa nsu nhohor ho ana: na ɔsɔfo no nhyew ne nyinara wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, ma ɔnyɛ ɔhyew afɔr, mekyerɛ ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ma EWURADZE.
    9. Na sɛ n'ayɛyɛdze fi nguankuw, nkɔsɔw anaa mpɔnkye mu a, ɔmfa onyin a onndzii dzɛm mbra.
    10. Na onku no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no ne nkyɛn mu kwaamu afamu wɔ EWURADZE enyim: na Aaron mbabanyin, asɔfo no, mfa no bɔgyaa mpetse afɔrmbukyia no do nhyia.
    11. Na ontwitwa no esinesin, nka ne tsir nye ne seradze ho; na ɔsɔfo no nsiesie no yie ngu nyena no a ɔwɔ gya no a ɔwɔ afɔrmbukyia no do no do:
    12. ayamudze nye ntwɛr no dze ɔmfa nsu nhohor ho ana: na ɔsɔfo no mfa ne nyinara mbra, na ɔmbɛhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do: ɔyɛ ɔhyew afɔr, mekyerɛ ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ma EWURADZE.
    13. Na sɛ n'ayɛyɛdze fi nnoma mu na ɔdze robɔbɔ EWURADZE ɔhyew afɔr a, ɔno ɔmfa ntamram, anaa mborɔnomamba bi nyɛ n'ayɛyɛdze mbra.
    14. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa mbra afɔrmbukyia no ho, na ɔntsew ne tsir, na ɔnhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do; na no bɔgyaa no nkyi ngu afɔrmbukyia no ho:
    15. na onyi no totuaa nka no mu ifi no ho, na ɔntow nkyen afɔrmbukyia no nkyɛn wɔ boka afamu, wɔ mbrɛ nso no gu no:
    16. na onsuan mu wɔ n'atsaban ho, na mbom mma ɔnntsew mu: na ɔsɔfo no nhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, wɔ nyena no a ɔwɔ gya no do no do: ɔyɛ ɔhyew afɔr, mekyerɛ ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ma EWURADZE.

    Tsir 2

    1. Na sɛ obiara dze edziban afɔr ayɛyɛdze rebrɛ EWURADZE a, n'ayɛyɛdze no nyɛ isiam pa; na onhue ngo ngu do, na ɔmfa krɔbɔw nto do:
    2. na ɔmfa mbrɛ Aaron mbabanyin asɔfo no: na ɔmfa isiam pa, nye no ngo no ne nsapuw, ɔnye no krɔbɔw no nyinara: na ɔsɔfo no nhyew no dɛ nkaadze wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ma EWURADZE:
    3. na edziban afɔr no mu dza ɔbɛka no nyɛ Aaron nye ne mbabanyin dze: ɔyɛ EWURADZE ogya afɔr mu krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn.
    4. Na sɛ edze edziban afɔr ayɛyɛdze a wɔto no wɔ fononoo mu bi rebɛba a, fa isiam pa epitsi tatar a wɔafrɔw no ngo, anaa epitsi tatar ntrɛtraba a wɔasera ho ngo.
    5. Na sɛ w'ayɛyɛdze no yɛ ɔtosɛn mu edziban afɔr a, ma ɔnyɛ isiam pa epitsi a wɔafɔw no ngo.
    6. Bubu mu esinesin, na hue ngo gu do: ɔyɛ edziban afɔr.
    7. Na sɛ w'ayɛyɛdze no yɛ ɔkyesɛn mu edziban afɔr a, fa isiam pa nye ngo yɛ.
    8. Na fa edziban afɔr a wɔdze ndzɛmba yinom ayɛ no brɛ EWURADZE: wɔmfa mma ɔsɔfo no, ma ɔmfa mbra afɔrmbukyia no ho.
    9. Na ɔsɔfo no nyi edziban afɔr no do nkaadze no, na ɔnhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do: ɔyɛ ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ma EWURADZE.
    10. Na edziban afɔr no mu dza ɔbɛka no nyɛ Aaron nye ne mbabanyin dze: ɔyɛ EWURADZE ogya afɔr mu krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn.
    11. Edziban afɔr biara a hom dze bɛbrɛ EWURADZE no, mma wɔmmfa mbɔrkaw nnyɛ: na mma hom nnhyew mbɔrkaw, anaa ewo biara, dɛ ogya afɔr mma EWURADZE.
    12. Hom mfa iyinom mbrɛ EWURADZE, dɛ nduadzewakan ayɛyɛdze: na afɔrmbukyia no do dze, na mma bi mmba mmbeyi nka huanhuam.
    13. Na w'edziban afɔr ayɛyɛdze biara fa nkyen to mu; nkyii so mma wo Nyankopɔn ahyɛmu nkyen no mmpa w'edziban afɔr mu: w'ayɛyɛdze nyinara mu fa nkyen ka ho bra.
    14. Na sɛ edze nduadzewakan ayɛyɛdze rebrɛ EWURADZE a, fa eburow mbɛtsem a wɔatoto no ogya mu, eburow mbɛtsem nomanoma a wɔapose, yɛ wo nduadzewakan mu ayɛyɛdze bra.
    15. Na fa ngo gu do, na fa krɔbɔw to do: ɔyɛ edziban afɔr.
    16. Na ɔsɔfo no nhyew ne nkaadze no, n'eburow mposee no bi, nye no ngo no bi, nye no krɔbɔw no nyinara; ɔyɛ ogya afɔr ma EWURADZE.

    Tsir 3

    1. Na sɛ n'ayɛyɛdze yɛ asomdwee afɔr afɔrbɔdze; sɛ ofi anantwi mu, dɛ onyin o, ɔber o, na ɔdze reba a, dza onndzii dzɛm na ɔmfa no mbra EWURADZE enyim.
    2. Na ɔmfa ne nsa nto n'ayɛyɛdze no etsifi, na onku no wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano: na Aaron mbabanyin asɔfo no mfa bɔgya no mpetse afɔrmbukyia no do nhyia.
    3. Na onyi asomdwee afɔr no mu bi mbra mbɛyɛ ogya afɔr mma EWURADZE. Onyi seradze a ɔkata ayamudze do no, nye seradze a ɔwɔ do no, LEV.3.4, nye nsɛbɛ ebien no, na seradze a ɔwɔ ho no, dza ɔwɔ sisiw mu, nye berɛbo do ntentan no mfi hɔ.
    4. Na Aaron mbabanyin no nhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do ɔhyew afɔr no a ɔwɔ nyena no a ɔwɔ ogya no do no do: ɔyɛ ogya afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam ma EWURADZE.
    5. Na sɛ n'ayɛyɛdze a ɔdze rebɛyɛ asomdwee afɔr afɔrbɔdze ama EWURADZE no fi nguan mu a; onyin o, ɔber o, dza onndzii dzɛm na ɔmfa no mbra.
    6. Sɛ ɔdze eguambaa rebɛyɛ n'ayɛyɛdze a, ɔno ɔmfa no mbra EWURADZE enyim:
    7. na ɔmfa ne nsa nto n'ayɛyɛdze no etsifi, na onku no wɔ ehyiadzibuw no enyim: na Aaron mbabanyin no mfa no bɔgyaa mpetse afɔrmbukyia no do nhyia.
    8. Na onyi asomdwee afɔr no bi mbra mbɛyɛ EWURADZE ogya afɔr; ne seradze no, nye seradze dua mu no nyinara, ɔntwe no wɔ n'ekyir kratse no ho. Na onyi seradze a ɔkata ayamudze no ho no, nye seradze a ɔwɔ ayamudze no do no nyinara, LEV.3.10, nye nsɛbɛ ebien no, nye no do seradze no a ɔwɔ sisiw mu no, nye berɛbo do ntentan no mfi hɔ.
    9. Na ɔsɔfo no nhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do: ɔyɛ ogya afɔr edziban ma EWURADZE.
    10. Na sɛ n'ayɛyɛdze yɛ apɔnkye a, ɔno ɔmfa no mbra EWURADZE enyim:
    11. na ɔmfa ne nsa nto n'etsifi, na onku no wɔ ehyiadzibuw no enyim; na Aaron ne mbabanyin no mfa no bɔgyaa mpetse afɔrmbukyia no do nhyia.
    12. Na onyi mu bi mbra mbɛyɛ n'ayɛyɛdze, mekyerɛ ogya afɔr mma EWURADZE; seradze a ɔkata ayamudze no ho no, nye seradze a ɔwɔ ayamudze no do no nyinara, LEV.3.15, nye nsɛbɛ ebien no, nye no do seradze no a ɔwɔ sisiw mu no, nye berɛbo do ntentan no dze, onyi mfi hɔ.
    13. Na ɔsɔfo no nhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do: ɔyɛ ogya afɔr edziban a oyi nka huanhuam: seradze no nyinara yɛ EWURADZE dze.
    14. Ɔnyɛ daa ahyɛdze wɔ hom ebusuasantsen mu wɔ hom tsenabea nyinara mu, dɛ hom nndzi seradze anaa bɔgya biara.

    Tsir 4

    1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.4.2, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, dɛ, Sɛ nyimpa biara yɛ bɔn a ɔnndze enyidaho wɔ ndzɛmba biara a EWURADZE ahyɛ ho mbra dɛ mma wɔnnyɛ no mu, na ɔkɛyɛ no mu kor biara a, dza wɔnyɛ nye iyi:
    2. sɛ ɔsɔfo a wɔasera no no kɛyɛ bɔn ma ɔdze asodzi ba ɔman no do a: ɔno no bɔn a ɔayɛ no ntsi, ma ɔmfa ɔnantwisaa eburwa a onndzii dzɛm mbrɛ EWURADZE mfa nyɛ bɔn ho afɔr.
    3. Na ɔmfa nantwisaa no mbra ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano wɔ EWURADZE enyim; na ɔmfa ne nsa nto nantwisaa no n'etsifi, na onku no wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
    4. Na ɔsɔfo a wɔasera no no mfa nantwisaa no no bɔgyaa no bi, na ɔmfa mbra ehyiadzibuw no mu:
    5. na ɔsɔfo no mfa ne nsatseaba mbɔ bɔgya no mu, na ɔmfa bɔgya no bi mpetse mpɛn esuon wɔ EWURADZE enyim, wɔ krɔnkrɔnbea no no ntwamutam no enyim.
    6. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa bɔgya no bi nkeka eduhuamhyew afɔrmbukyia no a ɔwɔ EWURADZE enyim no a ɔwɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu no ne mbɛn no ho; na onhue nantwisaa no no bɔgyaa nyinara ngu ɔhyew afɔr afɔrmbukyia no a ɔwɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano no ase.
    7. Na onyi bɔn ho afɔr nantwisaa no ne seradze no nyinara mfi no ho; seradze a ɔkata ayamudze no ho no, nye ayamudze no ho seradze no nyinara, LEV.4.9, nye nsɛbɛ ebien no, nye no do seradze no a ɔwɔ sisiw mu no, nye berɛbo do ntentan no, onyi mfi hɔ, LEV.4.10, dɛ mbrɛ woyi fi asomdwee afɔr ayɛyɛdze nantwinyin ho: na ɔsɔfo no nhyew no wɔ ɔhyew afɔr afɔrmbukyia no do.
    8. Na nantwisaa no no nwoma, nye ne nam nyinara, nye ne tsir, no ntwɛr, n'ayamudze, nye n'egyanan, LEV.4.12, mekyerɛ ɔnantwisaa mu no nyinara na ɔnsoa nkɔ nseraban no ekyir beebi a hɔ tsew, mbrɛ wohue nso gu no, na ɔmfa ogya nhyew no nyena do. Beebi a wohue nso gu no ara na wɔnhyew no.
    9. Na sɛ Israel mboanofo nyinara fom, a wɔammfa enyidaho annyɛ, na wɔyɛ ndzɛmba a EWURADZE ahyɛ ho mbra dɛ mma wɔnnyɛ no mu bi, na wodzi so;
    10. na ekyir yi wohu bɔn a wɔafom wɔ mu no a, ɔno bagua no mfa nantwisaa eburwa mbra mbɛyɛ bɔn ho afɔr, na wɔmfa no mbra ehyiadzibuw no enyim.
    11. Na mboanofo no mu mpanyimfo no mfa hɔn nsa ngu nantwisaa no n'etsifi na wonku nantwisaa no wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
    12. Na ɔsɔfo a wɔasera no no mfa nantwisaa no no bɔgyaa no bi mbra ehyiadzibuw no mu.
    13. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa ne nsatseaba mbɔ bɔgya no mu, na ɔmfa mpetse mpɛn esuon wɔ EWURADZE enyim wɔ ntwamutam no enyim.
    14. Na ɔmfa bɔgya no bi nkeka afɔrmbukyia no a ɔwɔ EWURADZE enyim no a ɔwɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu no ne mbɛn ho, na onhue bɔgya no nyinara ngu ɔhyew afɔr afɔrmbukyia no a ɔwɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano ho ase.
    15. Na onyi ne seradze no nyinara mfi ho, na ɔnhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do.
    16. Dɛ mbrɛ ɔyɛɛ bɔn ho afɔr nantwisaa no, dɛmara so na ɔnyɛ iyi na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma hɔn, na wɔdze bɛkyɛ hɔn.
    17. Na ɔmfa nantwisaa no mfir edzi nkɔ nseraban no ekyir, na ɔnhyew no dɛ mbrɛ ɔhyeew asɔpɔn nantwisaa no. Iyi yɛ bagua no hɔn bɔn ho afɔr.
    18. Sɛ otsitsir bi ammfa enyidaho na ɔyɛ bɔn, na ɔyɛ EWURADZE ne Nyankopɔn ndzɛmba nyinara a wɔahyɛ dɛ mma wɔnnyɛ no mu kor bi ma odzi so;
    19. na sɛ wɔma ohu no bɔn a ɔafom wɔ mu no a, ɔmfa n'ayɛyɛdze a ɔyɛ apɔnkye mbra, onyin a onndzii dzɛm;
    20. na ɔmfa ne nsa nto apɔnkye no n'etsifi, na onku no wɔ beebi mbrɛ woku ɔhyew afɔr no wɔ EWURADZE enyim no. Iyi yɛ bɔn ho afɔr.
    21. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa ne nsatseaba mfa bɔn ho afɔr bɔgyaa no bi, na ɔmfa nkeka ɔhyew afɔr afɔrmbukyia no ne mmbɛn ho, na onhue no bɔgyaa no ngu ɔhyew afɔr afɔrmbukyia no ase.
    22. Na ɔnhyew ne seradze no nyinara wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, dɛ asomdwee afɔr ayɛyɛdze seradze no: na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma no wɔ no bɔn no ho, na wɔdze bɛkyɛ no.
    23. Na sɛ ɔman mba no bi ammfa enyidaho na ɔyɛ bɔn wɔ ndzɛmba nyinara a EWURADZE ahyɛ ho mbra dɛ mma wɔnnyɛ no mu biara a, odzi so., #Nka 15.27
    24. Sɛ wɔma ohu no bɔn a ɔafom no a ɔno ɔmfa n'ayɛyɛdze, apɔnkyeber a onndzii dzɛm, mbra mbɛma, wɔ no bɔn a ɔayɛ no ntsi.
    25. Na ɔmfa ne nsa nto bɔn ho afɔr no n'etsifi, na onku bɔn ho afɔr no wɔ ɔhyew afɔr bea no do.
    26. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa ne nsatseaba mfa bɔgya no bi, na ɔmfa nkeka ɔhyew afɔr afɔrmbukyia no ne mmbɛn ho, na onhue no bɔgyaa no nyinara ngu afɔrmbukyia no ase.
    27. Na onyi ne seradze no nyinara, dɛ mbrɛ woyi asomdwee afɔr seradze no fi ho no; na ɔsɔfo no nhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do ma onyi nka huanhuam mma EWURADZE; na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma no, na wɔdze bɛkyɛ no.
    28. Na sɛ ɔdze eguambaa ba dɛ n'ayɛyɛdze wɔ bɔn ho afɔr ntsi a, ɔmfa ɔber a onndzii dzɛm mbra.
    29. Na ɔmfa ne nsa nto bɔn no afɔr abowa no n'etsifi, na onku no bɔn ho afɔr wɔ beebi mbrɛ woku ɔhyew afɔr no.
    30. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa ne nsatseaba mfa bɔn ho afɔr bɔgyaa no bi, na ɔmfa nkeka ɔhyew afɔr afɔrmbukyia no ne mbɛn ho, na onhue no bɔgyaa no nyinara ngu afɔrmbukyia no ase:
    31. na onyi ne seradze no nyinara, dɛ mbrɛ woyi asomdwee afɔr eguambaa seradze no fi ho no; na ɔsɔfo no nhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, wɔ EWURADZE ogya afɔr no do: na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma no wɔ no bɔn a ɔayɛ no ho, na wɔdze bɛkyɛ no.

    Tsir 5

    1. Sɛ obi tse ntanka bi na ɔyɛ ho dasefo, sɛ ohunii o, ɔtsee o, na sɛ ɔannka a, ɔno ɔnsoa no mbusu.
    2. Anaa sɛ obi ka adze fi biara, dɛ abowa bi a no ho nntsew no fun o, fiebowa bi a no ho nntsew no fun o, anaa aweaweabowa bi a no ho nntsew no fun o, na sɛ oennhu mpo na no ho gu fi a, ɔno odzi so:
    3. anaa sɛ ɔka nyimpa bi ne fi, wɔ adze fi biara a nyimpa dze gu no ho fi no mu, ber a onnyim mpo na sɛ ekyir yi ohu a, ɔno odzi so:
    4. anaa sɛ obi ka ntanhun dɛ ɔbɛyɛ bɔn, anaa ɔbɛyɛ papa, wɔ asɛnhun biara mu, na n'adwen annkɔ do na sɛ ekyir yi n'enyi ba noho do a, ɔno odzi ndzɛmba iyinom mu biara ho so:
    5. Na sɛ ɔba dɛ, odzi ndzɛmba iyinom mu biara ho so a, ɔnka dza ɔayɛ mu bɔn no:

    8. Na ɔmfa mbrɛ ɔsɔfo no, ma ondzi kan mfa dza ɔyɛ bɔn ho afɔr no, na ɔntsew ne tsir wɔ no kɔn do, na mbom mma ɔmmpaa mu koraa:
    9. na ɔmfa bɔn ho afɔr bɔgyaa no bi mpetse afɔrmbukyia no nkyɛn; na bɔgya no ne nkaa no ɔnsɔn ngu afɔrmbukyia no ase: ɔyɛ bɔn ho afɔr.
    10. Na ɔmfa dza otsia ebien no nyɛ ɔhyew afɔr, dɛ mbrɛ mbra no tse: na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma no wɔ no bɔn a ɔayɛ no ho, na wɔdze bɛkyɛ no.
    11. Na sɛ ne nsa annso ntamram ebien, anaa mborɔnomamba ebien do a, ɔno ɔmfa isiam pa efa ebupɛn du mu kor nyɛ no bɔn a ɔayɛ no ho ayɛyɛdze mbra mbɛyɛ bɔn ho afɔr; mma ɔmmfa ngo nngu do, nkyii so mma ɔmmfa krɔbɔw bi nnto do: na ɔyɛ bɔn ho afɔr.
    12. Na ɔmfa mbrɛ ɔsɔfo no, na ɔsɔfo no mfa mu ne nsapuw dɛ ne nkaadze, na ɔnhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, wɔ EWURADZE ogya afɔr no do; ɔyɛ bɔn ho afɔr.
    13. Na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma no wɔ no bɔn a ɔayɛ no wɔ ndzɛmba iyinom mu biara mu no ho, na wɔdze bɛkyɛ no: na nkaa no nyɛ ɔsɔfo no dze, dɛ edziban afɔr no.
    14. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.5.15, Sɛ obiara to mbra, dɛ ɔannyɛ dza ɔfata, wɔ EWURADZE ndzɛmba krɔnkrɔn no mu wɔ ber a ɔnndze enyidaho a; ɔno ɔmfa n'asodzii ho afɔr mbrɛ EWURADZE, nguan mu oguannyin a onndzii dzɛm, dɛ mbrɛ ebo a etwa wɔ dwetɛbona mu wɔ dwɛɛtɛɛ mu tse, dɛ krɔnkrɔnbea dwetɛbona nyɛ asodzi ho afɔr:
    15. na ɔnyɛ ahyɛnanmu mma dza ɔayɛ no bɔn wɔ krɔnkrɔndze mu no, na ɔmfa ebupɛn a otsia enum no nka ho, na ɔmfa mma ɔsɔfo no; na ɔsɔfo no mfa asodzi ho afɔr oguannyin no mpata mma no, na wɔdze bɛkyɛ no.
    16. Na sɛ obiara yɛ bɔn, na ɔyɛ ndzɛmba a EWURADZE ahyɛ ho mbra dɛ mma wɔnnyɛ no mu biara a; kaansa oennhu mpo a, odzi so, na ɔbɔsoa no mbusu.
    17. Na ɔmfa nguan mu oguannyin a onndzii dzɛm dɛ mbrɛ ebo a etwa tse, nyɛ asodzi ho afɔr mbrɛ ɔsɔfo no: na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma no wɔ adze a ɔafom wɔ mu na oennhu no no ho, na wɔdze bɛkyɛ no.
    18. Ɔyɛ asodzi ho afɔr: osiandɛ ampaara odzi EWURADZE enyim so.

    Tsir 6

    1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ
    2. Sɛ obiara yɛ bɔn, dɛ odzi EWURADZE afomfonsɛm, na odzi akɔhwi wɔ ne nyɛnko adzesiedze ho asɛm mu, anaa ahobadze, anaa afoadze, anaa odzi ne nyɛnko emim;
    3. anaa oehu dza ɔyewee na odzi akɔhwi wɔ mu, na ɔka ntanhun a, ɔayɛ bɔn wɔ iyinom mu.
    4. Sɛ ɔba dɛ ɔayɛ bɔn, na odzi so a, ɔmfa adze a ɔfaa no afoadze mu, anaa adze a onyaa no emim mu, anaa adzesiedze a wɔdze hyɛɛ ne nsa, anaa adze a ɔyewee a ohunii, LEV.6.5, anaa adze biara a ɔaka ho ntanhun no no nsan mbra; na ɔmbɛhyɛ ananmu ara pɛ, na ɔmfa ebupɛn enum mu kor so nto do mfa mma nyia adze no yɛ nedze no, wɔ da a woehu no dɛ odzi so no ara mu.
    5. Na ɔmfa n'asodzii ho afɔr mbrɛ EWURADZE, nguan mu oguannyin a onndzii dzɛm, dɛ mbrɛ ebo a etwa tse, mfa mbrɛ ɔsɔfo no nyɛ asodzi ho afɔr:

    9. Na onyi ne ntar no, na ɔnhyɛ ntar fofor, na ɔmfa nso no mfir edzi nkɔ nseraban no ekyir nkogu beebi mbrɛ ɔtsew.

    11. Ogya nkɔ do ndzɛw afɔrmbukyia no do daa; mma onndum ara da.
    12. Na iyi nye edziban afɔr ho mbra: Aaron ne mbabanyin no mfa mbra EWURADZE enyim, wɔ afɔrmbukyia no enyim.
    13. Na ɔmfa mu ne nsapuw, edziban afɔr isiam pa no bi, nye no ngo no bi, nye edziban afɔr no do krɔbɔw no nyinara, na ɔnhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, nyi nka huanhuam, dɛ ne nkaadze, mma EWURADZE.
    14. Na ne nkaa no, Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no ndzi: wondzi no epitsi wɔ mbrɛ ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn; ehyiadzibuw no ne pampamu mu na wondzi.
    15. Mma wɔmmfa mbɔrkaw nnka ho nnto. Hɔn kyɛfa a medze ama wɔ me gya afɔr mu nye no; ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn, dɛ bɔn ho afɔr, na asodzi ho afɔr no.
    16. Aaron ne mba mu banyin biara ndzi bi, dɛ EWURADZE ogya afɔr ho daa ahyɛdze, nkodu hom ebusuasantsen do: obiara a ɔka no no nyɛ krɔnkrɔn.
    17. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.6.20, Iyi nye Aaron nye ne mbabanyin ayɛyɛdze a wɔmfa mbrɛ EWURADZE wɔ da a wɔasera no ngo no mu; isiam pa efa ebupɛn du mu kor nyɛ daa edziban afɔr, anapa dze, fa, na ewimbir dze, fa.

    20. Na ɔsɔfo no n'edziban afɔr biara wɔnhyew no mu: mma wonndzi bi.
    21. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.6.25, Kasa kyerɛ Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no dɛ, Iyi nye bɔn ho afɔr abowa ho mbra: beebi mbrɛ woku ɔhyew abowa afɔr no hɔ ara na wonku bɔn ho afɔr no wɔ EWURADZE enyim: Ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn.
    22. Ɔsɔfo a ɔbɔ bɔn ho afɔr no ndzi: wondzi no wɔ mbrɛ ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn wɔ ehyiadzibuw no ne pampamu.
    23. Biribiara a ɔka ne nam no nyɛ krɔnkrɔn: na sɛ no bɔgyaa petse atar biara do a, hor dza apetse do no wɔ mbrɛ ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn.
    24. Mbom dɛtsesɛn a wɔanoa no wɔ mu no wɔmbɔ no: na sɛ wɔnoa no boransɛn mu a, wontwuw mu, na wɔmfa nsu nhohor mu.
    25. Banyin biara a ofi asɔfo ebusuakuw mu no ndzi bi: ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn.
    26. Na bɔn ho afɔr biara a wɔdze no bɔgyaa bi ba ehyiadzibuw no mu bɛpata wɔ bea krɔnkrɔn no, mma wonndzi: wɔmfa ogya nhyew no.

    Tsir 7

    1. Na iyi nye asodzi ho afɔr ho mbra: ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn.
    2. Beebi mbrɛ woku ɔhyew afɔr abowa no, hɔ ara so na wonku asodzi ho afɔr abowa no; na ɔmfa no bɔgyaa mpetse afɔrmbukyia no ho nhyia.
    3. Na onyi ne seradze nyinara mbra, seradze dua no, nye seradze a ɔkata ayamudze no ho no, LEV.7.4, nye nsɛbɛ ebien no, nye seradze a ɔwɔ ho wɔ sisiw mu no, nye berɛbo do ntentan no, nye nsɛbɛ no, onyi mfi hɔ:
    4. na ɔsɔfo no nhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia do nyɛ ogya afɔr mma EWURADZE: ɔyɛ asodzi ho afɔr.
    5. Asɔfo ebusuakuw no mu ɔbanyin biara ndzi bi: wondzi no wɔ mbrɛ ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn: ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn.
    6. Dɛ mbrɛ bɔn ho afɔr tse no, dɛmara na asodzi ho afɔr tse: mbra kor na ɔda ho: ɔsɔfo no a ɔdze pata no, na ɔnyɛ nedze.
    7. Na ɔsɔfo a ɔbɔ nyimpa biara ne hyew afɔr no, ɔhyew afɔr a ɔabɔ no no nwoma no nyɛ ɔsɔfo no dze.
    8. Na edziban afɔr biara a wɔto wɔ fononoo mu, nye dza wɔyɛ no wɔ kyesɛn mu, nye ɔtosɛn mu nyina, nyɛ ɔsɔfo a ɔdze ba no dze.
    9. Na edziban afɔr biara a wɔdze ngo afrɔw, anaa wesee no, nyɛ Aaron ne mbabanyin nyinara dze, baako nya dɛ mbrɛ baako so nya.
    10. Na iyi nye asomdwee afɔr a obi bɔ ma EWURADZE no ho mbra.
    11. Sɛ ɔyɛ no aseda afɔr dze ba a, ɔno, aseda afɔr a ɔdze ba no nyɛ epitsi tatar a wɔdze ngo afrɔw.
    12. Mbɔrkaw boodoo tatar na ɔmfa nka n'ayɛyɛdze nye n'asomdwee afɔr ho mbra mbɛyɛ aseda.
    13. Na ɔmfa ayɛyɛdze biara mu kor mbrɛ mbɛyɛ ɔmado afɔr mma EWURADZE; ɔsɔfo a ɔdze asomdwee afɔr bɔgyaa petse no na ɔnyɛ nedze.
    14. Na n'aseda asomdwee afɔr no nam no wondzi no wɔ n'ayɛyɛdze da no ara; mma onngya mu bi ma onnkodu adzekyee.
    15. Mbom sɛ n'ayɛyɛdze yɛ anohoba, anaa ayamuyi afɔr a, wondzi no da no ara a ɔbɔ n'afɔr no: na no mu dza ɔka ma adze kye do no wondzi:
    16. na mbom afɔr no ne nam a ɔbɛka ekodu da a otsia ebiasa no, wɔmfa gya nhyew no.
    17. Na sɛ wodzi n'asomdwee afɔr no ne nam biara da a otsia ebiasa no a, wɔremmpen do, nkyii so worummbu mma nyia ɔdze ba no: ɔyɛ ekyiwadze, na ɔkra a obedzi bi no bɔsoa no mbusu.
    18. Na nam a aka biribiara a no ho nntsew no, mma wonndzi; wɔmfa ogya nhyew no. Na nam no dze, obiara a no ho tsew ndzi bi:
    19. na ɔkra a odzi asomdwee afɔr a ɔyɛ EWURADZE dze no ne nam bi, na n'efifidze wɔ no ho no, ɔkra no wobotwa no efi ne man mu akyen.
    20. Na sɛ nyimpa biara ka biribiara a no ho nntsew nyimpa n'efifidze, anaa abowa a no ho nntsew, anaa ekyiwadze biara a no ho nntsew, na odzi asomdwee afɔr a ɔyɛ EWURADZE dze no ne nam bi no, ɔkra no wobotwa no efi ne man mu akyen.
    21. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.7.23, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba dɛ, Ɔnantwi, anaa ɔkɔsɔw, anaa apɔnkye seradze no, mma hom nndzi.
    22. Na dza noara ewu ne seradze, nye dza abowa atsetsew no ne seradze no, wɔmfa nyɛ edwuma biara: mbom dzi na mma hom nndzi bi.
    23. Na obiara a odzi abowa a adasa dze bi bɔ ogya afɔr ma EWURADZE no ne seradze bi no, mekyerɛ ɔkra a odzi no wobotwa no efi ne man mu akyen.
    24. Na mma hom nndzi bɔgya biara, anoma anaa aketsebowa dze, wɔ hom tsenabew biara, , #Gye 9.4; Lev 17.10-14; 19.26; Deut 12.16
    25. Obiara a odzi bɔgya biara no, no wobotwa no efi ne man mu akyen.
    26. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.7.29, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba dɛ, Nyia ɔbɔ n'asomdwee afɔr ma EWURADZE no ɔmfa n'asomdwee afɔr no mu ayɛyɛdze mbrɛ EWURADZE:
    27. ɔmfa noara ne nsa mfa EWURADZE gya afɔr no mbra: seradze no nye nyan no na ɔmfa mbra, ama nyan no wɔaatow no ayɛ ɔtow afɔr wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
    28. Na ɔsɔfo no nhyew seradze no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do: na nyan no nyɛ Aaron nye ne mbabanyin dze.

    35. EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses wɔ bepɔw Sinai do, wɔ da a ɔhyɛɛ Israelmba dɛ wɔmfa hɔn ayɛyɛdze mbrɛ EWURADZE, wɔ Sinai sar do no.

    Tsir 8

    1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.8.2, Fa Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no ka no ho, na ntar no, nye ɔserango no, nye bɔn ho afɔr nantwisaa no, nye nguannyin ebien no, nye epitsi boodoo kɛntsɛn no, LEV.8.3, na mboanofo no nyinara mbɔ ngua wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano.
    2. Na Moses yɛɛ dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ no no; na mboanofo bɔɔ ngua wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano.
    3. Na Moses see mboanofo no dɛ, Iyi nye adze a EWURADZE ahyɛ dɛ wɔnyɛ.
    4. Na Moses dze Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no bae, na ɔdze nsu hohoor hɔn ho.
    5. Na ɔdze sortar no hyɛɛ no, na ɔdze abɔyamu no bɔɔ no do, na ɔdze bataker no hyɛɛ no, na ɔdze egugudo no guu no, na ɔdze egugudo ne nkyermu nkyekyermu no bɔɔ no do, na ɔdze kyekyeer no.
    6. Na ɔdze akatabo no hyɛɛ no: na ɔdze Urim nye Tummim (Kan na Emudzi) no hyɛɛ akatabo no mu.
    7. Na ɔdze asɔfokyɛw no hyɛɛ ne tsir: na ɔdze sika abɔtsir, abɔenyim krɔnkrɔn no, bɔɔ asɔfokyɛw no ho, wɔ n'enyim afamu; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
    8. Na Moses faa ɔserango, na ɔdze seraa ntamadan no nye dza ɔwɔ mu nyinara, na ɔyɛɛ no krɔnkrɔn.
    9. Na ɔdze bi petsee afɔrmbukyia no do mpɛn esuon, na ɔseraa afɔrmbukyia no nye no ho ndzɛmba nyinara, nye eguarsɛn no nye ne ntaease no ngo dze yɛɛ no krɔnkrɔn.
    10. Na ohuee ɔserango no bi guu Aaron n'etsifi, na ɔseraa no ngo dze yɛɛ no krɔnkrɔn.
    11. Na Moses dze Aaron mbabanyin no bae, na ɔdze sorntar hyehyɛɛ hɔn, na ɔdze abɔyamu bobɔɔ hɔn do, na ɔdze abɔtsir bobɔɔ hɔn; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
    12. Na ɔdze bɔn ho afɔr nantwisaa no bae: na Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no dze hɔn nsa guu bɔn ho afɔr nantwisaa no n'etsifi.
    13. Na Moses kum no; na ɔdze ne nsatseaba faa mbɔgya no bi, na ɔdze kekaa afɔrmbukyia no ne mmbɛn ho hyiae, na ɔtseew afɔrmbukyia no ho, na ohuee bɔgya no guu afɔrmbukyia no ase, na ɔyɛɛ no krɔnkrɔn, dze yɛɛ mpatadze.
    14. Na ɔfaa seradze a ɔwɔ ayamudze no ho no nyinara, nye berɛbo do ntentan no, nye nsɛbɛ ebien no, nye no ho seradze no, na Moses hyeew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do.
    15. Na ɔnantwisaa no, nye no nwoma, nye ne nam, nye n'egyanan, ɔdze ogya hyeew no wɔ nseraban no ekyir; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
    16. Na ɔdze ɔhyew afɔr oguannyin no bae: na Aaron nye ne mbabanyin dze hɔn nsa guu oguannyin no etsifi.
    17. Na Moses kum no: na ɔdze bɔgya no petsee afɔrmbukyia no do hyiae.
    18. Na otwitwaa oguannyin no mu esinesin; na ɔhyeew tsir no, nye esinesin no, nye seradze no.
    19. Na ɔdze nsu hohoor ayamudze nye ntwɛr no ho; na afei ɔhyeew oguannyin mu no nyinara wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do: ɔyɛ ɔhyew afɔr a oyi nka huanhuam: ɔyɛ EWURADZE ogya afɔr; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
    20. Na ɔdze oguannyin kor no so, etuhoakyɛ afɔr oguannyin no, bae: na Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no dze hɔn nsa guu oguannyin no etsifi.
    21. Na Moses kum no; na ɔfaa no bɔgyaa no bi, na ɔdze kaa Aaron n'aso nyimfa ano, na ne nsa nyimfa kokrobetsir, nye ne nan nyimfa kokonyin no ano.
    22. Na ɔdze Aaron mbabanyin no bae, na Moses dze bɔgya no bi kekaa hɔn aso nyimfa ano, nye hɔn nsa nyimfa kokrobetsir, nye hɔn anan nyimfa kokonyin no ano: na Moses dze bɔgya no petsee afɔrmbukyia no do hyiae.
    23. Na ɔfaa seradze no, nye seradze dua no, nye nsɛbɛ ebien no, nye no ho seradze no, nye aserɛ nyimfa no:
    24. na ɔfaa epitsi boodoo kɛntsɛn a osi EWURADZE enyim no mu epitsi tatar kor, nye ngo boodoo tatar kor, nye tatar trɛtraba kor, na ɔdze guu seradze no nye aserɛ nyimfa no do:

    27. Na Moses faa nyan no, na ɔtowee yɛɛ ɔtow afɔr wɔ EWURADZE enyim: ɔyɛ Moses ne kyɛfa wɔ etuhoakyɛ afɔr oguannyin no mu; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no ara.
    28. Na Moses faa ɔserango no bi, nye afɔrmbukyia no do bɔgya no bi, na ɔdze petsee Aaron, nye ne ntar, nye ne mbabanyin, nye ne mbabanyin ntar do; dze yɛɛ Aaron nye ne ntar, nye ne mbabanyin, nye ne mbabanyin ntar no, krɔnkrɔn.
    29. Na Moses see Aaron, nye ne mbabanyin no dɛ, Hom nnoa nam no wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano: na hom ndzi no wɔ hɔ ara ɔnye etuhoakyɛ afɔr kɛntsɛn no mu boodoo no.
    30. Na nam no nye boodoo no mu dza ɔbɛka no hom mfa gya nhyew no.
    31. Na mma hom mmfi ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano nda esuon, kyerɛ hom etuhoakyɛ afɔr nda no aso ana: na nda esuon na ɔdze rutu hom akyɛ.
    32. Dɛ mbrɛ wɔayɛ no ndɛ da yi no, dɛmara na EWURADZE ahyɛ dɛ wɔnyɛ mfa mpata mma hom.
    33. Na hom ntsena ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano adzekyee nye adzesaa nda esuon, na hom nhwɛ EWURADZE ahwɛdze no, amma hom eennwuwu: na dɛm na wɔdze ahyɛ me mbra.
    34. Na Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no yɛɛ ndzɛmba a EWURADZE nam Moses nsa do hyɛe no nyinara.

    Tsir 9

    1. Na ɔbaa dɛ da a otsia awɔtwe no, Moses frɛɛ Aaron nye ne mbabanyin no, na Israel mpanyimfo no;
    2. na ɔsee Aaron dɛ, Fa nantwiba onyin a wɔdze bɛyɛ bɔn ho afɔr, nye oguannyin a wɔdze bɛyɛ ɔhyew afɔr a wonndzii dzɛm, na fa bra EWURADZE enyim.
    3. Na kasa kyerɛ Israelmba no dɛ, Hom mfa apɔnkyenyin mbɛyɛ bɔn ho afɔr; na nantwiba nye eguambaa a hɔn beenu nyina woedzi afrenhyia afrenhyia a wonndzii dzɛm, mbɛyɛ ɔhyew afɔr;
    4. na ɔnantwinyin nye oguannyin mbɛyɛ asomdwee afɔr, mfa mbɔ afɔr wɔ EWURADZE enyim; na edziban afɔr a wɔdze ngo afrɔw: na ndɛ na EWURADZE yi noho kyerɛ hom.
    5. Na wɔdze dza Moses hyɛe no baa ehyiadzibuw no enyim: na mboanofo no nyinara twee bɛnee begyinaa EWURADZE enyim.
    6. Na Moses see dɛ, Iyi nye adze a EWURADZE hyɛɛ dɛ hom nyɛ: na EWURADZE enyimnyam nyi noho nkyerɛ hom.
    7. Na Moses see Aaron dɛ, Twe bɛn afɔrmbukyia no ho, na bɔ wo bɔn ho afɔr, nye wo hyew afɔr, na pata ma woho, nye ɔman no: na fa ɔman no hɔn ayɛyɛdze bra, na pata ma hɔn; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛe no.
    8. Nna Aaron twee bɛɛn afɔrmbukyia no ho, na okum bɔn ho afɔr nantwiba no a ɔyɛ nankasa nedze no.
    9. Na Aaron ne mbabanyin no dze bɔgya no kɛmaa no; na ɔdze ne nsatseaba bɔɔ bɔgya no mu, na ɔdze kekaa afɔrmbukyia no ne mmbɛn no, na ohuee bɔgya no guu afɔrmbukyia no ase:
    10. na bɔn ho afɔr no mu seradze no, nye nsɛbɛ no, nye berɛbo do ntentan no, ɔhyeew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
    11. Na nam no nye nwoma no dze, ɔdze gya hyeew no wɔ nseraban no ekyir.
    12. Na okum ɔhyew afɔr no; na Aaron ne mbabanyin no dze bɔgya no kɛmaa no, na ɔdze petsee afɔrmbukyia no do hyiae.
    13. Na wɔdze ɔhyew afɔr no esinesin, nye tsir no, kɛmaa no: na ɔhyeew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do.
    14. Na ɔhohoor ayamudze nye ntwɛr no ho, na ɔhyeew no wɔ ɔhyew afɔr no do wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do.
    15. Na ɔdze ɔman no hɔn ayɛyɛdze kɛmae, na ɔfaa bɔn ho afɔr apɔnkye a ɔyɛ ɔman no dze no, na okum no, na ɔyɛɛ no bɔn ho afɔr, dɛ asɔpɔn dze no.
    16. Na ɔdze edziban afɔr no kɛmae, na ɔyɛɛ no dɛ mbrɛ mbra no tse no ara.
    17. Na ɔdze edziban afɔr no kɛmae, na ɔfaa mu ne nsapuw, na ɔhyeew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do, dze kaa anapa ɔhyew afɔr no ho.
    18. Na eso okum nantwinyin nye oguannyin no asomdwee afɔr a ɔyɛ ɔman no dze no: na Aaron mbabanyin no dze bɔgya no kɛmaa no, na ɔdze petsee afɔrmbukyia no do hyiae
    19. na ɔnantwinyin nye oguannyin no seradze no; seradze dua no, nye dza ɔkata ayamudze no ho, nye nsɛbɛ no, nye berɛbo do ntentan no:
    20. na wɔdze seradze no guu nyan no do, na ɔhyeew seradze no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do:
    21. na nyan no nye aserɛ nyimfa no Aaron towee dze yɛɛ ɔtow afɔr wɔ EWURADZE enyim; dɛ mbrɛ Moses hyɛe no.
    22. Na Aaron memaa ne nsa do wɔ ɔman no do, na ohyiraa hɔn; na ɔbɔɔ bɔn ho afɔr nye ɔhyew afɔr, nye asomdwee afɔr wiee no, osianee.
    23. Na Moses nye Aaron kɛhɛɛn ehyiadzibuw no mu, na wopuei, na wohyiraa ɔman no; na EWURADZE enyimnyam yii noho kyerɛɛ ɔman no nyinara.
    24. Na ogya fi EWURADZE enyim bae, na ɔbɛhyeew afɔrmbukyia no do hyew afɔr nye seradze no: na ɔman no nyinara hunii no, wɔbɔɔ ose, na wɔdze hɔn enyim butubutuuw famu.

    Tsir 10

    1. Na Aaron ne mbabanyin no, Nadab nye Abihu, fefaa hɔn nduhuamsɛn, na wɔdze ogya guu mu, na wɔdze eduhuam guguu do, na wɔdze ananagya a ɔnnhyɛɛ hɔn ho mbra, baa EWURADZE enyim.
    2. Na ogya fi EWURADZE enyim bae, na ɔbɛmeen hɔn, na wowuwui wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
    3. Nna Moses see Aaron dɛ, Iyi nye dza EWURADZE kae no, dɛ hɔn a wɔtwe bɛn me no mu na mebɛyɛ moho krɔnkrɔn, na ɔman no nyinara enyim na mebɛhyɛ moho enyimnyam. Na Aaron muaa n'ano.
    4. Na Moses frɛɛ Misael nye Elzafan, Aaron n'egya no nua banyin Uziel ne mbabanyin no, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom ntwe mbɛn, hom nsoasoa hom nuanom mfi krɔnkrɔnbea no enyim nkɔ nseraban no ekyir.
    5. Nna wɔtwee bɛnee, na wɔsoasoaa hɔn wɔ hɔn sorntar mu kɔr nseraban no ekyir; dɛ mbrɛ Moses kae no.
    6. Na Moses kaa kyerɛɛ Aaron, nye ne mbabanyin Eleazar nye Itamar, dɛ, Mma hom mmbɔ hom tsinhwi mpɛsɛw, nkyii so mma hom nnsunsuan hom ntar mu; amma hom eennwuwu, amma EWURADZE n'ebufuw ammba mboanofo no nyinara do; mbom hom mma hom nuanom, Israel fi nyinara, ngyam ɔhyew a EWURADZE dze gya ahyew hɔn yi.
    7. Na mma hom mmfi ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano, amma hom eennwuwu: na EWURADZE ɔserango no wɔ hom do. Na wɔyɛɛ dɛ mbrɛ Moses kae no.
    8. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Aaron dɛ, LEV.10.9, Mma nnom wenyin anaa nsa dzendzen, ɔwo nye wo mbabanyin nyinara, aber a hom rokɔ ehyiadzibuw no mu no, amma hom eennwuwu: ɔnyɛ daa ahyɛdze wɔ hom ebusuasantsen nyinara mu:
    9. na hom dze he aato adzekrɔnkrɔn nye adze a ɔnnyɛ krɔnkrɔn ntamu, nye adze a no ho nntsew nye dza no ho tsew ntamu;
    10. na hom aakyerɛkyerɛ Israelmba ahyɛdze a EWURADZE nam Moses nsa do aka akyerɛ hɔn no nyinara.
    11. Na Moses kasaa kyerɛɛ Aaron, na Eleazar nye Itamar, ne mbabanyin a wɔkae no dɛ, Hom mfa edziban afɔr a ɔka a ofi EWURADZE ogya afɔr do no, na hom nkwetsi mbɔrkaw ndzi no wɔ afɔrmbukyia ho: osiandɛ ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn:
    12. na hom ndzi no wɔ bea krɔnkrɔn do, osiandɛ ɔyɛ w'ahyɛdze, nye wo mbabanyin ahyɛdze, wɔ EWURADZE ogya afɔr do: na dɛm na wɔahyɛ.
    13. Na ɔtow nyan no nye ɔmado aserɛ no hom ndzi no wɔ beebi mbrɛ ɔtsew; ɔwo, nye wo mbabanyin, nye wo mbabaa a wɔka wo ho: na wɔdze ama dɛ ɔyɛ w'ahyɛdze, na wo mbabanyin ahyɛdze a ofi Israelmba hɔn asomdwee afɔr mu.
    14. Ɔmado aserɛ no nye ɔtow nyan no wɔmfa nka ogya afɔr seradze no ho mbra, mbɔtow no ɔtow afɔr wɔ EWURADZE enyim; na ɔnyɛ wodze, nye wo mbabanyin a wɔka wo ho no dze, dɛ daa ahyɛdze; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE ahyɛ no.
    15. Na Moses hwehwɛɛ bɔn ho afɔr apɔnkye no enyiberdo, na hwɛ, wɔahyew no: na no bo fuuw Eleazar na ɔnye Itamar, Aaron ne mbabanyin a wɔkae no, dɛ, LEV.10.17, Ebɛn ntsi na hom enndzi bɔn ho afɔr no wɔ beebi mbrɛ ɔyɛ bea krɔnkrɔn no, osiandɛ ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn, na ɔdze ama hom dɛ, hom nsoa mboanofo no hɔn mbusu, nyɛ mpata mma hɔn wɔ EWURADZE enyim?
    16. Hwɛ, wɔammfa no bɔgyaa no ammba krɔnkrɔnbea no no mu: ampaara ɔsee dɛ nkyɛ hom ndzi no wɔ krɔnkrɔn bea no do hɔ, dɛ mbrɛ mehyɛe no.
    17. Na Aaron kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, Hwɛ, ndɛ da yi na wɔdze hɔn bɔn ho afɔr nye hɔn ɔhyew afɔr aba EWURADZE enyim; na ndzɛmba a ɔtsetse dɛm yi aba mo do yi: na sɛ midzi bɔn ho afɔr ndɛ a, ana nkyɛ ɔbɛyɛ enyisɔ wɔ EWURADZE enyim?
    18. Na Moses tsee no, ɔyɛɛ no abodwe.

    Tsir 11

    1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses nye Aaron, see hɔn dɛ, LEV.11.2, hom nkasa nkyerɛ Israelmba dɛ, Iyinom nye mbowa a hom ndzi wɔ asaase do mbowa nyinara mu.
    2. Anansambowa mu biara a wɔapaa n'anansa mu, na ɔyɛ anansapaae koraa, na ɔkosa adzepuwii no, ɔno na hom ndzi.
    3. Naaso iyinom na mma hom nndzi wɔ hɔn a wɔkosa adzepuwii, anaa hɔn a hɔn anansa mu apaa mu: efupɔnkɔ, osiandɛ ɔkosa adzepuwii dze, naaso n'anansa mu mmpaae, ntsi no ho nntsew mma hom.
    4. Na etwabowa, osiandɛ ɔkosa adzepuwii dze, naaso n'anansa mu mmpaae, no ho nntsew mma hom.
    5. Na asoaso, osiandɛ ɔkosa adzepuwii dze, naaso n'anansa mu mmpaae, no ho nntsew mma hom.
    6. Na preko, osiandɛ n'anansa mu apaa, na ɔyɛ anansapaae koraa dze, naaso ɔnnkosa adzepuwii ntsi no ho nntsew mma hom.
    7. Mma hom nndzi hɔn nam bi, na hɔn efun so mma hom nnka; hɔn ho nntsew mma hom.
    8. Iyinom nye dza hom ndzi wɔ dza ɔwɔ nsu mu nyinara mu: biara a ɔwɔ tɛterɛ nye abon wɔ nsu mu, pomu, nye esutsen mu no, iyinom na hom ndzi.
    9. Na dza wonnyi tɛterɛ nye abon a wɔwɔ pomu, nye esutsen mu nyinara, nye dza wɔwɔ nsu mu nyinara a nkwa wɔ hɔn mu no yɛ ekyiwadze ma hom, LEV.11.11, na ɔnyɛ hom ekyiwadze; mma hom nndzi hɔn nam, na hɔn efun mpo, hom nkyir.
    10. Adze biara a onnyi tɛterɛ anaa abon wɔ nsu mu no, ɔyɛ ekyiwadze ma hom.
    11. Na iyinom na hom nkyir no wɔ ntumbowa mu; mma wonndzi, wɔyɛ ekyiwadze: ɔkɔr, nye ɔkɔrbia, nye nsukɔr;
    12. na ɔsansaa, nye ɔkom n'ahorow;
    13. akonkoran biara n'ahorow;
    14. nye sohor, nye anadwe akrɔma, nye po akokɔ, nye akrɔma n'ahorow;
    15. nye patubia, nye po akonkoran, nye patu kɛse;
    16. nye ambɛnpatu, nye nantwinoma, nye ɔpɛtɛ:
    17. nye esukɔnkɔn, bakanoma n'ahorow, nye esuakokɔ, nye apan.
    18. Ntumbowa ahorow a wɔnantsew anan anan anan do nyinara yɛ ekyiwadze ma hom.
    19. Naaso iyinom na hom ndzi no wɔ ntumbowa menyamenya a wɔnantsew anan anan anan do nyinara mu, hɔn a wɔwɔ aperɛ wɔ hɔn anan do a wɔdze huruw wɔ asaase do no, LEV.11.22, mekyerɛ hɔn mu iyinom na hom ndzi; mbɛbɛw n'ahorow, akyekyemawe n'ahorow, kukura n'ahorow, ɔhwedɔm n'ahorow.
    20. Na ntumbowa menyamenya nyinara a wɔwɔ anan anan anan no, yɛ ekyiwadze ma hom.
    21. Na iyinom na hom dze gu homho fi: obiara a ɔka hɔn fun no, no ho nntsew ara kodu ewimbir:
    22. Na obiara a ɔsoa hɔn fun no ɔnhor ne ntar, osiandɛ no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir.
    23. Anansabowa biara a wɔapaa n'anansa mu, na ɔnnyɛ anansapaae koraa, na ɔnnkosa adzepuwii no, no ho nntsew mma hom; obiara a ɔka hɔn no, no ho nntsew.
    24. Na abowa biara a ɔnantsew no werɛw ano no wɔ mbowa a wɔnantsew anan anan do nyinara mu no, hɔn ho nntsew mma hom: obiara a ɔka hɔn fun no, no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir.
    25. Na nyia ɔsoa hɔn fun no ɔnhor ne ntar, na no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir: hɔn ho nntsew mma hom.
    26. Na iyinom nye hɔn a hɔn ho nntsew mma hom wɔ aweaweambowa a wɔwea wɔ asaase do no mu; obirebi, nye okura, nye ɔketsew n'ahorow, LEV.11.30, nye ban yer abamba, nye ɔmampam, nye ɔsamanapa, nye anhweaketsew, nye abosomanketsew.
    27. Iyinom nye hɔn a hɔn ho nntsew mma hom wɔ dza wɔwea nyinara mu: sɛ wowu a, obiara a ɔka hɔn no, no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir.
    28. Na adze biara a hɔn mu dza oewu atɔ do no, no ho nntsew; dua adze o, atar o, nwoma o, bɔtɔ o, adze biara a wɔdze yɛ edwuma biara, wɔmfa nhyɛ nsu mu, na no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir; ansaana no ho atsew.
    29. Na dɛtsesɛn biara a hɔn mu bi bɔtɔ mu no, adze biara a ɔwɔ mu no no ho nntsew, na sɛn no hom mbɔ no.
    30. No mu edziban a wodzi biara a nsu bogu mu no, no ho nntsew: na anondze nyinara a wɔnom wɔ dɛm adze biara mu no, no ho nntsew:
    31. Na adze biara a hɔn fun no bi bɔtɔ do no no ho nntsew; fononoo o, mbukyia o, wombubu no esinesin: hɔn ho nntsew, na hɔn ho nntsew mma hom.
    32. Naaso nsubura anaa amona a nsu egyina mu no dze, no ho tsew: mbom dza ɔka hɔn fun no, no ho nntsew.
    33. Na sɛ hɔn fun bi tɔ ekuadze a woedua biara do a, no ho tsew.
    34. Mbom sɛ wɔdze nsu gu ekuadze no do a, na hɔn fun bi tɔ do a, ekuadze no ho nntsew mma hom.
    35. Na sɛ abowa biara a hom dzi wu a, nyia ɔka no fun no, no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir.
    36. Na nyia odzi no fun bi no, ɔnhor ne ntar, na no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir: onyia so a ɔsoa no fun no, nhor ne ntar, na no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir.
    37. Na aweaweabowa biara a ɔwea wɔ asaase do no, yɛ ekyiwadze; mma wonndzi.
    38. Adze biara a ɔnam ɔyafun do, nye adze biara a ɔnam anan anan do, anaa adze biara a ɔwɔ anan pii, mekyerɛ aweaweambowa a wɔwea wɔ asaase do nyinara, mma hom nndzi; na wɔyɛ ekyiwadze.
    39. Mma hom nnyɛ homho ekyiwadze wɔ aweaweabowa biara a ɔwea ho, nkyii so mma hom nnyɛ homho fi a ɔmma hom ho nntsew wɔ hɔn ho.
    40. Na emi nye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn: dɛm ntsi hom ntsew homho, na hom nyɛ krɔnkrɔn; osiandɛ meyɛ krɔnkrɔn. Nkyii so mma hom nngu homho fi wɔ aweaweabowa biara a otwuw noho adze wɔ asaase do no ho.
    41. Na emi nye EWURADZE a miyii hom fii Egypt asaase do bae, dɛ meyɛ hom Nyankopɔn no; dɛm ntsi hom nyɛ krɔnkrɔn, na meyɛ krɔnkrɔn.
    42. Iyi nye anansabowa, nye etubowa, nye abowa biara a ɔwɔ nsu mu na abowa biara a ɔwea wɔ asaase do no ho mbra:
    43. ma wɔdze apa adze a no ho nntsew nye dza no ho tsew mu, na adze a nkwa wɔ mu a wodzi nye dza nkwa wɔ mu a wonndzi mu.

    Tsir 12

    1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.12.2, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba dɛ, Sɛ ɔbaa bi nyinsen na ɔwo banyimba a, ɔno no ho nntsew araa nda esuon; dɛ mbrɛ no ho nntsew wɔ n'asabu nda no mu no.
    2. Na da a otsia awɔtwe no wontwa ne banyinwer no twatsia.
    3. Na ɔnko ntandan mu adaduasa nda ebiasa wɔ n'ahoguar bɔgya mu; mma ɔnnka adzekrɔnkrɔn biara, nkyii so ɔmmba krɔnkrɔn bea, kyerɛ n'ahoguar nda ewie du ana.
    4. Mbom sɛ ɔwo basiaba a, ɔno no ho nntsew araa adapɛn ebien, dɛ n'asabu mu: na ɔnko ntandan mu adaduosia nda esia wɔ n'ahoguar bɔgya mu.
    5. Na sɛ n'ahoguar nda no wie du, ma ɔbanyin, anaa ɔbabaa a, ɔmfa eguambaa a oedzi afrenhyia a wɔdze bɔbɔ ɔhyew afɔr, nye aborɔnoma ba, anaa atamram a wɔdze bɔbɔ bɔn ho afɔr, mbra ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano mbɛma ɔsɔfo no:
    6. na ɔmfa mbɔ afɔr wɔ EWURADZE enyim, na ɔmpata mma no; na no ho ntsew wɔ no bɔgyaagu no ho. Iyi nye mbra a ɔwɔ hɔ ma nyia ɔwo ɔbanyin anaa ɔbaa.
    7. Na sɛ ne nsa annso eguambaa do a, ɔno ɔmfa ntamram ebien; kor nyɛ ɔhyew afɔr, na kor so nyɛ bɔn ho afɔr: na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma no, na no ho ntsew.

    Tsir 13

    1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses nye Aaron dɛ, LEV.13.2, Sɛ nyimpa bi nya no honam wer mu biribi soaa, anaa ntwintwamee, anaa ntokowa ntokowa, anaa odzidzi no honam wer mu yɛ kwata ntsetsewee a, ɔno wɔmfa no mbra ɔsɔfo Aaron, anaa ne mbabanyin asɔfo no mu kor:
    2. na ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ honam wer no mu: na sɛ nhwi a ɔwɔ ntsetsewee no mu adan fitaa na ntsetsewee no enyi atotɔ mu asen no honam wer no a, ɔyɛ kwata ntsetsewee; na ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ no, na ɔnka dɛ no ho nntsew.
    3. Na sɛ ntokowa ntokowa no yɛ fitaa wɔ no honam wer no mu, na n'enyi nntotɔɔ mu nnsen wer no, na no nhwi no nndaan fitaa a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no ntow ɔyarfo no mu nda esuon:
    4. na da a otsia esuon no ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ no: na ɔhwɛ na sɛ ntsetsewee no esianka wɔ n'enyi do, na ntsetsewee no nntserɛwee wɔ wer no mu a, ɔno asɔfo no ntow no mu nda esuon bio:
    5. na da a otsia esuon no ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ no bio: na, hwɛ, sɛ ntsetsewee no enyi edum, na ntsetsewee no nntserɛwee wɔ wer no mu a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho tsew: ɔyɛ ntwintwamee: na ɔnhor ne ntar, na no ho ntsew.
    6. Mbom ɔdze noho akɛkyerɛ ɔsɔfo no wɔ n'ahotsewee ho ekyir no, na sɛ ntwintwamee no tserɛw wɔ wer no mu a, ɔmfa no ho nkɛkyerɛ ɔsɔfo no bio:
    7. na sɛ ɔsɔfo no hwɛ na sɛ ntwintwamee no atserɛw wɔ wer no mu a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho nntsew: ɔyɛ kwata.
    8. Sɛ kwata ntsetsewee wɔ nyimpa bi ho a, ɔno wɔmfa no mbra ɔsɔfo no;
    9. na ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ, na, hwɛ, sɛ biribi fitaa soaa bi wɔ wer no mu, na ɔadan nhwi no fitaa, na honam momon rupuw wɔ soaa no mu a, LEV.13.11, ɔyɛ kwata dadaw a ɔwɔ no honam wer no mu, na ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho nntsew: mma ɔnntow no mu: na no ho nntsew.
    10. Na sɛ kwata no epue efi wer no mu, na kwata no akata owuyarfo no no wer nyinara ho fi n'etsifi keka n'ananadze dɛ mbrɛ ɔda edzi ma ɔsɔfo no ara a;
    11. ɔno ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ: na, hwɛ, sɛ kwata no akata no honam nyinara do a, ɔnka dɛ owuyarfo no no ho tsew: ne nyinara adan fitaa: no ho tsew.
    12. Mbom aber biara a honam momon ada edzi wɔ no ho no, no ho nntsew.
    13. Na ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ honam momon no, na ɔnka dɛ no ho nntsew: honam momon no ho nntsew: ɔyɛ kwata.
    14. Anaa sɛ honam momon no dan fitaa bio a, ɔno ɔmbra ɔsɔfo no hɔ, LEV.13.17, na ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ no: na, hwɛ, sɛ ntsetsewee no dan fitaa a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ ɔyarfo no no ho tsew: na no ho atsew.
    15. Na sɛ pɔmpɔ bi bɔ wɔ honam no wer no mu, na ɔsa, LEV.13.19, na soaa fitaa bi bɔsoa anaa ntokowa ntokowa fitaa, ɔberhoamu, ba pɔmpɔ no ananmu a, ɔno wɔmfa nkɛkyerɛ asɔfo no;
    16. na ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ, na, hwɛ, sɛ n'enyi atotɔ mu asen wer no; na no nhwi no adan fitaa a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho nntsew: ɔyɛ kwata ntsetsewee, oepue wɔ pɔmpɔ no mu.
    17. Mbom sɛ ɔsɔfo no hwɛ, na sɛ, nhwi fitaa bi nnyi mu, na ɔnntotɔ mu nnsen wer no, na n'enyi dum a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no ntow no mu nda esuon:
    18. na sɛ ɔtserɛw wɔ wer no mu a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho nntsew: ɔyɛ ntsetsewee.
    19. Mbom sɛ ntokowa ntokowa no sianka n'ananmu, na ɔanntserɛw a, ɔyɛ pɔmpɔ no itua; na ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho tsew.
    20. Anaa sɛ gya hyew ano tua bi ba honam no wer no mu, na ɔhyew ano tua no do rupuw yɛ ntokowa ntokowa, ɔberhoamu, anaa fitaa a;
    21. ɔno ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ: na, hwɛ, sɛ nhwi a ɔwɔ ntokowa ntokowa no do adan fitaa, na n'enyi atotɔ mu asen wer no a; ɔyɛ kwata, oepue wɔ ɔhyew ano tua no mu: na ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho nntsew: ɔyɛ kwata ntsetsewee.
    22. Mbom sɛ ɔsɔfo no hwɛ, na sɛ nhwi fitaa bi nnyi ntokowa ntokowa no mu, na ɔnntotɔɔ mu nnseen wer no, na n'enyi dum a: ɔno ɔsɔfo no ntow no mu nda esuon:
    23. na da a otsia esuon no ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ no: sɛ ɔtserɛw wɔ wer no mu a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho nntsew: ɔyɛ kwata ntsetsewee.
    24. Na sɛ ntokowa ntokowa no sianka wɔ n'ananmu, na ɔanntserɛw wɔ wer no mu, na n'enyi dum a; ɔyɛ ɔhyew ano tua soaa, na ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho tsew: na ɔyɛ ɔhyew ano tua no itua.
    25. Na sɛ ɔbanyin anaa ɔbaa bi nya ntsetsewee wɔ n'etsifi anaa n'abɔdwesa mu a, LEV.13.30, ɔno ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ ntsetsewee no: na, hwɛ, sɛ n'enyi atotɔ mu asen wer no, na nhwi memen tseatseaba bi wɔ mu a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho nntsew: ɔyɛ ntutui, ɔyɛ tsir anaa abɔdwesa mu kwata.
    26. Na sɛ ɔsɔfo no hwɛ ntutui ne ntsetsewee no, na, hwɛ, n'enyi nntotɔ mu nnsen wer no, na nhwi tuntum bi nnyi mu a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no ntow ntutui ɔyarfo no mu nda esuon:
    27. na da a otsia esuon no ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ ntsetsewee no; na, hwɛ, sɛ ntutui no nntserɛwee, na nhwi memen bi nnyi mu, na ntutui no n'enyim nntotɔ mu nnsen wer no a, LEV.13.33, ɔno wɔntwerɛw nhwi no, mbom ntutui no na mma wɔnntwerɛw; na ɔsɔfo no ntow nyia ɔyar ntutui no mu nda esuon bio:
    28. na da a otsia esuon no mu ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ ntutui no: na, hwɛ, sɛ ntutui no nntserɛw wer no do, na n'enyi nntotɔ mu nnsen wer no a; ɔno ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho tsew: na ɔnhor ne ntar, na no ho ntsew.
    29. Mbom n'ahotsewee ekyir no sɛ ntutui no tserɛw wɔ wer no mu a;
    30. ɔno ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ no; na, hwɛ, sɛ ntutui no atserɛw wɔ wer no mu a, mma ɔsɔfo no nnhwehwɛ nhwi memen no; no ho nntsew.
    31. Mbom sɛ ntutui no yɛ n'enyiwa do dɛ oesianka, na nhwi tuntum efuw wɔ mu a; ntutui no asa, no ho atsew: na ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho atsew.
    32. Na sɛ ɔbanyin anaa ɔbaa nya ntokowa ntokowa fitaa wɔ no honam wer mu a;
    33. ɔno ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ: na, hwɛ, sɛ ntokowa ntokowa a n'enyi dum fitaa na ɔwɔ hɔn honam wer mu a; ɔyɛ ayam na oepue wɔ wer no mu; no ho tsew.
    34. Na sɛ ɔbanyin bi no nhwi pepa wɔ n'etsifi a, ɔyɛ otsipaa; naaso no ho tsew.
    35. Na sɛ no nhwi pepa wɔ n'etsifi enyim afamu a, ɔyɛ pampamu tsipaa; na no ho tsew.
    36. Mbom sɛ ntsetsewee ɔberhoamu bi tow ne tsipaa no mu, anaa ne pampamu paa no mu a; ɔyɛ kwata a orupue ne tsipaa, anaa ne pampamu paa no mu.
    37. Nkyii ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ no: na sɛ ntsetsewee no soaa no yɛ ɔberhoamu wɔ ne tsipaa no mu, anaa ne pampamu paa no mu, dɛ mbrɛ kwata firii tse wɔ honam wer no mu a;
    38. ɔyɛ ɔkwatanyi, no ho nntsew: ka ara na ɔsɔfo no nka dɛ no ho nntsew; ne ntsetsewee wɔ n'etsifi.
    39. Na ɔkwatanyi a ɔyar no wɔ no ho no, nsuansuan ne ntar mu, na ɔmbɔ ne tsinhwi mpɛsɛw, na ɔnkata n'anofamfa sor no, na ɔntsea mu dɛ, Ɔho nntsew o, ɔho nntsew o.
    40. Nda dodow a yar no wɔ no ho no nyinara no ho renntsew; no ho nntsew: noara nkotoo ntsena; nseraban no ekyir na ɔnyɛ ne tsenabew.
    41. Atar so a kwata ntsetsewee wɔ mu no, sɛ ɔyɛ kuntu atar o, ndwera atar o;
    42. sɛ ndwera, anaa kuntu, ne nsa anaa ne mfa mu o; sɛ nwoma, anaa adze biara a wɔdze nwoma ayɛ mu o;
    43. sɛ ntsetsewee no yɛ ebunebun anaa ɔberber wɔ atar no mu, anaa nwoma no mu, anaa nsa no mu, anaa mfa no mu, anaa nwoma adze biara mu a; ɔyɛ kwata ntsetsewee, na wɔmfa nkɛkyerɛ ɔsɔfo no:
    44. na ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ ntsetsewee no, na ɔntow ntsetsewee adze no mu nda esuon:
    45. na da a otsia esuon no ɔnhwɛ ntsetsewee no: sɛ ntsetsewee no atserɛw wɔ atar no mu, anaa nsa no mu, anaa mfa no mu, anaa nwoma no mu, anaa biribiara a wɔdze nwoma ayɛ no mu a; ntsetsewee no yɛ kwata a ɔwewe; no ho nntsew.
    46. Na ɔnhyew atar no, sɛ nsa no o, mfa no o, wɔ kuntu mu, anaa ndwera mu, anaa nwoma adze biara a ntsetsewee no wɔ mu no: na ɔyɛ kwata a ɔwewe; wɔnhyew no wɔ gya mu.
    47. Na sɛ ɔsɔfo no hwɛ, na, hwɛ, ntsetsewee no nntserɛw wɔ atar no mu, wɔ nsa no mu, anaa mfa no mu, anaa nwoma adze biara mu a;
    48. ɔno ɔsɔfo no nhyɛ ma wɔnhor adze no a ntsetsewee no wɔ mu no, na ɔntow no mu nda esuon bio:
    49. na ɔahor ntsetsewee no ekyir no, ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ: na sɛ ntsetsewee no nnsakyeer n'enyi, na ntsetsewee no nntserɛwee a, no ho nntsew; dza atow mu no wɔ mu o, ekyir o hyew no wɔ ogya mu: osiandɛ ɔyɛ nwewee.
    50. Na sɛ ɔsɔfo no hwɛ, na, hwɛ, ntsetsewee no no hor ekyir no n'enyi edum a, ɔno ɔntsew mfi atar no ho, anaa nwoma no ho, anaa nsa no ho, anaa mfa no ho:
    51. Na sɛ ɔda ho pue wɔ atar no mu, anaa nsa no mu, anaa mfa no mu, anaa nwoma adze biara no mu a, na orupue: fa gya hyew dza ntsetsewee no wɔ mu no.
    52. Na atar no, anaa nsa no, anaa mfa no, anaa nwoma adze biara a ebɔhor no, sɛ ntsetsewee no fi mu a, ɔno wɔnhor bio, na no ho atsew.
    53. Iyi nye mbra a ɔwɔ hɔ ma kwata ntsetsewee wɔ kuntu anaa ndwera atar ho, wɔ nsa anaa mfa, anaa nwoma adze biara mu, ma wɔdze aaka dɛ no ho tsew, anaa wɔdze aaka dɛ no ho nntsew.

    Tsir 14

    1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.14.2, Iyi nye mbra a ɔwɔ hɔ ma ɔkwatanyi wɔ n'ahotsewee da no mu: wɔmfa no mbra ɔsɔfo no:
    2. na ɔsɔfo no mfir edzi nkɔ nseraban no ekyir: na ɔsɔfo no nhwɛ, na, hwɛ, sɛ kwata ntsetsewee no asa wɔ ɔkwatanyi no mu a;
    3. ɔno ɔsɔfo no nhyɛ ma wɔmfa nnoma enyikan a hɔn ho tsew ebien, nye kyindur dua, nye serdaa, nye odwonhataw mma nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho no mbra:
    4. na ɔsɔfo no nhyɛ mma wonku nnoma no mu kor wɔ dɛtsesɛn bi mu wɔ mbasu do:
    5. anoma nyikan no dze, ɔmfa ɔno, nye kyindur dua no, nye serdaa no, nye odwonhataw no, na ɔmfa ne nyinara nye anoma nyikan no mbɔ anoma a wokum no wɔ mbasu do no no bɔgyaa mu:
    6. na ɔmfa mpetse nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho efi kwata ho no do mpɛn esuon, na ɔnka dɛ no ho atsew, na ongyaa anoma nyikan no ma ɔnkɔ haban mu.
    7. Na nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho no, nhor ne ntar, na onyi no nhwi nyinara, na onguar nsu mu, na no ho aatsew: na n'ekyir no ɔmbra nseraban no mu dze, na ɔntsena no buw ekyir nda esuon.
    8. Na ɔmbra dɛ da a otsia esuon no, na onyi no nhwi nyinara wɔ n'etsifi nye n'abɔdwesa nye no ntɔn, mekyerɛ no nhwi nyinara na onyi: na ɔnhor ne ntar, na onguar no honam wɔ nsu mu, na no ho aatsew.
    9. Na da a otsia awɔtwe no ɔmfa nguambaanyin ebien a wonndzii dzɛm, nye nguambaaber kor a oedzi afrenhyia a onndzii dzɛm, na edziban afɔr isiam pa efa ebupɛn du mu ebiasa a wɔdze ngo aforɔw, na ngo toa kor, mbra.
    10. Na ɔsɔfo no a ɔtsew no ho no mfa ɔbanyin a wɔrebɛtsew no ho no, nye ndzɛmba no, mbegyina EWURADZE enyim, wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano:
    11. na ɔsɔfo no mfa nguambaanyin no mu kor, na ɔmfa no mbra dɛ asodzi ho afɔr, nye ngo toa no, na ɔntow no ɔtow afɔr wɔ EWURADZE enyim:
    12. na onku eguambaanyin no wɔ beebi mbrɛ woku bɔn ho afɔr nye ɔhyew afɔr abowa no, wɔ ahotseweebea no do hɔ: na dɛ mbrɛ bɔn ho afɔr no yɛ ɔsɔfo no dze no, dɛmara na asodzi ho afɔr no so: ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn:
    13. na ɔsɔfo no mfa asodzi ho afɔr bɔgyaa no bi, na ɔsɔfo no mfa nkeka nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho no n'aso nyimfa ano, nye ne nsa nyimfa kokrobetsir, nye ne nan nyimfa kokonyin ano:
    14. na ɔsɔfo no mfa ngo toa no, na onhue bi ngu nankasa ne nsa bankum mu:
    15. na ɔsɔfo no mfa ne nsatseaba nyimfa mbɔ ngo no a ɔwɔ ne nsa bankum mu no mu, na ɔmfa ne nsatseaba mpetse ngo no bi mpɛn esuon wɔ EWURADZE enyim:
    16. na ngo no nkaa no a ɔwɔ ne nsamu no ɔsɔfo no mfa bi nkeka nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho no n'aso nyimfa ano, nye ne nsa nyimfa kokrobetsir, nye ne nan nyimfa kokonyin ano, wɔ asodzi ho afɔr bɔgyaa no do:
    17. na ngo nkaa no a ɔwɔ ɔsɔfo ne nsamu no ɔmfa ngu nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho no etsifi: na ɔsɔfo mpata mma no wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
    18. Na ɔsɔfo no mbɔ bɔn ho afɔr no, na ɔmpata mma nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho wɔ n'ahoɔfi ntsi; na n'ekyir no onku hyew afɔr abowa no:
    19. na ɔsɔfo no mbɔ hyew afɔr no nye edziban afɔr no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do: na ɔsɔfo no mpata mma no, na no ho ntsew.
    20. Na sɛ odzi hia, na onnkotum enya no dodow dɛm a, ɔno ɔmfa eguambaanyin kor mbɛyɛ asodzi ho afɔr mma wɔntow mfa mpata mma no, na edziban afɔr isiam pa efa ebupɛn du mu kor a wɔdze ngo aforɔw, na ngo toa;
    21. na ntamram ebien, anaa mborɔnoma mba ebien, dɛ mbrɛ ne nsa bɔso do ara; na kor nyɛ bɔn ho afɔr, na kor so, nyɛ ɔhyew afɔr.
    22. Na da a otsia awɔtwe no ɔmfa mbrɛ ɔsɔfo no, wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano, wɔ EWURADZE enyim, wɔ n'ahoguar ntsi.
    23. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa asodzi ho afɔr eguambaa no, nye ngo toa no, na ɔsɔfo no ntow no nyɛ ɔtow afɔr wɔ EWURADZE enyim:
    24. na onku asodzi ho afɔr eguambaa no, na ɔsɔfo no mfa asodzi no afɔr bɔgyaa no bi, na ɔmfa nkeka nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho no n'aso nyimfa ano, nye ne nsa nyimfa kokrobetsir, nye ne nan nyimfa kokonyin ano:
    25. na ɔsɔfo no nhue ngo no bi ngu nankasa ne nsa bankum mu:
    26. na ɔsɔfo no mfa ne nsatseaba nyimfa mfa ngo no a ɔwɔ ne nsa bankum mu no bi mpetse mpɛn esuon wɔ EWURADZE enyim:
    27. na ɔsɔfo no mfa ngo no a ɔwɔ ne nsamu no bi nkeka nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho no n'aso nyimfa ano, nye ne nsa nyimfa kokrobetsir, nye ne nan nyimfa kokonyin ano wɔ mbrɛ asodzi ho afɔr bɔgyaa no wɔ no:
    28. na ngo no ne nkaa no a ɔwɔ ɔsɔfo no nsamu no ɔmfa ngu nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho no etsifi, mfa nyɛ mpata mma no wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
    29. Na ɔmfa ntamram anaa mborɔnomamba no mu kor, dɛ mbrɛ ne nsa bɔso do no ara mbra;
    30. mekyerɛ dɛ mbrɛ ne nsa bɔso do no ara, kor nyɛ bɔn ho afɔr, na kor so nyɛ ɔhyew afɔr nka edziban afɔr no ho: na ɔsɔfo no mpata nyia wɔrebɛtsew no ho no wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
    31. Iyi nye mbra a ɔwɔ hɔ ma nyia kwata ntsetsewee aka no a ne nsa nnso n'ahoguar adze do no.
    32. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses nye Aaron dɛ, LEV.14.34, Sɛ hom du Keenan asaase a medze rema hom dɛ enyadze no do, na sɛ medze kwata ntsetsewee mobɔto hom enyadze asaase do fie bi mu a;
    33. ɔno fie no wura no mbra, na ɔmbɛka nkyerɛ ɔsɔfo no dɛ, Ɔyɛ me dɛ biribi tse dɛ ntsetsewee bi esi fie no mu hɔ:
    34. na ɔsɔfo no nhyɛ ma wonyi fie no mu ndzɛmba nyinara, ansaana ɔsɔfo no akɔ mu akɔhwɛ ntsetsewee no, amma dza ɔwɔ fie no mu nyinara ho eenngu fi: na n'ekyir no ɔsɔfo no nkɔ mu nkɔhwɛ fie no:
    35. na ɔnhwɛ ntsetsewee no, na, hwɛ, sɛ ntsetsewee no wɔ fie no ne fadum ho ntokurntokur mu nkekaa ebunebun anaa mberberee, na n'enyi atotɔ mu asen ɔfadum no a;
    36. ɔno ɔsɔfo no mfi fie no mu mpue fie no n'abowano, na ɔntow fie no mu nda esuon:

    49. na ɔmfa kyindur dua no, nye odwonhataw no, nye serdaa no, nye anoma nyikan no, na ɔmfa anoma a woeku no no no bɔgyaa mu, nye mbasu no mu, na ɔmfa mpetse fie no do mpɛn esuon:
    50. na ɔmfa anoma no no bɔgyaa, nye mbasu no, nye anoma nyikan no, nye kyindur dua no, nye odwonhataw no, nye serdaa no, ntsew fie no ho:

    Tsir 16

    1. Na Aaron ne mbabanyin beenu no hɔn wu a wɔtwee bɛɛn EWURADZE enyim, na wowuwui no ekyir no, EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses;
    2. na EWURADZE see Moses dɛ, Kasa kyerɛ wo nua Aaron dɛ, Mma ɔnnhɛn bea krɔnkrɔn no mu nnkɔ ntwamutam no mu, wɔ mbɔbɔrhu egua no a ɔwɔ adaka no do no enyim aber nyinara; amma woeennwuwu: na mununkum no mu na mibeyi moho akyerɛ wɔ mbɔbɔrhu egua no do.
    3. Iyi na Aaron mfa mbɛhɛn bea krɔnkrɔn no: ɔnantwisaa eburwa a wɔdze bɔbɔ bɔn ho afɔr, nye oguannyin a wɔdze bɔbɔ ɔhyew afɔr.
    4. Ɔnhyɛ ndwera sortar krɔnkrɔn no, na ndwera ntwontwo no nkata no ho, na ndwera abɔyamu no mbɔ do, na ɔnhyɛ ndwera asɔfokyɛw no: ntar krɔnkrɔn no nye no; onguar no honam wɔ nsu mu, na ɔnhyɛ.
    5. Na ɔngye Israelmba mboanofo no hɔ mpɔnkyenyin ebien mfa mbɔ bɔn ho afɔr, na oguannyin kor mfa mbɔ ɔhyew afɔr.
    6. Na Aaron mfa bɔn ho afɔr nantwisaa no a ɔyɛ nankasa nedze no, mbra, na ɔmfa mpata mma nankasa noho nye ne fie.
    7. Na ɔmfa mpɔnkye ebien no mbegyina EWURADZE enyim wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano.
    8. Na Aaron ntwe mpɔnkye ebien no ho tonto; tonto kor nyɛ EWURADZE dze, na tonto kor so nyɛ Azazel dze.
    9. Na Aaron mfa apɔnkye a EWURADZE tonto bɔɔ no no mbra, na ɔmfa mbɔbɔ bɔn ho afɔr.
    10. Apɔnkye a Azazel tonto bɔɔ no no dze, ɔmfa no enyikan mbegyina EWURADZE enyim, mfa mpata mma no, dɛ wokegyaa no ma Azazel ma ɔkɔ sar no do.
    11. Na Aaron mfa bɔn ho afɔr nantwisaa no a ɔyɛ nankasa nedze no mbra, ma ɔmfa mpata mma nankasa noho, nye ne fi, na onku bɔn ho afɔr nantwisaa no a ɔyɛ nankasa nedze no:
    12. na ɔmfa eduhuamsɛn gyabiriw mfi afɔrmbukyia no do wɔ EWURADZE enyim, na eduhuam a woesiw no mokomoko ne nsa ma, na ɔmfa mbra ntwamutam no mu:
    13. na ɔmfa eduhuam no ngu gya no do wɔ EWURADZE enyim, ama eduhuam no no wusiw aakata mbɔbɔrhu egua no a ɔwɔ dase no do no ho, ma oeennwu:
    14. na ɔmfa nantwisaa no ho bɔgyaa bi, na ɔmfa ne nsatseaba nka mpetse mbɔbɔrhu egua no do wɔ boka afamu; na ɔmfa ne nsatseaba nka bɔgya no bi mpetse mbɔbɔrhu egua no enyim mpɛn esuon.
    15. Nkyii onku bɔn ho afɔr apɔnkye no a ɔyɛ ɔman no dze no, na ɔmfa no bɔgyaa no mbra ntwamutam no mu, na ɔmfa no bɔgyaa no nyɛ dɛ mbrɛ ɔdze nantwisaa no no bɔgyaa yɛe no, na ɔmfa mpetse mbɔbɔrhu egua no do, na mbɔbɔrhu egua no enyim:
    16. na ɔmfa mpata mma bea krɔnkrɔn no, wɔ Israelmba hɔn efifidze, nye hɔn mbrato ntsi, mekyerɛ hɔn bɔn nyinara ho; na dɛm na ɔnyɛ mma ehyiadzibuw no a osi hɔn mu no ɔnam hɔn efifidze no ntsi.
    17. Na mma nyimpa biara nntsena ehyiadzibuw no mu aber a ɔrokɔ mu akɛpata wɔ bea krɔnkrɔn no araa nkodu dɛ obefir edzi, na ɔapata ama nankasa noho, nye ne fifo, nye Israel bagua no nyinara.
    18. Na omfir edzi nkɔ afɔrmbukyia no a ɔwɔ EWURADZE enyim no ho, na ɔmpata mma no; na ɔmfa nantwisaa no no bɔgyaa no bi, nye apɔnkye no no bɔgyaa no bi na ɔmfa nkeka afɔrmbukyia no ne mmbɛn ho nhyia.
    19. Na ɔmfa ne nsatseaba nka bɔgya no bi mpetse do mpɛn esuon, na ɔntsew ho, na ɔnyɛ no krɔnkrɔn mfi Israelmba hɔn efifidze no mu.
    20. Na sɛ owie mpata ma bea krɔnkrɔn no, nye ehyiadzibuw no, nye afɔrmbukyia no a, ɔmfa apɔnkye nyikan no mbra:
    21. na Aaron mfa ne nsa ebien nyinara ngu apɔnkye nyikan no etsifi, na ɔnka Israelmba hɔn emumuyɛ nyinara, nye hɔn mbrato nyinara, mekyerɛ hɔn bɔn nyinara, ngu apɔnkye no etsifi, na ɔmfa no nhyɛ ɔbanyin a woeyi no esi hɔ no nsa ma onkegyaa no ma ɔnkɔ sar no do:
    22. na apɔnkye no nsoa hɔn emumuyɛ nyinara mfa nkɔ.
    23. Na Aaron mbra ehyiadzibuw no mu, na onyi ndwera ntar no a ɔhyɛe kɔr bea krɔnkrɔn no, na ongyaa no wɔ hɔ:
    24. na onguar wɔ nsu mu wɔ beebi a ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn, na ɔnhyɛ ne ntar, na omfir edzi, na ɔnkɔbɔ ne hyew afɔr nye ɔman no hɔn hyew afɔr no, na ɔmpata mma nankasa noho, nye ɔman no.
    25. Na bɔn ho afɔr seradze no ɔnhyew no wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do.
    26. Na nyia okegyaa apɔnkye no ma Azazel no ɔnhor ne ntar, na onguar no honam wɔ nsu mu, na n'ekyir no ɔmbra nseraban no mu.
    27. Na bɔn ho afɔr nantwisaa no, nye bɔn ho afɔr apɔnkye a wɔdze hɔn bɔgyaa baa bɛpataa wɔ bea krɔnkrɔn no, wɔmfa nkɔ nseraban no ekyir; na wɔnhyew hɔn nwoma, nye hɔn nam, nye hɔn egyanan wɔ gya mu.
    28. Na nyia ɔhyew no no nhor ne ntar, na onguar wɔ nsu mu, na n'ekyir no ɔmbra nseraban no mu.
    29. Na ɔyɛ daa ahyɛdze ma hom: bosoom a otsia esuon no mu no, bosoom no ne da a otsia du no, hom mbrɛ homho adze, na mma wɔnnyɛ edwuma biara, okuromnyi o, ɔhɔho a ɔbɔsoɛ hom mu o
    30. na da yi mu na wɔmpata mma hom, mfa ntsew homho; ama hom ho aatsew efi hom bɔn nyinara mu wɔ EWURADZE enyim.
    31. Ɔyɛ ahomgye mu ahomgye afe ma hom, na hom mbrɛ homho adze; ɔyɛ daa ahyɛdze.
    32. Na ɔsɔfo a wɔasera no ngo a woetu no esi nkyɛn dɛ ondzi sɔfo n'egya n'ananmu no mpata, na ɔnhyɛ ndwera ntar no, mekyerɛ ntar krɔnkrɔn no;
    33. na ɔmpata mma ahotseweebea krɔnkrɔn no, na ɔmpata mma ehyiadzibuw no nye afɔrmbukyia no, na ɔmpata mma ɔsɔfo no nye ɔman baguafo no nyinara.
    34. Na iyi nyɛ daa ahyɛdze mma hom, mfa mpata mma Israelmba wɔ hɔn bɔn nyinara ntsi prɛko wɔ afrenhyia mu. Na ɔyɛɛ dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.

    Tsir 17

    1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.17.2, Kasa kyerɛ Aaron, nye ne mbabanyin no, nye Israelmba nyinara, na se hɔn dɛ: Iyi nye adze a EWURADZE ahyɛ ho mbra no dɛ, LEV.17.3, Nyimpa biara a ofi Israel fie mu a oku nantwinyin, anaa eguambaa, anaa apɔnkye wɔ nseraban no mu, anaa nseraban no ekyir dɛ ayɛyɛdze a ɔdze rebrɛ EWURADZE
    2. na ɔmmfa no mmba ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano wɔ EWURADZE ntamadan no enyim no: wobobu bɔgya no egu nyimpa no do; ɔaka bɔgya egu; na dɛm nyimpa no wobotwa no efi ne man mu akyen:
    3. ama Israelmba dze hɔn afɔrbɔdze a woku no wɔ sar no do dɛ wɔdze rebrɛ EWURADZE no aaba ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano, dze abrɛ asɔfo no, na woeeku no abɔ asomdwee afɔr ama EWURADZE.
    4. Na ɔsɔfo no mfa bɔgya no mpetse EWURADZE afɔrmbukyia no do wɔ ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano, na ɔnhyew seradze ma onyi nka huanhuam mma EWURADZE.
    5. Na mma wonnku hɔn afɔrbɔdze mma esunsum bɔn a wodzi hɔn ekyir wɔ sar no do no bio. Iyi nyɛ daa ahyɛdze mma hɔn wɔ hɔn ebusuasantsen nyinara mu.
    6. Na ɔwo ka kyerɛ hɔn dɛ, Nyimpa biara a ofi Israel mu, anaa ofi ahɔho a wɔabɔsoɛ hɔn mu no mu a ɔbɔ ɔhyew afɔr anaa okum afɔr bi, LEV.17.9, na ɔmmfa mmba ehyiadzibuw no n'abowano, mmbɔbɔ mma EWURADZE no: wobotwa no efi ne man mu akyen.
    7. Na nyimpa biara a ofi Israel fie mu, anaa ofi ahɔho a wɔabɔsoɛ hɔn mu no mu a odzi bɔgya biara no; m'enyi bɛsa no, na mubotwa no efi ne man mu akyen.
    8. Osiandɛ honam no nkwa wɔ bɔgya no mu: na medze ama hom wɔ afɔrmbukyia no do dɛ wɔmfa mpata mma homho: na bɔgya no na ɔpata ma akra.
    9. Dɛm ntsi na mesee Israelmba dɛ, Mma hom mu ɔkra biara nndzi bɔgya, nkyii so mma ɔhɔho biara a ɔsoɛ hom mu nndzi bɔgya.

    12. Na nyimpa biara a odzi dza noara ewu no, anaa dza abowa atsetsew no no, sɛ ɔyɛ okuromnyi o, ɔhɔho o, ɔnhor ne ntar, na onguar wɔ nsu mu, osiandɛ no ho nntsew araa kodu ewimbir: ana afei no ho atsew.
    13. Mbom sɛ ɔannhor ne ntar, nkyii so oennguar a, ɔno ɔnsoa no mbusu.

    20. ɔno mebɛhyɛ me nhyira wɔ hom do wɔ afe a otsia awɔtwe no mu, na wondzi nduadzewa no bi, akoradze dadaw no;

    22. Na asaase no mma wɔnntɔn no korakora; osiandɛ asaase no yɛ medze: na hom yɛ ahɔho nye amamforafo wɔ me nkyɛn.
    23. Na hom enyadze asaase no nyinara do na hom mma asaase no no mponee ho kwan.
    24. Sɛ ohia wo nua, na ɔtɔn n'egyapadze mu bi a, ɔno no busuanyi a ɔbɛn no no mbra, na ɔmbɔpon dza no nua atɔn no.

    26. ɔno wɔmma ombu no ntɔnee mu mfe dodow ano, na ɔnhyɛ dza ɔbor do no ananmu mma nyimpa no a ɔtɔnee maa no no; na ɔnsan nkɔ n'egyapadze ho.
    27. Mbom sɛ orunntum nngye bio mma noho a, ɔno dza wɔatɔn no nko nyia ɔatɔ no nsamu nkodu ahosɛpɛw afe no: na ahosɛpɛw afe no mu na omfi hɔ, na ɔnsan nkɔ n'enyadze ho.
    28. Na sɛ nyimpa bi tɔn ofi a wɔtsena mu wɔ kurow a afadum wɔ ho mu a, ɔno ɔmpon no wɔ no ntɔnee ekyir afrenhyia no mu; na afrenhyia mu na ɔwɔ mponee ho kwan.
    29. Na sɛ wɔammpon no wɔ afrenhyia mu ntamu a, ɔno fie a ɔwɔ kurow a afadum wɔ ho mu no, ɔnko hɔ daa mma nyia ɔtɔe no korakora, wɔ n'ebusuasantsen mu: ommfi hɔ wɔ ahosɛpɛw afe no mu.
    30. Na efiefi a ɔwowɔ nkuroba a onnyi fadum a ehyia ho mu dze, wombu nka ɔman no mu haban ho: wɔropon a wɔmpon, na ahosɛpɛw afe no mu omfi hɔ.
    31. Naaso Levifo nkurow no, hɔn enyadze nkurow mu efi no dze, Levifo no mpon no aber biara.
    32. Na sɛ Levifo mu kor pon a, ɔno fie no a wɔtɔnee, nye n'enyadze kurow no mfi hɔ wɔ ahosɛpɛw afe no mu: na Levifo nkurow mu efiefi no nye hɔn enyadze wɔ Israelmba mu.
    33. Na nkurow no no kurotsia haban dze, mma wɔnntɔn, na ɔyɛ hɔn daa enyadze.
    34. Na sɛ ohia wo nua, na ɔpa abaw wɔ wo nkyɛn a; ɔno suo no mu; na ɔntsena wo nkyɛn dɛ ɔhɔho nye ɔmamforanyi.
    35. Mma nngye nsiho anaa ndɔree wɔ no hɔ; mbom suro wo Nyankopɔn: ma wo nua nye wo ntsena.
    36. Mma mmfa wo sika anaa w'edziban mma no nsiho do, wɔ no ndɔree ntsi.
    37. Emi nye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn a, miyii hom fii Egypt asaase do, dɛ medze Keenan asaase rema hom, na maayɛ hom Nyankopɔn.
    38. Na sɛ ohia wo nua wɔ wo nkyɛn, na ɔtɔn noho ma wo a; mma mma ɔnnsom wo dɛ ɔdɔnkɔnyi:
    39. na ɔnyɛ wo nkyɛn dɛ mbrɛ ɔpaanyi nye ɔmamforanyi tse; ɔnsom wɔ wo nkyɛn araa ma onkodu ahosɛpɛw afe no mu:
    40. na nkyii omfi wo nkyɛn nkɔ, ɔno nye ne mba a wɔwɔ wo nkyɛn, na ɔnsan nkɔ nankasa n'ebusua mu, na n'egyanom enyadze ho na ɔnsan nkɔ.
    41. Na wɔyɛ mo nkowaa a miyii hɔn fii Egypt asaase do: mma wɔnntɔn hɔn dɛ adɔnkɔfo.
    42. Mma mmfa egyapadze nnhyɛ no do; mbom suro wo Nyankopɔn.
    43. Na wo nkowaa nye wo mfonabaa a ibenya no; amanaman a woehyia hom ho no, hɔn mu na hom ntotɔ nkowaa nye mfonabaa.
    44. Nkyii so ahɔho a wɔabɔsoɛ hom mu hɔn mba no, hɔn hɔ na hom ntɔ, nye hɔn ebusuafo a wɔwɔ hom nkyɛn a wɔawo hɔn wɔ hom asaase do no hɔ: na wɔnyɛ hom egyapadze.
    45. Na hom nyɛ hɔn egyapadze mma hom mba wɔ hom ekyir, ma woeesuo mu ayɛ egyapadze; na hɔn mu na hom mfa hom adɔnkɔfo daa: hom nuanom Israelmba dze mma hom nnhyɛ hɔn do, mma obiara mmfa egyapadze do nnhyɛ no nua do.
    46. Na sɛ ɔhɔho anaa ɔmamforanyi bi a ɔwɔ hom nkyɛn nya adze, na ohia wo nua a ɔnye no wɔ hɔ no, na ɔtɔn noho ma ɔhɔho no anaa ɔmamforanyi no a ɔwɔ wo nkyɛn no, anaa ɔhɔho no n'ebusua mu nyimpa bi a:
    47. no ntɔnee ekyir no wɔmpon no; na no nuanom mu baako mpon no:
    48. anaa no wɔfa, anaa no wɔfa ne babanyin mpon no, anaa n'ebusua mu bɔgya biara a ɔbɛn mpon no; anaa sɛ oenya adze a, ɔmpon noho.
    49. Na ɔnye nyia ɔtɔ no no mbubu mfitsi afe a ɔtɔɔn noho maa no no do nkodu ahosɛpɛw afe no do: na no ntɔnee bo no nyɛ dɛ mbrɛ mfe no no dodow tse.
    50. Sɛ mfe no da ho dɔɔ so a, ne tsisika mu mponee bo no a ɔbɛdan ama no so ndɔɔ so dɛmara.
    51. Na sɛ aka mfe kumaabi ma edu ahosɛpɛw afe no do a, ɔno ɔnye no mbubu; dɛ mbrɛ ne mfe no tse no na ɔmfa no mponee sika no ndan mma.
    52. Ɔnye no ntsena dɛ akowaa a wɔbɔ no paa afrenhyia afrenhyia: mma ɔmmfa egyapadze do nnhyɛ no do wɔ w'enyim.
    53. Na sɛ woenndua iyinom do ammpon no a, ɔno omfir edzi nkɔ wɔ ahosɛpɛw afe no mu, ɔno nye ne mba nka no ho.
    54. Osiandɛ Israelmba yɛ nkowaa ma me; wɔyɛ mo nkowaa a miyii hɔn fii Egypt asaase do: emi nye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn.

    Tsir 26

    1. Mma hom nnyɛ abosom, nkyii so wɔnnsen ohonyi, anaa dum bi mma homho, nkyii so mma hom mmfa ɔbo a woedzi no akye nnsi hom asaase mu, mmbɔ hom mu adze mma no: na emi nye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn.
    2. Hom ndzi m'ahomgye afe no, na wondzi m'ahotseweebea no nyi: emi nye EWURADZE.
    3. Sɛ hom nantsew m'ahyɛdze mu, na hom sie me mbra, na hom yɛ a;
    4. ɔno mebɛma hom nyankom atotɔ wɔ n'aber mu, na asaase no ama no ndɔbaa, na haban mu ndua asow n'aba.
    5. Na hom ewuporow bokodu wenyinaba tsetsew mu, na wenyinaba tsetsew no bokodu odua ber mu: na hom bedzi hom edziban amee, na wɔatsena hom asaase do dwudwoodwo.
    6. Na mebɛma asomdwee aba asaase no do, na hom ada, na obiara rinnyi hom hu: na mebɛma mbowa bɔn tun do etwa wɔ asaase no do, nkyii so nkrantsee remmfa hom asaase do.
    7. Na hom betsiw hom atamfo, na wɔbɔtotɔr hom enyim wɔ nkrantsee ano.
    8. Na hom mu beenu betsiw ɔha, na hom mu ɔha betsiw mpem du: na hom atamfo bɔtotɔr hom enyim wɔ nkrantsee ano.
    9. Na mebɛdan m'enyim ama hom, na mama hom awowo, na hom adɔr, na mama menye hom ahyɛmu no etsim.
    10. Na hom bedzi akoradze dadaw a wɔdze esie ma akyɛr no, na hom beyi dadaw no aba wɔ fofor no ntsi.
    11. Na mibesi me ntamadan wɔ hom mu: na me kra rinnkyir hom.
    12. Na mebɛnantsew hom mu, na mayɛ hom Nyankopɔn, na hom ayɛ me man.
    13. Emi nye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn a miyii hom fii Egypt asaase do dɛ mma hom nnyɛ hɔn nkowaa; na mebubu hom kɔndua, na memaa hom sii nkyiremben nantsewee no.
    14. Mbom sɛ hom enntsie me, na wɔannyɛ me mbrasɛm nyinara a;
    15. na sɛ hom pow m'ahyɛdze, na hom kra kyir me mbrasɛm, na hom annyɛ me mbransɛm nyinara, na mbom hom sɛɛ m'ahyɛmu a;
    16. mo so mebɛyɛ hom iyi; mebɛma nketsenketse akyer hom, mekyerɛ sasawaw nye etsiridii a ɔbɛsɛɛ enyiwa, na ɔbɛma ɔkra atsetsew: na hom bodua hom nduaba kwa, na hom atamfo bedzi.
    17. Na mebɛma m'enyi asa hom, na hom edzi hom atamfo enyim nkogu: hɔn a wokyir hom bɛhyɛ hom do; na hom boguan aber a obi nntsiw hom.
    18. Na sɛ hom enntsie me wɔ iyinom mpo ntsi a, ɔno mebɛtsea hom mpɛn esuon bio wɔ hom bɔn ntsi.
    19. Na mubobubu hom tum ahokyerɛ no; na mayɛ hom sor dɛ dadze, nye hom asaase dɛ boran:
    20. na hom ahoɔdzen bɛsa kwa: na asaase remma ndɔbaa, nkyii so asaase no do ndua ronnsow n'aba.
    21. Na sɛ hom nantsew tsia me, na woenntsie me a; medze ɔhaw mpɛn esuon so mebɛba hom do dɛ mbrɛ hom bɔn tse no.
    22. Na mobɔsoma haban mu mbowa wɔ hom mu a wɔbɛgye hom nsamu mba, na wɔasɛɛ hom fiembowa, na wɔama hom dodow esuar; na hom akwan ayɛ akwamfo.
    23. Na sɛ hom amma iyinom annsakyer hom amma me, na mbom wɔnantsew tsia me a:
    24. ɔno mo so mebɛnantsew etsia hom; na emi mara mabɔ hom mpɛn esuon wɔ hom bɔn ntsi.
    25. Na medze awerdzi nkrantsee a obedzi ahyɛmu no ho wer mebɛba hom do; na hom aboaboa hom ano wɔ hom nkurow mu: na mobɔsoma ɔyardɔm wɔ hom mu; na woeyi hom ahyɛ ɔtamfo no nsa.
    26. Sɛ mububu hom edziban dua a, mbaa du bɔto hɔn boodoo wɔ fononoo kor mu, na woeyi hom boodoo ama hom bio wɔ nsɛn do: na hom bedzi, na wɔremmee.
    27. Na sɛ iyi nyinara mu so hom enntsie me, na wɔnantsew tsia me a;
    28. ɔno mebɛnantsew etsia hom ebufuw mu; na mo so matsea hom mpɛn esuon wɔ hom bɔn ntsi.
    29. Na hom bedzi hom mbabanyin nam, na hom mbabaa nam so hom edzi.
    30. Na mebɛsɛɛ hom sorɔnsorɔmbea, na metwitwa hom iwihonyi no egu, na matotow hom efun egu hom abosom efun no do; na me kra bekyir hom.
    31. Na mebɛyɛ hom nkurow amamfo, na medze hom ahotseweebea akɔ ɔsɛɛ mu, na merenngye hom nduhuam ne nka.
    32. Na medze asaase no bɔkɔ ɔsɛɛ mu: na hom atamfo a wɔtse do no ayɛ hɔn nwanwa.
    33. Na hom dze mobɔbɔ hom apetse amanaman mu, na matwe nkrantsee no edzi hom ekyir: na hom asaase ayɛ ɔsɛɛ, na hom nkurow ayɛ amamfo.
    34. Ɔno na asaase no benya n'ahomgye mber ho enyigye, mber tsentsen a ɔda hɔ kɔnn no nyinara, na hom wɔ hom atamfo asaase do no; mekyerɛ ɔno na asaase no bɔhom, na woenya n'ahomgye mber ho enyigye.
    35. Mber tsentsen a ɔda hɔ kɔnn no nyinara obenya ahomgye; mekyerɛ ahomgye a woennya no wɔ hom ahomgyenda no mu, aber a hom tsenaa do no.
    36. Na hom mu hɔn a wɔaka no dze, medze abawpa bɛhɛn hɔn akoma mu wɔ hɔn atamfo asaase do; na ahataw a atsew ne ndze etsiw hɔn; na woeguan dɛ obi ruguan nkrantsee ano; na wɔahwehwe ase aber a obi rinntsiw hɔn.
    37. Na wobefintsiw hɔnho nkorkor, dɛ nkrantsee enyim, aber a obi rinntsiw hɔn: na hom rinnya tum nngyina hom atamfo enyim.
    38. Na hom bɛsɛɛ wɔ amanaman mu, na hom atamfo asaase bɛmemen hom.
    39. Na hom mu hɔn a wɔaka no bɛtsetsew wɔ hɔn emumuyɛ mu wɔ hom atamfo asaase do; na eso hɔn egyanom emumuyɛ mu na wɔnye hɔn bɛtsetsew.
    40. Na wɔbɛka hɔn emumuyɛ, nye hɔn egyanom emumuyɛ, wɔ hɔn mfom a wɔfom mu no mu, na eso dɛ wɔanantsew etsia me ntsi, LEV.26.41, mo so menantsewee tsiaa hɔn, na medze hɔn baa hɔn atamfo asaase do: sɛ nkyii hɔn akoma monomonomyɛ no brɛ adze, na nkyii wɔpen hɔn emumuyɛ ho ntsea do a;
    41. ɔno na mebɛkaa menye Jakob ahyɛmu no; na menye Isaak ahyɛmu no so, na menye Abraham ahyɛmu no so mebɛkaa; na mebɛkaa asaase no.
    42. Na asaase no so wobegya no hɔ, na woenya n'ahomgye mber mu enyigye, aber a woekwetsi hɔn ada hɔ kɔnn no; na wɔbɛpen hɔn emumuyɛ ho ntsea do: osiandɛ wɔpoow me mbrasɛm, na hɔn kra kyiir m'ahyɛdze.
    43. Na iyi nyinara so mpo ekyir, sɛ wɔwɔ hɔn atamfo asaase do a morommpow hɔn, nkyii so mirinnkyir hɔn, dɛ meresɛɛ hɔn korakora, na matsetsew menye hɔn ntamu ahyɛmu no mu; na emi nye EWURADZE hɔn Nyankopɔn:
    44. mbom hɔn ntsi mebɛkaa hɔn nananom ahyɛmu no, hɔn a miyii hɔn fii Egypt asaase do wɔ amanaman enyim, dɛ mebɛyɛ hɔn Nyankopɔn no: emi nye EWURADZE.
    45. Iyinom nye ahyɛdze nye ntsɛmbu nye mbra a EWURADZE dze nam Moses ne nsa do hyɛe too ɔno nye Israelmba ntamu wɔ kokwaa Sinai do.

    Tsir 27

    1. Na EWURADZE kasaa kyerɛɛ Moses dɛ, LEV.27.2, Kasa kyerɛ Israelmba, na se hɔn dɛ, Sɛ nyimpa bi hyɛ anohoba ma a, nkorɔfo no nyɛ EWURADZE dze dɛ mbrɛ w'abotwa tse.
    2. Na w'abotwa wɔ banyin a oedzi mfe eduonu do dze kodu nyia oedzi mfe eduosia do no ho nyɛ dwɛɛtɛɛ dwetɛbona eduonum, dɛ mbrɛ krɔnkrɔnbea dwetɛbona tse.
    3. Na sɛ ɔyɛ ɔbaa a, ɔno w'abotwa nyɛ dwetɛbona eduasa.
    4. Na sɛ ofi nyia oedzi mfe enum do dze kodu nyia oedzi mfe eduonu do a, ɔno w'abotwa wɔ ɔbanyin ho nyɛ dwetɛbona eduonu, na ɔbaa ho so dwetɛbona du.
    5. Na sɛ ofi nyia oedzi bosoom do dze kodu nyia oedzi mfe enum do a, ɔno w'abotwa wɔ ɔbanyin ho nyɛ dwɛɛtɛɛ dwetɛbona enum, na ɔbaa ho so w'abotwa nyɛ dwɛɛtɛɛ dwetɛbona ebiasa.
    6. Na sɛ ofi nyia oedzi mfe eduosia nye ekyir do a; sɛ ɔbanyin a, ɔno w'abotwa nyɛ dwetɛbona duenum, na ɔbaa ho so dwetɛbona du.
    7. Mbom sɛ ɔyɛ ohianyi dodow kyɛn w'abotwa no a, ɔno wɔmfa no mbegyina ɔsɔfo no enyim, na ɔsɔfo no ntwa no bo; dɛ mbrɛ nyia ɔbɔɔ anohoba no ne nsa bɔso do no ara na ɔsɔfo no ntwa no bo.
    8. Na sɛ ɔyɛ abowa a adasa dze bi yɛ ayɛyɛdze brɛ EWURADZE a, dza nyimpa biara dze mu bi ma EWURADZE no nyinara nyɛ krɔnkrɔn.
    9. Mma ɔnndan no, nkyii so ɔnnsakyer no, ɔmmfa papa nnsesa bɔn, anaa ɔmmfa bɔn nnsesa papa: na sɛ koraa ɔdze abowa bɛsesa abowa a, ɔno emu ebien nyina ɔno nye dza wɔdze sesaa no no nyɛ krɔnkrɔn.
    10. Na sɛ ɔyɛ abowa biara a no ho nntsew a wɔmmfa bi nnyɛ ayɛyɛdze mmbrɛ EWURADZE a, ɔno ɔmfa abowa no mbegyina ɔsɔfo no enyim:
    11. na ɔsɔfo no ntwa no bo, sɛ oye o, omuo o: dɛ mbrɛ woara ɔsɔfo no botwa no bo no, dɛmara na ɔnyɛ.
    12. Mbom sɛ pon ara na ɔbɔpon no a, ɔno ɔmfa no mu ebupɛn enum mu kor nto wo bo a itwae no do.
    13. Na sɛ nyimpa bi tsew ne fi ho dɛ ɔnyɛ krɔnkrɔn mma EWURADZE a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no ntwa no bo, sɛ oye o, omuo o: dɛ mbrɛ ɔsɔfo no botwa no bo no, dɛmara na ongyina.
    14. Na sɛ nyia ɔtsew ho no bɔpon ne fi a, ɔno ɔmfa wo bo a itwae no ne sika no mu ebupɛn enum mu kor nto do, na ɔnyɛ nedze.
    15. Na sɛ nyimpa bi tsew n'asaase mu haban mu bi ho ma EWURADZE a, ɔno w'abotwa nyɛ dɛ mbrɛ no nduae tse: atoko a woedua no homer kor no bo yɛ dwɛɛtɛɛ dwetɛbona eduonum.
    16. Sɛ ahosɛpɛw afe no do na ɔtsew ne haban ho a, ontua dɛ mbrɛ bo a woetwa no tse no ara.
    17. Mbom sɛ ahosɛpɛw afe no ekyir na ɔtsew ne haban ho a, ɔno ɔsɔfo no mbubu sika no ano mma no dɛ mbrɛ mfe a aka ma akɔso ahosɛpɛw afe no tse no, na wɔntsew bo a itwae no do.
    18. Na sɛ pon ara na nyia ɔtsew haban no ho no bɔpon a, ɔno ɔmfa wo bo a itwae no ne sika no mu ebupɛn enum mu kor nto do, na wonsi no pi mma no.
    19. Na sɛ ɔammpon haban no, anaa sɛ ɔatɔn haban no ama nyimpa bi so a, mma wɔmmpon bio;
    20. mbom haban no, sɛ ofir edzi ahosɛpɛw afe no mu kɔ a, ɔnyɛ krɔnkrɔn mma EWURADZE, dɛ haban a woehyira do ama; ɔnyɛ ɔsɔfo no n'enyadze.
    21. Na sɛ ɔtsew haban a ɔatɔ a ɔnnyɛ n'enyadze haban no bi ho ma EWURADZE a;
    22. ɔno ɔsɔfo no mbubu w'abotwa a ɔdze kodu ahosɛpɛw afe no do no, no bo a ɔsom no mma no: na ɔmma wo bo a itwae no da no ara, dɛ adzekrɔnkrɔn mma EWURADZE.
    23. Ahosɛpɛw afe no mu haban no nsan nkɔ nyia wɔtɔɔ no no hɔ, mekyerɛ nyia ɔyɛ n'enyadze asaase no no hɔ.
    24. Na w'abotwitwa nyinara nyɛ dɛ mbrɛ krɔnkrɔnbea dwetɛbona no tse: gera eduonu yɛ dwetɛbona kor.
    25. Mbowa mu abakan a wɔayɛ no abakan ama EWURADZE no, noara nkotoo na nyimpa bi nntsew ho; sɛ ɔnantwinyin o, ɔkɔsɔw o, EWURADZE dze a.
    26. Na sɛ ɔyɛ abowa a no ho nntsew bi a, ɔno ɔmma n'apondze dɛ mbrɛ w'abotwa tse no, na ɔmfa no mu ebupɛn enum mu kor nto do: anaa sɛ wɔammpon no no a, ɔno wɔntɔn no dɛ mbrɛ w'abotwa tse no.
    27. Naaso ehyiradze biara a nyimpa bi behyira do ama EWURADZE wɔ adze a ɔwɔ nyinara mu no, sɛ nyimpa o, abowa o, n'enyadze mu haban o, mma wɔnntɔn anaa wɔmmpon: ehyiradze biara yɛ krɔnkrɔndze mu adzekrɔnkrɔn ma EWURADZE.
    28. Na mpo, nyimpa biara a woebu no kumfɔ no, mma wɔmmpon no; oku na wonku no.
    29. Na asaase no do ebupɛn du du nyinara, sɛ ofi asaase no do o, nduadzewa mu o, dua n'aba mu o, ɔyɛ EWURADZE dze; ɔyɛ krɔnkrɔn ma EWURADZE.
    30. Na sɛ nyimpa bi bɔpon n'ebupɛn du du no mu bi a, ɔmfa no mu ebupɛn enum mu kor nto do.
    31. Na anantwi anaa nguan mu ebupɛn du du nyinara, adze a ɔfa poma ase nyinara mu dza otsia du du no nyɛ krɔnkrɔn mma EWURADZE.
    32. Mma ɔnnhwehwɛ mu dɛ ɔyɛ papa anaa bɔn, nkyii so mma ɔnnsesa bi: na sɛ koraa ɔbɛsesa bi a, ɔno emu ebien nyina, ɔno nye dza wɔdze sesa no no nyɛ krɔnkrɔn; mma wɔmmpon no.
    33. Iyinom nye mbransɛm a EWURADZE dze hyɛɛ Moses wɔ bepɔw Sinai do ma ɔdze maa Israelmba no.
