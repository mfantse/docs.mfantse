    Mark

    Tsir 1

    1. Jesus Kraest, [Nyankopɔn Ba] no, ne nsɛmpa no ahyɛse.
    2. Dɛ mbrɛ wɔakyerɛw no wɔ nkɔnhyɛnyi Isaia mu dɛ, Hwɛ, morosoma mo bɔfoe ma oeedzi w'enyim kan, Obesiesie wo kwan mu;
    3. Obi ne ndze retsea mu wɔ sar no do dɛ, Hom nsiesie Ewuradze no kwan, Hom mma n'atsempɔn ntsentsen;
    4. John bae bonumae wɔ sar no do na ɔpaa mu kaa nnuho enuma a ɔdze kɔ bɔnfakyɛ mu no ho asɛm.
    5. Na Judea man nyina, na Jerusalɛmfo nyina puee kɔr no hɔ kɛkaa hɔn bɔn, na onumaa hɔn wɔ Esutsen Jordan mu.
    6. Nna John fura efupɔnkɔnhwi tam na nwoma abɔdo bɔ n'asen, na odzi mbɛbɛw na kwaa mu ewo.
    7. Na ɔpaa mu kaa dɛ, Obi a no ho yɛ dzen kyɛn me dzi m'ekyir reba a no nsokota ho ahoma mpo mennsɛ dɛ mobɔkotow masan.
    8. Emi dze, munumaa hom nsu mu, na ɔno, dze, obonuma hom wɔ Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn mu.
    9. Na ɔbaa dɛ nda no mu no Jesus fii Galilii Nazarɛt bae, na John numaa no wɔ Esutsen Jordan mu.
    10. Na ntsɛmara a ofi nsu no mu reba no, ohun dɛ sor apaa ebien na Sunsum no tse dɛ ebubur risian ba no do:
    11. na ndze bi fii sor baa dɛ, Ɔwo nye mo Dɔba a esɔ m'enyi.
    12. Na amonmu hɔ ara na Sunsum no faa no ɔhardo kɔr sar no do.
    13. Na odzii sar no do hɔ adaduanan, ma Satan sɔɔ no hwɛe; nna ɔnye hambowa wɔ hɔ; na abɔfo no soom no.
    14. Na afei woyii John mae no ekyir no, Jesus baa Galilii bɛkaa Nyankopɔn ho asɛmpa no, na ɔsee dɛ, MRK.1.15, Aber no aso na Nyankopɔn ahenman no abɛn: hom nnu homho, na hom ngye asɛmpa no ndzi.
    15. Na ɔnenam Galilii po ho no, ohun Simon nye Simon no nua Andrew dɛ wɔrotow eboa wɔ po mu, osiandɛ wɔyɛ apofo.
    16. Na Jesus see hɔn dɛ, Hom mbedzi m'ekyir na mebɛma hom ayɛ nyimpa enufo.
    17. Na ntsɛmara wɔdaan eboa no gyaae na wobedzii n'ekyir.
    18. Na ɔkɔr enyim kakra no, ohun James, Zebedee ne ba no, ɔnye no nua John a hɔn so wɔwɔ hɛn mu resa hɔn mboa.
    19. Na ntsɛmara ɔfrɛɛ hɔn; na wɔdaan hɔn egya Zebedee nye apaafo no gyaa hɛn no mu, na wokedzii n'ekyir.
    20. Na wɔkɔr Karpernaum; na ntsɛmara ahomgyeda no ɔkɛhɛɛn nhyiamudan no mu kɛkyerɛkyerɛe.
    21. Na ne nkyerɛkyerɛ yɛɛ hɔn ahobow, osiandɛ ɔkyerɛkyerɛɛ hɔn dɛ nyia ɔwɔ tum, na ɔnntse dɛ kyerɛwfo no., #Mat 7.28
    22. Ber no ara mu no, nna nyimpa bi wɔ nhyiamudan no mu a ɔwɔ sunsum fi;
    23. na ɔtseaa mu see dɛ, Ebɛnadze na yɛnye wo wɔ yɛ, Jesus Nazarɛtnyi? Ana ebae dɛ erebɛsɛɛ hɛn? Minyim wo nyimpa ban, Nyankopɔn no Hotseweenyi no a.
    24. Na Jesus bɔɔ no ham dɛ, Ka w'ano to mu, na fi no mu fi!
    25. Na sunsum fi no twaa no hwee hɔ na ɔkaa mu kɛse fii no mu firii.
    26. Na hɔn nyina ho boow hɔn ara ma wobisabisaa hɔnho dɛ, Ebɛnadze nye yi? Nkyerɛkyerɛ fofor bɛn a! Ɔdze tum hyɛ esunsum fi mpo, na wotsie n'asɛm.
    27. Na ntsɛmara ne nka tahyee ɔman a etwa Galilii ho nyina mu.
    28. Na ntsɛmara wofii nhyiamudan no mu firii no wɔbaa Simon nye Andrew fie, wɔnye James nye John.
    29. Nna Simon n'asew da hɔ yar papu; na ntsɛmara wɔkaa no ho asɛm kyerɛɛ no;
    30. na Jesus kosuoo ne nsamu maa no do; na papu no fii no do, na ɔhyɛɛ ase soom hɔn.
    31. Na ewimbir a ewia kɔtɔree no wɔdze hɔn a wɔyaryar na hɔn a mbɔnsam afa hɔn nyina brɛɛ no.
    32. Na kurow no nyina behyiaa mu wɔ abow no ano.
    33. Na ɔsaa dodowara a wɔwɔ nyarba horow no yarba, na otuu mbɔnsam pii; na ɔamma mbɔnsam no kwan dɛ wɔnkasa, osiandɛ wonyim no.
    34. Na anapaatu, ansaana adze rebɛkye no, ɔsoɛree na opuei kɔr sar bi do, na ɔkɔbɔɔ mpaa wɔ hɔ.
    35. Na Simon nye hɔn a wɔka no ho no, tsiiw no;
    36. na wohun no na wɔsee no dɛ, Nyimpa nyina rohwehwɛ wo.
    37. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom mma yɛnkɔ nkurow nkaa no so mu, ama maakɛka asɛm no wɔ hɔ so, osiandɛ iyi ntsi na mebae.
    38. Na ɔkɔr hɔn nhyiamudan a ɔwɔ Galilii nyina mu kɛkaa asɛm no, na otuu mbɔnsam.
    39. Na kwatanyi bi baa no hɔ bɛserɛɛ no, bɔkotoow no, na ɔsee no dɛ, Sɛ epɛ a, ibotum dɛ ema mo ho san.
    40. Na Jesus ne yamu hyehyee no maa no, na ɔtseen ne nsa dze kaa no, na ɔsee no dɛ, Mepɛ, wo ho nsan wo.
    41. Na ntsɛmara na kwata no fii no ho, ma no ho sanee.
    42. Na Jesus braa no dzendzenndzen, na ɔmaa ofii hɔ ntsɛmara, na ɔsee no dɛ, MRK.1.44, Hwɛ ma aannka biribi annkyerɛ obiara; na san kɔ, fa woho kɛkyerɛ ɔsɔfo no, na w'ahosan ntsi kotua dza Moses hyɛe no fa yɛ dase ma hɔn.
    43. Na ɔno dze, ɔdze kɛtahyee pii, na ɔmaa asɛm no trɛe, ma dɛm ntsi Jesus enntum annkɔ kurow bi mu petee bio, mbom sar do hɔ beebi na ɔtsenae; na wofii mbea nyina baa ne nkyɛn.

    Tsir 2

    1. Nda kakra bi ekyir no, ɔkɔr Karpernaum bio, na wɔtsee dɛ ɔwɔ fie.
    2. Na dodowara behyiaa mu, ma nna wonnya kwan bio mma hɔn, nnyɛ abowano hɔ mpo; na ɔkaa asɛm no kyerɛɛ hɔn.
    3. Na wɔdze mbubuinyi a nyimpa baanan so no baa no hɔ.
    4. Na ɔnam nyimpadodow no ntsi na wonnya kwan nntwe mmbɛn no no, wɔkɛpaa dan a ɔwɔ mu no etsifi; na wotuu mu tu na wɔdze kɛtɛ a mbubuinyi no da do no duaa mu gyaagyaa mu sii famu.
    5. Na Jesus hun hɔn gyedzi no, ɔsee mbubuinyi no dɛ, Ɔba, wɔdze wo bɔn akyɛ wo.
    6. Nna kyerɛwfo no binom tsetse hɔ, na wɔdwendween hɔn akoma mu dɛ, MRK.2.7, Ɔyɛ dɛn na oyi kasa dɛm yi? Ebususɛm na ɔka: woana botum dze bɔn akyɛ dɛ Nyankopɔn nkotsee?
    7. Na ntsɛmara Jesus hun wɔ no sunsum mu dɛ wɔrodwendwen dɛm wɔ hɔn akoma mu no, ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ebɛnadze ntsi na hom rodwendwen dɛm wɔ hom akoma mu yi?
    8. Dza ɔwɔ hen na ɔyɛ mberɛw kɛse, dɛ ebɛse mbubuinyi no dɛ, Wɔdze wo bɔn akyɛ wo, anaa ebɛse dɛ, Soɛr, fa wo kɛtɛ, na nantsew, a?
    9. Na mbrɛ ɔbɛyɛ ma hom ehu dɛ nyimpa Ba no wɔ tum wɔ asaase do dɛ ɔdze bɔn kyɛ ntsi, ɔsee mbubuinyi no dɛ, MRK.2.11, Mese wo dɛ, Soɛr, fa wo kɛtɛ na kɔ wo fie.
    10. Na ɔsoɛree, na ntsɛmara ɔfaa kɛtɛ no, na osii mu wɔ hɔn nyina enyim kɔree. Na ɔmaa hɔn nyina ho boow hɔn, na wɔhyɛɛ Nyankopɔn enyimnyam dɛ, Yennhun dɛm adze yi bi da.
    11. Na opuee bio kɔr mpoano; na nyimpadodow no nyina kɔr no hɔ, na ɔhyɛɛ ase kyerɛkyerɛɛ hɔn.
    12. Na ɔresen no ohun Alfaeus ne ba Levi dɛ ɔtse atowgyebea, na ɔsee no dɛ, Ka mo do. Na ɔsoɛree kaa no do.
    13. Na ɔbaa dɛ ɔtse edzidzi wɔ ne fie no; atowgyefo nye ndzebɔnyɛfo pii nye Jesus na n'esuafo bɛtsenatsenaa hɔ: nna hɔn a wɔka no do no dɔɔ so.
    14. Na akyerɛwfo a wofi Farisiifo no mu no hun dɛ ɔnye ndzebɔnyɛfo nye atowgyefo ridzidzi no, wobisaa n'esuafo no dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn na ɔnye atowgyefo nye ndzebɔnyɛfo dzidzi nom yi?
    15. Na Jesus tsee no ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hɔn a wɔtse apɔw no, wonnhia oduyɛfo, na mbom hɔn a wɔyar no a: mammbɛfrɛ atseneneefo, na mbom ndzebɔnyɛfo a.
    16. Nna John n'esuafo nye Farisiifo no ridzi mbuada: na binom bae bebisaa no dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn na John n'esuafo nye Farisiifo hɔn esuafo no wodzi mbuada, na w'esuafo dze, wonndzi mbuada yi?
    17. Na Jesus see hɔn dɛ, Ayeforpia mu mba no, aber a ayeforkun no wɔ hɔn nkyɛn no, aso wobotum edzi mbuada anaa? Aber tsentsen biara a wɔwɔ ayeforkun no wɔ hɔn nkyɛn no, worunntum nndzi mbuada.
    18. Na nda bɛba a wobeyi ayeforkun no efi hɔn nkyɛn, na da no mu nkyii wobedzi mbuada.
    19. Obi mmfa tam fofor sin nntaa atar dadaw mu: dɛm no, dza wɔdze taa mu no yi fi mu, fofor no fi dadaw no mu, na ntsewee no yɛ kɛse.
    20. Na obi mmfa wenyin fofor nngu nwomantoa dadaw mu; dɛm no, wenyin no bɛpaa nwomantoa no, na wenyin no asɛɛ, ɔnye nwomantoa no so: na wenyin fofor dze, wɔdze gu nwomantoa fofor mu.
    21. Na ɔbaa dɛ Jesus kɛfaa eburow kwa mu ahomgyeda bi; na n'esuafo no nam rokɔ no, wɔhyɛɛ ase pempaan eburow mbɛtsem no bi.
    22. Na Farisiifo no see no dɛ, Hwɛ, ɔyɛ dɛn na wɔreyɛ dza ɔmmfa mbra mu wɔ ahomgyeda mu yi?
    23. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ana hom nnkenkaan dza David yɛe wɔ aber a ohiaa no, na ɔkɔm dzee ɔnoara na hɔn a wɔka no ho no, pɛn anaa?
    24. Mbrɛ ɔkɛhɛɛn Nyankopɔn ne fie, aber a Abiathar yɛ ɔsɔfopanyin no, na okedzii kyerɛpaanoo a ɔmmfa mbra mu dɛ obiara dzi, gyedɛ asɔfo no nko, na ɔmaa hɔn a wɔka no ho no so bi no?
    25. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Nyimpa ntsi na wɔyɛɛ ahomgyeda, na nnyɛ ahomgyeda ntsi na wɔyɛɛ nyimpa:
    26. dɛm ntsi nyimpa Ba no yɛ ahomgyeda no so mpo no wura.

    Tsir 3

    1. Na ɔkɛhɛɛn nhyiamudan no mu bio; na nyimpa bi wɔ hɔ a ne nsa adwedwe.
    2. Na wɔtsɛɛw no hwɛɛ dɛ ɔbɛyɛ no edur ahomgyeda no, ama wɔaatoa no.
    3. Na ɔsee nyimpa no a ne nsa adwedwe no dɛ, Soɛr gyina finimfin.
    4. Na obisaa hɔn dɛ, Ana ɔfa mbra mu dɛ ahomgyeda wɔbɛyɛ dza oye, anaa wɔbɛyɛ dza omuo? Wɔbɛgye nkwa, anaa woboku a?
    5. Na womuaa hɔn ano. Na ɔhwɛɛ hɔn hyiae ebufuw do, na hɔn akoma dzen ntsi ɔyɛɛ no yaw no, ɔsee nyimpa no dɛ, Tsen wo nsa. Na ɔtsenee; na ne nsa no sanee dzii mu.
    6. Na Farisiifo no puei, na ntsɛmara wɔnye Herod akadofo kɛfaa apam wɔ mbrɛ wɔbɛyɛ asɛɛ no ho.
    7. Na Jesus nye n'esuafo no twee hɔnho kɔr mpoano; na nyimpadodow pii fii Galilii kaa no do;
    8. na wofi Judea, Jerusalɛm, Idumea, na Jordan egya, na Taere nye Sidon nkyɛn, nyimpadodow pii a wɔtsee ndzɛmba akɛse pii a ɔyɛe no, na wɔbaa ne nkyɛn.
    9. Na ɔkaa kyerɛɛ n'esuafo no dɛ wɔnyɛ hɛn kakraba bi krado ntweɔn no wɔ nyimpadodow no ntsi, amma wɔaannkyer no do;
    10. ɔnam dɛ ɔsaa pii yarba ntsi, dodowara a mbusu horow aba hɔn do no bɛkyeer no do dɛ worubosusuo no mu.
    11. Na esunsum fi so, sɛ wohu no a, wotwa hwe n'enyim, na wɔka mu dɛ, Ɔwo nye Nyankopɔn Ba no.
    12. Na Jesus braa hɔn ketsee dɛ mma wonnyi no nnkyerɛ.
    13. Na ɔkɔr koko do, na ɔfrɛɛ hɔn a noara rohwehwɛ hɔn no, na wɔkɔr ne nkyɛn.
    14. Na oyii duebien, dɛ wɔnye no bɛtsena, MRK.3.15, na dɛ ɔbɔsoma hɔn ama wɔaakɛka asɛm no na woeenya tum dze etu mbɔnsam.
    15. Na Simon ɔtoo no dzin Peter, MRK.3.17, na Zebedee ba James, nye James no nua John; na ɔtoo hɔn dzin Boanerges, ɔno nye dɛ Senaman Mba;
    16. nye Andrew, nye Filip, nye Bartholomew, nye Matthew, nye Tomas, nye Alfaeus ba James, nye Tadeus, MRK.3.19, nye Keenannyi Simon, nye Judas Iskariot a ɔno na oyii no mae no.
    17. Na ɔbaa fie; na nyimpadodow no behyiaa bio, ma dɛm ntsi woenntum enndzidzi mpo.
    18. Na n'ebusuafo no tsee no, wofiir edzi dɛ wɔrobɔtwe no akɔ, osiandɛ nna binom reka dɛ, N'adwen mu aka.
    19. Na kyerɛwfo no a wofi Jerusalɛm bae no see dɛ, Ɔwɔ Beelzebul, na mbɔnsam hɔn panyin do na ɔnam tu mbɔnsam no.
    20. Na ɔfrɛɛ hɔn baa ne nkyɛn no, obubuu mbɛ see hɔn dɛ, Ɔbɛyɛ dɛn na Satan botum etu Satan?
    21. Na sɛ ahenman nye no ho dzi mpaapaamu a, ahenman no nntum nngyina.
    22. Na sɛ ofi nye no ho dzi mpaapaamu a, ofi no runntum nngyina.
    23. Na sɛ Satan asoɛr nankasa no do na oedzi mpaapaamu a, onnkotum egyina, nna n'ewiei edu.
    24. Na obi nntum nnkɔ ɔkatakyi bi ne fie nnkotu nhyɛ n'efidze do mfom mfa, gyedɛ odzi kan kyekyer ɔkatakyi no, ansaana ɔafom ne fie.
    25. Mereka akyerɛ hom pefee dɛ, Bɔn nyina wɔdze bɛkyɛ nyimpa mba, nye ebususɛm a wɔbɛka nyina;
    26. naaso nyia ɔbɛka ebususɛm etsia Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn no dze, orinnya fakyɛ da, na onnyiewiei afɔdzi da no do:
    27. osiandɛ wɔsee dɛ, Ɔwɔ sunsum fi.
    28. Na ne na nye no nuanom bae begyinaa abow no enyim somae ma wɔkɛfrɛɛ no.
    29. Nna nyimpadodow tsetse ne nkyɛn, na wɔsee no dɛ, Hwɛ, wo na nye wo nuanom gyina abow enyim rohwehwɛ wo.
    30. Na obuae see hɔn dɛ, Woana nye me na nye mo nuanom?
    31. Na ɔtoo n'enyi hwɛɛ hɔn a wɔtsetse ne nkyɛn hyiae no, ɔsee dɛ, Hwɛ, me na nye mo nuanom.
    32. Na obiara a ɔbɛyɛ Nyankopɔn ne pɛ no, ɔno nye mo nua, nye m'akyerɛbaa, nye me na.

    Tsir 4

    1. Na ɔhyɛɛ ase bio kyerɛkyerɛe wɔ mpoano. Na nyimpadodow pii behyiaa ne nkyɛn, ma dɛm ntsi ɔkɔr hɛn mu kɛtsenaa ase wɔ po enyi, nna nyimpadodow no nyina wɔ mpoano wɔ daadze.
    2. Na ɔkyerɛkyerɛɛ hɔn ndzɛmba pii wɔ abɛbu mu, na ɔsee hɔn wɔ ne nkyerɛkyerɛ mu dɛ, MRK.4.3, Hom ntsie: hwɛ, oduafo no kɔree koguu aba;
    3. na orugu no, ɔbaa dɛ bi koguu kwan nkyɛn na nnoma bae bɔsosɔwee.
    4. Na bi koguu abosamdo mbrɛ oennya dɛtse pii; na ntsɛmara na ofirii, osiandɛ oennya dɛtse a no mu dɔ;
    5. na ewia puei no, ɔhyewee, na osiandɛ onnyi ndwow ntsi ɔkyenkyenee.
    6. Na bi so koguu nsoɛ mu, na nsoɛ no nyinii, na omiaa no, ma ɔannsow aba.
    7. Na bi so koguu asaase pa mu, na ɔsoow aba a onyinii na ɔdɔɔ so; na ɔsoow bi eduasa, bi eduosia, bi ɔha.
    8. Na ɔsee dɛ, Nyia ɔwɔ aso a wɔdze tsie no, ma ontsie.
    9. Na ɔkaa no nko no, hɔn a wɔwɔ ne nkyɛn no wɔnye duebien no bisaa no mbɛ no ase.

    21. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom nhwɛ dza hom tse no yie: esusudze kor a hom dze susu no, wɔdze bosusu ama hom, na wɔdze bi bɛka hom ho.

    24. na ɔbɛda na obenyan adzesaa nye adzekyee, na aba no befifir na oenyin, na onnyim mbrɛ ɔyɛe.
    25. Na asaase nankasa na ɔsow aba, ahaban no dzi kan, na nkyii ɔayɛ bɛtsem, na nkyii ɔayɛ eburow wɔ bɛtsem no mu.

    27. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ebɛnadze na yɛdze bɔto Nyankopɔn ahenman ho? Anaadɛ ebɛn bɛ na yɛdze bɛkyerɛ?

    29. naaso sɛ wodua a, onyin yɛ kɛse kyɛn nhaban nyina, na ɔtoto abaw akɛse, ma wimu nnoma tum yɛ hɔn mberɛbuw wɔ no nwin mu.

    Tsir 5

    1. Na wɔbaa po no n'egya wɔ Gerasafo asaase do.

    12. Na ɔmaa hɔn kwan. Na esunsum fi no fiir edzi kɛhɛɛn mpreko no mu, na okuw no nyina faa twitwi kesiaan ɔbo kronn no koguguu po no mu, wɔbɛyɛ mpemebien, na wɔtseetsee wui wɔ po no mu.
    13. Na hɔn a wɔrenyɛn mpreko no guanee na wɔkɛkae wɔ kurow no mu na no nkwaado. Na wopuei bɔhwɛɛ adze kor a aba no.
    14. Na wɔbaa Jesus nkyɛn no, wobohun nyia mbɔnsam faa no no dɛ ɔtse hɔ hyɛ atar, na n'adwen mu atsew no, mekyerɛ nyia a onyaa Legyin no a; na wosuroi.
    15. Na hɔn a wohunii no kaa mbrɛ ɔyɛɛ nyia mbɔnsam faa no nye mpreko no ho asɛm kyerɛɛ hɔn.
    16. Na wɔhyɛɛ ase serɛɛ Jesus dɛ omfi hɔn apaamu hɔ nkɔ.
    17. Na ɔrokɔ hɛn no mu no, nyimpa a nkan no mbɔnsam faa no no serɛɛ no dɛ ɔnye no bɛtsena.
    18. Naaso Jesus amma no kwan, na ɔsee no dɛ, Kɔ wo fie wɔ w'ebusuafo nkyɛn kɛka ndzɛmba akɛse a Ewuradze ayɛ ama wo, na mbrɛ oesi ehu wo mbɔbɔr no.
    19. Na osii mu kɔree, na ɔkɛhyɛɛ ase kaa ndzɛmba akɛse a Jesus yɛe maa no no wɔ Dekapolis, na ɔyɛɛ nyimpa nyina nwanwa.
    20. Na Jesus nam hɛn no mu twae baa esuegya bio no, nyimpadodow pii behyiaa no ho: nna ɔwɔ mpoano.
    21. Na nhyiamudan mu mpanyimfo no mu baako a ne dzin dze Jairus bae; na ohun no no, ɔhwee n'ananadze, MRK.5.23, na ɔserɛɛ no pii dɛ, Me babaa kakraba ruwu: meserɛ wo, bra bɛfa wo nsa to no do, ma no ho nyɛ no dzen na onnya nkwa.
    22. Na ɔnye no kɔree, na nyimpadodow pii kaa no do, na wɔkyeer no do.
    23. Na ɔbaa bi a oenya bɔgyaagu mfe duebien, MRK.5.26, na oehu amandze pii wɔ eduyɛfo pii nsa, na ɔahwer dza ɔwɔ nyina, na ɔnnyɛ yie biara, na mbom sor araa na yarba no kɔ no, MRK.5.27, tsee Jesus ne nka no, obedzii n'ekyir nyimpadodow no mu dze ne nsa bɛkaa n'atar;
    24. osiandɛ ɔsee dɛ, Sɛ meka n'atar nko mpo a, mo ho bɛyɛ me dzen.
    25. Na ntsɛmara no bɔgya bura wewee, na ɔtsee wɔ no honam mu dɛ woeyi no mbusu no kɔ.
    26. Na ntsɛmara Jesus hun wɔ no mu dɛ ahoɔdzen a ɔwɔ no mu no bi efi mu no, ɔdaan noho wɔ nyimpadodow no mu see dɛ, Woana dze ne nsa kaa m'atar?
    27. Na n'esuafo no see no dɛ, Ehu dɛ nyimpadodow no akyer wo do, na afei iribisa dɛ, Woana dze ne nsa kaa me yi ɛ?
    28. Na ɔtoo n'enyi aprɔw dɛ obohu nyia ɔyɛɛ dɛm adze no.
    29. Na ɔbaa no, onyim dza wɔayɛ wɔ no mu no, dze suro nye ahopopo bae botwae hwee n'enyim na ɔkaa nokwar no nyina kyerɛɛ no.

    34. Na wɔbaa nhyiamudan mu panyin no ne fie; na Jesus hun dɛ hɔ ayɛ gyigyeegye, na nkorɔfo rusu na wɔreka mmɔn.
    35. Ɔkɛhɛɛn hɔ no, ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn na hom reyɛ dede na hom rusu yi? Abofra no nnwui, na ɔada.
    36. Na hɔn dze, wɔsereew no twee. Na Jesus yii hɔn nyina edzi no, ɔfaa abofraba no n'egya nye ne na na ɔnye hɔn a wɔka no ho no kɔr mbrɛ abofraba no wɔ.
    37. Na osuoo abofraba no ne nsamu, na ɔsee dɛ, ne nkyerɛase nye dɛ, Akataasia, mese wo dɛ, Nyan., Talita
    38. Na ntsɛmara na akataasia no soɛree na ɔnantsewee: (nna oedzi mfe duebien.) Na ntsɛmara hɔn ho boow hɔn kɛse.
    39. Na ɔbraa hɔn ketsee dɛ mma wɔnnka ma obiara nntse, na ɔsee dɛ wɔmma abofra no biribi ma ondzi.

    Tsir 6

    1. Na Jesus fii hɔ kɔree, na ɔbaa nankasa no kurow mu, na n'esuafo no dzii n'ekyir.
    2. Na ahomgyeda no durii no, ɔhyɛɛ ase kyerɛkyerɛe wɔ nhyiamudan no mu, na nyimpadodow no tsiee no no, hɔn ho boow hɔn dɛ, Henfa na oyi nyaa dɛm ndzeyɛɛ yi? Ebɛn nyansa nye dza wɔdze ama no yi, na ebɛn tum ndwuma nye dza ɔdze ne nsa ayɛ yi?
    3. Ana nnyɛ odwimfo no a ɔyɛ Mary ne ba no, James nye Judas nye Simon hɔn nua no nye yi bi? Ana nnyɛ ne nkyerɛbaa na wɔwɔ hɛn nkyɛn ha yi? Na ɔyɛɛ hɔn fintsidua.
    4. Na Jesus see hɔn dɛ, Enyidzi mmbɔ nkɔnhyɛnyi gyedɛ nankasa no kurow mu, nye n'ebusuafo mu, nye nankasa ne fie.
    5. Na oenntum annyɛ tum edwuma biara wɔ hɔ, gyedɛ ayarfo kumaabi a ɔdze ne nsa too hɔn do yɛɛ hɔn edur.
    6. Na gyedzi a wonnyi yɛɛ no nwanwa. Nna okekyiin nkurobaase kyerɛkyerɛe.
    7. Na ɔfrɛɛ duebien no, na ɔhyɛɛ ase somaa hɔn beenu beenu, na ɔmaa hɔn tum wɔ esunsum fi do;
    8. na ɔhyɛɛ hɔn dɛ mma wɔmmfa biribiara nntu kwan, gyedɛ poma nko: mma wɔmmfa edziban anaa kotoku, anaa sika wɔ hɔn abɔdo mu;
    9. na wɔnhyɛ mpaboa, na mma wɔnnhyɛ ntar ebien.
    10. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Beebiara mbrɛ hom bɛhɛn fi biara mu no, hom ntsena hɔ nkodu dɛ hom befi hɔ akɔ.
    11. Na kurow biara a ɔnnkɛgye hom no, na wonnketsie hom wɔ mu no, hom fi hɔ rokɔ a, hom mporoporow hom ananadze huntuma ngye dase ntsia hɔn.
    12. Na wɔkɔree kɛpae mu kaa dɛ wonnu hɔnho.
    13. Na wotuu mbɔnsam pii na wɔdze ngo seraa ayarfo pii saa hɔn yarba.
    14. Na Ɔhen Herod tsee, osiandɛ Jesus ne dzin tahyee: na ɔsee dɛ, John Onumanyi no enyan efi ewufo mu: dɛm ntsi na tum ndzɛmba yi reyɛ no mu edwuma no.
    15. Na binom see dɛ, Elija a; na binom so see dɛ, Nkɔnhyɛnyi a, tse dɛ nkɔnhyɛfo no mu baako.
    16. Na Herod tsee no, ɔsee dɛ, John a mutwaa ne tsir no enyan bio.
    17. Nna Herod nankasa asoma ma woekosuo John mu akyer no akɔto efiadze, wɔ no nua Filip yer Herodias ntsi: osiandɛ nna Herod awar no., #Luk 3.19
    18. Na John see Herod dɛ, Ɔmmfa mbra mu mma wo dɛ ebɔwar wo nua ne yer.
    19. Na Herodias kyekyeer no hyɛɛ ne yamu, na ɔpɛɛ dɛ oku no, naaso oenntum:
    20. osiandɛ Herod suroo John, nna onyim dɛ ɔyɛ nyimpa tsenenee nye krɔnkrɔn ntsi, ɔbɔɔ no ho ban. Sɛ oritsie no a, no ho hyehye no dodow, naaso ɔdze dɛw tsiee no.
    21. Na da bi a Herod ridzi n'awoda no, ɔtoo pon maa n'ekunyin nye n'asafohen nye Galilii mpanyimfo;
    22. na Herodias ne babaa bae bɛsawee ma ɔsɔɔ Herod nye hɔn a wɔtse edzidzi no enyiwa: na ɔhen no see akataasia no dɛ, Bisa me biribiara a epɛ, na medze bɛma wo.
    23. Na ɔkaa ntam kyerɛɛ no dɛ, Biribiara a ibebisa me no, medze bɛma wo dze ekesi m'ahenman ne fa.
    24. Na ɔkɔree kɛsee ne na dɛ, Mimbisa ebɛnadze? Na ne na see dɛ, John Onumanyi no ne tsir.
    25. Na ntsɛmara ɔyɛɛ har baa ɔhen ne nkyɛn, na ɔbɛsee no dɛ, Mepɛ dɛ edze John Onumanyi no ne tsir to prɛtse mu ma me seseiara.
    26. Na ɔyɛɛ ɔhen no yaw dodoodow, na osian ne ntam nye hɔn a wɔnye no tse edzidzi no ntsi, ɔammpɛ dɛ ɔbɛka oho akyerɛ no.
    27. Na ntsɛmara ɔhen no somaa ɔbrafo hyɛɛ no dɛ ɔnkɛfa tsir no mbra; na ɔkɔree kotwaa John ne tsir wɔ efiadze, MRK.6.28, na ɔdze ne tsir no too prɛtse mu bae, na ɔdze maa akataasia no, na akataasia no dze kɛmaa ne na.
    28. Na n'esuafo no tsee no, wɔbae bɛfaa no fun na wɔdze kɔtoo nda bi mu.
    29. Na asomafo no boaa hɔn ano baa Jesus nkyɛn na wɔbɛkaa dza wɔayɛ nye dza wɔakyerɛkyerɛ nyina kyerɛɛ no.
    30. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Homankasa hom mbra sar do nkyɛn beebi, na hom mbɔhom kakra. Osiandɛ nna dodowara reba rokɔ ma wonnya adagyer nndzidzi mpo.
    31. Na wosii mu nam hɛn mu kɔr sar do beebi.
    32. Na nyimpa pii hun dɛ worisi mu, na wonyim hɔn no, wofi nkurow nyina mu tutuu mbirika nam daadze kɔr hɔ, na wotwaar hɔn ano.
    33. Na ɔbaa daadze no, ohun nyimpadodow pii na ne yamu hyehyee no maa hɔn osiandɛ wɔtse dɛ nguan a wonnyi ɔhwɛfo; na ɔhyɛɛ ase kyerɛkyerɛɛ hɔn ndzɛmba pii.
    34. Na adze rebɛsa no, n'esuafo no baa ne nkyɛn bɛsee dɛ, Ha yɛ sar do, na adze so resa;
    35. gyaa hɔn ma wɔnkɔ nkuroba nye nkuraase a etwa ehyia yi mu nkɔtɔ biribi ndzi.
    36. Na Jesus buaa hɔn dɛ, Homara hom mma hɔn biribi ma wondzi. Na wɔsee no dɛ, Ana yɛnkɔtɔ paanoo sika denare ahaenu mfa mma hɔn ma wondzi?
    37. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom wɔ paanoo ahen? Hom nkɔhwɛ. Na wɔkɔhwɛe no wɔsee dɛ, Enum, nye esunam ebien.
    38. Na ɔhyɛɛ hɔn dɛ wɔmma hɔn nyinara ntsenatsena ekuw ekuw wɔ efuw momon no do.
    39. Na wɔtsenatsenaa akyerɛpɛn akyerɛpɛn, ɔha, ɔha, nye eduonum, eduonum.
    40. Na ɔfaa paanoo enum no, nye esunam ebien no, na ɔhwɛɛ sor no, ohyiraa do, na obubuu paanoo no, na ɔdze maa n'esuafo no dɛ wɔmfa ntoto hɔn enyim; na ɔkyɛɛ esunam ebien no mu maa hɔn nyinara.
    41. Na hɔn nyina dzidzii na wɔmee.
    42. Na wɔtasee mporoporoba nkɛntsɛn duebien nye esunam no so bi.
    43. Na hɔn a wodzii paanoo no, wɔyɛ mbarimba mpemenum.
    44. Na ntsɛmara opiapiaa n'esuafo no dɛ wɔnkɔ hɛn mu na wondzi n'enyim kan wɔ esuegya noho wɔ Bɛtsaeda, ma ɔno nhwɛ, ngya nyimpadodow no kwan.
    45. Na ɔkraa hɔn no, osii mu kɔr kokwaa do kɔsɔree.
    46. Na odur anafua no, nna hɛn no wɔ po finimfin, na no nko wɔ daadze.
    47. Na ohun dɛ wɔrofona akwan, osiandɛ mframa esi hɔn enyim no, bɛyɛ anafua dasu a otsia anan no mu no ɔnantseew po enyi baa hɔn nkyɛn, na ɔyɛɛ dɛ ɔrepɛ apa hɔn ho;
    48. na hɔn dze, wohun dɛ ɔrenantsew po enyi no, wɔdween dɛ saman a, na wɔkekaa mu, MRK.6.50, osiandɛ hɔn nyina hun no ma hɔn akoma tui. Na ntsɛmara ɔnye hɔn kasae, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom nhyɛ dzen: mara a, mma hom nnsuro.
    49. Na ɔkɔr hɔn nkyɛn wɔ hɛn no mu, na mframa no gyaae. Na hɔn ho boow hɔn dodoodow koraa, MRK.6.52, osiandɛ wɔanntse paanoo no ho asɛm no ase, nna hɔn akoma epirim.
    50. Na wotwae no, wɔbaa Genesaret asaase do na wodwirii.
    51. Na wofii hɛn mu no, ntsɛmara na wohun no, MRK.6.55, na wotutuu mbirika kekyiin asaase nyina mu, na wɔhyɛɛ ase soaa hɔn a wɔyar wɔ nkɛtɛ do baa beebiara a wɔtsee dɛ ɔwɔ no.

    26. Na hɔn ho boow hɔn mbordo, na wɔsee no dɛ, Dɛm a nna woana koraa na wobotum agye no nkwa?
    27. Na Jesus hwɛɛ hɔn see dɛ, Nyimpa afamu na iyi nntum nnyɛ dɛm, na nnyɛ Nyankopɔn afamu a: na Nyankopɔn afamu dze, adze biara nnso no yɛ.

    36. Na Jesus see hɔn dɛ, Hom nnyim dza hom reserɛ yi: ana hom botum anom kɔɔpow a monom no, anaa wɔdze enuma a wɔdze numa me no enuma hom?
    37. Na wɔsee no dɛ, Yebotum. Na Jesus see hɔn dɛ, Kɔɔpow a monom no dze, hom bɔnom ampa, na enuma a wɔdze numa me no, wɔdze bonuma hom;

    39. Na du no tsee no wɔhyɛɛ ase nyaa ebufuw wɔ James nye John ho.
    40. Na Jesus frɛɛ hɔn baa ne nkyɛn, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom nyim dɛ hɔn a wobu hɔn dɛ wodzi amanaman do, wodzi hɔn do wura, na hɔn mu akɛse no dzi hɔn do tum., #Luk 22.25
    41. Naaso onnyi dɛ ɔba dɛm wɔ hom mu: na obiara a ɔpɛ dɛ ɔbɛyɛ ɔkɛse wɔ hom mu no, ɔbɛyɛ hom somfo;
    42. na obiara a ɔpɛ dɛ odzi hom mu kan no, ɔbɛyɛ hom nyina hom akowaa.

    Tsir 11

    1. Na wɔbɛɛn Jerusalɛm wɔ Bɛtfage nye Bɛtani, wɔ Ngo Bepɔw ho no, ɔsomaa n'esuafo no mu beenu, MRK.11.2, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom nkɔ ekuroba a ɔda hom enyim no mu: na hom du hɔ ara pɛr a, hom bohu asoasopɔnkɔba bi a wɔamontan no a nyimpa biara nntsenaa do da: hom nsan no mfa no mbra.
    2. Na sɛ obi bisa hom dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn na hom reyɛ yi a, hom nse dɛ, Ewuradze wɔ no ho hia, na ntsɛmara ɔbɛma wɔdze no aba ha.
    3. Na wɔkɔree no wokohun asoasopɔnkɔba no dɛ wɔamontan no abow ho, wɔ abɔntsen petee mu, na wɔsaan no.
    4. Hɔn a nna wogyina hɔ no bi bisaa dɛ, Hom resan asoasopɔnkɔba no ayɛ dɛn?
    5. Na wobuaa hɔn dɛ mbrɛ Jesus see hɔn no ara; na wɔmaa hɔn kwan.
    6. Na wɔdze asoasopɔnkɔba no brɛɛ Jesus, na wɔtoow hɔn ntar guu no do; na ɔtsenaa no do.

    9. Nhyira nye hɛn egya David ahendzi a ɔreba no! Hosanna wɔ sorsor!

    13. Na Jesus see adɔmbadua no dɛ, Mma obi nndzi wo do eduaba bio da. Na n'esuafo no tsee.
    14. Na wodur Jerusalɛm; na ɔkɔr asɔrfi no mu, na ɔhyɛɛ ase paam hɔn a wɔtɔn nye hɔn a wɔtɔ wɔ asɔrfi no mu, na obutuuw sikasesafo hɔn apon nye hɔn a wɔtɔn mbubur no hɔn ngua;
    15. na ɔamma kwan amma obiara aannsoa adze ammfa asɔrfi no mu.
    16. Na ɔkyerɛkyerɛe see hɔn dɛ, Ana wɔnnkyerɛw no dɛ, Wɔbɛfrɛ me fi asɔryɛbea ama amanaman nyinara? Naaso hom dze, hom ayɛ no edwotwafo tu.
    17. Na asɔfompanyin nye kyerɛwfo no tsee, na wɔhwehwɛɛ mbrɛ wɔbɛyɛ asɛɛ no: na wosuroo no, osiandɛ ne nkyerɛkyerɛ yɛɛ nyimpadodow nyina ahobow.

    23. Dɛm ntsi mese hom dɛ, Biribiara a hom sɔr bisa no, hom ngye ndzi dɛ hom enya na hom benya.
    24. Na sɛ hom gyina hɔ rosɔr, na sɛ hom nye obi wɔ asɛm bi a, hom mfa nkyɛ, ama hom Egya a ɔwɔ sor no so dze hom mfom aakyɛ hom., #Mat 6.14
    25. [Na sɛ hom ammfa annkyɛ a, hom egya a ɔwɔ sor no so nnkɛfa hom mfom akyɛ hom. ]

    31. Ntsi wobuaa Jesus see no dɛ, Yennyim. Na Jesus see hɔn dɛ, Mo so mennkɛkyerɛ hom tum kor a medze yɛ yinom.

    Tsir 12

    1. Na ɔhyɛɛ ase kasaa kyerɛɛ hɔn abɛbu mu dɛ, Nyimpa bi yɛɛ wenyin ture, na ɔgyee ho ban hyiae, na otuu wenyintwer, na ɔtoow abantsentsen wɔ mu, na ɔdze fɛɛm ekuafo bi, na ɔdɔɔ mu kɔr beebi., #Isa 5.1
    2. Na ber durii no, ɔsomaa akowaa bi wɔ ekuafo no hɔ dɛ ɔnkɛgye wenyin ture eduaba no bi wɔ ekuafo no hɔ mbra.
    3. Na ekuafo no kyeer no hwee no, na wɔmaa ɔdze nsapan kɔree.
    4. Na bio ɔsomaa akowaa fofor wɔ hɔn hɔ, na wɔbobɔɔ ne tsir pirapiraa no, na woguu n'enyim ase.
    5. Na ɔsanee somaa ɔfofor so; na ɔno dze, wokum no, nna afofor pii so: wɔhwee binom na wokum binom.
    6. Nna ɔwɔ obi so wɔ hɔ a ɔno yɛ no dɔba; na ekyir no ɔsomaa ɔno so wɔ hɔn hɔ, na ɔsee dɛ, Wɔbɛfɛr me ba.
    7. Na ekuafo no dze, wɔsesee hɔnho dɛ, Odziadzenyi no nye yi: hom mbra ma yenku no, na egyapadze no bɛyɛ hɛndze.
    8. Na wɔkyeer no kum no, na wɔtoow no fii wenyin ture mu hɔ kyenee.
    9. Dɛm a nna ebɛnadze na wenyin ture no wura bɛyɛ? Ɔbɛba abɛsɛɛ ekuafo no, na ɔdze wenyin ture no ama afofor.
    10. Ana hom nnkenkaan kyerɛw yi pɛn dɛ, Ɔbo a adantofo powee no, Ɔnoara na, ɔabɛyɛ kutuwdo bo;, #Dwom 118.22
    11. Iyi Ewuradze na Ɔayɛ, Na ɔyɛ nwanwa wɔ hɛn enyiwa do?
    12. Na wɔpɛɛ dɛ nkyɛ wɔkyer no; osiandɛ wohun dɛ bɛ yi a obui no tsia hɔn; naaso wosuroo nyimpadodow no ntsi wogyaa no hɔ kɔree.
    13. Na wɔsomaa Farisiifo nye Herod akadofo no mu bi dɛ wonkodua ne kasaa do mfa nsun no efir nkyer no.
    14. Na wɔbae no wɔsee no dɛ, Kyerɛkyerɛfo, yenyim dɛ eyɛ nokwafo, na ennyɛ nyimpa enyim nhwehwɛmu; eso innsuro nyimpa enyim asɛnka, na ekyerɛkyerɛ Nyankopɔn no kwan no wɔ nokwar mu. Ana ɔfa mbra mu dɛ yɛyi ntoboa ma Kaesar anaa oho?
    15. Yɛmma anaa mma yɛmma? Na ohun hɔn ndabraba no ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ebɛnadze ntsi na hom rosɔ me ahwɛ yi? Hom mfa sika denare mbrɛ me ma monhwɛ.
    16. Na wɔdze bae. Na obisaa hɔn dɛ, Woana no mfonyi nye akyerɛw nye yi? Na wɔsee no dɛ, Kaesar dze a.
    17. Na Jesus see hɔn dɛ, Hom mfa dza ɔyɛ Kaesar dze mma Kaesar, na dza ɔyɛ Nyankopɔn dze mma Nyankopɔn. Na no ho yɛɛ hɔn nwanwa dodow.
    18. Na Sadusiifo so bi a wɔka dɛ wunyan nnyi hɔ no baa ne nkyɛn; na wobisaa no dɛ
    19. Kyerɛkyerɛfo, Moses kyerɛwee maa hɛn dɛ, Sɛ obi no nua wu na ogya ne yer, na oenngya mba a, no nua nwar ne yer no na ɔnwo mba mma no nua.
    20. Na enuanom beesuon wɔ hɔ: na nyia odzi kan no waar yer, na owui no, oenngya mba;
    21. na nyia otsia ebien no waar no, na owui, na no so enngya mba; nyia otsia ebiasa no dɛmara;
    22. na beesuon no enngya mba. Ne nyina ekyir no, ɔbaa no so wui.
    23. Na wunyan no mu, hɔn mu woana na ɔbɛyɛ ne yer? Osiandɛ beesuon no awar no.
    24. Jesus see hɔn dɛ, Ana nnyɛ yi ntsi na hom fom yi, dɛ hom nnyim kyerɛwsɛm no nye Nyankopɔn no tum no so?
    25. Na wɔsoɛr fi ewufo mu a, wɔnnwar eso wɔmma awar, na wɔtse dɛ abɔfo a wɔwɔ sor.
    26. Na ewufo ho no dze, dɛ wobenyan no, ana hom nnkenkaan no wɔ Moses nwoma no mu, mbrɛ Nyankopɔn kasaa kyerɛɛ no wɔ Haban no mu dɛ, Emi nye Abraham Nyankopɔn, Isaak Nyankopɔn, Jakob Nyankopɔn?
    27. Ampaara, nnyɛ ewufo Nyankopɔn, mbom enyikamfo Nyankopɔn a: dɛm ntsi hom fom pii.
    28. Na kyerɛwfo no mu baako bae bɛtsee dɛ woribisabisa nsɛm no, na ohun dɛ Jesus ebua hɔn yie no, obisaa no dɛ, Mbransɛm no nyina dza ɔwɔ hen na odzi kan?

    30. na dɔ Ewuradze wo Nyankopɔn wɔ w'akoma nyina mu nye wo kra nyina mu, nye w'adwen nyina mu, nye w'ahoɔdzen nyina mu.
    31. Na dza otsia ebien no nye yi: Dɔ wo nyɛnko dɛ wankasa wo ho. Mbrasɛm biara nnyi hɔ a ɔso kyɛn yinom.
    32. Na kyerɛwnyi no see no dɛ, Kyerɛkyerɛfo, ampa aka no yie dɛ Nyankopɔn yɛ kor; na obi nnyi hɔ dɛ ɔno nko.

    35. Na Jesus rekyerɛkyerɛ wɔ asɔrfi no mu no, obisaa dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn na kyerɛwfo no ka dɛ Kraest no yɛ David ba?
    36. Na David nankasa nam Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn mu ka dɛ, Ewuradze see m'Ewuradze dɛ, Tsena m'abanyimfa Kodu dɛ medze w'atamfo mebɛyɛ w'ananadzegua.
    37. Dɛm ntsi David noara frɛ no Ewuradze; na ofi hen bɛyɛɛ ne ba? Nna nyimpadodow nyina dze enyigye tsiee no.
    38. Na ɔkae wɔ ne nkyerɛkyerɛ mu dɛ, Hom nhwɛ yie wɔ akyerɛwfo a wɔpɛ ntar atsentsen mu nantsew, nye gua do nkyiakyia;
    39. ɔnye mpanyin gua wɔ nhyiamudan mu, nye mpanyin tsenabea wɔ edzidzi ase ho:
    40. hɔn na wɔbɔ ekunafo epoo, na wopetuw bɔ mpaa atsentsen kyerɛ: mese, woyinom benya afɔbu kɛsenara.
    41. Na ɔtsenaa ase wɔ sikakorabea no ne tsentsendo na ɔhwɛɛ mbrɛ nyimpadodow no sii dze sika guu sikakorabea no mu; na dodow a wɔyɛ ahonyafo no dze pii guu mu.
    42. Na ɔbaa kunafo hianyi bi so bae bɔtoow mpɛsewamba ebien a wɔbɛyɛ pɛsewa kor guu mu.
    43. Na Jesus frɛɛ n'esuafo see hɔn dɛ, Meka no nokwar mu kyerɛ hom dɛ, okunafo hianyi yi dze pii ebogu mu akyɛn hɔn a wɔdze bi ebogu sikakorabea no mu no nyina:
    44. osiandɛ hɔn nyina dza wɔwɔ ma abor hɔn do no, nna wɔdze bi ebogu mu, na oyi dze, ofi ne hia mu dze dza ɔwɔ nyina, n'asetsena nyina na ɔdze ebogu mu.

    Tsir 13

    1. Na orifi asɔrfi mu no, n'esuafo no mu baako see no dɛ, Kyerɛkyerɛfo, hwɛ, abo akɛse bi nye aban akɛse bi so!
    2. Na Jesus see no dɛ, Ehu aban akɛse yi? Wonnkegya ɔbo kor a ɔda kor do a wonnkodwuruw egu famu.
    3. Na ɔtse Ngo Bepɔw no do wɔ asɔrfi tsentsendo no, Peter, James, John nye Andrew bisaa no nsumaa mu dɛ, MRK.13.4, Kyerɛ hɛn aber a iyinom bɛyɛ hɔ: na sɛ iyinom nyina rebɛba mu a, n'asɛnkyerɛdze nye dɛn?
    4. Na Jesus hyɛɛ ase see hɔn dɛ, Hom nhwɛ yie amma obi aanndaadaa hom.
    5. Na dodowara bɛba wɔ me dzin mu dɛ, Emi nye no, na wɔadaadaa pii.
    6. Na sɛ hom tse ko nye akosɛm a, mma hom akoma nntu: na otwar dɛ ɔba, na ewiei no nnyae nndurii.
    7. Na ɔman bɔsoɛr ɔman do, na ahenman bɔsoɛr ahenman do; na asaasewosow bɛba mbeambea, na ɔkɔm bɛba: iyinom dze, awoko no ahyɛse ara a.
    8. Na hom nhwɛ homho yie: na wobeyi hom akɔ mpanyimfo bagua mu, na wɔahwe hom wɔ nhyiamudan mu, na hom egyina ambradofo nye ahemfo enyim wɔ me ntsi, dze edzi dase akyerɛ hɔn., #Mat 10.17-20; Luk 12.11
    9. Na asɛmpa no, otwar dɛ wɔka kyerɛ amanaman nyina ansaana.
    10. Na sɛ wɔdze hom rokɔ ekeyi hom ama a, mma hom nndwendwen dza hom bɛka ho ansaana: na biribi a wɔdze bɛma hom dɔn no mu no, ɔnoara na hom nka: osiandɛ nnyɛ hom na hom kasa, na Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn no a.
    11. Na nua beyi nua ama owu, egya beyi ɔba ama; na mba bɔsoɛr wɔ awofo do na wɔama woeku hɔn.

    13. Na sɛ hom hu amambɔe ekyiwadze no dɛ ogyina mbrɛ ɔnnsɛ a (ma nyia ɔrekenkan no ntse ase dɛ ogyina hɔ ma ohonyi no), ɔno na hɔn a wɔwɔ Judea no nguan nkɔ nkokwaa do;
    14. na mma nyimpa a ɔwɔ dan etsifi nnsian mmba dan mu, na eso mma ɔnnhɛn mu nnkɛfa biribi wɔ ne dan mu;
    15. na mma nyia ɔwɔ haban mu no nnsan n'ekyir bio mmbɛfa ne nkatado.
    16. Na hɔn a wɔafa efur nye hɔn a wɔma numfo wɔ nda no mu ndue!
    17. Na hom nsɔr ma iyinom aammba awɔwber mu.
    18. Na nda no mu bɛba ahohiahia kɛse a ofitsi Nyankopɔn ne wiadze mbɔe ahyɛse araa bodu aber no ɔmmbaa dɛm da, eso bi remmba da.
    19. Na sɛ Ewuradze enntwa nda no do tsia a, nkyɛ honam biara nnkenya nkwa, na ne mpamufo a ɔpaa hɔn no ntsi oetwa nda no do tsia.
    20. Na aber no sɛ obiara se hom dɛ, Hwɛ, Kraest no wɔ ha, anaaso, Hwɛ, ɔwɔ hɔ no a, mma hom nngye nndzi, MRK.13.22, na Kraest atorfo, nye nkɔnhyɛfo atorfo bɔsoɛr na wɔakyerɛ nsɛnkyerɛdze nye anwanwadze a sɛ anhwɛ a, wɔdze bɛdaadaa mpamufo no mpo.
    21. Na hom dze, hom nhwɛ yie: osiandɛ medzi kan aka ne nyina akyerɛ hom.

    23. na nworaba fi sor bɔtotɔ, na sor etum bɔwosow.

    26. Hom nsua adɔmbadua no ho bɛ no: sɛ n'abaw yɛ momon, na oriyi ahaban a, nna hom nyim dɛ ɔhyewber abɛn;
    27. dɛmara na hom so sɛ hom hu dɛ yinom yɛ hɔ a, hom nhu dɛ abɛn, edu abowano.
    28. Nokwar mese hom dɛ, Mbasantsen yi nnkotwa mu akɔ gyedɛ yinom nyina ayɛ hɔ ansaana.

    30. Na da no nye dɔn no dze obiara nnyim: abɔfo a wɔwɔ sor no so nnyim, Ɔba no so nnyim, gyedɛ Egya no nko.
    31. Hom nhwɛ yie, hom nweɔn na hom mbɔ mpaa, na hom nnyim da pɔtsee a ber no bodu.
    32. Ɔtse dɛ nyimpa a ɔrodɔ mu a ogyaa ne fi, na ɔmaa no nkowaa tum, na ɔmaa hɔn nyina hɔn edwuma nkorkor, na ɔhyɛɛ abowanohwɛfo no dɛ ɔnweɔn.
    33. Dɛm ntsi hom nweɔn, na hom nnyim dɔn kor a ofiwura bɛba: adansaber o, esuom o, akokɔbɔnee o, anapahema o;
    34. amma ɔaammba awerɛfir mu emmbohu hom dɛ hom deda.
    35. Na dza meka kyerɛ hom yi, meka kyerɛ nyimpa nyina so dɛ, Hom nweɔn.

    Tsir 14

    1. Nna aka nda ebien ma apaho nye epitsi afahyɛ no edu; na asɔfompanyin nye kyerɛwfo no hwehwɛɛ mbrɛ wɔbɛyɛ nam nyansa mu akyer Jesus na woeku no:
    2. na wɔsee dɛ, Nnyɛ afahyɛ mu bi a, amma wentwiwentwi aammba ɔman no mu.
    3. Na ɔwɔ Bɛtani Simon kwatanyi no fie, na ɔtse edzidzi no, ɔbaa bi baa hɔ a ɔdze alabastron toa a nard ngohuam a ɔsom bo wɔ mu; na ɔbɔɔ toa no na ohuei guu n'etsifi.
    4. Na binom wɔ hɔ a wonyaa ebufuw wɔ hɔn mu, na wɔsee dɛ, Ebɛnadze ntsi na wɔresɛɛ ngohuam yi?
    5. Nkyɛ wobotum atɔn ngohuam yi agye bɔbor sika denare ahaasa na wɔdze ama ehiafo. Na wohwinhwinii tsiaa no.
    6. Na Jesus see dɛ, Hom ngyaa no; ebɛnadze ntsi na hom rehaw no? Ɔayɛ edwuma pa wɔ mo ho.
    7. Na hom wɔ ehiafo wɔ hom nkyɛn daa, na aber biara a hom pɛ, hom botum ayɛ hɔn yie, na emi dze, hom nnyi me daa.
    8. Ɔayɛ dza obotum: oedzi kan abɛsera me nyimpadua akɔ sie mu.
    9. Nokwar mese hom dɛ, Beebiara a wɔbɛka asɛmpa yi wɔ wiadze nyina mu no, dza ɔbaa yi ayɛ so wɔbɛka dze ayɛ ne nkaa.
    10. Na Judas Iskariot a ɔyɛ duebien no mu baako fii hɔ kɔr asɔfompanyin no nkyɛn dɛ obeyi Jesus ama hɔn.
    11. Na wɔtsee no, ɔyɛɛ hɔn dɛw, na wɔbɔɔ anohoba dɛ wɔbɛma no sika. Na ɔhwehwɛɛ mbrɛ obesi eyi no ama wɔ ber a ɔsɛ mu.
    12. Na epitsi da a odzi kan a woku apahoguan no, n'esuafo no see no dɛ, Hen na epɛ dɛ yekesiesie ma ikedzi apaho no?
    13. Na ɔsomaa n'esuafo no mu beenu, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom nkɔ kurow no mu, na nyimpa bi a ɔso nsuhina behyia hom: hom nka no do;
    14. na beebiara a ɔbɛhɛn no hom nse ofiwura no dɛ, Kyerɛkyerɛfo no se, M'ahɔhodan a menye m'esuafo bedzi mu apaho no wɔ hen?
    15. Na ɔnoara ɔbɛkyerɛ hom sordan kɛse bi a wɔahyehyɛ mu, na woesiesie mu; hɔ na hom nsiesie mma hɛn.
    16. Na esuafo no sii mu kɔree, na wɔbaa kurow no mu na wobohun dɛ mbrɛ ɔsee hɔn no, na wosiesiee apaho no.
    17. Na odur ewimbir no, ɔnye duebien no bae.
    18. Na wɔtwetwer hɔ ridzidzi no, Jesus see dɛ, Ampa mese hom dɛ, Hom mu baako a ɔnye me dzidzi na obeyi me ama.
    19. Na ɔyɛɛ hɔn awerɛhow na wɔhyɛɛ ase nkorkor bisaa no dɛ, Ana emi a?
    20. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Duebien no mu baako a ɔnye me to nsa wɔ ɔsɛn no mu no a.
    21. Na nyimpa Ba no rokɔ, dɛ mbrɛ wɔakyerɛw wɔ no ho; naaso nyimpa a wɔnam no do beyi nyimpa Ba no ama no ndue! Sɛ wɔannwo dɛm nyimpa no a, nkyɛ oye ma no.
    22. Na woridzidzi no, ɔfaa paanoo na ohyiraa do na obubui, na ɔdze maa hɔn; na ɔsee dɛ, Hom ngye: iyi nye me nyimpadua.
    23. Na ɔfaa kɔɔpow na ɔdaa ase no, ɔdze maa hɔn ma hɔn nyina nomee.
    24. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Iyi nye mo bɔgyaa a ɔyɛ ahyɛmu no a dodowara ntsi woruhue egu no.
    25. Nokwar mese hom dɛ, moronnom wenyinaba no bi bio, dze ekodu da a mobɔnom no fofor wɔ Nyankopɔn ahenman no mu.
    26. Na wɔtoow ndwom no, wofiir edzi kɔr Ngo Bepɔw no do.
    27. Na Jesus see hɔn dɛ, Wɔbɔto hom nyina fintsidua: na wɔakyerɛw dɛ, Mobɔbɔ oguanhwɛfo no, na nguan no abɔ apetse.
    28. Naaso mo wusoɛr ekyir no mibedzi hom enyim akɔ Galilii.
    29. Na Peter see no dɛ, Sɛ wɔbɔto hɔn nyina fintsidua mpo a, nnyɛ emi a.
    30. Na Jesus see no dɛ, Nokwar mese wo dɛ, Ndɛ anadwe yi, ansaana akokɔ bɔbɔn mprɛnu no, ebɛpa me mprɛnsa.
    31. Na Peter dze, mbom ɔkaa no enyiberdo dɛ, Sɛ menye wo bowu mpo a, meremmpa wo da; na dɛmara na hɔn nyina so kae.
    32. Na wodur beebi a wɔfrɛ hɔ Getsemane; na ɔsee n'esuafo no dɛ, Hom ntsena ha ma monkɔsɔr.
    33. Na ɔfaa Peter nye James nye John kaa noho, na ɔhyɛɛ ase bɔɔ hu na ɔbɔmee.
    34. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Awerɛhow ahyɛ me kra do dze kɔ owu mu: hom ntsena ha, na hom nweɔn.
    35. Na ɔkɔr enyim kakra, na ɔkɔhwee famu na ɔsɔɔr dɛ, sɛ ɔbɛyɛ a, dɔn no mpa no ho nkɔ.
    36. Na ɔsee dɛ, Aba, Egya! Adze biara nnso wo yɛ: yi kɔɔpow yi pa mo ho; naaso nnyɛ dza mepɛ bi a, na dza ɔwo epɛ a.
    37. Na ɔbae bohun dɛ wɔdeda, na ɔsee Peter dɛ, Simon, ana ada? Ana enntum annweɔn dɔnhwer kor mpo?
    38. Hom nweɔn na hom nsɔr, amma hom aannkɔ nsɔhwɛ mu: sunsum no pɛ dze, naaso honam yɛ mberɛw.
    39. Na osii mu kɔr bio, na ɔkɔsɔree kaa asɛm kor no ara.
    40. Na ɔsanee bae bohun hɔn dɛ wɔdeda bio, na hɔn enyiwa akom; na woennya mbuae bi amma no.
    41. Na ɔbaa ne mpɛn ebiasa, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom nda dza aka yi, na hom nhom. Ayɛ yie! Dɔn no edu; hwɛ, woriyi nyimpa Ba no ahyɛ ndzebɔnyɛfo nsa.
    42. Hom nsoɛr ma yɛnkɔ; hwɛ, nyia oriyi me ama no abɛn.
    43. Na hɔnara, ɔda do rekasa no, Judas a ɔyɛ duebien no mu baako bae, na nyimpadodow a wɔdzedze nkrantsee nye mpɔtsibaa fi asɔfompanyin nye kyerɛwfo nye mpanyimfo no hɔ ka no ho.
    44. Na nyia oyii no mae no maa hɔn asɛnkyerɛdze dɛ, Nyia mebɛfew n'ano no, nye ɔno: hom nkyer no na hom nsuo no mu yie mfa no nkɔ.
    45. Na odur hɔ no, ntsɛmara na ɔbaa Jesus nkyɛn na ɔsee no dɛ, Kyerɛkyerɛfo, na ɔfeew n'ano.
    46. Na wɔdze hɔn nsa susuoo no mu na wɔkyekyeer no.
    47. Na hɔn a wogyinagyina hɔ no mu baako twee ne krantsee bɔɔ ɔsɔfopanyin n'akowaa twaa n'aso.
    48. Na Jesus buae see hɔn dɛ, Ana hom firii baa mo do dɛ hom reba owifo do, na hom dze nkrantsee nye mpɔtsibaa rebɛkyer me yi?
    49. Daa mowɔ hom nkyɛn wɔ asɔrfi mu kyerɛkyerɛe no, hom annkyer me; na dɛ kyerɛwsɛm no bɛba mu ntsi a.
    50. Na esuafo no nyinara gyaa no hɔ, na woguanee.
    51. Na aberantsɛ baako bi kaa no do a nna oetu ndwera akata n'adagyaw do; na wɔkyeer no;
    52. na ogyaa ndwera no hɔ na ɔdze adagyaw guanee.
    53. Na wɔdze Jesus kɔr ɔsɔfopanyin no hɔ; na asɔfompanyin nye mpanyimfo nye kyerɛwfo no nyina behyiaa ne nkyɛn.
    54. Na Peter dzii n'ekyir wɔ ekyirekyir dze kodur ɔsɔfopanyin n'abanguado, na ɔnye asomfo no kɛtsenaa hɔ too gya.
    55. Na asɔfompanyin nye baguafo no nyina hwehwɛɛ dɛ wobenya Jesus ho dase bi dze eeku no; naaso woennya bi.
    56. Na dodowara gyee no ho dasewia, na hɔn dasee annsaa.
    57. Na binom soɛree gyee no ho dasewia dɛ, MRK.14.58, Yɛtsee dɛ ɔsee dɛ, Mubodwuruw asɔrdan a wɔdze nsa ayɛ yi na ndaansa mu matow bi a wɔammfa nsa annyɛ.
    58. Na dɛm no mpo so hɔn dasee annsaa.
    59. Na ɔsɔfopanyin no gyinaa finimfin na obisaa Jesus dɛ, Immbua bi? Ebɛnadze na yinom regye wo ho dase yi?
    60. Na omuaa n'ano, na oemmbua bi. Na ɔsɔfopanyin no bisaa no bio dɛ, Ana ɔwo nye Kraest no, nyia woehyira no no ne Ba no?
    61. Na Jesus see dɛ, Emi nye no: na hom bohu nyimpa Ba no dɛ ɔtse tum no abanyimfa, na ɔreba wɔ sor nsuwusiw mu.
    62. Na ɔsɔfopanyin no sunsuaan n'atar mu, na ɔsee dɛ, Ebɛn hia na ohia hɛn adasefo bio?
    63. Hom atse ebususɛm no: hom dwen dɛn? Na hɔn nyinara buu no fɔ dɛ ɔsɛ owu.
    64. Na binom hyɛɛ ase hinaa ntafi guu no do, na wɔkataa n'enyi, na wɔbobɔɔ no ekutuku, na wɔsee no dɛ, Hyɛ nkɔm! Na asomfo no bobɔɔ no asotɔr.
    65. Na Peter wɔ ase wɔ abangua no do hɔ no, ɔsɔfopanyin ne mbaawa no mu baako bae, MRK.14.67, na ohun Peter dɛ ɔroto gya no, ɔhwɛɛ no dzinn na ɔsee no dɛ, Ɔwo so nye Nazarɛtnyi Jesus nam.
    66. Na ɔsaan dɛ, Minnyim no, eso menntse dza ereka yi ase mpo. Na ofiir edzi kɔr paado; na akokɔ bɔnee.
    67. Na abaawa no hun no na ɔhyɛɛ ase see hɔn a wogyina hɔ no dɛ, Oyi so fi hɔn mu.
    68. Na ɔsaan bio. Na ɔkyɛɛr kakra no, hɔn a wogyina hɔ no see Peter bio dɛ, Ampa dɛ ifi hɔn mu, osiandɛ eyɛ Galiliinyi.
    69. Na ɔhyɛɛ ase dzii nsew na ɔkekaa ntam dɛ, Minnyim nyimpa yi a hom reka no ho asɛm yi.
    70. Na ntsɛmara na akokɔ bɔɔn ne mpɛn ebien. Na Peter kaa asɛm no a Jesus kaa kyerɛɛ no no dɛ, Ansaana akokɔ bɔbɔn mprɛnu no, ebɛpa me mprɛnsa. Na ɔhyɛɛ ase sui.

    Tsir 15

    1. Na adze kyee ara nna asɔfompanyin nye mpanyimfo no nye kyerɛwfo nye baguafo no nyina kɔr apam, na wɔkyekyeer Jesus dze no kɔree kɛhyɛɛ Pilate nsa.
    2. Na Pilate bisaa no dɛ, Ana ɔwo nye Jewfo Hen no? Na Jesus buaa no see dɛ, Ɔwoara na aka.
    3. Na asɔfompanyin no toatoaa no pii.
    4. Na Pilate bisaa no bio see dɛ, Ana irummbua hwee? Hwɛ ndzɛmba pii a wɔdze toatoa wo yi.
    5. Naaso Jesus emmbua hwee bio, na ɔyɛɛ Pilate nwanwa.
    6. Nna afahyɛ du a, ɔtɔ dɛ Pilate gyaa daduanyi kor biara a wɔbɛserɛ no ma hɔn.
    7. Nna woegu nyimpa bi a wɔfrɛ no Barabbas nye hɔn a wɔka no ho a wɔtseew etua na wɔnam etuatsew no do dzii ewu no mpokyerɛ.
    8. Na nyimpadodow no kɔr Pilate hɔ hyɛɛ ase serɛɛ no dɛ ɔnyɛ mma hɔn dɛ mbrɛ ɔtɔ dɛ ɔyɛ no.
    9. Na Pilate buaa hɔn dɛ, Ana hom pɛ dɛ migyaa Jewfo Hen no mema hom?
    10. Na ohun dɛ enyitan ntsi na asɔfompanyin no yii no mae.
    11. Na asɔfompanyin no hyɛɛ nyimpadodow no tsipia dɛ mbom ongyaa Barabbas mma hɔn.
    12. Na Pilate buae see hɔn bio dɛ, Ebɛnadze na hom pɛ dɛ meyɛ nyia hom frɛ no Jewfo Hen no?
    13. Na wɔtseaa mu bio dɛ, Bɔ no mbeamudua mu!
    14. Na Pilate see hɔn dɛ, Ebɛn bɔn na ɔayɛ? Na wɔtseatseaa mu mbordo dɛ, Bɔ no mbeamudua mu!
    15. Na Pilate pɛɛ dɛ ɔma nyimpadodow no abodwee no, ogyaa Barabbas maa hɔn, na Jesus dze, ɔkaa no mpire no, oyii no mae dɛ wɔnkɔbɔ no mbeamudua mu.
    16. Na nsordaafo no dze no kɔr abangua no a ɔyɛ Ambrado Aban no do; na wɔfrɛfrɛɛ nsordaafokuw no nyina boaa ano.
    17. Na wɔdze tam memen furaa no, na wɔbɔɔ nsoɛ katraka soaa no;
    18. na wɔhyɛɛ ase kyiakyiaa no dɛ, Jewfo Hen nye yi o!
    19. Na wɔdze ndzɛmbir bɔɔ n'etsifi, na wohinahinaa ntafi guu no do, na wobuu ntwɛr sɔɔr no.
    20. Na wosii no atwetwe wiei no woyii tam memen no wɔ no ho, na wɔdze nankasa n'atar hyɛɛ no, na wɔdze no kɔr dɛ wɔrokɔbɔ no mbeamudua mu.
    21. Na wɔhyɛɛ Simon Sirenenyi bi a ofi kwaa mu rutwa mu, Alɛksandar nye Rufus hɔn egya no, dɛ ɔnsoa ne mbeamudua no.
    22. Na wɔdze no kɔr beebi a wɔfrɛ hɔ Golgota a ne nkyerɛase nye Tsikwankora Bea.
    23. Na wɔmaa no wenyin a wɔdze eduhuam mɛɛr afora mu, naaso ɔannom.
    24. Na wɔbɔɔ no mbeamudua mu, na wɔkyɛɛ n'atar mu, twee tonto hwehwɛɛ dza obiara bɛfa.
    25. Na anapa ndɔnkron no mu na wɔbɔɔ no mbeamudua mu.
    26. Na wɔkyerɛɛw dza wogyinaa do buu no fɔ no wɔ n'etsifi dɛ JEWFO HEN.
