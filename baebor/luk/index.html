    4. Nna hɔn beenu nyina yɛ atseneneefo wɔ Nyankopɔn enyim, na wodzii Ewuradze ne mbransɛm ɔnye n'ahyɛdze no nyina do, dɛ asɛm biara ho nnyi, LUK.1.7, mbom nna wonnyi ba, osiandɛ nna Elizabet yɛ ɔbɔnyin, na afei hɔn beenu nyina so enyinyin papaapa.
    5. Da bi nna esi n'asɔfokuw do dɛ wɔyɛ edwuma wɔ Nyankopɔn enyim, LUK.1.9, na dɛ mbrɛ asɔfo dwuma no ne nhyehyɛɛ tse no, wɔtwee tonto yii no dɛ ɔnkɔ Ewuradze asɔrdan no mu nkɛhyew eduhuam.
    6. Na ber a ɔrehyew eduhuam no, nna ɔman no nyimpadodow nyina gyina abowano rosɔr.
    7. Na Ewuradze no bɔfoe begyinaa eduhuam afɔrmbukyia no nyimfa yii noho kyerɛɛ no.
    8. Na Zekaria hun no no, n'akoma tui, na hu kaa no.
    9. Na bɔfo no see no dɛ, Mma nnsuro, Zekaria, na wɔatse wo nkotoserɛ, na wo yer Elizabet bɔwo babanyin ama wo, na ato no dzin John.
    10. Na ibenya dɛw nye enyigye, na nyimpa dodowara bedzi n'awoo ho dɛw.
    11. Na ɔbɛyɛ kɛse wɔ Ewuradze enyim, na ɔnnkɔnom wenyin anaaso nsa biara, na Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn bɛhyɛ no ma fi ne na ne yamu mpo.
    12. Na ɔbɛdan Israelmba dodowara aba Ewuradze hɔn Nyankopɔn hɔ.
    13. Na ɔnam Elija no sunsum nye no tum mu bedzi n'enyim akɛdan egyanom hɔn akoma aba hɔn mba ho, nye asoɔdzenfo aba tseneneefo hɔn nyimdzee mu, na oesiesie ɔman a wɔaboaboa hɔnho ama Ewuradze., #Mal 4.5
    14. Na Zekaria see bɔfo no dɛ, Mubodua dɛn mehu yi? Osiandɛ meyɛ akɔdaa na me yer so edzi mfe pii.
    15. Na bɔfo no buae see no dɛ, Emi nye Gabriel a migyina Nyankopɔn enyim, na wɔasoma me dɛ membɛkasa nkyerɛ wo, na menka wo nsɛmpa yi.
    16. Na hwɛ, ebɔtɔ mmum, na irunntum nnkasa dze akɛpem da a yinom bɛyɛ hɔ, osiandɛ anngye me nsɛm a ɔbɛba mu wɔ n'aber do no enndzi.
    17. Na ɔyɛɛ nkorɔfo a wɔrotweɔn Zekaria no nwanwa dɛ ɔakyɛr dɛm wɔ asɔrdan no mu.
    18. Na opuei no, oenntum annkasa annkyerɛ hɔn; na wohun dɛ oekenya enyido adzehu bi wɔ asɔrdan mu hɔ; na ɔnodze, ɔkɔr do araa dze ne nsa yɛɛ nsɛnkyerɛdze osiandɛ nna ɔda ho araa tɔ mmum.
    19. Na ɔbaa dɛ no som nda wiee du no, osii mu kɔr nankasa ne fie.
    20. Na nda no ekyir no ne yer Elizabet nyinsenii na osumaa noho abosoom enum see dɛ, LUK.1.25, Dza Ewuradze ayɛ me, wɔ nda a ɔabɔhwɛ me dɛ obeyi m'enyimguase wɔ nyimpa mu no, nye yi.
    21. Na bosoom a otsia esia no mu no, Nyankopɔn somaa ɔbɔfo Gabriel wɔ Galilii kurow bi a wɔfrɛ no Nazarɛt, LUK.1.27, wɔ ɔbaabun bi a wɔdze no ama ɔbanyin bi a wɔfrɛ no Josef a ofi David ebusua mu no awar no hɔ; na ɔbaabun no ne dzin dze Mary.
    22. Na ɔbaa no hɔ bɛsee no dɛ, Ei, ɔwo a wɔadom wo kɛse, dzi dɛw! Ewuradze dzi w'ekyir.
    23. Na n'akoma tui wɔ kasa no ho, na ɔdwendween ne tsir mu dɛ ebɛn nkyia na iyi bɛyɛ.
    24. Na ɔbɔfo no see no dɛ, Mary, mma nnsuro, na enya Nyankopɔn enyim adom.
    25. Na hwɛ, ibenyinsen, na awo babanyin na ato no dzin JESUS.

    27. na obedzi Jakob fi do hen daapem, na n'ahendzi nnkɔbɔ adze da.
    28. Na Mary see ɔbɔfo no dɛ, Minnyim banyin yi, yinom besi dɛn aba mu?
    29. Na bɔfo no buae see no dɛ, Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn bɛba wo do, na nyia ɔwɔ sorsor no n'ahoɔdzen bɛkata wo do; dɛm ntsi so adze krɔnkrɔn a wɔbɔwo no no, wɔbɛfrɛ no Nyankopɔn Ba.
    30. Na hwɛ, wo busuanyi Elizabet so enyinsen babanyin wɔ ne mberwaber mu, na nyia wɔfrɛ no ɔbɔnyin no, n'abosoom esia nye yi.
    31. Na Nyankopɔn afamu dze, biribiara nnyi hɔ a onntum nnyɛ hɔ.
    32. Na Mary see dɛ, Hwɛ, Ewuradze afonabaa nye me: ɔnyɛ hɔ mma me dɛ mbrɛ w'asɛm se. Na bɔfo no fii ne nkyɛn kɔree.
    33. Na nda no mu ara Mary soɛɛr ntsɛntsɛm kɔr Juda nkokodo nkurow no bi mu;
    34. na ɔkɛhɛɛn Zekaria fie kekyiaa Elizabet.
    35. Na ɔbaa dɛ Elizabet tsee Mary ne nkyia no, ɔba no huruuw ne yamu; na Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn hyɛɛ Elizabet ma, LUK.1.42, na ɔdze ndze kɛse kasae see dɛ, Nhyira aka wo wɔ mbasiafo mu, na nhyira aka wo yamu aba.
    36. Na iyi fi hen ba mo hɔ, dɛ m'Ewuradze ne na bɛba me nkyɛn?
    37. Na hwɛ, ntsɛmara a wo nkyia twaa m'asomu no, na abofra no dze dɛw huruhuruwii wɔ mo mu.
    38. Na nhyira aka nyia ɔagye edzi: na dza Ewuradze ama wɔabɛka akyerɛ no no bɛba mu.
    39. Na Mary see dɛ: Me kra tonton Ewuradze
    40. Na mo sunsum dzi dɛw wɔ me Nyankopɔn m'Agyenkwa no mu.
    41. Osiandɛ ɔabɔhwɛ n'afonabaa wɔ ne mbrɛadze asetsena mu: Na hwɛ, ofitsi ndɛ ebusuasantsen nyina bɛfrɛ me osiarfo.

    46. Ɔdze ndzepa amee hɔn a ɔkɔm dze hɔn, na oegya ahonyafo kwan wɔ yafumpan mu.
    47. Oesuo n'akowaa Israel mu dɛ ɔdze bɛkaa n'ehumbɔbɔr, LUK.1.55, dɛ mbrɛ ɔkaa kyerɛɛ hɛn egyanom no, ama Abraham nye n'asefo daa.
    48. Na Mary nye Elizabet tsenaa bɛyɛ abosoom ebiasa, na ɔsanee kɔr noara ne fie.
    49. Na Elizabet n'aber a ɔdze bɔwo durii, na ɔwoo babanyin.
    50. Na no sɔnmufo nye n'ebusuafo tsee ehumbɔbɔr kɛse a Ewuradze dze ehu no, na wɔnye no dzii dɛw.

    52. naaso ne na buae see dɛ, Oho, na wɔbɛfrɛ no John.

    56. Na n'ano nye ne tɛkyerɛma buee amonmu hɔ ara, na ɔkasae yii Nyankopɔn ayɛw.
    57. Na hu kaa hɔn a wɔtsetse hɔn nkyɛn nyina, na nsɛm yi nyina wɔkekae wɔ Judea nkokodo nkurow nyina mu.
    58. Na hɔn a wɔtsee nyina dze too hɔn akoma mu dɛ, Abofra bɛn na oyi bɛyɛ? Osiandɛ Ewuradze ne nsa wɔ no do.

    63. dze nkwagye ho nyimdzee akɛma no nkorɔfo wɔ hɔn bɔnfakyɛ mu a

    Tsir 2

    1. Na ɔbaa dɛ nda no mu no, mbra bi fii Kaesar Augustus hɔ baa dɛ wɔnkan wiadze nyina nkyerɛw hɔn edzin.
    2. Iyi nye ɔkankyerɛw asɔpɔn no a wɔyɛɛ no ber a Kerinius dzi Siria ambrado no.
    3. Na nyimpa nyina kɔr hɔnara hɔn nkurow mu dɛ wɔnkyerɛw hɔn dzin.
    4. Na Josef so fii Galilii, wɔ Nazarɛt kurow no mu, kɔr Judea, David kurow a wɔfrɛ no Bɛtlɛhɛm no mu, osiandɛ ofi David fie nye n'ebusua mu ntsi, LUK.2.5, ama wɔaakan ɔno nye n'esiyer Mary a ne yafun no enyin no.
    5. Na ɔbaa dɛ, wɔwɔ hɔ no, ne nda a ɔsɛ dɛ ɔdze wo durii.
    6. Na ɔwoo n'abakan banyimba, na ɔdze adasoe kyekyeer no ho, na ɔdze no too mbowa edzidzidaka mu, osiandɛ ɔkwan nnyi ahɔhodan no mu ma hɔn.
    7. Nna nguanhwɛfo bi wɔ ɔman no ara mu, da haban mu roweɔn hɔn nguankuw anadwe.
    8. Na Ewuradze bɔfoe begyinaa hɔn nkyɛn, na Ewuradze n'enyimnyam hyerɛnee bɔɔ hɔn ho aprɔw, na hu kaa hɔn kɛse.
    9. Na ɔbɔfo no see hɔn dɛ, Mma hom nnsuro; na hwɛ, mebrɛ hom nsɛmpa a ɔyɛ dɛw kɛse a ɔbɛyɛ aman nyina dze, LUK.2.11, na ndɛ wɔawo Agyenkwa ama hom wɔ David kurow mu, ɔno nye Kraest Ewuradze.
    10. Na iyi nye hom asɛnkyerɛdze: hom bohu abofra bi dɛ ɔkyekyer adasoe mu da mbowa edzidzidaka mu.
    11. Na awerɛfir mu ara ɔsor dɔm pii bɛkaa ɔbɔfo no ho yii Nyankopɔn ayɛw see dɛ, LUK.2.14, Enyimnyam nka Nyankopɔn wɔ sorsor, Na asomdwee nka nyimpa a wɔsɔ n'enyi wɔ asaase do.
    12. Na ɔbaa dɛ abɔfo no fii hɔn nkyɛn kɔr sor no, nguanhwɛfo no sesee hɔnho dɛ, Hom mma yɛnkɔ Bɛtlɛhɛm nkɔhwɛ dza ɔaba a Ewuradze eyi akyerɛ hɛn yi.
    13. Na wɔbaa ntsɛntsɛm, na wobohun Mary nye Josef nye abofra no dɛ ɔda mbowa edzidzidaka mu.
    14. Na wobohun no no, wɔkaa asɛm a wɔkaa kyerɛɛ hɔn wɔ abofra no ho no ma ɔtahyee.
    15. Na hɔn a wɔtsee nyina, dza nguanhwɛfo no kaa kyerɛɛ hɔn no yɛɛ hɔn nwanwa.
    16. Na Mary dze, ɔdze nsɛm yi nyina siei, na ɔdween ho wɔ n'akoma mu.
    17. Na nguanhwɛfo no sanee kɔree kɛhyɛɛ Nyankopɔn enyimnyam na woyii no ayɛw wɔ dza wɔtsee na wokohunii dɛ mbrɛ wɔkaa kyerɛɛ hɔn nyina tse no.
    18. Na ndaawɔtwe durii a worubotwa no twatsia no, wɔtoo no dzin JESUS, dɛ mbrɛ bɔfo no dze too no ana woribenyinsen no yafun mu no.
    19. Na hɔn ahoguar da no durii dɛ mbrɛ Moses mbra se no, wɔdze no kɔr Jerusalɛm dɛ wɔdze no rekɛkyɛ Ewuradze
    20. dɛ mbrɛ wɔakyerɛw no Ewuradze ne mbra mu dɛ, Banyimba biara a obobue awotoa ano no, wɔbɛfrɛ no krɔnkrɔn ama Ewuradze, , #Eks 13.2
    21. na wɔakɔbɔ afɔr dɛ mbrɛ wɔkaa no Ewuradze mbra no mu no, Ntamram ebien, anaadɛ mbuburmba ebien.
    22. Na hwɛ, ɔbarimba bi wɔ Jerusalɛm a ne dzin dze Simeon; na ɔyɛ nyimpa nyamesuronyi a ɔtsen, ɔrotweɔn Israel awerɛkyekyer, na Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn wɔ no do.
    23. Na Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn yii kyerɛɛ no dɛ onnkohu wu gyedɛ oehu Ewuradze ne Kraest no ana.
    24. Na Sunsum no dze no baa asɔrdan no mu: ber a abofra Jesus n'awofo dze no baa dɛ wɔrebɛyɛ mbrɛ mbra no n'atɔrmuadze tse ama no no.
    25. Nna Simeon faa no too n'abaw mu, yii Nyankopɔn ayɛw na afei ɔkaa dɛ, LUK.2.29, Ewuradze, nkyii gya w'akowaa kwan asomdwee mu, dɛ mbrɛ w'asɛm se;
    26. Osiandɛ m'enyiwa ehu wo nkwagye a
    27. Esiesie wɔ amanaman nyina enyim;
    28. Ɔkandzea a ɔbɛma amanamamfo do atsew, Na wo man Israel enyimnyam.
    29. Na ɔyɛɛ abofra no n'egya nye ne na nwanwa wɔ dza wɔkaa no wɔ no ho no ho;
    30. na Simeon hyiraa hɔn, na ɔsee ne na Mary dɛ, Hwɛ, oyi na wɔdze no esi hɔ ama Israel mu dodowara hɔn asehwe nye hɔn soɛr, ɔnye asɛnkyerɛdze a wɔbɛkasa etsia no;
    31. ama akoma dodowara mu adwen aada edzi, na ɔwoara so nkrantsee bohwira w'akoma mu.
    32. Nna Anna, ɔbaa nkɔnhyɛnyi bi a ɔyɛ Fanuel babaa a ofir Aser ebusuakuw mu wɔ hɔ: oenyin, na ɔwaar ne nkataasiaber mu nye no kun tsenaa mfe esuon, LUK.2.37, na ɔayɛ okunafo bɛyɛ mfe eduowɔtwe anan, na ɔammpa asɔrdan mu da, na ɔdze akɔnkyen nye mpaabɔ som Nyankopɔn adzekyee nye adzesaa.

    1.     Tsir 9

    1. Na ɔfrɛɛ duebien no boaa ano, na ɔmaa hɔn ahoɔdzen nye tum wɔ mbɔnsam nyina na ayarsa do.

    3. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Mma hom mmfa biribiara nntu kwan, mma hom mmfa poma anaa kotoku anaa edziban anaa sika, na mma hom mmfa ntar ebien so.
    4. Na fie biara a hom bɛhɛn mu no, hom ntsena hɔ ara, na hom eefi hɔ akɔ.
    5. Na hɔn a wɔrenngye hom no, hom fi kurow mu hɔ rokɔ a, hom mporoporow hom ananadze huntuma ngye dase ntsia hɔn.

    10. Na asomafo no sanee bae no, wɔbɛkaa dza wɔakɛyɛ nyina kyerɛɛ no. Na ɔfaa hɔn kɔr nkyɛn beebi, wɔ kurow a wɔfrɛ no Bɛtsaeda no mu.
    11. Na nyimpadodow no hunii no, wɔkɛkaa no do; na ɔgyee hɔn na ɔkaa Nyankopɔn ahenman no ho asɛm kyerɛɛ hɔn, na ɔsaa hɔn a wohia ayarsa no yarba.
    12. Na wi rokɔtɔ no, duebien no bae bɛsee no dɛ, Gya nyimpadodow yi kwan ma wɔnkɔ nkurow nye nkuraase a woetwa ehyia yi mu nkɛtsena hɔ, ama woeenya edziban: na mbrɛ yɛwɔ yi dze, sar do a.

    15. Na wɔyɛɛ dɛm na wɔmaa hɔn nyina tsenaa famu.
    16. Na ɔfaa paanoo enum no, nye esunam ebien no, na ɔhwɛɛ sor no, ohyiraa do, na obubui dze maa n'esuafo no dɛ wɔnkyɛ mma nyimpadodow no.
    17. Na hɔn nyina dzidzii na wɔmee; na wɔtasee mporoporoba a ɔboor do no nkɛntsɛn duebien.
    18. Na aber bi a noara nko wɔ hɔ rosɔr na n'esuafo no ka no ho no, obisaa hɔn dɛ, Nyimpadodow se dɛ woana nye me?

    20. Na obisaa hɔn dɛ, Na hom dze, hom se dɛ woana nye me? Na Peter buae see dɛ, Nyankopɔn ne Kraest no a., #Joh 6.68

    41. Na ɔreba no, sunsum fi no twaa no hwee famu, na ɔtsetseew no, na Jesus bɔɔ sunsum fi no ham, na ɔsaa abofra no yarba, na ɔdze no maa n'egya.
    42. Na hɔn nyina ho boow hɔn, wɔ Nyankopɔn no tumdzi kɛse no ntsi. Na ber a obiara no ho abow no wɔ ndzɛmba a Jesus yɛe nyina ho no, ɔsee n'esuafo no dɛ, LUK.9.44, Hom mma nsɛm yi nkɔ hom asomu: na nyimpa Ba no, woribeyi no ahyɛ nyimpa nsa.
    43. Na wɔanntse asɛm yi ase, na ohuntaa hɔn amma woeennhu, na wosuroo dɛ wobebisa no asɛm yi ho biribi.
    44. Na atsɛnyiyi baa hɔn mu dɛ hɔn mu woana na ɔyɛ kɛse.
    45. Na Jesus hun hɔn akoma mu adwen no, ɔfaa abofraba bi gyinaa ne nkyɛn, LUK.9.48, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Obiara a ɔbɛgye abofraba yi wɔ me dzin mu no, ɔgye emi mara; na obiara a ɔgye me no, ɔgye nyia ɔsomaa me no: na nyia ɔyɛ ekumaba wɔ hom nyina mu no, ɔno na ɔyɛ kɛse.
    46. Na John buae see dɛ, Hɛn wura, yehun obi dɛ ɔdze wo dzin rutu esunsum fi, na yesiiw no kwan, osiandɛ ɔnnka hɛn ho.
    47. Na Jesus see no dɛ, Mma hom nnsiw no kwan: na nyia onntsia hom no, ɔwɔ hom afa.
    48. Na ɔbaa dɛ nda a wɔrebɛfa no akɔ sor reyɛ edu no, osii n'adwen pi dze n'enyi kyerɛɛ dɛ ɔrokɔ Jerusalɛm, LUK.9.52, na ɔsomaa abɔfo dzii n'enyim kan; na wɔkɔree no, wɔkɛhɛɛn Samariafo kurow bi mu dɛ worikesiesie hɔ ama no.

    50. Na n'esuafo James nye John hunii no, wɔsee dɛ, Ewuradze, ana epɛ dɛ yɛka ma ogya fi sor ba bɛhyew hɔn?
    51. Naaso Jesus daan noho twuuw hɔn enyim;, #9.55-56 Akyerɛwfo binom dze sɔw do dɛ: hom nnyim sunsum ko a hom yɛ nedze, osiandɛ Nyimpa Ba no ammbɛsɛɛ nyimpa

    Tsir 10

    1. Na iyi ekyir no, Ewuradze yii eduosuon afofor so, na ɔsomaa hɔn beenu beenu dzii n'enyim kan kɔr kurow anaa beebiara a nankasa robɔkɔ hɔ no mu.

    3. Hom nkɔ: hwɛ, mosoma hom dɛ nguambaa wɔ mpataku finimfin.

    5. Na ofi biara a hom bɛhɛn mu no, hom ndzi kan nse dɛ, Asomdwee mbra ofi yi do.
    6. Na sɛ asomdwee ba wɔ hɔ a, hom asomdwee bɛtsena no do, na sɛ ɔnntse dɛm a, ɔbɛsan aba hom hɔ bio.
    7. Na fie hɔ ara na hom ntsena, na hom ndzi na hom nnom dza wɔbɛma: osiandɛ odwumayɛfo n'apaadze sɛ no: mma hom mmfi fie kor mu nnkɔ fofor mu.

    9. na hom nsa no mu ayarfo yarba, na hom nse hɔn dɛ, Nyankopɔn ahenman no abɛn hom.

    11. Hom kurow mu huntuma a ayeyɛ hɛn ho mpo yɛprapra gu hom do: na ɔyɛ dɛn ara a, hom nhu iyi dɛ Nyankopɔn ahenman no abɛn hom.

    Tsir 11

    1. Na ɔbaa dɛ ɔwɔ beebi rosɔr, na ogyaae no, n'esuafo no mu baako see no dɛ, Ewuradze, kyerɛkyerɛ hɛn asɔr yɛ, dɛ mbrɛ John so kyerɛkyerɛɛ n'esuafo no.
    2. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Sɛ hom rosɔr a, hom nse dɛ, Egya, wo dzin ho ntsew, w'ahendzi mbra.
    3. Ma hɛn hɛn daa daa edziban daa.
    4. Na fa hɛn bɔn kyɛ hɛn, osiandɛ hɛn so yɛdze kyɛ obiara a ɔdze hɛn kaw. Na mma mmfa hɛn nnkɔ nsɔhwɛ mu.
    5. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom mu woana na ɔwɔ nyɛnko, na ɔbɔkɔ ne nkyɛn esuom akɛse no dɛ, Damfo, fɛm me paanoo ebiasa:
    6. na me nyɛnko bi nam kwan aba me nkyɛn na minnya hwee nnsi n'enyim;
    7. na ɔno dze, obefi dan mu noho ebua dɛ, Mma nnhaw me: wɔatow abow mu dada na me mba wɔ me nkyɛn wɔ mpa do: murunntum nnsoɛr mmbɛma wo bi?
    8. Mese hom dɛ, Kaansa ɔnnkɔsoɛr abɛma no dɛ ɔyɛ ne nyɛnko ntsi a, n'anoɔdzen ntsi ɔbɔsoɛr abɛma no dodow a ohia no.
    9. Na mese hom dɛ, Hom mbisa na wɔbɛma hom; hom nhwehwɛ na hom bohu; hom mbɔ mu na wobobue ama hom:
    10. na obiara a obisa no onya; na nyia ɔhwehwɛ no ohu; na nyia ɔbɔ mu no wobobue no.
    11. Na hom mu woana na ɔyɛ egya, na sɛ ne ba bisa no paanoo a, ɔbɛma no ɔbo? Anaa esunam a, ɔdze ɔwɔ bɛyɛ esunam ama no?
    12. Anaadɛ obisa kyirefuwa a, ɔbɛma no nyankomaketsebowa?
    13. Na sɛ hom a hom muo yi mpo nyim mbrɛ hom si dze akyɛdze pa ma hom mba a, mpɛn ahen ara na hom Egya a ɔwɔ sor no nnkɛfa Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn ama hɔn a wobisa no?
    14. Nna orutu sunsum fi bi a ɔyɛ mmum. Na ɔbaa dɛ sunsum fi no fiir edzi no, mmum no kasae; na ɔyɛɛ nyimpadodow no nwanwa.
    15. Na hɔn mu binom see dɛ, Ɔnam sunsum fi panyin Beelzebul do na otu sunsum fi.
    16. Na binom so sɔɔ no hwɛe bisaa no hɔ asɛnkyerɛdze a ofi sor.
    17. Na ɔno dze onyim hɔn adwen no, ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ahenman biara nye noho dzi mpaapaamu a, ɔyɛ amamfo, na fie bi so nye fie bi dzi a, ɔhwe ase.
    18. Na sɛ Satan so nye noho dzi mpaapaamu a, ɔbɛyɛ dɛn na n'ahenman egyina? Osiandɛ hom se dɛ menam Beelzebul do tu esunsum fi.
    19. Na sɛ emi menam Beelzebul do tu sunsum bɔn a, woana do na hom mba nam tu hɔn? Dɛm ntsi hɔn na wɔbɛyɛ hom atsɛmbuafo.
    20. Na sɛ menam Nyankopɔn nsatseaba do tu esunsum bɔn a, nna Nyankopɔn ahenman enya aba hom nkyɛn.
    21. Sɛ ɔkatakyi emia roweɔn n'aban a, n'ahodze wɔ hɔ komm:
    22. na sɛ obi a ɔkyɛn no ahoɔdzen ba no do, na odzi no do nyim a, ɔgye n'akodze a no werɛ twer, na ɔkyɛ n'asadze no.
    23. Nyia ɔnnka mo ho no, na ɔnye me enya; nyia ɔnye me mmboaboa ano no, ɔhotse gu.
    24. Na sunsum bɔn, sɛ ofi nyimpa mu fir a, okekyin wewewdo hwehwɛ ahomgye na onnya bi a, ɔse dɛ, Mebɛsan akɔ me fie a mifii mu bae no mu;
    25. na ɔba a, ɔto no dɛ wɔaprapra mu na woesiesie mu.
    26. Nna ɔkɔ kɛfa esunsum esuon afofor a womuo sen nankasa ka noho, na wɔbɛhɛn mu tsena hɔ: na nyimpa no n'ekyir asetsena yɛ bɔn sen nkan no dze.
    27. Na ɔbaa dɛ ɔreka yinom no, ɔbaa bi a ɔwɔ nyimpadodow no mu maa ne ndze do see no dɛ, Nhyira aka yafun a ɔwoo wo, nye numfo a inumii.
    28. Na ɔno dze, ɔsee dɛ, Mbom nhyira aka hɔn a wotsie Nyankopɔn asɛm na wodzi do.
    29. Na nyimpadodow boaboaa ano no, ɔhyɛɛ ase kaa dɛ, Ɔsantsen yi yɛ ɔsantsen a omuo: wɔhwehwɛ asɛnkyerɛdze, na wɔremma hɔn asɛnkyerɛdze biara dɛ Jona n'asɛnkyerɛdze no.
    30. Na dɛ mbrɛ wɔyɛɛ Jona asɛnkyerɛdze maa Ninivefo no, dɛmara na nyimpa Ba no bɛyɛ ama ɔsantsen yi.
    31. Pomu afamu ɔhembaa nye ɔsantsen yi mbarimba bɔsoɛr wɔ atsɛmbu no mu, na oebu hɔn fɔ: osiandɛ ofi asaase ano noho bae betsiee Solomon ne nyansaa, na hwɛ, nyia ɔsen Solomon wɔ ha.
    32. Ninive mbarimba nye ɔsantsen yi bɔsoɛr wɔ atsɛmbu no mu, na woebu hɔn fɔ: osiandɛ wonuu hɔnho wɔ Jona n'asɛnka ase, na hwɛ, nyia ɔsen Jona wɔ ha.
    33. Obi nnsɔ kandzea nnsi kukua mu, anaa kuruw ase, na ɔdze si kandzeadua do, ma hɔn a wɔhɛn mu eehu kan no.
    34. Nyimpadua ne kandzea nye w'enyiwa: dɛm ntsi ber a w'enyiwa yɛ kann no, nna wo nyimpadua nyina so bɛyɛ kann; na ber a omuo no, nna wo nyimpadua no so yɛ kusuww.
    35. Dɛm ntsi hwɛ ma kan a ɔwɔ wo mu aannyɛ sum.
    36. Na sɛ wo nyimpadua nyina yɛ kann, na beebiara nnyi sum a, ne nyina bɛyɛ kann, dɛ mbrɛ kandzea dze ne hyerɛn hyɛnhyɛnn ma wo kan no.
    37. Na ɔrekasa no, Farisiinyi bi bebisaa no dɛ ɔnye no nkedzidzi, na ɔkɔr mu kɛtsenaa edzidzii.
    38. Na Farisiinyi no hun dɛ ɔannhohor ne nsa ho ansaana oridzidzi no, ɔyɛɛ no nwanwa.
    39. Na Ewuradze see no dɛ, Afei hom Farisiifo dze, hom twuw nkɔɔpow nye mprɛtse ho, na hom mu dze, apempensi nye emumuyɛ ahyɛ ma.
    40. Nkwasea, ana nnyɛ nyia ɔyɛɛ ekyir no, ɔnoara na ɔyɛɛ no mu so?
    41. Na hwɛ, hom mfa dza ɔwɔ mu no bi nyɛ ayɛ, na ɔno na ɔbɛma adze nyina ho efir ama hom.
    42. Na hom ndue, hom Farisiifo! Na hom yi eme nye nunum nye fan nyina do ebupɛn du du ma, na hom twa atsɛmbu nye Nyankopɔn dɔ no gya: na iyi na ɔsɛ dɛ nkyɛ hom yɛ, ber a hom nndan dza ɔaka no so nngya.
    43. Hom ndue, hom Farisiifo! Na hom pɛ mpanyin ngua wɔ nhyiamudan mu nye gua do nkyiakyia.
    44. Hom ndue! Na hom tse dɛ nda a ɔnnda edzi, na nyimpa a wɔnam do nnhu.
    45. Na mbrakyerɛfo no mu baako buae see no dɛ, Kyerɛkyerɛfo, ese dɛm a, nna erotom hɛn so.
    46. Na ɔsee dɛ, Hom mbrakyerɛfo so, hom ndue! Na hom soa nyimpa ndzesoadze a no soa yɛ yaw, na homankasa dze, hom mmfa hom nsatseaba kor mpo nnka ndzesoadze no.
    47. Hom ndue! Na hom tow nkɔnhyɛfo nda do, naaso homara hom egyanom kum hɔn.
    48. Dɛm ntsi hom dzi hom egyanom ndwuma ho dase, na hom so pen do, osiandɛ hɔn na wokum hɔn, na hom na hom tow hɔn nda do.
    49. Iyi ntsi na Nyankopɔn ne nyansaa se dɛ, Mobɔsoma nkɔnhyɛfo nye asomafo wɔ hɔn hɔ, na woboku, na wobehiahia hɔn mu binom ho;
    50. ama nkɔnhyɛfo nyina bɔgyaa a wɔkae gui, ofitsi wiadze ahyɛse no, wɔaahwehwɛ no ɔsantsen yi nsamu:
    51. ofitsi Abel no bɔgyaa do besi Zekaria a ɔtɔr afɔrmbukyia nye asɔrdan ntamu no no bɔgyaa do, ɛhɛɛ, mese hom dɛ wɔbɔhwehwɛ no ɔsantsen yi nsamu.
    52. Hom mbrakyerɛfo, hom ndue! Na hom afa nyimdzee saafee: homankasa hom annkɔ mu, na hɔn a wɔrokɔ mu no so, hom siiw hɔn kwan.
    53. Na ɔreka yinom kyerɛ hɔn no, kyerɛwfo nye Farisiifo no hyɛɛ ase hyehyɛɛ no do pii, na wɔgyegyee no kasa wɔ ndzɛmba pii ho;
    54. na wɔtsɛɛw no hwehwɛɛ dɛ wobenya n'anomu asɛm bi eesuo mu.

    Tsir 12

    1. Aber no mu ara, nyimpadodow mpempem boaboaa ano, ma mpo wotsiatsiaa hɔnho do no, ɔhyɛɛ ase kaa kyerɛɛ n'esuafo no kan dɛ, Hom nhwɛ yie wɔ Farisiifo mbɔrkaw ho: ɔno nye ndabraba.
    2. Na biribi do nnkatae a worinnyi no edzi, na onnhuntae a worunnhu.
    3. Dɛm ntsi biribi a hom aka wɔ sum mu no, wɔbɛtse no wɔ kan mu; na dza hom aka wɔ asomu wɔ adankyir no, wɔbɛpaa mu aka wɔ adan etsifi.
    4. Na meka kyerɛ hom, m'anyɛnkofo dɛ, Mma hom nnsuro hɔn a woku honam, na n'ekyir no wonnyi biribi a wobotum ayɛ bio.
    5. Na mebɛkyerɛ hom nyia hom nsuro no: hom nsuro nyia oeku ewie no, ɔwɔ tum dɛ ɔdze kɔto amandzehu kurow mu: nyew, mese hom dɛ, Ɔno na hom nsuro no.
    6. Ana nsorɔm enum wɔnntɔn nngye mpɛsewa ebien? Naaso hɔn mu kor ara mpo nnyi hɔ a enyi pa no do wɔ Nyankopɔn enyim.
    7. Na hom tsinhwi mpo wɔakan ne nyina. Mma hom nnsuro: osiandɛ hom som bo kyɛn nsorɔm pii.
    8. Na mese hom dɛ, Obiara a ɔbɔbɔ me dzin wɔ nyimpa enyim no, ɔno na nyimpa Ba no bɔbɔ ne dzin wɔ Nyankopɔn abɔfoe enyim;
    9. na nyia ɔpa me nyimpa enyim no, ɔno na wɔbɛpa no Nyankopɔn abɔfoe enyim.
    10. Na obiara a ɔbɛka asɛm etsia nyimpa Ba no, wɔdze bɛkyɛ no; na nyia ɔka ebususɛm tsia Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn no, wɔnnkɛfa akyɛ no.
    11. Na sɛ wɔdze hom ba nhyiamudan mu nye mpanyimfo nye etumfo enyim a, mma hom nndwendwen mbrɛ hom bɛyɛ anaa dza hom bɛka eyi homho, anaa dza hom bɛse:
    12. osiandɛ Sunsum Krɔnkrɔn no bɛkyerɛ hom dɔn no ara mu dza ɔsɛ dɛ hom bɛka.
    13. Na obi fi nyimpadodow no mu see no dɛ, Kyerɛkyerɛfo, ka kyerɛ mo nua ma ɔnye me nkyɛ ewungyadze no.
    14. Na ɔsee no dɛ, Nyimpa, woana yɛɛ me ɔsɛndzifo nye adzekyɛfo wɔ hom do?
    15. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom nhwɛ yie na hom nsie homho wɔ enyikaber nyina ho: na nyimpa no nkwa nnyi ndzɛmba a oenya abor do mu.
    16. Na obuu hɔn bɛ bi dɛ, Nyimpa bi a ɔyɛ ɔdzefo n'asaase soow pii;
    17. na osusuu ne tsir mu dɛ, Mebɛyɛ dɛn? Osiandɛ minnyi beebi mbrɛ medze mo nduadzewa besie.
    18. Na ɔsee dɛ, Iyi na mebɛyɛ: mubodwuruw m'asan egu famu, na mesi akɛse; na hɔ na medze m'ekuadze nye me ndzɛmba nkaa no nyina besie.
    19. Na mase me kra dɛ, Ɔkra, ewɔ ndzɛmba pii a wɔdze egu hɔ ama mfe pii: gye w'ahom, dzidzi, nom, na ma woho ntɔ wo.
    20. Na Nyankopɔn see no dɛ, Ɔkwasea, anafua yi wɔbɛgye wo kra wɔ wo nsamu; na dza esiesie no bɛyɛ woana dze?
    21. Mbrɛ nyia ɔboaboa akoradze ano ma noho, na ɔnnyɛ ɔdzefo wɔ Nyankopɔn afamu tse nye no.
    22. Na ɔsee n'esuafo no dɛ, Dɛm ntsi mese hom dɛ, Mma hom nndwendwen wɔ hom nkwa ho, dɛ ebɛnadze na hom bedzi, anaaso hom nyimpadua ho, dɛ ebɛnadze na hom bofura.
    23. Nkwa kyɛn edziban, na nyimpadua kyɛn efuradze.
    24. Hom nhwɛ nkonkoran dɛ wonndua na wommbu, wonnyi efiadze anaa ɔsan, naaso Nyankopɔn nyɛn hɔn: ɔkyɛn bɛn na hom nsom bo nkyɛn nnoma no.
    25. Na hom mu woana na ɔdwendwen a, obotum dze basafa kor ato ne tsentsen do?
    26. Na sɛ hom nntum adze ketseaba dɛm mpo yɛ a, ɔyɛ dɛn na hom dwendwen nkaa no ho?
    27. Hom nhwɛ haban mu nsukookoo, mbrɛ wonyin: wɔnnyɛ edwuma, eso wɔnntow asaaba; naaso mese hom dɛ Solomon n'enyimnyam nyina mu no mpo, n'ahosiesie annto iyinom mu kor.
    28. Na sɛ Nyankopɔn ma haban mu efuw a ɔwɔ hɔ ndɛ na ɔkyena wɔdze gu fononoo mu efuradze dɛm a, mpɛn ahen na ɔnnkɛma hom ma akyɛn dɛm, hom a hom gyedzi suar?
    29. Na mma hom nnhwehwɛ dza hom bedzi anaa dza hom bɔnom, eso mma hom adwen nnyɛ hom konsee.
    30. Na dɛm ndzɛmba yi nyina na wiadze amanaman hwehwɛ: na hom Egya nyim dɛ yinom hia hom.
    31. Na mbom hom nhwehwɛ n'ahenman no, na wɔdze yinom bɛka hom ho.
    32. Mma hom nnsuro, oguankuw ketseaba: na ɔyɛ hom Egya fɛw dɛ ɔdze ahenman no bɛma hom.
    33. Hom ntɔn dza hom wɔ mfa nyɛ ayɛ; hom nyɛ nkotoku a ɔrennyɛ dadaw mma homho, akoradze wɔ sor a ɔnnsa, mbrɛ owifo mmbɛn ho, na nkakraka nnsɛɛ no.
    34. Na mbrɛ hom akoradze wɔ no, hɔ na hom akoma so wɔ.
    35. Hom mbɔ hom asen do, na hom nkandzea ndzɛw;
    36. na hom nyɛ dɛ nyimpa a wɔrotweɔn hɔn wura, aber kor a obefi ayefor ase aba, ma sɛ ɔba na ɔbɔbɔ mu a, woeebue no ntsɛmara.
    37. Nhyira aka nkowaa no a, sɛ owura no ba a, ɔbɔto hɔn dɛ wɔroweɔn: nokwar mese hom dɛ ɔbɔbɔ noho do, na ɔama wɔatsena edzidzii, na ɔaba abɔsom hɔn.
    38. Na sɛ ɔba dasu ebien anaa ebiasa mu, na ɔbɔto hɔn dɛm a, nhyira aka nkowaa no.
    39. Na hom nhu yi, dɛ sɛ ofiwura no nyim dɔn kor a owifo no bɛba a, nkyɛ ɔbɔweɔn amma woeemmbobubu ne dan mu enndua mu.
    40. Na hom so hom nyɛ krado, osiandɛ dɔn a hom nnsusu mu na nyimpa Ba no reba.
    41. Na Peter see dɛ, Ewuradze, hɛn nko na ibu hɛn bɛ yi, anaa edze ma obiara so?
    42. Na Ewuradze see dɛ, Woana koraa nye ofihwɛfo nokwafo nye nyansafo a no wura dze no besi ne fifo do, dɛ ɔmma hɔn edziban a ɔyɛ hɔn kyɛpɛn wɔ ber a ɔsɛ mu?
    43. Nhyira aka akowaa no a, sɛ no wura ba a, obohu no dɛ ɔreyɛ dɛm.
    44. Nokwar mese hom dɛ ɔdze no besi n'adze a ɔwɔ nyina do.
    45. Na sɛ akowaa no se n'akoma mu dɛ, Mo wura rikyim ne ba; na ɔhyɛ ase bobɔ nkowaa nye mfonabaa no, na odzidzi na ɔnom bow a;
    46. akowaa no wura bɛba da a ɔronnhwɛ no kwan, ɔnye dɔn a onnyim mu, na oetwa no mu ebien, na ɔama ɔnye ndabrabafo no edzi afora.
    47. Na akowaa no a onyim no wura ne pɛ, na oennsiesie noho, na eso ɔannyɛ dɛ mbrɛ ɔpɛ no, wɔbɔhwe no mbaa pii.
    48. Na nyia onnyim, na ɔyɛɛ ndzɛmba a ɔsɛ ɔhwe no, wɔbɔhwe no kakrabaa. Na obiara a wɔama no dodow na wɔbɛgye dodow wɔ no hɔ; na nyia wɔdze pii ahyɛ ne nsa no, nna wobebisa no dza ɔbor do.
    49. Mebae dɛ morobɔsɔ gya wɔ asaase do, na sɛ asɔ dada a, ebɛnadze bio na mepɛ sen dɛm?
    50. Na mowɔ enuma bi a wɔdze bonuma me, na mbrɛ mo ho dua kyer me gyedɛ ayɛ hɔ ana!

    Tsir 17

    26. Dɛmara na ɔyɛe wɔ Lot nda no mu: wodzidzii, wɔnomee, wɔtɔe, wɔtɔnee, woduae, wɔtowee;

    Tsir 18

    1. Na obuu hɔn bɛ bi dze kyerɛɛ dɛ otwar dɛ nyimpa bɔ mpaa daa, na ɔnntɔ bahaw:

    18. Inyim mbransɛm no, Mma nndzi ewu, Mma nnto awar, Mma nnwia adze, Mma nngye dasewia, Dzi w'egya nye wo na nyi.
    19. Na ɔsee dɛ, Yinom nyinara mifitsi me mbofraber mu na medzi do.
    20. Na Jesus tsee dɛm no, ɔsee no dɛ, Ɔda ho ka wo adze kor: kɔtɔn dza ewɔ nyinara, na kyɛ ma ehiafo, na ibenya akoradze wɔ sor, na bra bɛka mo do.

    22. Na Jesus hun no no, ɔsee dɛ, Ebɛn dzen na ɔyɛ ma hɔn a wɔwɔ ahonya dɛ wɔbɔkɔ Nyankopɔn n'ahenman mu!
    23. Na ɔyɛ mberɛw ma efupɔnkɔ dɛ obodua doroba enyiwa mu kyɛn dɛ ɔhonyanyi bɔkɔ Nyankopɔn n'ahenman mu.
    24. Na hɔn a wɔtsee no kaa dɛ, Dɛm a nna woana na wobotum agye no nkwa?

    28. Na ɔfaa duebien no na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hwɛ, yɛrokɔ Jerusalɛm, na ndzɛmba a nkɔnhyɛfo no akyerɛw no nyimpa Ba no ho nyina bɛba mu.

    30. na wɔaka no mpire, na woeku no; na da a otsia ebiasa no ɔbɔsoɛr bio.
    31. Na hɔn dze wɔanntse iyinom mu biara ase, na asɛm yi sumaa hɔn, na woennhu dza ɔreka no.

    33. na ɔtsee dɛ nyimpadodow rutwa mu no, obisaa dɛ ebɛn asɛm rokɔ do?
    34. Na wɔkaa kyerɛɛ no dɛ Jesus Nazarɛtnyi no na ɔresen.

    41. Na owuyaw mu no, ɔbɔɔ mpaa enyiberdo, na ne fifir yɛɛ dɛ bɔgya atɔwatɔw a ɔsosɔ famu.
    42. Na ɔsoɛree fii n'asɔryɛ mu no, ɔbaa n'esuafo no nkyɛn na obohun hɔn dɛ awerɛhow ntsi wɔadeda.
    43. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn na hom ada yi? Hom nsoɛr nsɔr mma hom aannkɔ nsɔhwɛ mu.
    44. Na ɔda do rekasa no, hwɛ, nyimpadodow nye nyia wɔfrɛ no Judas a ɔyɛ duebien no mu baako dzi hɔn enyim na ɔtwee bɛɛn Jesus dɛ ɔrebɛfew n'ano.
    45. Na Jesus see no dɛ, Judas, edze mfewano riyi nyimpa Ba no ama anaa?
    46. Na hɔn a wɔwɔ ne nkyɛn no hun dza ɔreba no, wɔsee dɛ, Ewuradze, yɛmfa afona mbobɔ hɔn anaa?
    47. Na hɔn mu baako bɔɔ ɔsɔfopanyin n'akowaa, na otwaa n'asowa nyimfa.
    48. Na Jesus buae see dɛ, Hom mma ɔmpa ha ara; na ɔdze ne nsa kaa asowa no famee.
    49. Na Jesus see asɔfompanyin nye asɔrfi mu asafohen, nye mpanyimfo a wɔbaa no do no dɛ, Ana hom puei baa mo do dɛ owifo do, na hom dze mfona nye mpɔtsibaa yi?
    50. Mowɔ hom nkyɛn wɔ asɔrfi mu hɔ daa no, hom anntsen hom nsa annka me: na iyi nye hom ber, na sum mu tum.
    51. Na wɔfaa no kɔree, na wɔdze no baa ɔsɔfopanyin no fie. Na Peter dzii n'ekyir wɔ ekyirekyir.
    52. Na wokuaa gya wɔ abangua no finimfin na wɔtsenaa ho no, Peter bɛtsenaa hɔn mu.
    53. Na ɔtse gya no ho no, abaawa bi hun no, na ɔhwɛɛ no komm, na ɔsee dɛ, Oyi so nye no nam.
    54. Na Peter saan dɛ, Ɔbaa, minnyim no.
    55. Na ɔkyɛɛr kakra no obi so hun no, na ɔsee dɛ, Wo so fi hɔn mu. Na Peter see dɛ, Onua, mimmfi mu.
    56. Na n'ekyir bɛyɛ dɔnhwer kor na obi so kaa gyinaa do dɛ, Ɔyɛ ampa, oyi so nye no nam: na ɔyɛ Galiliinyi.
    57. Na Peter see dɛ, Onua, minnyim dza ereka yi. Na amonmu hɔ ara a ɔda do rekasa no, na akokɔ bɔnee.
    58. Na Ewuradze daan noho hwɛɛ Peter. Na Peter kaa asɛm a Ewuradze kae no, mbrɛ ɔsee no dɛ, Ansaana akokɔ bɔbɔn ndɛ, ebɛpa me mprɛnsa.
    59. Na opuei, na okosuu yayaayaw.
    60. Na mbanyin a wɔdze Jesus mu no, sii no atwetwe bobɔɔ no.
    61. Na wɔkataa n'enyiwa, na wobisaa no dɛ, Hyɛ nkɔm! Woana na ɔbɔɔ wo?
    62. Na ebususɛm pii keka ho a wɔkae tsiaa no.
    63. Na adze kyee no, ɔman mpanyimfo nye asɔfompanyin nye kyerɛwfo no behyiae, na wɔdze no kɔr hɔn asɛndzii, dɛ, LUK.22.67, Sɛ ɔwo nye Kraest no a, ka kyerɛ hɛn. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Sɛ meka kyerɛ hom a, hom renngye nndzi;
    64. na mo so mibisa hom a, hom rummbua bi.
    65. Na daakye nyimpa Ba no bɛtsena Nyankopɔn n'ahoɔdzen nyimfa.
    66. Na hɔn nyina see dɛ, Nna kyerɛ dɛ, ɔwo nye Nyankopɔn Ba no a? Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom na hom ka dɛ emi nye no.
    67. Na wɔsee dɛ, Ebɛn dase na ohia ma hɛn bio? Osiandɛ hɛnankasa yɛatse wɔ n'anomu.

    Tsir 23

    1. Na nyimpadodow no nyina soɛree, na wɔdze no kɔr Pilate nkyɛn.
    2. Na wɔhyɛɛ ase toatoaa no dɛ, Yeehu oyi dɛ ɔresɛɛ hɛn man yi na ɔbra dɛ mma wɔmma Kaesar ntoboa, na ɔka dɛ nankasa nye Kraest, ɔhen no.
    3. Na Pilate bisaa no dɛ, Ana Jewfo Hen nye wo? Na obuaa no dɛ, Aka.
    4. Pilate see asɔfompanyin nye nyimpadodow no dɛ, Munnhu afɔdzi biara wɔ nyimpa yi ho.
    5. Na mbom wogyinaa do dzendzen dɛ, Ohuenyan ɔman no, na ɔkyerɛkyerɛ wɔ Judea man nyina mu, ɔhyɛɛ ase wɔ Galilii na ɔdze ebodu ha yi.
    6. Na Pilate tsee no, obisaa dɛ nyimpa yi Galiliinyi anaa?
    7. Na ohun dɛ ofi Herod ase no, ɔsomaa no wɔ Herod nkyɛn, na aber no mu nna Herod nankasa so wɔ Jerusalɛm.
    8. Na Herod hun Jesus no, ɔyɛɛ no dɛw pii: na ɔpɛɛ dɛ obohu no akyɛr, osiandɛ ɔatse no ho asɛm, na n'enyi daa kwan dɛ obohu asɛnkyerɛdze bi a ɔbɛyɛ.
    9. Na obisabisaa no nsɛm pii, naaso ɔnodze, oemmbua no hwee.
    10. Na asɔfompanyin nye kyerɛwfo no gyinae toatoaa no dzendzenndzen ara.
    11. Na Herod nye no nsordaafo no bɔɔ no adapaa, na wosii no atwetwe, na wɔhyɛɛ no efuradze dɛhyɛdɛhyɛ bi dze no kɛmaa Pilate bio.
    12. Na da no ara na Pilate nye Herod yɛɛ nyɛnko: na nkan no dze nna wɔrotwe manso wɔ hɔn ntamu.
    13. Na Pilate frɛɛ asɔfompanyin nye mpanyimfo nye ɔman no boaa ano no, LUK.23.14, ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom dze nyimpa yi brɛɛ me dɛ nyia ɔresɛɛ ɔman no, na hwɛ, mahwehwɛ no mu wɔ hom enyim, naaso mennhu ndzɛmba a hom dze toatoa no yi ho afɔdzi biara wɔ nyimpa yi mu;
    14. oho, nnyɛ Herod so a: na ɔsomaa no sanee brɛɛ hɛn; na hwɛ, ɔnnyɛɛ okumadze biara.
    15. Dɛm ntsi mobɔhwe no, na megyaa no.
    16. [Nna otwar dɛ ogyaa kor ma hɔn afahyɛ no mu.]
    17. Na hɔn nyina tseaa mu prɛko dɛ, Fa oyi kɔ, na gyaa Barabbas ma hɛn:
    18. (ɔno na osian sakasaka a wɔyɛɛ no kurow mu hɔ nye ewudzi ntsi wɔdze no too efiadze.)
    19. Na Pilate kasaa kyerɛɛ hɔn bio, pɛɛ dɛ nkyɛ ogyaa Jesus;
    20. na wɔtseatseaa mu dɛ, Bɔ no mbeamudua mu, bɔ no mbeamudua mu!
    21. Na obisaa hɔn ne mprɛnsa dɛ, Ebɛn bɔn na nyimpa yi ayɛ? Munnhu dza ɔayɛ biara a ɔsɛ dɛ owu: dɛm ntsi mobɔhwe no, na megyaa no.
    22. Na wɔdze ndze kɛse daa ho tseaa mu dɛ wɔmbɔ no mbeamudua mu. Na hɔn ndze hyɛɛ nedze no do.
    23. Na Pilate buu atsɛn dɛ wɔnyɛ hɔn ebisadze mma hɔn.
    24. Na nyia osian sakasaka nye ewudzi ntsi wɔdze no too efiadze no a wɔserɛɛ maa no no, ogyaa no, na Jesus dze, oyii no maa hɔn pɛ.
    25. Na wɔdze no rokɔ no, wosuoo Simon bi a ofi Sirene a nna ofi haban mu reba mu, na wɔdze mbeamudua no too no do dɛ ɔnsoa ndzi Jesus n'ekyir.
    26. Na ɔman no mu nyimpadodow kɛse kaa no do, nye mbaa a woridzi no nkɔmbɔ na worusu.
    27. Na Jesus daan noho hwɛɛ hɔn, na ɔsee dɛ, Jerusalɛm mbabaa, mma hom nnsu mma me, na hom nsu mma homho nye hom mba.
    28. Na hwɛ, nda reba a wɔbɛse dɛ, Nhyira nye abɔnyin, nye yafun a ɔnnwoo da, nye numfo a wonnuum da.
    29. Ɔno na wɔbɛhyɛ ase aka akyerɛ nkokwaa dɛ, Hom nhwe hɛn do, na mbepɔw dɛ, Hom nkata hɛn do.
    30. Na sɛ wɔdze iyi yɛ duamon a, ebɛnadze na wɔdze bɛyɛ duawui?
    31. Na wɔdze mbratofo beenu kaa no ho kɔree, dɛ wonkoku hɔn.
    32. Na wodur beebi a wɔfrɛ hɔ Tsikwankora Bea no, wɔbɔɔ no mbeamudua mu wɔ hɔ, ɔnye mbratofo no, ɔbaako wɔ ne nyimfa, na ɔbaako wɔ n'abankum.
    33. Na Jesus see dɛ, Egya, fa kyɛ hɔn, na wonnyim dza wɔreyɛ. Na wɔkyɛɛ n'atar mu twee ho tonto.
    34. Na ɔman no gyinaa hɔ hwɛe. Na mpanyimfo no so sii no atwetwe dɛ, Ɔgyee binom: ma ɔngye noho, ɛhɛɛ, sɛ ɔno nye Kraest a Nyankopɔn eyi no no a.
    35. Na nsordaafo no so sii no atwetwe, na wɔdze wenyin yerɛyerɛw bɛmaa no
    36. na wɔsee dɛ, Sɛ ɔwo nye Jewfo Hen no a, gye woho.
    37. Nna akyerɛw bi so wɔ n'etsifi dɛ, OYI NYE JEWFO HEN NO.
    38. Na mbratofo a wɔsɛɛn hɔn no mu baako yaaw no dɛ, Nnyɛ ɔwo nye Kraest no a? Gye woho na gye hɛn ɛ.
    39. Na ɔbaako no buaa no kaa n'enyim dɛ, Ana innsuro Nyankopɔn, dɛ ewɔ afɔbu kor yi ara mu?
    40. Na hɛn dze, ɔfata hɛn, osiandɛ hɛn nsa aka akatua a ɔsɛ hɛn ndzeyɛɛ; na oyi dze, ɔmmfomee wɔ biribiara ne nyɛe mu.
    41. Na ɔsee dɛ, Jesus, sɛ eba w'ahendzi mu a, kaa me.
    42. Na Jesus buaa no dɛ, Ampaara mese wo dɛ ndɛ ebɛka mo ho wɔ Paradise hɔ.
    43. Na bɛyɛ dɛ ewiaber duebien no, sum hyɛɛ asaase no nyina do kodur ndɔnebiasa no.
    44. Na ewia kɔr sum; na asɔrdan no mu ntwamutam no tseew ebien wɔ finimfin.
    45. Na Jesus dze ndze kɛse kaa mu dɛ, Egya, wo nsamu na medze mo sunsum mehyɛ. Na ɔsee dɛm no, ogyaa no sunsum mu.
    46. Na ɔhamupanyin no hun dza ayɛ no, ɔhyɛɛ Nyankopɔn enyimnyam see dɛ, Ampaara nyimpa yi tseneneenyi a.
    47. Na nyimpadodow a wɔbaa hɔ bɔhwɛɛ adze nyina no hun dza esi no, wɔdze hɔn nsa bobɔɔ hɔn koko do sanee kɔree.
    48. Na n'amanyimfo nyina, nye mbaa a wodzii n'ekyir fii Galilii nyina kegyinaa ekyirekyir hwɛɛ ndzɛmba yi., #Luk 8.2
    49. Na hwɛ, ɔbarimba bi a ne dzin dze Josef a ɔyɛ nyimpa papa na ɔtsen, ka abaguafo no ho, LUK.23.51, naaso ɔnodze ɔammpen hɔn apam nye hɔn ndzeyɛɛ no do. Ofi Jewfo kurow Arimatea mu, nna ɔrotweɔn Nyankopɔn n'ahendzi no.
    50. Ɔno na ɔkɔr Pilate hɔ kɛserɛɛ Jesus no fun no.
    51. Na oyii no dze ndwera tam kyekyeer no ho, na ɔdze no too ɔboda a obiara nndaa mu da mu.
    52. Nna da no yɛ Ahosiesieda, na ahomgyeda no rebɛn.
    53. Na mbaa a wɔnye no fii Galilii bae no dzii ekyir bɔhwɛɛ nda no, nye mbrɛ wɔdze no fun toe.
    54. Na wɔsanee kesiesiee nduhuam nye ngohuam. Na ahomgyeda no wɔhoom dzinn, dɛ mbrɛ mbrasɛm no tse.

    Tsir 24

    1. Na da a odzi dapɛn no kan no, anapaatu no na mbaa no dze nduhuam a wosiesiei no kɔr nda no do.
    2. Na wokohun ɔbo no dɛ woepirew no efi nda no abowano.
    3. Na wɔhɛɛn mu no, woennhu Ewuradze Jesus no fun.
    4. Na ɔbaa dɛ iyi yɛɛ hɔn adwen mu basabasa no, hwɛ, mbarimba beenu begyinaa hɔn nkyɛn a wɔhyɛ ntar hyɛnhyɛn;
    5. na hu kaa hɔn, na wɔbɔɔ hɔn enyi adze sensɛɛn famu no, wɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn na hom rohwehwɛ nyia ɔtse ase no wɔ ewufo mu yi?
    6. Onnyi ha, na ɔasoɛr: hom nkaa mbrɛ ɔda ho wɔ Galilii no, ɔkaa kyerɛɛ hom dɛ, #Mat 16.21; 17.22, 23; 20.18, 19; Mar 8.31; 9.31; 10.33
    7. ɔsɛ nyimpa Ba no dɛ wobeyi no ahyɛ ndzebɔnyɛfo nsa na wɔabɔ no mbeamudua mu na da a otsia ebiasa no ɔasoɛr bio no.
    8. Na wɔkaa ne nsɛm no, LUK.24.9, na wofii nda no do sanee bɛkaa ndzɛmba yi nyina kyerɛɛ dubiako no, nye nkaa no nyina.
    9. Mary Magdalene nye Joana nye James ne na Mary, nye mbaa nkaa a wɔka hɔn ho no na wɔbɛkaa nsɛm yinom kyerɛɛ asomafo no.
    10. Na nsɛm yi yɛɛ hɔn dɛ nsɛnhun, na wɔanngye hɔn enndzi.
    11. Na Peter dze, ɔsoɛree tutuu mbirika kɔr nda no do, na ɔbɔɔ no mu adze no, ohun dɛ ndwera atam no nko gugu hɔ; na ɔsanee kɔr ne fie, na dza esi no yɛɛ no nwanwa.
    12. Na hwɛ, da no ara, hɔn mu beenu sii mu kɔr ekuroba bi a ne dzin dze Emmaus a ofi Jerusalɛm kɔ hɔ bɛyɛ akwansimba eduosia mu.
    13. Nna woridzi nsɛm a aba yi nyina ho nkɔmbɔ.
    14. Na ɔbaa dɛ, woridzi nkɔmbɔ na woribisabisa hɔnho nsɛm no, Jesus nankasa twee bɛɛn hɔn nye hɔn siaan anan kɔree.
    15. Naaso wosuoo hɔn enyiwa mu, ma woennhu ne nyimpa ban.
    16. Na obisaa hɔn dɛ, Ebɛn nsɛm nye yi a hom dze ridzi nkɔmbɔ nam yi? Na wogyinae a hɔn werɛ ahow.
    17. Na hɔn mu baako a ne dzin dze Kleopas see no dɛ, Ana ɔwo nko na eyɛ hɔho wɔ Jerusalɛm, ma innyim dza ɔaba nda yi mu yi?
    18. Na obisaa hɔn dɛ, Ebɛnadze a? Na wɔsee no dɛ, Dza ɔfa Jesus Nazarɛtnyi a ɔyɛ nkɔnhyɛnyi tumfo wɔ ndzeyɛɛ nye kasa mu wɔ Nyankopɔn nye ɔman nyina enyim no ho:
    19. mbrɛ asɔfompanyin nye hɛn mpanyimfo no yii no maa dɛ wombu no fɔ nku no, na wɔbɔɔ no mbeamudua mu.
    20. Na hɛn dze, hɛn enyi daa kwan dɛ nkyɛ ɔno nye nyia ɔrebɛgye Israel. Na iyi nyina ekyir so, wɔyɛɛ ndzɛmba yi ndaansa nye ndɛ.
    21. Na mbaa bi so a wofi hɛn mu a wɔkɔr nda no do anapahema abɛma hɛn ho abow hɛn;
    22. wɔbae bɛkaa dɛ woennhu no fun no, mbom woehu abɔfo bi wɔ enyido adzehu mu so a wɔkaa dɛ ɔtse ase.
    23. Na hɔn a wɔnye hɛn wɔ hɔ binom kɔr nda no do, na wokohun dɛ mbrɛ mbaa no so kae no ara, naaso ɔno dze, woennhu no.
    24. Na ɔnoara see hɔn dɛ, Ao hom a hom adwen mu nndɔ na hom yɛ nyaa wɔ hom akoma mu dɛ hom bɛgye dza nkɔnhyɛfo no kae no nyina edzi!
    25. Ana ɔnnsɛ Kraest no dɛ obohu dɛm amandze na ɔakɔ n'enyimnyam mu?
    26. Na ɔhyɛɛ ase fi Moses nye nkɔnhyɛfo no nyina do kyerɛɛ hɔn nkyerɛw nyina mu dza ɔfa no ho no ase.
    27. Na wɔbɛɛn ekuroba a wɔrokɔ mu no, nna ɔyɛɛ dɛ dza ɔrokɔ n'enyim.
    28. Na wɔhyɛɛ no dɛ, Nye hɛn ntsena, na orudu ewimbir, na adze reyɛ asa. Na ɔmanee dɛ ɔnye hɔn bɛtsena.
    29. Na ɔbaa dɛ ɔnye hɔn tse edzidzii no, ɔfaa paanoo, na ohyiraa do, na obubui maa hɔn.
    30. Na hɔn enyiwa buei ma wohun no, na ɔyeew hɔn enyiwa do.
    31. Na wɔsesee hɔnho dɛ, Ɔnye hɛn rekasa wɔ kwan mu, na obuee kyerɛwnsɛm no mu kyerɛɛ hɛn no, aso hɛn akoma anndzɛw hɛn mu dɛɛdɛɛw?
    32. Na wɔsoɛɛr dɔn no ara mu sanee baa Jerusalɛm, na wobohun dubiako no nye hɔn a wɔka hɔn ho no dɛ wɔaboa hɔn ano, LUK.24.34, na wɔreka dɛ, Ewuradze asoɛr ampa, na oeyi noho akyerɛ Simon.
    33. Na hɔn so wɔkaa dza ɔbaa kwan no mu, na mbrɛ wohun no wɔ paanoo bu mu no kyerɛɛ hɔn.
    34. Na wɔrekasa dɛm no, Jesus nankasa begyinaa hɔn finimfin, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Asomdwee nka hom.
    35. Na wɔbobɔɔ piriw na hu kaa hɔn, na wosusuu dɛ woehu ɔsaman.
    36. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ɔyɛ dɛn na hom akoma etutu yi? Na ebɛnadze ntsi na adwendwen soɛrsoɛr hom akoma mu yi?
    37. Hom nhwɛ me nsa nye me nan dɛ emi mankasa a: hom nka me nhwɛ; na ɔsaman nnyi honam nye mbiew dɛ mbrɛ hom hu dɛ mowɔ yi.
    38. Na ɔsee dɛm no, ɔdze ne nsa nye ne nan kyerɛɛ hɔn.
    39. Nna wɔda ho nngye nndzi wɔ dɛw ntsi, na ɔyɛ hɔn nwanwa no, ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Ana hom wɔ edziban bi wɔ ha?
    40. Na wɔmaa no esunam a wɔatoto sin.
    41. Na ɔgyee na odzii wɔ hɔn enyim.
    42. Na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Iyi nye me nsɛm a meda ho wɔ hom nkyɛn no mekaa kyerɛɛ hom dɛ ɔsɛ dɛ dza wɔakyerɛw no wɔ mo ho wɔ Moses mbra, nye nkɔnhyɛfo, nye ndwom no mu nyina bɛba mu.
    43. Nna obuee hɔn adwen mu, ama wɔaatse kyerɛwnsɛm no ase;
    44. na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Dɛmara na wɔakyerɛw no, dɛ Kraest no bohu amandze na da a otsia ebiasa no ɔasoɛr efi ewufo mu;
    45. na woedua ne dzin do akɛka nnuho nye bɔnfakyɛ akyerɛ amanaman nyina, wobefitsi ase wɔ Jerusalɛm.
    46. Hom yɛ yinom ho adasefo.
    47. Na hwɛ, morosoma m'Egya n'anohoba maba hom do: na hom ntsena kurow no mu nkodu dɛ wobefi sor abrɛ hom ahoɔdzen.
    48. Na ɔdze hɔn fiir edzi kodur Bɛtani tsentsendo, na ɔmaa ne nsa do hyiraa hɔn.
    49. Na ɔbaa dɛ, orihyira hɔn no, ɔnye hɔn paapaa mu na wɔfaa no kɔr sor.
    50. Na hɔn dze, wɔkotowee sɔɔr no, na wɔdze dɛw kɛse sanee baa Jerusalɛm;
    51. na wɔtsenaa asɔrfi mu hɔ daa yii Nyankopɔn ayɛw.
