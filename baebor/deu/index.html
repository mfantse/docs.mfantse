    Deuteronomi

    Tsir 1

    1. Iyinom nye nsɛm a Moses kaa kyerɛɛ Israel nyinara wɔ Jordan egya sar no do, wɔ Araba Suf tsentsendo, wɔ Paran, nye Tofel, nye Laban, nye Hazerot, nye Di-zahab ntamu.
    2. Ofi Horeb wɔ bepɔw Seir kwan do kɔ Kades-barnea ɔyɛ nda dubiako kwan.
    3. Na ɔbaa dɛ afe a otsia eduanan no mu, bosoom a otsia dubiako no ne da a odzi kan no, Moses kasaa kyerɛɛ Israelmba no dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ no dɛ ɔnka nkyerɛ hɔn no nyinara;
    4. wɔber a oeku Amorifo hen Sihon a ɔtse Hesbon, nye Basanhen Ɔge a ɔtse Astarot, wɔ Edrei no ekyir no.
    5. Jordan egya wɔ Moab asaase do, na Moses hyɛɛ ase kyerɛkyerɛɛ mbra yi mu dɛ, DEU.1.6, EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn kasaa kyerɛɛ hɛn wɔ Horeb dɛ, Hom atsena bepɔw yi ho akyɛr dodow:
    6. hom ndan homho, na hom ntu nkɔ Amorifo nkokodo nkurow mu, nye mbeambea a ɔbɛn ho hɔ nyinara, wɔ Araba, nkokodo nkurow mu, nye tɛtɛr mu, nye Pomu, nye mpoano, Keenanfo asaase do, nye Lebanon do, nkodu esutsen kɛse Eufrates no ho.
    7. Hwɛ, medze asaase no ato hom enyim: hom nkɔ nkɛfa asaase a EWURADZE kaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ hom egyanom, Abraham, Isaak, nye Jakob, dɛ ɔdze bɛma hɔn nye hɔn asefo a wodzi hɔn ekyir no.
    8. Na mekasaa kyerɛɛ hom aber no mu dɛ, Mara nkotsee runntum monnkora hom:
    9. EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ama hom adɔr, na hwɛ, ndɛ da yi hom dodow tse dɛ ɔsor nworaba.
    10. EWURADZE hom egyanom Nyankopɔn, nyɛ hom dɛ mbrɛ hom tse yi mbɔho apem so nka ho, na onhyira hom, dɛ mbrɛ ɔabɔ hom anohoba no!
    11. Mebɛyɛ dɛn monko metum asoa hom haw, nye hom adzesoa, nye hom aperper?
    12. Hom nyiyi mbanyin enyansafo, nye enyimdzefo, na wɔda nsew, dɛ mbrɛ hom ebusuakuw no tsetsee, na menyɛ hɔn etsitsir wɔ hom do.
    13. Na hom buaa me see dɛ, Dza aka no oye ma hɛn dɛ yɛyɛ.
    14. Nna mefaa hom ebusuakuw ebusuakuw mu etsitsir no, mbanyin enyansafo wɔda nsew, na meyɛɛ hɔn etsitsir wɔ hom do, apem apem do asafohemfo, nye ɔha ɔha do asafohemfo, nye eduonum eduonum do asafohemfo, nye du du do asafohemfo, nye ahwɛdofo, dɛ mbrɛ hom ebusuakuw tsetsee.
    15. Na mehyɛɛ hom atsɛmbuafo mbra aber no mu dɛ, Hom ntsie hom nuanom ntamu nsɛm, na hom mbu ntsɛn tsenenee wɔ nyimpa nye no nua, nye ɔhɔho a ɔnye no wɔ hɔ ntamu.
    16. Mma hom nnhwɛ nyimpa enyim wɔ atsɛmbu mu; hom ntsie aketseaba nye ɔkɛse nyina pɛ; mma hom nnsuro nyimpa enyim; na atsɛmbu yɛ Nyankopɔn dze: na asɛm a ɔyɛ dzen dodow ma hom no na hom mfa mbrɛ me, na mintsie.
    17. Na mehyɛɛ hom dza hom nyɛ no nyinara ho mbra aber no mu.
    18. Na yetui fii Horeb, na yɛnantsewee faa sar kɛse a ɔyɛ hu a hom hunii no nyinara do wɔ Amorifo nkokodo nkurow kwan do, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn hyɛɛ hɛn no; na yedur Kades Barnea.
    19. Na mesee hom dɛ, Hom edu Amorifo nkokodo nkurow a EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn dze rema hɛn no ho.
    20. Hwɛ, EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dze asaase no ato hom enyim: hom mfow kɔ, nkɛfa, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE, hom egyanom Nyankopɔn aka akyerɛ hom no; mma hom nnsuro, nkyii so mma hom akoma nntu.
    21. Na hom baa me nkyɛn, hom nyinara; na wɔsee dɛ, Ma yɛnsoma mbanyin ma wondzi hɛn enyim nkɛsera asaase no mma hɛn, na wɔmbɛka ɔkwan kor a yɛbɛfa do, nye nkurow kor a yebodu do ho asɛm nkyerɛ hɛn.
    22. Na asɛm no yɛɛ me enyisɔ dɛ: na mefaa hom mu nyimpa duebien, ebusuakuw biara mu nyimpa kor:
    23. na wɔdanee kɔfoow bepɔw no, na wokodur Eskol ɔbɔn mu, na wɔserae.
    24. Na wɔfaa asaase no do aba bi kitsae, na wɔdze sanee baa hɛn nkyɛn, na wɔbɛkaa hɛn asɛm see dɛ, Asaase a EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn dze rema hɛn no oye.
    25. Naaso hom ammpɛ dɛ hom fow kɔ, na hom tseew EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn mbrasɛm do etua:
    26. na hom hwinhwinii wɔ hom asesee mu see dɛ, Osiandɛ EWURADZE tan hɛn ntsi na oyii hɛn wɔ Egypt asaase do, dɛ ɔdze hɛn rebɛhyɛ Amorifo nsa, ma wɔaasɛɛ hɛn.
    27. Henfa na yɛmfa nkɔ? Hɛn enuanom etutu hɛn akoma, dɛ, Ɔman no wɔsoso na wɔwowar sen hɛn; nkurow no yɛ akɛse na wɔatotow ho afasu akɔ sor; na nkyii so yehun Anakfo mbabanyin wɔ hɔ.
    28. Nna mesee hom dɛ, Mma hu nnka hom, nkyii so mma hom nnsuro hɔn.
    29. EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn a odzi hom enyim rokɔ no, ɔno na ɔbɔko ama hom, dɛ mbrɛ ɔyɛe maa hom wɔ Egypt wɔ hom enyiwa mu no nyinara;
    30. nye sar no do, beebi a hom hun mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn sii tuur hom, dɛ mbrɛ nyimpa tur ne babanyin, wɔ kwan a hom nantsewee no nyinara mu araa dze bodur dɛ hom dur ha yi.
    31. Naaso iyi mu hom anngye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn enndzi, DEU.1.33, ɔno a odzii hom enyim wɔ ɔkwan no mu dɛ ɔrokɔhwehwɛ beebi a ma hom abɔ asesee, wɔ gya mu adzesaa, dɛ ɔkyerɛ hom ɔkwan a hom mfa do, nye mununkum mu adzekyee.
    32. Na EWURADZE tsee hom nsɛm, na no bo fuwii, na ɔkaa ntam dɛ, DEU.1.35, Ampaara ebusuasantsen bɔn yi mu nyimpa yi mu biara runnhu asaase pa a mekaa ho ntam dɛ medze bɛma hom egyanom no, DEU.1.36, gyedɛ Jefune ne babanyin Kaleb, ɔno na obohu; na ɔno na medze asaase a oetsia do no bɛma no, nye ne mba; osiandɛ oedzi EWURADZE ekyir koraa.
    33. Eso hom ntsi EWURADZE nyaa no ho ebufuw, dɛ, Wo so ronnkɔ hɔ:
    34. Nun ne babanyin Josua a ogyina w'enyim no, ɔno na ɔbɔkɔ hɔ: hyɛ no dzen; na ɔno na ɔbɛyɛ ma Israel enya edzi.
    35. Nkyii so hom mbofraba no a hom se dɛ wɔbɛyɛ afonam no, nye hom mba no a ndɛ da yi wonnyi papa anaa bɔn ho nyimdzee no, hɔn na wɔbɔkɔ hɔ, na hɔn na medze mebɛma hɔn, na wɔafa.
    36. Na hom dze, hom ndan homho, na hom ntu nkɔ sar no do wɔ Mbɛw Po kwan do.
    37. Nna hom buae na wɔsee me dɛ, Yɛayɛ bɔn etsia EWURADZE, yɛbɔfow akɔ akɔko, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn hyɛɛ hɛn no nyinara. Na hom mu nyimpa biara dze n'akodze miamiae, na wodzii kan dɛ wɔrokɔfow akɔ bepɔw no do.
    38. Na EWURADZE see me dɛ, Se hɔn dɛ, Mma wɔmmfow, nkyii so mma wɔnnko; na minnyi hom mu; bɛyɛ na hom akɔtotɔr wɔ hom atamfo enyim.
    39. Na mekasaa mekyerɛɛ hom, na hom enntsie; na hom tseew EWURADZE mbrasɛm do etua, na hom tsitsii hom enyi ho ara fowee kɔr bepɔw no do.
    40. Na Amorifo a wɔtse bepɔw no do no fiir edzi behyiaa hom, na wotsiiw hom, dɛ mbrɛ ndowa yɛ, na wokunkum hom wɔ Seir araa kodur Horma.
    41. Na hom sanee na wobosui wɔ EWURADZE enyim; na EWURADZE enntsie hom ndze, na eso ɔannyɛ aso amma hom.
    42. Nna hom tsenaa Kades dzii nda pii, dɛ mbrɛ nda a hom dze tsenaa hɔ tse no.

    Tsir 2

    1. Nna yɛdanee, na yetuu hɛn kwan kɔr sar no do wɔ Mbɛw Po kwan do, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE kaa kyerɛɛ me no: na yekyiin Seir bepɔw ho nda pii.
    2. Na EWURADZE kaa kyerɛɛ me dɛ, DEU.2.3, Hom ekyin bepɔw yi ho akyɛr dodow: hom ndan homho wɔ kwaamu afamu.
    3. Na hyɛ ɔman no dɛ, Hom rubotwa mu wɔ hom nuanom Esau mba a wɔtse Seir no ntoaado; na wobosuro hom: dɛm ntsi hom nhwɛ homho yie papa:
    4. mma wɔnnye hɔn nndzi aper; na meremmfa hɔn asaase no memma hom, oho, nnyɛ mbrɛ ɔnamɔn mpo betsia a: osiandɛ medze Seir bepɔw mama Esau dɛ ɔmfa.
    5. Hom mfa sika ntɔ hɔn hɔ edziban, na hom eedzi; na hom mfa sika ntɔ hɔn hɔ nsu so, na hom aanom.
    6. Na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ehyira wo wɔ wo nsaano edwuma nyinara mu: oehu wo nantsew wɔ sar kɛse yi do: mfe eduanan yi EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn edzi w'ekyir; ennhia wo biribiara.
    7. Nna yetwae fi hɛn nuanom Esaumba a wɔtse Seir no ho, fi Araba kwan do fi Elat nye Ezion-geber. Na yɛdanee, na yekotwaa mu wɔ Moab sar no do kwan do.
    8. Na EWURADZE see me dɛ, Mma nnhiahia Moab ho, nkyii so mma nnye hɔn nndzi aper nnko: na meremmfa n'asaase mu bi memma wo mma emmfa; osiandɛ medze Ar ama Lotmba dɛ wɔmfa.
    9. Emfo no na wɔtsenaa do nkan no, ɔman kɛse a wɔdɔɔ so, na wɔwowar dɛ Anakfo:
    10. iyinom so na wobu hɔn Refafo, dɛ Anakfo; na Moabfo dze wɔfrɛ hɔn Emfo.
    11. Horfo so na nkyɛ nkan no wɔtsenaa Seir, na Esaumba no hyɛɛ hɔn ananmu; na wɔsɛɛ hɔn wɔ hɔn enyim, na wɔtsenaa hɔn ananmu dɛ mbrɛ Israel yɛɛ n'enyadze asaase a EWURADZE dze maa hɔn no.
    12. Afei hom nsoɛr, na hom nkotwa ɔwora Zered. Na yekotwaa ɔwora Zered.
    13. Na mfe dodow a yɛdze nantsewee fi Kades-barnea bodur dɛ yebotwaa ɔwora Zered no, yɛ mfe eduasa awɔtwe; kodur dɛ akofo ebusuasantsen no nyinara wuwui wɔ nseraban no mu, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE kaa ho ntam no.
    14. Nkyii so EWURADZE nsa tsiaa hɔn, dɛ ɔresɛɛ hɔn efi nseraban no mu, ekodu dɛ wɔbɛhyew.
    15. Nna ɔbaa dɛ, aber a akofo no nyinara hyewee na wowuwui fii ɔman no mu no, DEU.2.17, EWURADZE kaa kyerɛɛ me dɛ, DEU.2.18, Ndɛ da yi na irubotwa mu wɔ Ar, Moab ntoaado no:
    16. na sɛ ikodu Ammonmba tsentsendo a, mma nnhiahia hɔn ho, na nnye hɔn nndzi aper so: na meremmfa Ammonmba asaase mu bi memma wo mma emmfa: osiandɛ medze mama Lotmba dɛ wɔmfa.
    17. Ɔno so wobu no Refafo asaase; Refafo tsenaa do nkan no; na Ammonfo dze wɔfrɛ hɔn Zamzumfo;
    18. ɔman kɛse a, wɔdɔɔ so, na wɔwowar dɛ Anakfo; na EWURADZE sɛɛ hɔn wɔ hɔn enyim; na wɔhyɛɛ hɔn ananmu:
    19. dɛ mbrɛ ɔyɛe maa Esaumba a wɔtse Seir, aber a ɔsɛɛ Horfo wɔ hɔn enyim; na wɔhyɛɛ hɔn ananmu, na wɔtsenaa hɔn ananmu araa dze bodu ndɛ da yi;
    20. na Avifo a wɔtsenatsenaa nkuroba araa dze kodu Gaza mu no, Kaftorfo a, wofi Kaftor bae no, bɛsɛɛ hɔn, na wɔtsenaa hɔn ananmu.
    21. Hom nsoɛr, wontu hom kwan, na wonkotwa Arnon bɔnsa; hwɛ, medze Amorinyi Sihon, Hesbon hen no, nye n'asaase ahyɛ wo nsa: hyɛ ase fa, na nye no ndzi aper nko.
    22. Ndɛ da yi na mebɛhyɛ ase medze wo ho suro nye wo ho hu megu amanaman a wɔwɔ asaase nyina do a wɔbɛtse wo nka do, na hɔn ho apopo, na hɔn ho eguan hɔn wɔ wo ntsi.
    23. Na Kedamot sar do na mifi mosomaa abɔfo ma wɔdze asomdwee nsɛm kɔr Hesbon hen Sihon nkyɛn dɛ, DEU.2.27, Ma muntwa mu wɔ w'asaase do: ɔtsempɔn do ara na mebɛfa akɔ, merenndan mmfa enyimfa anaa abankum.
    24. Tɔn me edziban gye sika, ma mindzi; na ma me nsu gye sika ma monnom: ma memfa m'anan kwa mumbotwa mu;
    25. dɛ mbrɛ Esaumba a wɔtse Seir, na Moabfo a wɔtse Ar, yɛɛ me no; ma onkodu dɛ mubotwa Jordan akɔ asaase a EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn dze rema hɛn no do.
    26. Na Hesbonhen Sihon amma hɛn kwan dɛ yentwa mu wɔ ne nkyɛn: osiandɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn maa no asoɔdzen sunsum, na opiriim n'akoma, ama ɔdze no aahyɛ wo nsa, dɛ mbrɛ ɔtse ndɛ da yi.
    27. Na EWURADZE see me dɛ, Hwɛ, mahyɛ ase meyi Sihon nye n'asaase mama wɔ w'enyim: hyɛ ase fa, na enya n'asaase no edzi.
    28. Nna Sihon fiir edzi behyiaa hɛn, ɔno nye ne man nyinara, dɛ ɔrobɔko wɔ Jahaz.
    29. Na EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn yii no mae wɔ hɛn enyim; na yekunkum ɔno, nye ne mbabanyin, nye ne man nyinara.
    30. Na yɛfaa no nkurow nyinara aber no mu, na yɛsɛɛ kurow biara a nyimpa tse mu no pasapasa, dze kaa mbaa nye mbofraba ho; yeenngyaa biara mma ɔannka:
    31. ofiembowa nko na hɛnara yɛfaa no afonam, dze kaa nkurow a yɛfae no mu asadze ho.
    32. Ofi Aroer a ɔwɔ Arnon ɔbɔn no ano, na ofi kurow a ɔwɔ Arnon ɔbɔn no mu no do, araa dze kɔ Gilead, kurow biara nnyi hɔ a ɔyɛɛ dzen dodow maa hɛn: EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn yii ne nyinara mae wɔ hɛn enyim:
    33. Ammonmba asaase no nko na ɔwo ammbɛn ho; esutsen Jabbok ne nkyɛn nyinara, nye nkurow a ɔwɔ nkokodo nkurow mu no, nye beebiara a EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn amma hɛn ho kwan no.

    Tsir 3

    1. Nna yɛdanee, na yɛfowee kɔr Basan kwan do: na Basan hen Ɔge fiir edzi behyiaa hɛn, ɔno nye ne man nyinara, dɛ ɔrobɔko wɔ Edrei.
    2. Na EWURADZE see me dɛ, Mma nnsuro no: na meyi ɔno, nye ne man nyinara, nye n'asaase, ahyɛ wo nsa; na yɛ no dɛ mbrɛ ɔyɛɛ Amorifo hen Sihon a ɔtsenaa Hesbon no.
    3. Dɛmara na EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn yii Basanhen Ɔge so, nye ne man nyinara, hyɛɛ hɛn nsa: na yekum no araa kodur dɛ woenngyaa biara mma wɔannka amma no.
    4. Na yɛgyee no nkurow nyinara aber no mu; kurow biara nnyi hɔ a yɛanngye no hɔn hɔ; nkurow eduosia, Argob mbew nyinara, Ɔge n'ahenman a ɔwɔ Basan.
    5. Iyinom nyinara yɛ nkurow a no ho wɔ afasu atsentsen, mbotsen, nye adaban; ɔsor nkurow a ɔdɔɔ so piigyan a no ho nnyi afasu no.
    6. Na yɛsɛɛ hɔn pasapasa, dɛ mbrɛ yɛyɛɛ Hesbonhen Sihon no, yɛsɛɛ kurow biara a nyimpa tse mu no pasapasa, dze kaa mbaa nye mbofraba ho.
    7. Na ofiembowa nyinara, na nkurow no mu asadze no dze, hɛnara yɛfaa no afonam.
    8. Na yɛgyee asaase no wɔ Amorifo ahemfo beenu no a wɔwɔ Jordan egya no nsamu aber no mu, fi Arnon ɔbɔn mu kodur bepɔw Hermon ho;
    9. (Hermon no Zidonfo frɛ no Sirion, na Amorifo frɛ no Senir;)
    10. tɛtɛr mu nkurow nyinara, nye Gilead nyinara, nye Basan nyinara, kodur Saleka nye Edrei, Ɔge n'ahenman nkurow a ɔwɔ Basan.
    11. (Na Basanhen Ɔge nko na ɔkae wɔ Refafo nkaa no mu; hwɛ, nna ne mpadua ayɛ dadze mpadua; nnda ho nnsi Ammonmba Rabaa bi a? Ne tsentsen yɛ basafa anan nye fa, na ne tɛtɛr basafa ebien, dɛ mbrɛ nyimpa basafa tse.)
    12. Na asaase yi na yɛfae wɔ aber no mu: ofi Aroer a ɔwɔ Arnon ɔbɔn ho, nye Gilead nkokodo nkurow ɔfa, nye no nkurow, na medze maa Reubenfo nye Gadfo;
    13. na Gilead nkaa, nye Basan nyinara, Ɔge n'ahenman no, na medze memaa Manaasɛ ebusuakuw ɔfa no; Argob mbew nyinara, mekyerɛ Basan nyinara. (Ɔnoara na wɔfrɛ no Refaim asaase no.
    14. Manaasɛ ne babanyin Jair faa Argob mbew nyinara, kodur Gersurfo nye Maakatfo ntoaado, na ɔtoo no dzin, mekyerɛ Basan a, dɛ mbrɛ ne dzin tse, Havot-jair, dze bodu ndɛ da yi.)
    15. Na medze Gilead memaa Makir.
    16. Na Reubenfo nye Gadfo na medze ofi Gilead araa dze kodu Arnon ɔbɔn ho, ɔbɔn no ne finimfin, nye no ntoaado memaa hɔn; mekyerɛ dze kodu esutsen Jabbok a ɔyɛ Ammonmba ɔhe no do;
    17. Araba so, nye Jordan nye no ntoaado, ofi Kinneret araa dze kodu Araba epo ho, Mbɛw Po no, wɔ Pisga ntserɛwee ase boka afamu.
    18. Na mehyɛɛ hom aber no mu dɛ, EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dze asaase yi ama hom dɛ hom mfa: hom mmia ntwa mu ndzi hom nuanom Israelmba enyim, mbanyin a wɔyɛ ndam nyinara.
    19. Hom yernom, nye hom mbofraba, nye hom fiembowa dze (minyim dɛ hom wɔ fiembowa piigyan) wɔntsena hom nkurow a medze mama hom no mu;
    20. nkodu dɛ EWURADZE bɛma hom nuanom ahomgye, dɛ hom, na hɔn so wɔaakɛfa asaase a EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dze rema hɔn wɔ Jordan egya no: n'ekyir no na hom mu nyimpa biara nsan mbra n'enyadze do, dza medze mama hom no a.
    21. Na mehyɛɛ Josua aber no mu dɛ, W'enyiwa ehu dza EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dze ayɛ ahemfo beenu yi nyinara:
    22. Mma hom nnsuro hɔn: na EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn, ɔno nye nyia ɔroko ma hom.
    23. Na meserɛɛ EWURADZE aber no mu dɛ, DEU.3.24, Ao EWURADZE Nyankopɔn, ɔwo na ahyɛ ase dze wo kɛseyɛ, nye wo nsa dzendzen no akyerɛ w'akowaa: na onyame bɛn na ɔwɔ ɔsor anaa asaase do a obotum ayɛ dɛ mbrɛ wo ndwuma, nye mbrɛ w'akatakyidze tsetsee?
    24. Mepa wo kyɛw, ma muntwa nkɔ, na monkɔhwɛ asaase pa a ɔwɔ Jordan egya no, bepɔw fɛɛfɛw no, nye Lebanon.
    25. Na hom ntsi EWURADZE no bo fuuw me, na oenntsie me: na EWURADZE see me dɛ, Ma ɔndɔɔ wo so; mma nnye me nnkasa asɛm yi ho bio.
    26. Fow kɔ Pisga etsifi, na ma w'enyiwa do wɔ anee afamu, nye kwaamu afamu, nye pomu afamu nye boka afamu, na fa w'enyiwa hwɛ; na ɔwo runntwa Jordan yi.
    27. Mbom hyɛ Josua, na hyɛ no nsi, na hyɛ no dzen: na ɔno na obedzi ɔman yi enyim etwa, na ɔno na ɔbɛyɛ ma woeenya asaase a ibohu no edzi.
    28. Nna yɛtsenaa ɔbɔn no mu wɔ Bɛt-peor tsentsendo.

    Tsir 4

    1. Na afei, Israel, hom ntsie ahyɛdze nye atsɛmbu a mekyerɛkyerɛ hom dɛ hom nyɛ no; na hom eenya nkwa, na hom akɔ akɛfa asaase a EWURADZE, hom egyanom Nyankopɔn dze rema hom no.
    2. Mma hom mmfa hwee nnka asɛm a merehyɛ hom yi ho, nkyii so mma hom nyi do bi, na hom eedzi EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn mbransɛm a merehyɛ hom yi do.
    3. Hom enyiwa ehu dza osian Baal-peor ntsi EWURADZE yɛe; na nyimpa a wodzii Baal-peor ekyir no nyinara, EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn asɛɛ hɔn efi hom mu.
    4. Na hom a hom takyeer EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ho no dze, hom nyinara tse ase ndɛ da yi.
    5. Hwɛ makyerɛkyerɛ hom ahyɛdze nye ntsɛmbu, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE me Nyankopɔn hyɛɛ me no ara, dɛ hom nyɛ dɛm wɔ asaase a hom rokɔ do akɛfa no mu.
    6. Dɛm ntsi hom ndzi do na hom nyɛ; na iyi nye hom nyansaa nye hom ntseasee wɔ aman a wɔbɛtse ahyɛdze yi nyinara enyim, na wɔbɛse dɛ, Ampaara ɔman kɛse yi yɛ nyansa nye nyimdzee man.
    7. Na ɔman kɛse bɛn na ɔwɔ hɔ a ɔwɔ nyame a ɔbɛn hɔn dɛm, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn tse wɔ aber biara a yɛfrɛ no no?
    8. Na ɔman kɛse bɛn na ɔwɔ hɔ a ɔwɔ ahyɛdze nye atsɛmbu tsenenee dɛ mbrɛ mbra yi nyinara a medze risi hom enyim ndɛ da yi tse?
    9. Hom ho yie ara na nhwɛ, na hom nsie hom kra yie, bɛyɛ na hom werɛ efir ndzɛmba a enyiwa hunii no, na bɛyɛ na efi hom akoma mu hom nkwa nda nyinara mu; mbom ma wo mba nye wo mba hɔn mba nhu;
    10. da a hom gyinaa EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn enyim wɔ Horeb, aber a EWURADZE see me dɛ, Boaboa ɔman no ano ma me, na memma wontsie me nsɛm, na woeesuasua dɛ wosuro me wɔ nda a wɔdze tse ase wɔ asaase do nyinara, na wɔdze akyerɛkyerɛ hɔn mba no.
    11. Na hom twee pinii na hom begyinaa bepɔw no ase; ber a bepɔw no dzɛɛw gya kɔr sor tɔnn, sum, mununkum, nye sum kebii.
    12. Na EWURADZE fi gya no mu kasaa kyerɛɛ hom: hom tsee ne nsɛm ndze, na no su dze hom ennhu; ne ndze nko na hom tsee.
    13. Na ɔkaa n'ahyɛmu a ɔhyɛɛ hom dɛ hom nyɛ no kyerɛɛ hom, mekyerɛ mbransɛm du no; na ɔkyerɛɛw no wɔ abopon ebien do.
    14. Na EWURADZE hyɛɛ me aber no mu dɛ menkyerɛkyerɛ hom ahyɛdze nye ntsɛmbu, na hom aayɛ no wɔ asaase a hom rutwa mu akɛfa no do.
    15. Dɛm ntsi hom nhwɛ homho yie papa; osiandɛ hom ennhu su biara da no a EWURADZE fi ogya no mu kasaa kyerɛɛ hom wɔ Horeb no:
    16. bɛyɛ na hom asɛɛ homho, na hom ayɛ ohonyi a wɔasen wɔ adze biara no su mu ama hom ho, ɔbanyin anaa ɔbaa saso, DEU.4.17, abowa biara a ɔwɔ asaase do ne saso, etubowa biara a otu wɔ wimu ne saso, DEU.4.18, adze biara a ɔwea famu ne saso, nam biara a ɔwɔ nsu mu wɔ asaase ase ne saso:
    17. na bɛyɛ na hom ama hom enyiwa do wɔ sor, na sɛ hom hu ewia nye bosoom, nye nworaba, mekyerɛ sor dɔm no nyinara a, wɔatwe hom ma hom akɔsɔr hɔn, na asom hɔn, dza EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ahyehyɛ ama aman nyinara wɔ asaase nyina do no.
    18. Hom dze, EWURADZE afa hom, na oeyi hom efi dadze fononoo mu, efi Egypt dɛ hom mbɛyɛ n'egyapadze mma no, dɛ mbrɛ ɔtse ndɛ da yi.
    19. Nkyii so hom ntsi EWURADZE nyaa me ebufuw, na ɔkaa ntam dɛ murunntwa Jordan, na moronnkɔ asaase pa no a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ egyapadze no do.
    20. Emi dze muruwu wɔ asaase yi do, murunntwa Jordan: na hom dze wobotwa, na wɔakɛfa asaase pa no.
    21. Hom nhwɛ hom ho yie, bɛyɛ na hom werɛ efir EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ahyɛmu a ɔnye hom dzii no, na hom ayɛ ohonyi a wɔasen wɔ adze bi a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn abra wo no su do ama woho.
    22. Na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn yɛ ogya a ɔmen, Nyame nyinkufo a.
    23. Sɛ hom wowo mba, na hom mba so wo mba, na hom kyɛr wɔ asaase no do, na hom sɛɛ homho, na wɔyɛ ohonyi a wɔasen wɔ adze biara no su do, na hom yɛ dza omuo wɔ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn enyiwa do, dze yi no ebufuw a:
    24. mefrɛ sor nye asaase dɛ wondzi hom dase ndɛ da yi, dɛ ɔhera na hom bɛhera ntsɛmara wɔ asaase a hom rutwa Jordan akɛfa no do; hom nda ronnwar wɔ do, mbom ɔsɛɛ na hom bɛsɛɛ pasapasa.
    25. Na EWURADZE bɔbɔ hom apetse aman mu, na ɔbɛka hom nyimpa kakrabaabi wɔ amanaman mu mbrɛ EWURADZE begya hom akɔ no.
    26. Na hɔ na hom bɔsom enyame, nyimpa nsaano edwuma, ndua nye abo a wonnhu, wɔnntse, wonndzidzi, wonnhua.
    27. Na sɛ hom fi hɔ hwehwɛ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn a, hom bohu no, sɛ hom dze hom akoma nyinara nye hom kra nyinara hwehwɛ n'ekyir kwan a.
    28. Sɛ hom wɔ ahohia mu, na ndzɛmba yi nyinara ba hom do a, nda a odzi ekyir no mu no nsan mbra EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn nkyɛn, na hom ntsie ne ndze:
    29. na EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn yɛ mbɔbɔrhufo Nyankopɔn; ɔremmpa hom, nkyii so ɔrennsɛɛ hom, no werɛ rimmfir hom egyanom ahyɛmu a ɔkaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ hɔn no so.
    30. Na afei hom nkaa tsetse nda a etwa mu kɔ no, fitsi da a Nyankopɔn bɔɔ nyimpa wɔ asaase do, na ofi ɔsor n'ano kor kodu kor so, kyerɛ dɛ biribi esi a ɔtse dɛ mbrɛ adze kɛse yi tse, anaa wɔatse dza ɔnye no sɛ?
    31. Ana ɔman bi atse Nyankopɔn ndze dɛ ofi gya mu rekasa pɛn, dɛ mbrɛ hom atse no, na woenya nkwa?
    32. Ana Nyankopɔn asɔ ahwɛ dɛ ɔbɔkɔ akɛfa ɔman bi efi ɔman kor so bi mu afa, wɔ nsɔhwɛ, nsɛnkyerɛdze, nye anwanwadze, nye ɔsa, nye nsa a ɔyɛ dzen, nye abasa a wɔatserɛw mu, nye suro akɛse mu, dɛ mbrɛ dza EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn yɛe maa hom wɔ Egypt wɔ hom enyiwa mu no nyinara tsetsee a?
    33. Hom na wɔdze kyerɛɛ hom, ma hom eehu dɛ EWURADZE nye Nyankopɔn; obi nnyi hɔ bio ka no ho.
    34. Ofi sor maa hom tsee ne ndze, ma ɔaakyerɛkyerɛ hom: na asaase do na ɔmaa hun ne gya kɛse no; na ofi gya no mu na etsee ne nsɛm.
    35. Na osiandɛ ɔdɔɔ hom egyanom ntsi, na ɔpaa hɔn asefo a wodzi hɔn ekyir no na ɔdze n'enyim nye no tum kɛse no, yii hom fii Egypt;
    36. dɛ orubotutu amanaman a wɔyɛ akɛse na wɔyɛ dzen sen hom efi enyim, dɛ ɔdze hom rokɔ mu, dze hɔn asaase akɛma hom dɛ ewungyadze, dɛ mbrɛ ɔtse ndɛ da yi.
    37. Dɛm ntsi hom nhu ndɛ da yi, na hom mfa nto hom akoma mu, dɛ EWURADZE nye Nyankopɔn wɔ sor nye asaase do: na obi nnyi hɔ bio.
    38. Na hom ndzi dzi n'ahyɛdze, nye ne mbransɛm a merehyɛ hom ndɛ da yi do, na eesi hom yie, na ɔnye hom mba a wodzi ekyir, na hom nda aawar wɔ asaase a EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dze rema hom no do daa.
    39. Nna Moses yiyii nkurow ebiasa wɔ Jordan egya a odzi ba wipuei no;
    40. dɛ onyimpakumfo a okum no nyɛnko awerɛfir mu, na ɔnntan no dada bi no, nguan nkɔ hɔ; na sɛ oguan kɔ nkurow yi kor mu a, oeenya nkwa:
    41. ɔno nye yi, oyii Bezer wɔ sar no do, wɔ ɔman tɛtɛr no mu maa Reubenfo; na Ramot wɔ Gilead, maa Gadfo; na Golan wɔ Basan maa Manaasɛfo.
    42. Na iyi nye mbra a Moses dze too Israelmba enyim:
    43. iyinom nye dase, nye ahyɛdze, nye ntsɛmbu a Moses kaa kyerɛɛ Israelmba, wɔ aber a wofii Egypt bae no;
    44. wɔ Jordan egya, Bɛt-peor tsentsendo ɔbɔn no mu, wɔ Amorifo hen Sihon a ɔtsenaa Hesbon a Moses nye Israelmba kum no wɔ aber a wofii Egypt bae no, n'asaase do:
    45. na wɔfaa n'asaase no yɛɛ hɔndze, na Basan hen Ɔge asaase no Amorifo ahemfo beenu no a wɔwɔ Jordan egya a odzi ba wipuei no;
    46. ofi Aroer a ɔwɔ Arnon ano no araa dze kodu bepɔw Sion a ɔnoara nye Hermon no ho, DEU.4.49, nye Araba nyinara wɔ Jordan egya boka afamu, mekyerɛ ɔdze kodu Araba epo ho, wɔ Pisga ntserɛwee ase.

    Tsir 5

    1. Na Moses frɛɛ Israel nyinara, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Israel, hom ntsie ahyɛdze nye atsɛmbu a mereka no hom asomu ndɛ da yi, na hom eesua, na wɔaadwen do ayɛ.
    2. EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn nye hɛn dzii ahyɛmu wɔ Horeb.
    3. Nnyɛ hɛn egyanom na EWURADZE nye hɔn dzii ahyɛmu yi, mbom hɛn a, hɛnara a ndɛ da yi hɛn nyinara tsetse ase ha yi a.
    4. EWURADZE fii ogya no mu nye hom kasaa enyim nye enyim wɔ bepɔw no ho.
    5. Emi dze migyinaa EWURADZE nye hom ntamu aber no mu, dɛ merekyerɛ hom EWURADZE asɛm; na osian gya no ntsi hom suroi, na wɔammfow bepɔw no na ɔsee dɛ:
    6. Emi nye EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn a miyii wo fii Egypt asaase do, wɔ nkowaasom ofi mu no.
    7. Mma nnya enyame bi so nnka mo ho.
    8. Mma nnyɛ ohonyi bi, adze a ɔwɔ wimu ɔsor, anaa dza ɔwɔ asaase do famu, anaa dza ɔwɔ nsu mu wɔ asaase ase no su biara ne saso mma woho:
    9. mma mmbɔ wo mu adze mma hɔn, na nnsom hɔn: na emi EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn meyɛ Nyame nyinkufo a medze egyanom hɔn emumuyɛ mohwɛ mba kodu ebusuasantsen ebiasa nye anan a wɔtan me do;
    10. na muhu mpempem a wɔdɔ me na wodzi me mbransɛm do no mbɔbɔr.
    11. Mma mmbɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dzin gyangyan na EWURADZE nnkɛma nyia ɔbɔ ne dzin gyangyan no afa noho tɔtrɔtɔ.
    12. Dwen ahomgyeda no do sie no krɔnkrɔn, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn hyɛɛ wo no.
    13. Nda esia na yɛ edwuma na dzi wo dwuma nyinara:
    14. na da a otsia esuon no yɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ahomgyeda: mma nnyɛ edwuma biara wɔ mu, ɔwo o, wo babanyin o, wo babaa o, w'akowaa o, w'afonabaa o, wo nantwi o, w'asoaso o, wo fiembowa mu biara o, wo hɔhoe a ɔwɔ wo kuropon mu o; ama w'akowaa nye w'afonabaa aahom dɛ ɔwo.
    15. Na kaa dɛ ɔwo yɛɛ akowaa wɔ Egypt asaase do, na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze nsa a ɔyɛ dzen nye abasa a wɔatsɛw mu yii wo fii hɔ: dɛm ntsi na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn hyɛɛ wo dɛ sie ahomgyeda no.
    16. Dzi w'egya nye wo na nyi, dɛ mbrɛ wo Nyankopɔn hyɛɛ wo no: na wo nda aawar, na eesi wo yie, wɔ asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo no do.
    17. Mma nndzi ewu.
    18. Nkyii so nnto awar.
    19. Nkyii so nnwia adze.
    20. Nkyii so nngye wo nyɛnko ho dasewia.
    21. Nkyii so mma w'enyi mmber wo nyɛnko yer; nkyii so mma wo kɔn nndɔ wo nyɛnko fi, ne haban, anaa n'akowaa, anaa n'afonabaa, ne nantwi, anaa n'asoaso, anaa adze biara a ɔyɛ wo nyɛnko dze.
    22. Nsɛm yi na EWURADZE fi ogya, mununkum nye sum kebii no mu, dze ndze kɛse kaa kyerɛɛ hom baguaa no nyinara wɔ bepɔw no do: na wɔammfa bi annka ho. Na ɔkyerɛɛw no wɔ abopon ebien do, na ɔdze maa me.
    23. Na ɔbaa dɛ, aber a hom tsee ndze no wɔ sum no mu, wɔ aber a bepɔw no dzɛɛw gya no, hom twee piin me nkyɛn, mekyerɛ hom ebusuakuw mu etsitsir nyinara, nye hom mpanyimfo;
    24. na hom see dɛ, Hwɛ, EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn eyi n'enyimnyam nye ne kɛseyɛ akyerɛ hɛn, na yɛatse ne ndze wɔ gya no mu: ndɛ da yi na yeehu dɛ Nyankopɔn nye nyimpa kasa, naaso ɔtsena ase.
    25. Dɛm ntsi afei ebɛnadze ntsi na yenwu? Osiandɛ gya kɛse yi rebɛhyew hɛn: sɛ yɛtse EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn ndze bio a, ɔno yebowuwu.
    26. Na honam nyinara mu woana na ɔatse Nyankopɔn tseasefo ndze dɛ ofi ogya mu rekasa, dɛ mbrɛ hɛn yɛatse yi, na ɔtsena ase?
    27. Woara twe pin, na tsie dza EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn bɛse nyinara: na woara bɛka dza EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn bɛka akyerɛ wo nyinara kyerɛ hɛn; na yebetsie, na yɛayɛ.
    28. Na EWURADZE tsee hom nsɛm no ndze, aber a hom kasaa kyerɛɛ me no; na EWURADZE see me dɛ, Matse ɔman yi nsɛm no a wɔaka akyerɛ wo no ndze: dza wɔaka no nyinara, wɔase no yie.
    29. Ao kɛtseankyɛ woeenya dɛm akoma wɔ hɔn mu, dɛ wɔdze bosuro me, na woedzi me mbransɛm nyinara do daa daa, na eesi hɔn na ɔnye hɔn mba yie daa!
    30. Kɔ kɛse hɔn dɛ, Hom nsan nkɔ hom asesee mu.
    31. Na ɔwo dze, gyina me nkyɛn ha, na menka mbrasɛm, nye ahyɛdze, nye atsɛmbu a edze bɛkyerɛkyerɛ hɔn no nyinara menkyerɛ wo, na wɔnyɛ no wɔ asaase a medze rema hɔn dɛ wɔmfa no do.
    32. Dɛm ntsi hom ndwen do nyɛ dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ahyɛ hom no: mma hom nndan mmfa enyimfa anaa abankum.
    33. Hom nnantsew kwan no a EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ahyɛ hom no nyinara mu, na hom aatsena ase, na eesi hom yie, na hom nda aawar wɔ asaase a hom rekɛfa no do.

    Tsir 6

    1. Afei iyi nye mbrasɛm, ahyɛdze, nye atsɛmbu a EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn hyɛɛ dɛ wɔmfa nkyerɛkyerɛ hom, na hom nyɛ no wɔ asaase a hom rutwa mu akɛfa no do:
    2. na eesuro EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn, eedzi n'ahyɛdze nye ne mbransɛm a merehyɛ wo yi nyinara do, ɔwo, nye wo babanyin, nye wo babanyin ne babanyin, wo nkwa nda nyinara; na wo nda aawar.
    3. Dɛm ntsi, Israel tsie, na dwen do yɛ; na eesi wo yie, na hom aadɔr pii, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE, w'egyanom Nyankopɔn abɔ wo anohoba no, wɔ asaase a numfonsu nye ewo pem wɔ do no do.
    4. Israel, tsie: EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn yɛ EWURADZE kor:
    5. na fa w'akoma nyinara, na ɔnye wo kra nyinara na ɔnye w'ahoɔdzen nyinara dɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn.
    6. Na nsɛm yi a merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi, ɔnko w'akoma do;
    7. na fa kyerɛkyerɛ wo mba yie, na twetwe ho nkɔmbɔ aber a etse wo fie, nye aber a enam ɔkwan mu, nye aber a ereda, nye aber a erosoɛr.
    8. Na fa yɛ asɛnkyerɛdze bɔ w'abaw do, na ɔnyɛ w'enyiwa ntamu nkaakyekyeree.
    9. Na kyerɛw no wɔ wo fie mpondua ho, nye wo mbobotsen ho.
    10. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, sɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze wo du asaase a ɔkaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ w'egyanom, Abraham, Isaak nye Jakob, dɛ ɔdze rema wo no do; nkurow akɛse fɛɛfɛw a nnyɛ ɔwo na ekyekyeree, DEU.6.11, nye adan a ndzepa nyinara ahyɛ no amaama a nnyɛ ɔwo na edze hyɛɛ mu, nye mbura a woetutu a nnyɛ ɔwo na itutui, wenyin nture nye olive ndua a nnyɛ ɔwo na etsetsɛwee, na sɛ idzidzi na emee a;
    11. ɔno hwɛ yie bɛyɛ na wo werɛ efir EWURADZE a oyii wo fii Egypt asaase do, wɔ nkowaasom ofi mu no.
    12. Suro EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn; na ɔno na som no, na ka ne dzin ho ntam.
    13. Mma hom nndzi enyame fofor a wofi aman a woetwa hom ho ehyia no enyame mu ekyir;
    14. na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn a ɔwɔ wo mu no yɛ Nyame nyinkufo; bɛyɛ na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ebufuw adzɛw wɔ wo do, na ɔasɛɛ wo efi asaase enyi.
    15. Mma hom nnsɔ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn nnhwɛ, dɛ mbrɛ hom sɔɔ no hwɛe wɔ Massa no.
    16. Hom ndzi EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ne mbransɛm, nye ne dasesɛm, nye n'ahyɛdze a ɔdze ahyɛ wo no do yie.
    17. Na yɛ dza ɔtsen na oye wɔ EWURADZE enyiwa do; na eesi wo yie, na aakɔ akɛfa asaase pa a EWURADZE kaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ w'egyanom no, DEU.6.19, na eetu w'atamfo nyinara efi w'enyim, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE aka no.
    18. Sɛ daakye bi wo babanyin bisa wo dɛ, Dasesɛm nye ahyɛdze, nye atsɛmbu a EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn dze hyɛɛ hom yi ase nye dɛn a?
    19. Ɔno se wo ba no dɛ, Nna yɛyɛ Farao no nkowaa wɔ Egypt; na EWURADZE dze nsa a ɔyɛ dzen yii hɛn fii Egypt:
    20. na EWURADZE kyerɛɛ nsɛnkyerɛdze nye anwanwadze, akɛse yayaayaw, wɔ Egypt, Farao, nye ne fi nyinara do, wɔ hɛn enyiwa mu:
    21. na oyii hɛn fii hɔ dɛ ɔdze hɛn reba, dze asaase a ɔkaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ hɛn egyanom abɛma hɛn.
    22. Na EWURADZE hyɛɛ hɛn dɛ yɛnyɛ ahyɛdze yi nyinara, yensuro EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn, wɔ hɛn yie ntsi, na ɔaagye hɛn nkwa, dɛ mbrɛ ɔtse ndɛ da yi.
    23. Na ɔbɛyɛ tsenenee ama hɛn, sɛ yɛdwen do dzi mbrasɛm yi nyinara do wɔ EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn enyim, dɛ mbrɛ ɔahyɛ hɛn no a.

    Tsir 7

    1. Sɛ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dze hom du asaase a hom rokɔ akɛfa no do, na ɔpam amanaman pii wɔ hom enyim, Hitifo, Gergasfo, Amorifo, Keenanfo, Perizfo, Hivifo, nye Jebusfo, amanaman esuon a wɔyɛ akɛse na wɔyɛ dzen sen hom no;
    2. na sɛ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn yi hɔn ma wɔ hom enyim, na hom ku hɔn a; ɔno hom nsɛɛ hɔn pasapasa; mma hom nnye hɔn nndzi ahyɛmu, na mma hom nnhu hɔn mbɔbɔr so:
    3. nkyii so mma hom nnye hɔn nndzi awarwar; mma mmfa hom mbabaa mma hom mbabanyin, mma hom nngye hɔn mbabaa so awar mma hom mbabanyin.
    4. Na wɔbɛdan hom mbabanyin enyi ma woeegyaa m'ekyir dzi, na waakɔsom enyame fofor; ma EWURADZE ebufuw adzɛw hom do, na ɔasɛɛ hom ntsɛm.
    5. Mbom mbrɛ hom nye hɔn ndzi no nye yi; hom ndwuruw hɔn afɔrmbukyia na wombubu hɔn edum mu esinesin, na wontwitwa hɔn abosompɔw Aserim no ngu, na wɔmfa gya nhyew hɔn ehonyi a wɔasen no.
    6. Na hom yɛ ɔman krɔnkrɔn ma EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn: EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn asan mu eyi hom dɛ bɛyɛ nankasa no soronko ɔman wɔ aman a wɔwɔ asaase enyi nyinara mu.
    7. Nnyɛ dɛ hom dodow so sen ɔman biara ntsi na EWURADZE dze no dɔ sii hom do, na eso ɔsaan mu yii hom: na hom suar dodow wɔ aman nyinara mu.
    8. Mbom osiandɛ EWURADZE dɔ hom, na ɔpɛ dɛ odzi ne ntam a ɔkaa kyerɛɛ hom egyanom no do ntsi, na EWURADZE dze nsa a ɔyɛ dzen eyi hom, na ɔapon hom efi no mu, wɔ Egypthen Farao nsamu.
    9. Dɛm ntsi hom nhu dɛ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn, ɔno nye Nyankopɔn; Nyame nokwafo a osie ahyɛmu nye mbɔbɔrhu ma hɔn a wɔdɔ no na wodzi ne mbransɛm do dze kodu ebusuasantsen apem do;
    10. na otua hɔn a wɔtan no kaw dze bɔ hɔn enyim, dze sɛɛ hɔn: ɔrennyɛ nyaa mma nyia ɔtan no, obotua no kaw dze abɔ enyim.
    11. Dɛm ntsi dzi mbrasɛm, nye atsɛmbu a merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi do, na yɛ.
    12. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, osiandɛ hom tsie atsɛmbu yi, na wodzi do, na wɔyɛ no, EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn so bedzi ahyɛmu nye mbɔbɔrhu a ɔkaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ hom egyanom no do ama hom:
    13. na ɔbɔdɔ hom, na oehyira hom, na ɔama hom adɔr: na so oehyira hom honam mu aba na hom asaase mu aba, hom eburow nye hom nsa hom wo ngo, hom anantwi mu ngu mu nye hom nguankuw mu mburwa, wɔ asaase a ɔkaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ hom egyanom dɛ ɔdze bɛma hom no do.
    14. Wobehyira hom asen aman nyinara: ɔbanyin anaa ɔbaa anaa hom fiembowa biar rennyɛ ɔbɔnyin wɔ hom mu.
    15. Na EWURADZE beyi yarba nyinara efi hom do; na ɔremmfa Egypt yarba bɔn a hom nyim no bi nnto hom do, na ɔdze bɔto hɔn a wɔtan hom nyinara do.
    16. Na hom bɛhyew aman a EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn beyi hɔn ama hom no nyinara; mma hom enyi nnhu hɔn mbɔbɔr: nkyii so mma hom nnsom hɔn enyame; na ɔno bɛyɛ efir ama hom.
    17. Sɛ hom se wɔ hom akoma mu dɛ, Amanaman yi dɔɔ so sen hɛn; na yɛbɛyɛ dɛn na yeetum etu hɔn a, DEU.7.18, mma nnsuro hɔn: kaa dza EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dze yɛɛ Farao, nye Egypt nyinara no yie;
    18. nsɔhwɛ akɛse no a hom enyiwa hunii, nye nsɛnkyerɛdze, nye anwanwadze, nye nsa a ɔyɛ dzen, nye abasa a wɔatserɛw mu a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze yii hom no: dɛmara na EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn bɛyɛ aman a hom suro hɔn no nyinara.
    19. Nkyii so EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn bɔsoma nkotokrodo wɔ hɔn mu, ekodu dɛ hɔn a wɔaka, na woesuma hɔnho no, bɛhera wɔ hom enyim.
    20. Mma hɔn ho nnyɛ hom hu: na EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn wɔ hom finimfin, Nyame kɛse a no ho yɛ hu no a.
    21. Na EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn bɛpam amanaman no efi hom enyim nkakrankakra: mma hom nnhyew hɔn prɛko, bɛyɛ na haban mu mbowa adɔɔ so hom wo do.
    22. Na EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn beyi hɔn ama wɔ hom enyim, na ɔdze ɔkogu kɛse bɛka hɔn egu, ekodu dɛ wɔbɛsɛɛ.
    23. Na obeyi hɔn ahemfo ahyɛ hom nsa, na hom aama hɔn dzin ayera efi wiadze: nyimpa biara runntum nngyina hom enyim, ekodu dɛ hom asɛɛ hɔn.
    24. Hom mfa gya nhyew hɔn enyame ehonyi a wɔasen no: mma hom enyi mmber hɔn ho dwetɛ anaa sika, na so mma hom nnyiyi mmfa, bɛyɛ na woesua hom efir wɔ mu: na ɔyɛ ekyiwadze ma EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn:
    25. na mma hom mmfa ekyiwadze nnkɔ hom fie, na hom aannyɛ ebusudze dɛ ɔno: ma ɔnyɛ hom tantan koraa, na hom nkyir hɔn koraa: na ɔyɛ ebusudze.

    Tsir 8

    1. Mbransɛm a merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi nyinara hom ndwen do nyɛ, na hom aatsena ase, na wɔaadɔr, na wɔaakɔ akɛfa asaase a EWURADZE kaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ hom egyanom no.
    2. Na hom nkaa ɔkwan nyinara a EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn egya wo wɔ mu mfe eduanan yi wɔ sar no do no, dɛ ɔrebrɛ hom adze, asɔ hom ahwɛ, ehu dza ɔwɔ hom akoma mu, dɛ hom bedzi ne mbransɛm do, anaa oho.
    3. Na ɔbrɛɛ hom adze, na ɔmaa ɔkɔm dzee hom, na ɔdze manna a hom nnyim, na hom egyanom so nnyim maa hom dzii; na ɔaama hom ehu dɛ nnyɛ edziban nko bi na nyimpa nam do tsena ase, na asɛm biara a ofi EWURADZE anomu so nyimpa nam do tsena ase.
    4. Hom nkataho ennsuw wɔ hom ho, nkyii so w'anan annhohon mfe eduanan yi mu.
    5. Na dwen wɔ w'akoma mu dɛ, dɛ mbrɛ nyimpa tsea ne ba no, dɛmara so na EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn tsea wo.
    6. Na hom ndzi EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ne mbransɛm do, dɛ hom bɛnantsew n'akwan do, na hom esuro no.
    7. Na EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dze hom rokɔ asaase pa do, asaase a no do wɔ nworansu, nsunyiwa nye ebun a ofifi abɔn nye akoko mu pem no;
    8. asaase a ewifua nye atoko, nye wenyinndua nye adɔmbandua nye pɔmegranate ekutu wɔ do; asaase a olive na ewo wɔ do no;
    9. asaase a irinndzi edziban a wɔ do, adze biara so rinnhia wo wɔ do; asaase a n'abo yɛ dadze, na n'akokoe mu na hom botu boran.
    10. Na sɛ hom bedzidzi mee a, hom na nhyira EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn wɔ asaase pa a ɔdze ama hom no ntsi.
    11. Hom nhwɛ yie ɔno bɛyɛ na hom werɛ efir EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dɛ hom rinndzi ne mbransɛm, nye ne ntsɛmbu nye n'ahyɛdze a merehyɛ hom ndɛ da yi do:
    12. bɛyɛ so na hom edzidzi amee, na hom esisi adan fɛɛfɛw, atsenatsena mu;
    13. na sɛ hom anantwikuw nye hom nguankuw adɔr, na hom dwetɛ nye hom sika adɔɔ so, na adze a hom wɔ nyinara adɔɔ so a;
    14. nkyɛ hom akoma amema no ho do, na hom werɛ efir EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn a oyii hom fii Egypt asaase do, wɔ nkowaasom ofi mu no;
    15. nyia ogyaa hom faa sar kɛse huhuhuhu no do, mbrɛ mbowatsena a bɔr wɔ hɔn ano nye nyankoma nketsebowa wɔ, nye asaase kwawee mbrɛ nsu nnyi; nyia oyi nsu fii ɔtwerɛbo botan mu maa hom;
    16. nyia ɔdze manna a hom egyanom nnyim nyɛɛn hom wɔ sar no do; dɛ ɔdze rebrɛ hom adze, na ɔasɔ hom ahwɛ, na ɔayɛ hom yie wɔ hom ewiei ekyir no mu:
    17. na sɛ wɔ hom akoma mu dɛ, Hɛn tum nye hɔn nsa ne dzen na wɔama yenya ahonya yi.
    18. Na hom nkaa EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn, na ɔno na ɔmaa hom tum dɛ hom mfa nya homho; na ɔaama n'ahyɛmu a ɔkaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ hom egyanom no etsim, dɛ mbrɛ ɔtse ndɛ da yi.
    19. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, sɛ hom werɛ fir EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn, na hom kedzi enyame fofor ekyir, na esom hɔn, na hom sɔr hɔn a, midzi hom dase ndɛ da yi, dɛ ɔsɛɛ na hom bɛsɛɛ.
    20. Dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE sɛɛ amanaman fi hom enyim no, dɛmara na hom bɛsɛɛ; osiandɛ hom enntsie EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ndze no.

    Tsir 9

    1. Israel, tsie: rubotwa Jordan ndɛ da yi, akɔ akɛfa amanaman a wɔyɛ akɛse na wɔyɛ dzen sen ɔwoara, nkurow akɛse a wɔatotow ho afasu edu sor, DEU.9.2, ɔman a wɔyɛ akɛse na wɔwowaree, Anakfo mbabanyin a inyim hɔn, na atse dɛ wɔka no hɔn ho dɛ, Woana na obotum egyina Anak mbabanyin no enyim?
    2. Dɛm ntsi hu ndɛ da yi, dɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn nye nyia odzi w'enyim rukotwa dɛ ogya a ɔmemen; ɔbɛsɛɛ hɔn, na ɔabrɛ hɔn adze wɔ w'enyim ma eetu hɔn, na ama wɔaasɛɛ ntsɛm, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE aka akyerɛ wo no.
    3. EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enya etu hɔn efi w'enyim egu no, mma nnka wɔ w'akoma mu dɛ, Me ndzeyɛtsenenee ntsi na EWURADZE dze me aba dɛ mebɛfa asaase yi: naaso na amanaman yi hɔn ndzebɔnyɛ ntsi na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn rutu hɔn efi w'enyim.
    4. Nnyɛ wo ndzeyɛtsenenee, anaa w'akoma ne tsenenee ntsi na erokɔ akɛfa hɔn asaase: mbom amanaman yi hɔn ndzebɔnyɛ ntsi na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn rutu hɔn efi w'enyim, na ɔaama asɛm a ɔkaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ w'egyanom, Abraham, Isaak, nye Jakob no etsim.
    5. Dɛm ntsi hu dɛ nnyɛ wo ndzeyɛtsenenee bi ntsi na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze asaase pa yi rema wo dɛ fa; na ɔman akɔnkyefo nye wo.
    6. Kaa, mma wo werɛ mmfir, mbrɛ isii yii EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ebufuw wɔ sar no do; ofi da a ifii Egypt asaase do edze bodur dɛ hom baa bea yi do yi, hom atsew etua wɔ EWURADZE do.
    7. Na Horeb so hom yii EWURADZE ebufuw, na EWURADZE no bo fuuw hom dɛ nkyɛ ɔbɛsɛɛ hom.
    8. Aber a mofoow bepɔw no dɛ merekɛgye abopon no, mekyerɛ ahyɛmu a EWURADZE nye hom dzii n'apon no, nna metsenaa bepɔw no do adaduanan adzekyee nye adzesaa; menndzi edziban mannom nsu so.
    9. Na EWURADZE dze abopon ebien a wɔdze Nyankopɔn nsatseaba akyerɛw do no maa me; na no do na wɔakyerɛw nsɛm no nyinara a ofi gya mu wɔ bepɔw no do nye hom kasae wɔ nguabɔ da no mu no.
    10. Na ɔbaa dɛ adaduanan adzekyee nye adzesaa n'ewiei no, EWURADZE dze abopon ebien no, mekyerɛ ahyɛmu apon no, maa me.
    11. Na EWURADZE see me dɛ, Soɛr, fi ha sian ntsɛm; na wo man a ikeyii hɔn wɔ Egypt no asɛɛ hɔnho; wɔadan efi ɔkwan a mehyɛɛ hɔn no do ntsɛm; wɔayɛ ohonyi a woegu ama hɔnho.
    12. Nkyii so EWURADZE kaa kyerɛɛ me dɛ, Mehu ɔman yi, na, hwɛ, wɔyɛ ɔman asoɔdzenfo:
    13. gyaa me ma mensɛɛ hɔn, na mempepa hɔn dzin mfi ɔsor ase: na menyɛ wo ɔman a ɔyɛ dzen na ɔso sen hɔn.
    14. Nna medanee na mifii bepɔw no do sianee, na bepɔw no dzɛɛw gya: nna ahyɛmu apon ebien no kitsakitsa me nsamu.
    15. Na mohwɛe, na, hwɛ, hom ayɛ bɔn etsia EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn; hom ayɛ ɔnantwiba a woegu ama hom ho: hom adan efi ɔkwan a EWURADZE hyɛɛ hom no do afa nkyɛn ntsɛm.
    16. Na musuoo apon ebien no mu, na motowee fii me nsa ebien no mu gui, na mobobɔɔ no wɔ hom enyim.
    17. Na mohwee ase wɔ EWURADZE enyim, dɛ ahyɛse no, adaduanan adzekyee nye adzesaa; menndzi edziban, mannom nsu so; osian hom bɔn nyinara a hom yɛe no ntsi, dɛ wɔyɛɛ dza omuo wɔ EWURADZE enyi do, dze yii no ebufuw.
    18. Na musuroo ebufuw nye ebufuhyew a EWURADZE nyaa no hom ho dɛ ɔdze rebɛsɛɛ hom no. Na EWURADZE tsiee me aber no mu so.
    19. Na EWURADZE nyaa Aaron ho ebufuw dodoodow dɛ nkyɛ ɔbɛsɛɛ no; na mepaa kyɛw maa Aaron so aber no ara mu.
    20. Na mefaa hom bɔn a ɔyɛ ɔnantwiba no a hom yɛe no, ma medze gya hyeew no, na misiwii, nyaam no mokomoko kodur dɛ ɔyɛɛ fekɔfekɔ dɛ huntuma: na motoow no huntuma no guu ɔwora no a ofi bepɔw no do pem no mu.
    21. Na Tabera, nye Massa, nye Kibrot-hataava so hom yii EWURADZE ebufuw.
    22. Na aber a EWURADZE somaa hom fi Kades-barnea, dɛ, Hom mfow nkɛfa asaase a medze mama hom no, nna hom tseew etua wɔ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn mbrasɛm do, na hom anngye no enndzi, na woenntsie ne ndze so.
    23. Hom ayɛ EWURADZE do etuatsewfo ara fi da a muhun hom no.
    24. Dɛm ntsi na mohwee ase wɔ EWURADZE enyim adzekyee nye adzesaa adaduanan; osiandɛ EWURADZE ase dɛ ɔrebɛsɛɛ hom.
    25. Na mepaa EWURADZE kyɛw, see dɛ, Ao EWURADZE Nyankopɔn, mma nnsɛɛ wo man nye w'egyapadze a enam wo kɛseyɛ do apon hɔn a edze nsa a ɔyɛ dzen eyi hɔn wɔ Egypt no.
    26. Kaa wo nkowaa, Abraham, Isaak, nye Jakob; mma nnhwɛ ɔman yi hɔn asoɔdzen, nye hɔn ndzeyɛbɔn, nye hɔn bɔn so:
    27. ɔno bɛyɛ na asaase a iyii hɔn fii do no ase dɛ, Osiandɛ EWURADZE enntum ammfa hɔn annkɔ asaase a ɔbɔɔ hɔn ho anohoba no do, na osiandɛ ɔtan hɔn ntsi, na ɔdze hɔn efir edzi dɛ orukokuku hɔn wɔ sar no do.
    28. Naaso wɔyɛ wo man nye w'egyapadze a enam wo tum kɛse nye w'abasa a wɔatsɛw mu do eyi hɔn.

    Tsir 10

    1. Aber no mu na EWURADZE see me dɛ, sen abopon ebien a ɔtse dɛ kan dze no ma woho, na fow bra me nkyɛn wɔ bepɔw no do, na yɛ duadaka ma woho.
    2. Na mebɛkyerɛw nsɛm a ɔwɔ nkan no apon a ebobɔɔ no no do no, wɔ apon no do, na fa hyɛ adaka no mu.
    3. Nna meyɛɛ akeesia duadaka, na meseen abopon ebien a ɔtse dɛ kan dze no, na mofoow bepɔw no, na apon ebien no kitsa me nsamu.
    4. Na EWURADZE kyerɛɛw apon no do, dɛ mbrɛ kan no nkyerɛwee no tse, mbransɛm du no a ɔnoara fi gya no mu kaa kyerɛɛ hom wɔ bepɔw no do nguabɔ da no: na ɔdze maa me.
    5. Na medanee fii bepɔw no do sianee, na medze apon no bɛhyɛɛ adaka a meyɛe no mu; na hɔ na ɔwɔ dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ me no.
    6. Na Israelmba tui fii Beerot Bene-jaakan kɔr Mosera: hɔ na Aaron wui, na hɔ na wosiee no; na ne babanyin Eleazar hyɛɛ n'ananmu dzii ɔsɔfo.
    7. Wofi hɔ tui kɔr Gudgoda; na wofi Gudgoda kɔr Jotbata, asaase a nworansu wɔ do.
    8. Aber no mu na EWURADZE yii Levi ebusuakuw, dɛ wɔnsoa EWURADZE ahyɛmudaka no, wongyina EWURADZE enyim nsom no, na wonhyira wɔ ne dzin mu, a wɔayɛ araa dze ebodu ndɛ da yi.

    Tsir 11

    1. Dɛm ntsi dɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn, na sie n'ahwɛdodze, nye n'ahyɛdze, nye ne atsɛmbu, nye ne mbransɛm dabaa.

    3. nye dza ɔdze yɛɛ Egyptdɔm, nye hɔn mpɔnkɔ, nye hɔn ntseaseanam; mbrɛ ɔmaa Mbɛw Po nsu praam hɔn do aber a woritsiw hom, na mbrɛ EWURADZE asɛɛ hɔn ebodu ndɛ da yi;

    23. Nyimpa biara runntum nngyina hom enyim: EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn dze hom ho suro nye hom ho hu bogu asaase a hom betsia do nyinara do, dɛ mbrɛ ɔaka akyerɛ hom no.

    25. nhyira no, sɛ hom tsie EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn mbransɛm a merehyɛ hom ndɛ da yi a;
    26. na ndom no, sɛ hom enntsie EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn mbransɛm, na hom dan fi ɔkwan a merehyɛ hom ndɛ da yi do fa nkyɛn dɛ worikedzi enyame fofor a hom nnyim hɔn ekyir a.
    27. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, sɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze wo du asaase a erokɔ akɛfa no do a, ibesi nhyira no wɔ bepɔw Gerizim do, na ndom no wɔ bepɔw Ebal do.
    28. Ana wonnyi Jordan egya, witɔree kwan no ekyir, wɔ Keenanfo a wɔtse Araba do no asaase do, Gilgal tsentsendo, wɔ More nsudur ho bi a?

    30. Na hom ndwen do nyɛ ahyɛdze nye atsɛmbu a medze mirisi hom enyim ndɛ da yi nyinara.

    Tsir 12

    1. Iyinom nye ahyɛdze nye ntsɛmbu a hom ndwen do nyɛ wɔ asaase a EWURADZE, w'egyanom Nyankopɔn dze ama wo dɛ fa no do, nda a hom dze tse asaase do nyinara.

    11. ɔno ɔbɛba dɛ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛsan mu eyi ma ne dzin atsena hɔ no, hɔ na hom mfa dza merehyɛ hom no nyinara nkɔ; hom ɔhyew afɔr, nye hom okum afɔr, hom ebupɛn du du, nye hom nsamu ɔmado afɔr, nye hom soronko anohobadze a hom bɔ ma EWURADZE no nyinara:
    12. na hom mma hom enyi ngye wɔ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn enyim, hom, nye hom mbabanyin, nye hom mbabaa, nye hom nkowaa, nye hom mfonabaa, nye Levinyi a ɔwɔ hom kuropon mu, susuampa dɛ onnyi kyɛfa anaa egyapadze wɔ hom mu no.

    14. gyedɛ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE bɛsan mu eyi wɔ hom ebusuakuw kor mu, hɔ na hom mbɔ hom hyew afɔr, na hɔ na hom nyɛ dza merehyɛ hom nyinara.

    17. Mma nndzi w'eburow, anaa wo nsa, anaa wo ngo mu ebupɛn du du wɔ wo kuropon mu, anaa w'anantwikuw anaa wo nguankuw mu mbakan, nye w'anohobadze biara a ebɔ ho anohoba, nye w'ayamuyi afɔr, nye wo nsamu ɔmado afɔr:
    18. mbom dzi no wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyim wɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛsan mu eyi no, ɔwo, nye wo babanyin, nye wo babaa, nye w'akowaa, nye w'afonabaa, nye Levinyi a ɔwɔ wo kuropon mu: na ma w'enyi ngye wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyim wɔ dza wo nsa ka nyinara ho.

    20. Sɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn trɛw wo he mu, dɛ mbrɛ ɔabɔ wo anohoba no, na ese dɛ, mibedzi nam, osiandɛ wo kra no kɔn dɔ dɛ obedzi nam a, dzi nam, dɛ mbrɛ wo kra no kɔn dɔ nyinara.

    22. Mekyerɛ dɛ mbrɛ wodzi adoa nye ɔtrɔm no, dɛmara so na dzi bi: nyia no ho nntsew nye nyia no ho tsew nyina ndzi bi.
    23. Nsew ara na hyɛ mma enndzi bɔgya no: na mma mmfa nam no nnka bɔgya no ho nndzi.
    24. Mma nndzi; hue gu famu dɛ nsu.
    25. Mma nndzi; ma onsi ɔwo, nye wo mba a wodzi w'ekyir yie, aber a ereyɛ dza ɔtsen wɔ EWURADZE enyiwa do no.
    26. Wo ndzɛmba krɔnkrɔn a ewɔ no nko, nye w'anohobadze, na fa kɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE bɛsan mu eyi no:
    27. na bɔ wo hyew afɔr, nam nye bɔgya no, wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn afɔrmbukyia no do: na wo kum afɔr bɔgyaa no wonhue ngu EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn afɔrmbukyia no do, na nam no na dzi.
    28. Dwen do tsie nsɛm a merehyɛ wo yi nyinara, ma eesi ɔwo, nye wo mba a wodzi w'ekyir yie daa, aber a ereyɛ dza oye na ɔtsen wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyiwa do no.
    29. Sɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn twitwa amanaman fi w'enyim, mbrɛ erokɔ akɛfa hɔn no, na efa hɔn, na etsena hɔn asaase do a;
    30. hwɛ woho yie amma wɔasɛɛ hɔn efi w'enyim n'ekyir no, hɔn ekyirdzi aannyɛ wo efir; na eemmbisa hɔn enyame ekyir kwan, dɛ, Amanaman yi dua dɛn som hɔn enyame, na mo so menyɛ dɛmara?
    31. Mma nnyɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dɛm: na EWURADZE n'ekyiwadze a okyir no nyinara na wɔayɛ ama hɔn enyame; na hɔn mbabanyin nye hɔn mbabaa mpo na wɔdze gya hyew hɔn ma hɔn enyame.
    32. Adze biara a merehyɛ hom no, na hom ndwen do nyɛ: mma mmfa bi nnka ho, na mma nnyi do bi so.

    Tsir 13

    1. Sɛ nkɔnhyɛnyi bi, anaa ɔdaasonyi bi soɛr wɔ wo mu, na ɔma wo asɛnkyerɛdze anaa anwanwadze bi, DEU.13.2, na asɛnkyerɛdze anaa anwanwadze a ɔkasaa ho kyerɛɛ wo no ba mu, dɛ, Ma yɛnkɔ nkedzi enyame fofor a ɔwo nnyim hɔn no ekyir, na yɛnkɔsom no a;

    3. Hom nnantsew ndzi EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ekyir, na hom nsuro no, na hom ndzi ne mbransɛm do, na hom ntsie ne ndze, na hom nsom no; na hom ntakyer no ho.
    4. Na nkɔnhyɛnyi no, anaa ɔdaasonyi no, wonku no; osiandɛ wɔaka etuatsew ho asɛm etsia EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn a oyii hom fii Egypt asaase do, na ɔpoon wo fii nkowaasom ofi mu no, dɛ ɔdze rotwetwe wo efi ɔkwan a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn hyɛɛ wo dɛ nantsew mu no do esi nkyɛn. Ama eetu bɔn no efi wo mu.
    5. Sɛ wo nuabanyin, wo na ne babanyin, anaa wo babanyin, anaa wo babaa, anaa wo yer a ɔda wo bo mu, anaa wo nyɛnko a ɔtse dɛ wankasa wo kra, daadaa wo nsumaa mu, dɛ, Ma yɛnkɔ nkɔsom enyame fofor a ɔwo nye w'egyanom nnyim no;
    6. aman a woetwa hom ho ehyia a wɔbɛn wo, anaa wɔnye wo ntamu twe, ofi asaase ano kor ara kodu asaase ano kor so hɔn enyame mu bi a;
    7. mma mmpen no, na nntsie no so; nkyii so mma w'enyiwa nnhu no mbɔbɔr, na nkora no do, na nnhuntsa no do so;
    8. na oku na ku no; wo nsa na ondzi kan ntɔ no do dɛ woboku no, na n'ekyir no na ɔman no nyinara nsa.
    9. Na siw no abo ma onwu; osiandɛ ɔhwehwɛɛ dɛ ɔtwetwe wo fi EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn a oyii wo fii Egypt asaase do, wɔ nkowaasom ofi mu no ho.
    10. Na Israel nyinara ntse, na wonsuro, na wɔannda ho ara annyɛ dɛm ndzebɔn a ɔtse dɛ mbrɛ iyi tse yi wɔ wo mu.
    11. Na sɛ etse dɛ wɔreka wɔ wo nkurow a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ tsena mu hɔ no kor bi ho, dɛ, DEU.13.13, Ebusuyɛfo bi efi wo mu asoɛr, na wɔadaadaa hɔn kurow mu atsefo dɛ, Hom mma yɛnkɔ nkɔsom enyame fofor a hom nnyim hɔn a;
    12. ɔno hwehwɛ mu, na bisabisa mu yie; na hwɛ, sɛ ɔyɛ nokwar, na asɛm no yɛ ampa dɛ dɛm ekyiwadze yi wɔayɛ no wɔ wo mu a:
    13. oku na fa nkrantsee ano ku kurow no mu atsefo no, na fa nkrantsee ano sɛɛ no, nye dza ɔwɔ mu nyinara nye no mu fiembowa so pasapasa.
    14. Na boaboa no mu asadze nyinara ano wɔ no brɔn finimfin, na fa gya hyew kurow no, nye no mu asadze no nyinara dɛ ɔhyew afɔr mu, fa ma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn: na ɔnyɛ ɔmamfo daa; mma wɔnnkyekyer no bio.
    15. Na mma ebusudze no mu bi mmfam wo nsa ho: ama EWURADZE aadan efi n'ebufuhyew no ho, na oeehu wo mbɔbɔr, na oennya ayamuhyehye ama wo, na ɔaama adɔr dɛ mbrɛ ɔaka ntam akyerɛ w'egyanom;
    16. sɛ a itsie EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ndze, dɛ idzi ne mbransɛm a merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi nyinara do, na eyɛ dza ɔtsen wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyiwa do a.

    Tsir 14

    1. Hom yɛ EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn ne mba: mma hom mmbobɔ homho akam, na mma hom nnyɛ hom momapɔw do ahyɛnsewdze mma ewufo.

    3. Mma nndzi ekyiwadze biara.
    4. Iyinom nye mbowa a hom ndzi: ɔnantwi, ɔkɔsɔw, nye apɔnkye, DEU.14.5, ɔtrɔm, nye adoa, nye ɔwansan, nye iwi, nye ɔtwe, nye eko, nye ɔwɛr.
    5. Na abowa biara a ɔwɔ anansa a no mu apae, na n'anansa no mu apae ebien koraa, na ɔkosa adzepuwii, wɔ mbowa mu no, ɔno na hom ndzi.

    7. na preko, osiandɛ n'anansa mu apae dze na ɔnnkosa adzepuwii, no ho nntsew mma hom: hɔn nam no mma hom nndzi bi, na hɔn efun no mma hom nnka.
    8. Iyinom na hom ndzi wɔ dza ɔwɔ nsu mu nyinara mu: dza ɔwɔ tɛterɛ nye abon no hom ndzi:
    9. na biara a onnyi tɛterɛ nye abon no mma hom nndzi; no ho nntsew mma hom.
    10. Nnoma a hɔn ho tsew no nyinara hom ndzi.
    11. Na iyinom nye hɔn a mma hom nndzi bi: ɔkɔr, nye ɔkɔrbia, nye nsukɔr;
    12. nye ɔkom pɛtɛ, nye ɔkom, nye ɔsansaa n'ahorow;
    13. nye akonkoran biara n'ahorow;
    14. nye sohor, na anadwe krɔma, nye epo akokɔ, nye akrɔma n'ahorow;
    15. patu ketseaba, nye patu kɛse, nye ambɛn patu;
    16. na nantwinoma, nye ɔpɛtɛ, nye epo akonkoran;
    17. nye esukɔnkɔn, nye bakanoma n'ahorow, nye esuakwa nye apan.
    18. Na ntumbowa a wɔwea nyinara hɔn ho nntsew mma hom: mma wonndzi.
    19. Ntumbowa a hɔn ho tsew no nyinara hom ndzi.
    20. Mma hom nndzi biribi a noara oewu: fa ma ɔhɔho a ɔwɔ wo kuropon mu ma ondzi; anaa tɔn ma ɔnanaben: na ɔwo yɛ ɔman krɔnkrɔn ma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn. Mma nnoa apɔnkyeba wɔ ne na no numfonsu mu.
    21. W'aba a idua no ndɔbaa a ofi wo haban mu ba afrenhyia afrenhyia no, yiyi do ebupɛn du du.
    22. Na dzi wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyim, wɔ beebi mbrɛ ɔbɛsan mu eyi dɛ ne dzin ntsena hɔ no, w'eburow, nye wo nsa, nye wo ngo do ebupɛn du du, nye w'anantwikuw, nye wo nguankuw mu mbakan; ama eesua dɛ isuro EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dabaa.
    23. Na sɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn hyira wo na sɛ ɔkwan no war dodow ma wo ma irunntum nnsoa, osiandɛ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn asan mu eyi dɛ ɔdze ne dzin bɔto no nye wo ntamu twe dodow, DEU.14.25, ɔno dan no sika mu, na kyekyer sika no kitsa, na kɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn asan mu eyi no.
    24. Fa sika no tɔ adze biara a wo kra no kɔn dɔ, ɔnantwi, anaa oguan, anaa wenyin, anaa nsa dzendzen, anaa adze biara a wo kra pɛ no: na dzi no wɔ hɔ ara wo EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyim, na ma w'enyi ngye, ɔwo nye wo fifo:
    25. na Levinyi a ɔwɔ wo kuropon mu no, mma nngya no ekyir; osiandɛ onnyi kyɛfa nye egyapadze ka wo ho.
    26. Mfe ebiasa biara n'ewiei no, yiyi afe no ara mu ndɔbaa do ebupɛn du du no nyinara, na fa gu wo kuropon mu:
    27. na Levinyi, osiandɛ onnyi kyɛfa nye egyapadze biara dɛ mbrɛ ewɔ, na ɔhɔho, nye egyanka, nye okunafo a wɔwɔ wo kuropon mu, mbra, na wombedzidzi mmee; na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn eehyira wo wɔ wo nsaano edwuma a eyɛ nyinara mu.

    Tsir 15

    1. Mfe esuon biara n'ewiei no yɛ akwangya.
    2. Na mbrɛ wonsi ngya kwan nye yi: ɔdzefɛmfo biara dza ɔdze fɛm ne nyɛnko no ɔmfa nkyɛ no; mma ɔnnhyɛ ne nyɛnko nye no nua no nngye.

    4. Naaso mma ohianyi bi mmba wo mu; (na nhyira na EWURADZE behyira wo wɔ asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ egyapadze a kɛfa no do;)
    5. sɛ iritsie EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ndze ara yie, dɛ erodwen do ayɛ ahyɛdze yi a merehyɛ wo da yi nyinara a.
    6. Na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn behyira wo, dɛ mbrɛ ɔbɔɔ wo anohoba no: na edze afemfɛm amanaman pii, na ɔwo dze erennkɛfɛm: na ɔwo bɔhom amanaman pii do, na hɔn dze, wɔronnhom wo do.

    9. Hwɛ yie ma adwen huhuw ammba w'akoma mu dɛ, Afe a otsia esuon no, akwangya afe no abɛn; na annyɛ bɔn enntsia wo nua ohianyi no, dɛ eremma no hwee; ma ɔaanntsea mu ammfrɛ EWURADZE enntsia wo, na aannyɛ bɔn amma wo.
    10. Ɔma na ma no, na mma ɔnnyɛ w'akoma mu yaw dɛ ebɛma no: osiandɛ dɛm adze yi ntsi na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn behyira wo wɔ w'edwuma nyinara mu, nye dza edze wo nsa bosuo mu nyinara mu.
    11. Na ohianyi remmpa wɔ asaase no do: dɛm ntsi na merehyɛ wo dɛ, Bue na bue wo nsa ma wo nua, wo kɔhweaban, nye wo hianyi, wɔ w'asaase do.
    12. Sɛ wɔtɔn wo nua Hebriwnyi ɔbanyin, anaa ɔbaa, ma wo, na ɔsom wo mfe esia a; ɔno afe a otsia esuon no gya no kwan na omfi wo nkyɛn mfa noho ndzi nkɔ.
    13. Na sɛ irigya no kwan ma oeefi wo nkyɛn afa noho edzi akɔ a, mma ɔmmfa nsapan nnkɔ:
    14. fa wo nguankuw mu bi, nye w'ewuporoweebea do bi, nye wo wenyintwer mu bi ma no kwa: dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ehyira wo no na ma no.
    15. Na kaa dɛ eyɛɛ akowaa wɔ Egypt asaase do, na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn poon wo: dɛm ntsi na merehyɛ wo asɛm yi ndɛ.
    16. Na ɔmbra dɛ, sɛ ɔse wo dɛ, Mirimmfi wo nkyɛn nnkɔ; osiandɛ ɔdɔ wo nye wo fi ntsi, wɔ dɛ ɔtse no yie wɔ wo nkyɛn ntsi a;
    17. ɔno fa fitsii, na fa fitsi n'asowa mu suo abow mu, na ɔnyɛ w'akowaa daa. Na w'afonabaa no so yɛ no dɛmara.
    18. Mma ɔnnyɛ dzen mma wo, dɛ ibegya no kwan ma oeefi wo nkyɛn afa noho edzi akɔ; na ɔasom wo mfe esia ayɛ ɔpaanyi akatua mbɔho ebien ho adze: na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn behyira wo wɔ dza eyɛ nyinara mu.
    19. W'anantwikuw nye wo nguankuw mu mbakan enyin a wɔbɔwo hɔn no tu hɔn si nkyɛn ma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn: mma mmfa wo nantwi abakan nnyɛ edwuma, na nnsusua wo nguankuw mu abakan ho nhwi so.
    20. Dzi no wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyim afrenhyia afrenhyia wɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛsan mu eyi no, ɔwo nye wo fifo.
    21. Na sɛ ɔwɔ dzɛm bi wɔ no ho dɛ ɔyɛ apakye anaa n'enyi efura, dzɛm bɔn biara wɔ no ho a, mma mmfa mmbɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn afɔr.
    22. Dzi no wɔ wo kuropon mu: nyia no ho nntsew nye nyia no ho tsew nyina ndzi, dɛ mbrɛ wodzi ɔwansan nye ɔforote.
    23. No bɔgyaa nko na mma nndzi; hue gu famu dɛ nsu.

    Tsir 16

    1. Hyɛ bosoom Abib nsew, na dzi apaho no ma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn; na bosoom Abib mu na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn yii wo wɔ Egypt anadwe.
    2. Na bɔ apaho okum afɔr no ma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn, nguankuw nye anantwikuw mu, wɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE bɛsan mu eyi dɛ ne dzin ntsena hɔ no.
    3. Mma mmfa mbɔrkaw boodoo nnka ho nndzi; nda esuon na fa epitsi boodoo ka ho dzi, mekyerɛ amandzehu boodoo no; osiandɛ ɔhar mu na ifiir edzi fii Egypt asaase do: ama aakaa da a edze fiir edzi fii Egypt asaase do no wo nkwa nda nyinara.
    4. Na mma wonnhu wo nkyɛn mbɔrkaw wɔ w'ahe nyinara do nda esuon; nkyii so mma nam a iku hɔn da a odzi kan ewimbir no bi nnka anadwe no nyinara mma onnkodu anapa.
    5. Mma nnku apaho no wɔ wo kuropon a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo no biara mu:
    6. gyedɛ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛsan mu eyi dɛ ne dzin ntsena hɔ no, hɔ na ku apaho no ewimbir, wi rokɔtɔ no, wɔ wo Egypt fi aber no mu.
    7. Na toto na dzi no wɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛsan mu eyi no: na anapa no san, na kɔ w'asesee mu.
    8. Nda esia na dzi epitsi boodoo: na da a otsia esuon no nyɛ asomdwee nguabɔ mma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn; mma nnyɛ edwuma wɔ mu.
    9. Adapɛn esuon na kan ma woho: da a erehyɛ ase dze kantankrankye aka eburow a osisi hɔ no na fi kan adapɛn esuon.
    10. Na hyɛ adapɛn afahyɛ no ma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn fa ka wo nsa mu ayamuyi afɔr ntoboa a edze rema no no ho, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ehyira wo no:
    11. na ma w'enyi ngye wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyim, ɔwo, nye wo babanyin, nye wo babaa, nye w'akowaa, nye w'afonabaa, nye Levinyi a ɔwɔ wo kuropon mu, nye ɔhɔho, nye egyanka, nye okunafo a wɔwɔ wo mu, wɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛsan mu eyi dɛ ne dzin ntsena hɔ no.
    12. Na kaa dɛ eyɛɛ akowaa wɔ Egypt: na dwen do yɛ ahyɛdze yi.
    13. Hyɛ ntamadan afahyɛ nda esuon, aber a aboaboa w'ewuporoweebea do nye wo wenyintwer mu adze ano no:
    14. na ma w'enyi ngye wɔ w'afahyɛ no mu, ɔwo, nye wo babanyin, nye wo babaa, nye w'akowaa, nye w'afonabaa, nye Levinyi, nye ɔhɔho, nye egyanka, nye okunafo a wɔwɔ wo kuropon mu.
    15. Nda esuon na hyɛ fa ma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn wɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE bɛsan mu eyi no: osiandɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn behyira wo wɔ wo ndɔbaa nyinara mu, na wo nsaano edwuma nyinara mu, na w'enyi bɛgye ara.
    16. Afrenhyia kor mu mpɛn ebiasa na wo mbanyin nyinara nkefir EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyim wɔ beebi mbrɛ ɔbɛsan mu eyi no; wɔ epitsi boodoo afahyɛ no mu, nye adapɛn afahyɛ no mu, nye ntamadan afahyɛ no mu: na mma wɔmmfa nsapan mmbefir EWURADZE enyim:
    17. nyimpa biara mma dza obotum, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn nhyira a ɔdze ehyira wo no tse.
    18. Hyɛ ntsɛmbuafo nye mpanyimfo wɔ wo nkuropon a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ mbrɛ w'ebusuakuw tsetsee nyinara mu: na wɔmfa ntsɛn tsenenee mbu ɔman no ntsɛn.
    19. Mma nndan atsɛmbu enyi; mma mmfɛr nyimpa: nkyii so mma nngye akyɛdze; na akyɛdze fura enyansafo enyi, na ɔdan atseneneefo nsɛm enyi.
    20. Dza ɔtsen ara na dzi ekyir, ama eenya nkwa, na eenya asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo no edzi.
    21. Mma nntsɛw dua biara mmfa nnyɛ abosompɔw Asera wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn afɔrmbukyia a ebɛyɛ ama woho no nkyɛn.
    22. Nkyii so mma nnsi dum a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn kyir mma woho.

    Tsir 17

    1. Mma mmfa ɔnantwi, anaa oguan a no ho wɔ dzɛm, anaa adzebɔn biara, mmbɔ okum afɔr mma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn na ɔyɛ ekyiwadze ma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn.
    2. Sɛ wohu wɔ wo mu, wɔ wo nkuropon a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo no biara mu, ɔbanyin anaa ɔbaa bi a ɔyɛ dza omuo wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyi do dɛ ɔto n'ahyɛmu, DEU.17.3, na ɔkɔ kɔsom enyame fofor bi, ɔsɔr dɛ, ewia, bosoom, anaa ɔsor dɔm no mu biara a mennhyɛɛ ho mbra no, DEU.17.4, na wɔka kyerɛ wo, ma etse a, ɔno hwehwɛ mu yie na hwɛ, sɛ ɔyɛ nokwar, na asɛm no wɔ mu ampa, dɛ ɔayɛ dɛm ekyiwadze yi wɔ Israel a;
    3. ɔno fa ɔbanyin anaa ɔbaa no a ɔayɛ dɛm adze bɔn yi fir edzi kɔ wo kuropon ho, na wonsiw no abo ma onwu.
    4. Adasefo beenu, anaa adasefo baasa anomu do na wɔmfa nku nyia ɔsɛ owu no; mma wɔmmfa ɔdasefo kor anomu do nnku no.
    5. Adasefo no nsa na ondzi kan wɔ no do nku no, na n'ekyir no ɔman no nyinara hɔn nsa. Ama eeyi mbusu no efi wo mu.
    6. Sɛ asɛm bi ho atsɛmbu yɛ dzen dodow ma wo, wɔ ewudzi nye nsa a apa ntamu, mansosɛm mu, na opira no, dɛ ɔyɛ akasakasa bi wɔ wo kuropon mu a, ɔno soɛr, na kɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛsan mu eyi no;
    7. na kɔ asɔfo Levifo, nye nda no mu ɔtsɛmbufo a ɔwɔ do no nkyɛn na kebisa; na wɔnkyerɛ wo asɛm no n'atsɛmbu:
    8. na yɛ dɛ mbrɛ asɛm a wɔbɛkyerɛ wo wɔ beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE bɛsan mu eyi no tse no ara; na dwen do yɛ dɛ mbrɛ wɔbɛkyerɛkyerɛ wo no nyinara:
    9. dɛ mbrɛ mbra a wɔbɛkyerɛkyerɛ wo, na mbrɛ atsɛmbu a wɔbɛka akyerɛ wo no tse no ara, na yɛ: mma nndan mmfi asɛm a wɔbɛkyerɛ wo no ho nnkɔ enyimfa anaa abankum.
    10. Na nyimpa a obedzi nkontompɔsɛm, dɛ orinntsie ɔsɔfo a ogyina hɔ som wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn enyim, anaa ɔtsɛmbufo no, dɛm nyimpa no onwu: na yi mbusu no fi Israel.
    11. Na ɔman no nyinara ntse, na wonsuro, na wonndzi nkontompɔsɛm bio.
    12. Sɛ idu asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo no do, na efa, na etsena do, na ese dɛ, Mibesi mo do hen, dɛ amanaman a wotwa mo ho hyia nyinara no a;
    13. nyia EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛsan mu eyi no no noara na fa no si wo do hen: wo nuanom mu bi na fa no si wo do hen: mma mmfa ɔnanaben bi a ɔnnyɛ wo nua nnsi wo do.
    14. Mma ɔhen no mma no mpɔnkɔ nndɔɔ so mma no, nkyii so mma ɔmma ɔman no nnsan nnkɔ Egypt, nnkɛpɛ no mpɔnkɔ dodow: mber dodowara a EWURADZE ase hom dɛ ɔdzekɔr yi mma hom nnsan mmfa ɔkwan no do bio no.
    15. Nkyii so mma ɔmma ne yernom nndɔɔ so.
    16. Na ɔmbra dɛ, sɛ ɔtse n'ahendzi ahengua do a, onsusu mbra yi a ɔwɔ asɔfo Levifo no enyim no do nkyerɛw bi wɔ nwoma mu mma noho:
    17. na ɔnko ne nkyɛn, na ɔnkenkan mu no nkwa nda nyinara: na oeesua dɛ obosuro EWURADZE no Nyankopɔn, na odzi mbra yi mu nsɛm nyinara nye ahyɛdze yi do, na ɔyɛ:
    18. na n'akoma aamma noho do annsen no nuanom, na ɔaanndan emmfi mbrasɛm no ho ammfa enyimfa, anaa abankum: ama ne nda aawar wɔ n'ahendzi mu, ɔno nye ne mba wɔ Israel.

    Tsir 18

    1. Levifo Asɔfo nye Levi ebusuakuw nyinara rinnya kyɛfa nye egyapadze wɔ Israel mu: EWURADZE ogya afɔr nye n'egyapadze na wondzi.
    2. Na worinnya egyapadze wɔ hɔn nuanom mu: EWURADZE nye hɔn egyapadze, dɛ mbrɛ ɔaka akyerɛ hɔn no.
    3. Na iyi nyɛ asɔfo no hɔn ndzinoa mfi ɔman no hɔ, mfi hɔn a wɔbɔ afɔr no hɔ, ɔnantwi o, oguan o, wɔmfa abasa, nye adadaw ebien no, nye efur no mma ɔsɔfo no.
    4. W'eburow, wo nsa, nye wo ngo mu nduadzewakan, nye wo nguan ho nhwi a woesusua no kan no, fa ma no.
    5. Osiandɛ ɔno na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn asan mu eyi no wɔ w'ebusuakuw nyinara mu, dɛ ongyina nsom wɔ EWURADZE ne dzin mu, ɔno na ne mbabanyin daa nyina.
    6. Na sɛ Levinyi bi fi wo nkuropon no biara mu, fi Israel mu beebiara mbrɛ ɔakɔsoɛ, na ɔdze ne kra ne pɛ nyinara ba beebi mbrɛ EWURADZE asan mu eyi no a;
    7. ɔno ɔnsom wɔ EWURADZE ne Nyankopɔn ne dzin mu, dɛ mbrɛ no nuanom Levifo no a wogyina EWURADZE enyim hɔ no nyinara yɛ no.
    8. Wonnya kyɛfa dɛ hɔndze no ndzi, mfa nka dza ofi n'egyanom adzetɔn mu ba no ho.
    9. Sɛ inya du asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo no do a, mma nnsua amanaman no hɔn ndzeyɛɛ a ɔyɛ ekyiwadze no yɛ.
    10. Mma wonnhu wo mu obi a ɔma ne babanyin anaa ne babaa fa ogya mu, nyia ɔhyɛ mbusunkɔm, nyia ɔyɛ abayidze, anaa adzebisafo, anaa osumantunyi, DEU.18.11, anaa kaberkyernyi, anaa nyia ɔfrɛfrɛ esunsum, anaa anyɛn, anaa ɔsamamfrɛnyi.
    11. Osiandɛ obiara a ɔyɛ iyinom no yɛ ekyiwadze ma EWURADZE: na osian ekyiwadze yi ntsi na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn tutuu hɔn fii w'enyim yi.
    12. Enye EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ntsena no emudzi mu.
    13. Na amanaman yi a erekɛfa yi wotsie hɔn a wɔyɛ abayidze, na mbusunkɔnhyɛfo: na ɔwo dze, EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn mmaa wo kwan dɛ yɛ dɛm.
    14. Nkɔnhyɛnyi a ofi wo mu, ofi wo nuanom mu a ɔtse dɛ emi, na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛma no do ama wo; ɔno na hom ntsie no;
    15. dɛ mbrɛ dza ibisae wɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn hɔ wɔ Horeb, wɔ nguabɔ da no tse nyinara, dɛ, Mma menntse EWURADZE me Nyankopɔn ndze bio, nkyii so mma munnhu ogya kɛse yi bio, na meennwu.
    16. Na EWURADZE see me dɛ, Dza wɔaka no, wɔaka no yie.
    17. Mebɛma nkɔnhyɛnyi do ama hɔn wɔ hɔn nuanom mu tse dɛ ɔwo; na medze me nsɛm bɛhyɛ n'anomu, na ɔaka dza mebɛhyɛ no nyinara akyerɛ hɔn.
    18. Na ɔbɛba dɛ obiara a onnketsie me nsɛm a ɔbɛka wɔ me dzin mu no, mibebisa noara.
    19. Na nkɔnhyɛnyi a ɔbɛka asɛm bi nkontompɔ mu wɔ me dzin mu a, mennhyɛɛ no dɛ ɔnka no, anaa nyia ɔbɛkasa wɔ enyame fofor bi dzin mu no, nkɔnhyɛnyi kor no bowu.
    20. Bi a nna ase wɔ w'akoma mu dɛ, Yɛbɛyɛ dɛn bohu asɛm a EWURADZE nnkae no?
    21. Ɔno nye dɛ, sɛ nkɔnhyɛnyi bi kasa wɔ EWURADZE ne dzin mu, na sɛ asɛm no annyɛ hɔ anaa ammba mu so a, nna asɛm a EWURADZE nnkae no nye no: nkɔnhyɛnyi no aka no ator mu, mma nnsuro no.

    Tsir 19

    1. Sɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn twitwa amanaman a ɔdze hɔn asaase rema wo gu, na itu hɔn, na etsena hɔn nkurow, nye hɔn adan mu a;
    2. yiyi nkurow ebiasa si hɔ ma woho wɔ asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ fa no mu.
    3. Siesie ɔkwan no ma woho, na paapaa asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn rema enya edzi no n'ahe mu ebiasa na onyimpakumfo biara eeguan akɔ hɔ.
    4. Na onyimpakumfo a onguan nkɔ hɔ na onnya nkwa no n'asɛm nye yi: nyia oku ne nyɛnko awerɛfir mu, na ɔnntan no dada bi;
    5. dɛ nyimpa nye ne nyɛnko kɔ kwaa mu rukotwa dua, na ne nsa ma abɔndua do dze eetwa dua no, na ne tsidadze no hɔn fi no kura mu, na ɔka ne nyɛnko no, ma owu a; onguan nkɔ nkurow yi kor mu na onnya nkwa:
    6. amma bɔgya ho ɔwerdzinyi no eenntsiw nyimpakumfo no, aber a n'akoma ayɛ hyew no, na osiandɛ ɔkwan no war ntsi ɔaannkɔto no, na ɔaammbɔ no ɔbɔtɔr; naaso na nyimpakumfo no nnsɛ wu, susuampa dɛ ɔnntan no dada bi no.
    7. Dɛm ntsi na merehyɛ wo dɛ yiyi nkurow ebiasa si hɔ ma woho.
    8. Na sɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn tserɛw wo he mu, dɛ mbrɛ ɔaka ntam akyerɛ w'egyanom, na ɔdze asaase no a ɔbɔɔ anohoba dɛ ɔdze bɛma w'egyanom no nyinara ma wo:
    9. sɛ idzi mbrasɛm yi nyinara a merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi do eyɛ, dɛ ebɔdɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn, na anantsew n'akwan do daa a; ɔno yiyi nkurow ebiasa so ka ebiasa yi ho si hɔ ma woho:
    10. na wɔaannka bɔgya a onndzi so enngu wɔ w'asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ egyapadze no mu, na bɔgya eengu wo do.
    11. Na sɛ nyimpa bi tan ne nyɛnko, na ɔkɔ no atsɛwee, na ɔsoɛr no do, na ɔbɔ no ɔbɔtɔr ma owu; na oguan kɔ nkurow yi kor mu a:
    12. ɔno no kurow no mu mpanyimfo nsoma ma wɔnkɛfa no wɔ hɔ na wɔmfa no nhyɛ bɔgya ho ɔwerdzinyi no nsa, ma onwu.
    13. Mma w'enyiwa nnhu no mbɔbɔr, na yi bɔgya a onndzi so fi Israel, na eesi wo yie.
    14. Mma nntwe wo nyɛnko n'asaasehe a tsetsefo no dze esi, wɔ w'egyapadze a ibenya edzi wɔ asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ fa no do.
    15. Ɔdasefo baako nnsoɛr nntsia nyimpa bi wɔ emumuyɛ biara, anaa bɔn biara ho, wɔ bɔn biara a ɔyɛ ho: adasefo beenu anomu, anaa adasefo baasa anomu na wɔmfa do nsi asɛm gyinae.
    16. Sɛ ɔdasekrumfo bi soɛr tsia nyimpa bi dzi no mfom ho dase a;
    17. ɔno nyimpa beenu nyina a ɔham no da hɔn ntamu no, mbegyina EWURADZE enyim, wɔ asɔfo nye ntsɛmbufo a wɔwɔ hɔ nda no mu no enyim;
    18. na ntsɛmbufo no nhwehwɛ mu yie: na, hwɛ, sɛ ɔdasefo no yɛ ɔdasekrumfo, na oedzi no nua dasekrum a;
    19. ɔno hom nyɛ no dɛ mbrɛ ɔdween dɛ nkyɛ ɔbɛyɛ no nua no: ama eeyi mbusu no efi wo mu.
    20. Na hɔn a wɔaka no ntsi, na wonsuro na ɔdzekɔr yi wɔaammbɔ mbusu biara a ɔtse dɛm wɔ wo mu bio.
    21. Na mma w'enyiwa nnhu mbɔbɔr; nkwa nhyɛ nkwa ananmu, enyiwa nhyɛ enyiwa ananmu, ese nhyɛ ese ananmu, nsa nhyɛ nsa ananmu, anan nhyɛ anan ananmu.

    Tsir 20

    1. Sɛ itu ɔsa nye w'atamfo aakɔko, na ihu mpɔnkɔ, nye ntseaseanam, nye ɔman a wɔdɔɔ so sen wo a, mma nnsuro hɔn: na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn a ɔdze wo fi Egypt asaase do bae no ka wo ho.
    2. Na ɔmbra dɛ sɛ hom rehɛn akono a, ɔsɔfo no ntwe mpin na ɔmbɛkasa nkyerɛ ɔman no, DEU.20.3, na ɔnse hɔn dɛ, Israel, hom ntsie, hom rebɛn akono ndɛ da yi nye hom atamfo akɔko: mma hom akoma nntu, mma hom nnsuro, na hom ho mmpopo, nkyii so mma hɔn ho nnyɛ hom hu;
    3. na EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn nye nyia ɔnye hom rokɔ, nye hom atamfo rokɔko ama hom, na ɔaagye hom nkwa.
    4. Na ahwɛdofo no nkasa nkyerɛ ɔman no, dɛ, Nyimpa mmuana na oesi dan fofor, na ommbuei? Wɔmma ɔnsan nkɔ ne fie, ma ɔaanntɔ wɔ ɔko mu, na nyimpa fofor eennkobue.
    5. Na nyimpa mmuana na ɔatsew wenyin ture, na onnyae nnyii n'aba bi? Wɔmma ɔnsan nkɔ ne fie, na ɔaanntɔ wɔ ɔko mu, na nyimpa fofor eennkeyi n'aba no.
    6. Na nyimpa biara a ɔagye basia bi dɛ yer, na onnyae nnhyiaa no no, wɔmma ɔnsan nkɔ ne fie, ma ɔaanntɔ wɔ ɔko mu, na nyimpa fofor bi annkɛfa no dɛ ɔyer.
    7. Na ahwɛdofo no nwɔw do nkasa nkyerɛ ɔman no, na wɔnse dɛ, Nyimpa mmuana na osuro na n'akoma apa? Wɔmma ɔnsan nkɔ ne fie, amma no nuanom hɔn akoma eenntu dɛ n'akoma.
    8. Na ɔmbra dɛ, sɛ ahwɛdofo no wie ɔman no kasa kyerɛ a, wɔnhyehyɛ asafohemfo wɔ ɔman no ne tsir.
    9. Sɛ ebɛn kurow bi dɛ enye no rokɔko a, ɔno bɔ asomdwee ho dawur kyerɛ no.
    10. Na ɔmbra dɛ, sɛ ɔma wo asomdwee mbuae, na obue wo a, ɔno, ma nkorɔfo a ibohu hɔn wɔ mu no nyinara mbɛyɛ ɔtowyiman mma wo, na wɔnsom wo.
    11. Na sɛ ɔnnye wo ennsiesie, na otu wo do sa a, ɔno kɔ no ntoa:
    12. na sɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn yi no hyɛ wo nsa a, ɔno fa nkrantsee ano bobɔ no mu mbanyin nyinara:
    13. na mbaa nye mbofraba, nye ofiembowa, nye dza ɔwɔ kurow no mu nyinara dze, mekyerɛ no mu asadze nyinara, fa no afonam fa ma woho; na dzi w'atamfo asadze a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze ama wo no.
    14. Dɛm na yɛ nkurow a wɔnye wo ntamu twe dodow nyinara a wɔnnyɛ amanaman yi nkurow bi no.
    15. Na aman yi nkurow a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ egyapadze no dze, mma nngye biribiara a ogu ahom no nkwa:
    16. mbom sɛɛ hɔn pasapasa; Hetfo, nye Amorifo, Keenanfo, nye Perizfo, nye Hivifo, nye Jebusfo; dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ahyɛ wo no:
    17. amma wɔaannkyerɛ wo mma aannyɛ hɔn ekyiwadze nyinara a wɔyɛ ma hɔn enyame no bi; na hom aannyɛ bɔn enntsia EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn.
    18. Sɛ itua kurow bi ma ɔkyɛr, dɛ ereyɛ ho sa afa a, mma mmfa abɔndua nnsɛɛ no ho ndua; na no do aba bi na ibedzi, na mma nntwitwa nngu; ana haban mu dua yɛ nyimpa, na asɛɛ no?
    19. Ndua a inyim dɛ ɔnnyɛ ndua a wodzi no nko, na sɛɛ no na twitwa gu; na sisi nduaban wɔ kurow a ɔnye wo dzi ako no ho araa ma onkodu dɛ ɔbɔhwe ase.

    Tsir 21

    1. Sɛ wohu obi a woeku no da haban mu, wɔ asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ fa no do, na wonnyim nyia okum no a:
    2. ɔno wo mpanyimfo nye wo atsɛmbufo mfir edzi, na wonkosusu nkodu nkurow a etwa ehyia nyia woeku no no ho no do:
    3. na ɔmbra dɛ, kurow a ɔbɛn nyimpa a woeku no no nkyɛn no, mekyerɛ kurow no mu mpanyimfo no mfa anantwikuw mu ɔnantwiber a wɔmmfaa no nnyɛɛ edwuma, na ɔnntwee kɔndua pɛn;
    4. ɔnantwiber no nsian nkɔ ɔbɔn a nsu pem wɔ do a wɔnndɔwee na wonnduaa mu adze mu, na wombu ɔnantwiber no no kɔn wɔ ɔbɔn no mu:
    5. na Levi mbabanyin asɔfo no mbra ho; na hɔn na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn asan mu eyi hɔn dɛ wɔnsom no, na wonhyira wɔ EWURADZE ne dzin mu; na hɔn anomu asɛm do na wɔmfa ndzi ɔham biara nye ɔbɔ biara ho asɛm:
    6. na kurow no mu mpanyimfo a wɔbɛn nyimpa a woku no no, nhohor hɔn nsa ho ngu ɔnantwiber a wobuu no kɔn mu wɔ ɔbɔn no mu no do:
    7. na wombua nse dɛ, Nnyɛ hɛn nsa bi kaa bɔgya gui, nkyii so hɛn enyiwa ennhu.
    8. EWURADZE, fa fir wo man Israel a apon hɔn no, na mma bɔgya a onndzi so nnka wo man Israel mu. Na bɔgya no wɔdze bɛkyɛ hɔn.
    9. Ama eeyi bɔgya a onndzi so no efi wo mu, aber a ereyɛ dza ɔtsen wɔ EWURADZE enyiwa do no.
    10. Sɛ itu sa nye w'atamfo kɔko, na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn yi hɔn hyɛ wo nsa, na efa hɔn ndɔmmum, DEU.21.11, na ihu ndɔmmum no mu ɔbaa fɛɛfɛw bi, na inya no ho akɔndɔ, na epɛ dɛ efa no yɛ wo yer a;
    11. ɔno fa no bra fie; na onyi ne tsir na ombubu n'awerɛw;
    12. na onyi no ndɔmmum akataho no mfi noho, na ɔnka wo fie, na ongyam n'egya nye ne na bosoom ara mu: na n'ekyir no akɔ ne nkyɛn, na ayɛ no kun, na ɔayɛ wo yer.
    13. Na ɔmbra dɛ, sɛ emmpɛ no a, ɔno ma ɔnkɔ beebi mbrɛ ɔpɛ; na ɔtɔn dze, mma nntɔn no nngye sika, mma nnye no nndzi dɛ afonabaa, osiandɛ abrɛ no adze.
    14. Sɛ ɔbanyin bi wɔ yernom beenu na, ɔdɔ kor, na ɔtan kor, na hɔn beenu no nyina awowo mba ama no; na sɛ abakan no nye nyia ɔtan no ne babanyin a:
    15. ɔno ɔmbra dɛ, da a ɔkyɛ n'adze a ɔwɔ mu ma ne mbabanyin no mma ɔmmfa nyia ɔdɔ no no ne babanyin nnyɛ abakan nndzi nyia ɔtan no ne babanyin a ɔyɛ abakan no enyim:
    16. mbom ɔngye abakan no nto mu, nyia ɔtan no no ne babanyin no, ɔmma no dza ɔwɔ nyinara mu kyɛfa mbɔho ebien: na ɔno nye n'ahoɔdzen ahyɛse; abakan ndzinoa yɛ nedze.
    17. Sɛ nyimpa wɔ babanyin ɔsoɔdzennyi nye etuatsewnyi a onntsie n'egya ne ndze, anaa ne na ne ndze, na wɔtsea no so, na onntsie hɔn a:
    18. ɔno n'egya nye ne na nkyer no, na wɔmfa no mfir edzi nkɔ no kurow mu mpanyimfo nkyɛn wɔ no kuropon ano;
    19. na wɔnse no kurow no mu mpanyimfo no dɛ, Hɛn babanyin yi yɛ ɔsoɔdzennyi nye otuatsewnyi, onntsie hɛn ndze. Ɔyɛ ɔkɔhwinyi, nye ɔsabokwanyi.
    20. Na no kurow no mu mbanyin nyinara nsiw no abo, ma ɔntɔ: ama eeyi mbusu no efi wo mu; na Israel nyinara ntse, na wonsuro.
    21. Na sɛ nyimpa bi yɛ bɔn a ɔsɛ owu, na woku no, na edze no sɛn dua bi do a;
    22. mma ne nyimpadua no nnka dua no do anadwe no nyinara, na osie na sie no da no ara; na nyia wɔasɛn no no Nyame apa no; amma eenngu w'asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze rema wo dɛ egyapadze no ho fi.

    Tsir 22

    1. Mma nnhu wo nua ne nantwi anaa no guan dɛ ɔatɔ gon, na mmfi hɔn ho nnkosuma: fa hɔn san brɛ wo nua.
    2. Na sɛ wo nua no mmbɛn wo, anaa sɛ innyim no a, ɔno fa no bra wo fie wo, na ɔnko wo nkyɛn nkodu dɛ wo nua no bɔhwehwɛ no, na aadan no ama no bio.
    3. Na dɛmara so na fa yɛ n'asoaso; anaa wo nua n'adze biara a ayew no a ehu no: mma nnguan mmfi ho.
    4. Mma nnhu wo nua n'asoaso anaa ne nantwi dɛ ɔahwe ase wɔ ɔkwan mu, na mmfi ho nnkosuma: boa no ma ɔmma hɔn do bio.
    5. Mma ɔbaa nnhyɛ adze a ɔyɛ ɔbanyin dze, nkyii so ɔbanyin nnhyɛ ɔbaa n'atar: na obiara a ɔyɛ iyinom no ɔyɛ ekyiwadze ma EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn.
    6. Sɛ ikopue anoma ne birebuw bi do awerɛfir mu wɔ ɔkwan mu, wɔ dua biara do anaa famu, mba anaa nkyirefuwa wɔ mu, na na no butuw na mba no, anaa nkyirefuwa no do a, mma nnkyer na no nnka mba no ho;
    7. ɔno ma na no nkɔ, na fa mba no; na eesi wo yie, na ama wo nda aawar.
    8. Sɛ isi dan fofor a, ɔno gye wo dan etsifi ban, ama sɛ obi fi do hwe ase a, aammfa bɔgya ammba wo dan no do.
    9. Mma mmfa aba horow ebien nndua wo wenyin ture mu: amma aannhwer nduadzewa no nyina, aba a edua no, nye wenyin ture no ndɔbaa no nyina.
    10. Mma mmfa ɔnantwi nye asoaso mmbɔ mu mmfuntum.
    11. Mma mmfura tam a wɔdze kuntu nye ndwera afora awen.
    12. Tu wo tam a edze kata wo do no ntoado anan no do ndwow.
    13. Sɛ ɔbanyin bi hyia yer, na ɔkɔ ne nkyɛn, na ɔtan no, DEU.22.14, na ɔdze enyimguasesɛm to no do, na ɔbɔ no dzin bɔn, se dɛ, Mihyiaa ɔbaa yi, na mokɔr ne nkyɛn no, mennhu no ho mbaabunyɛ nsɛnkyerɛdze no a:
    14. ɔno akataasia no n'egya nye ne na mfa akataasia no ne mbaabunyɛ nsɛnkyerɛdze no na wɔmfa mbra kurow no mu mpanyimfo nkyɛn wɔ kuropon no mu;
    15. na akataasia no n'egya nse mpanyimfo no dɛ, Medze me babaa maa ɔbanyin yi awar, na ɔtan no;
    16. na, hwɛ, ɔdze enyimguasesɛm ato no do, dɛ, Mennhu mbaabunyɛ nsɛnkyerɛdze wɔ wo babaa yi ho; naaso me babaa ne mbaabunyɛ nsɛnkyerɛdze nye yi. Na wɔntserɛw tam no mu wɔ kurow no mu mpanyimfo no enyim.
    17. Na kurow no mu mpanyimfo no mfa ɔbanyin no na wɔntwe n'aso;
    18. na wɔngye no mbratodze dwetɛ ɔha, na wɔmfa mma akataasia no n'egya, osiandɛ ɔabɔ Israel ɔbaabun dzin bɔn: na nwar no; mma ɔnnko no wɔ ne nda nyinara mu.
    19. Na sɛ asɛm yi yɛ nokwar, dɛ woennhu mbaabunyɛ nsɛnkyerɛdze wɔ akataasia no ho dze a:
    20. ɔno wonyi akataasia no mbra n'egya ne fi abowano, na no kurow no mu mbanyin nsiw no abo ma onwu: osiandɛ ɔayɛ nkwaseadze wɔ Israel, dɛ ɔagor ndwamangor wɔ n'egya fie: ama eeyi mbusu no efi wo mu.
    21. Sɛ wohu ɔbanyin bi dɛ ɔnye ɔbaa a ɔwɔ kun da a, ɔno hɔn beenu nyina nwu, ɔbanyin a ɔnye ɔbaa no dae no, nye ɔbaa no: ama eeyi mbusu no efi Israel.
    22. Sɛ wɔdze akataasia bi a ɔyɛ ɔbaabun ma banyin bi awar, na banyin bi hu no wɔ kurow mu, na ɔnye no da a;
    23. ɔno hom mfa hɔn emu ebien nyina mfir edzi nkɔ kurow no n'abotsen ekyir, na hom nsiw hɔn abo ma wonwuwu; akataasia no, dɛ ɔwɔ kurow mu na ɔannkeka mu ntsi; na ɔbanyin no, dɛ ɔabrɛ ne nyɛnko ne yer adze ntsi: ama eeyi mbusu no efi wo mu.
    24. Na sɛ banyin bi hu akataasia ɔwarfo bi yer no wɔ haban mu, na ɔbanyin no kyer no, na ɔnye no da a; ɔno ɔbanyin a ɔnye no dae no nko na onwu:
    25. na akataasia no dze mma nnyɛ no hwee; bɔn a ɔsɛ owu nnyi akataasia no do: na dɛ mbrɛ nyimpa soɛr ne nyɛnko do, na oku no no, dɛmara so na asɛm yi tse:
    26. na haban mu na ohun no; na akataasia a wɔagye no yer no kekaa mu, na nna obi nnyi hɔ a ɔbɛgye no.
    27. Sɛ banyin bi hu akataasia a ɔyɛ ɔbaabun a wɔnngyee no yer, na ɔkyer no, na ɔnye no da, na wohu hɔn a;
    28. ɔno banyin a ɔnye no dae no, mfa dwetɛ dwetɛbona eduonum mma akataasia no n'egya, na ɔnyɛ ne yer, osiandɛ ɔabrɛ no adze; mma ɔnnko no ne nda nyinara.
    29. Ɔbanyin bi nnwar n'egya ne yer, na onnyi n'egya n'akatado.

    Tsir 23

    1. Nyia no ntɔhwɛbo abɔ, anaa ne banyin etwa no, mma ɔnnhɛn EWURADZE ne baguaa mu.
    2. Nyia wɔawo no wɔ edwaman bi nnhɛn EWURADZE ne baguaa mu; n'ebusuasantsen a otsia du mpo mu biara nnhɛn EWURADZE ne baguaa mu.
    3. Ammonnyi anaa Moabnyi nnhɛn EWURADZE ne baguaa mu; hɔn ebusuasantsen a otsia du mpo mu biara nnhɛn EWURADZE ne baguaa mu ara da:
    4. osiandɛ wɔammfa edziban na ɔnye nsu emmbehyia hom wɔ ɔkwan no mu, aber a hom fi Egypt reba no; na dɛ wɔkɔbɔɔ Beer ne babanyin Balaam a ofi Petor a ɔwɔ Mesopotemia no paa dɛ ɔmbɔdom wo ntsi.
    5. Naaso EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ammpɛ dɛ otsie Balaam; na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn daan ndom no nhyira maa wo, osiandɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dɔɔ wo.
    6. Mma nnhwehwɛ hɔn asomdwee nye hɔn eyiedzi wo nda nyinara daa.
    7. Mma nnkyir Edomnyi; na ɔyɛ wo nua: mma nnkyir Egyptnyi; osiandɛ ekɛyɛɛ n'asaase do ɔhɔho.
    8. Ebusuasantsen a otsia ebiasa mba a wɔbɔwo hɔn ama hɔn no na wɔnhɛn EWURADZE ne baguaa mu.
    9. Sɛ ifir edzi kɛkyer w'atamfo ho nsera a, ɔno sie woho yie wɔ adze bɔn biara ho.
    10. Sɛ ɔbanyin bi wɔ hom mu a no ho nntsew wɔ no ha a ɔsen anadwe bi ntsi a, ɔno omfi nseraban no mu nkɔ ekyir, mma ɔmmba nseraban no mu;
    11. na ɔmbra dɛ orudu ewimbir no, onguar noho wɔ nsu mu: na sɛ wi kɔtɔ a, ɔmbra nseraban no mu.
    12. Nya beebi so wɔ nseraban no ekyir, mbrɛ ibefir edzi akɔ:
    13. na nya ɔsɔsɔw wɔ wo ndadzedze mu; na ɔmbra dɛ, sɛ ekɔkotow ekyir hɔ a, edze eefun, na aasan ekyir akata dza ofi wo mu ba no do:
    14. na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn nenam wo nseraban mu, dɛ ɔregye wo, na ɔdze w'atamfo ama wɔ w'enyim; dɛm ntsi wo nseraban nyɛ krɔnkrɔn: amma oeennhu efifidze bi wɔ wo mu, na ɔaanndan emmfi wo nkyɛn.
    15. Mma nnyi akowaa a oefi no wura nkyɛn eguan aba wo hɔ mma no wura:
    16. ma ɔnye wo ntsena, wɔ wo mu, wɔ beebi mbrɛ ɔbɛsan mu eyi no wɔ wo nkuropon no kor mu, mbrɛ ɔpɛ no dodow no: mma nnhyɛ no do.
    17. Mma Israel mbabaa mu biara nnyɛ edwaman, nkyii so mma Israel mbabanyin mu biara nndan edwaman.

    Tsir 25

    Tsir 28

    1. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, sɛ itsie EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ne ndze yie, dɛ ebɔdwen do ayɛ ne mbransɛm a merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi nyinara a, EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛma wo do akɔ sor asen asaase do amanaman nyinara:

    15. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, sɛ enntsie EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ne ndze dɛ ebɔdwen do edzi ne mbransɛm nye n'ahyɛdze a merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi nyinara do a; mpaa yi nyinara bɛba wo do, na wɔabɛmen wo.
    16. Mpaa bɛka wo wɔ kurow mu, na mpaa aka wo wɔ haban mu.
    17. Mpaa bɛka wo kɛntsɛn, nye wo mbɔrkorow.
    18. Mpaa bɛka wo honam mu aba, nye w'asaase mu aba, nye w'anantwi mu ndɔree, nye wo nguankuw mu mburwa.
    19. Mpaa bɛka wo wɔ wo fie ba mu, na mpaa bɛka wo wɔ w'edzifir mu.
    20. EWURADZE bɛma mpaa, nkogu, nye adapaabɔ aba wo do, wɔ dza edze wo nsa bɛka ayɛ nyinara mu, dze ekodu dɛ ebɛsɛɛ, na ayew ntsɛm; osian wo ndzeyɛɛ a enam do apow me ntsi.
    21. EWURADZE bɛma ɔyardɔm afam wo ho, dze ekodu dɛ ɔahyew wo efi asaase a erokɔ do akɛfa no do.
    22. EWURADZE dze asasawaw, nye etsiridii, nye ahohur, nye ahohyehye kɛse, nye nkrantsee, nye ɔhow, nye ɔpo bɔbɔ wo; na wobetsiw wo araa ekodu dɛ ebɛsɛɛ.
    23. Na ɔsor bɛyɛ dɛ boran ama wo, na asaase adan dadze ama wo.
    24. EWURADZE bɛma etudur na huntuma atɔ dɛ nsu egu wo do araa ekodu dɛ wɔbɛsɛɛ wo.
    25. EWURADZE bɛma edzi w'atamfo enyim ɔkogu: enam ɔkwan kor do befir edzi ekehyia hɔn, na enam akwan esuon do eguan wɔ hɔn enyim: na wobedzi wo akɔnaaba wɔ asaase do ahendzi nyinara mu.
    26. Na wo fun bɛyɛ wimu ntumbowa nyinara, nye asaase do mbowa edziban, na obiara rennka hɔn.
    27. EWURADZE dze Egypt pɔmpɔ, tukuruw, satwem, nye ntwem a worunntum wo eduyɛ bɔbobɔ wo.
    28. EWURADZE dze abɔdam, nye enyifura, nye akoma mu ahodwuruw bɔbɔ wo;
    29. na ebɛkeka ewiaber ketee, dɛ ofurafo rekeka wɔ sum mu, na w'akwan mu rennyɛ wo yie: na ɔhyɛ nko na wɔbɛhyɛ wo na wɔafow wo dabaa, na obiara renngye wo.
    30. Ebɛgye ayewar, na ɔbanyin fofor bi nye no ada: ibesi dan, na erenntsena mu: ebɛtsɛw wenyin ture, na erenntsew n'aba bi.
    31. Woboku wo nantwi wɔ w'enyim, na irinndzi bi: wɔbɛfa w'asoaso enyimpi do wɔ w'enyim, na wɔrenndan mma wo: wɔdze wo nguan bɛma w'atamfo na innkenya obi ma ɔaagye wo.
    32. Wɔdze wo mbabanyin nye wo mbabaa bɛma ɔman fofor bi na w'enyiwa bɔhwɛ, na hɔn ho enyigyinae egura wo daa nyinara: na tum biara nnyi wo nsamu.
    33. W'asaase do aba, nye w'edwumadze nyinara, na ɔman a innyim hɔn bedzi; na ɔhyɛ nko na wɔbɛhyɛ wo na wɔadwerew wo dabaa:
    34. dɛm ntsi w'enyiwa do adze a ibohu no bɔbɔ wo dam.
    35. EWURADZE dze pɔmpɔ bɔn bɔbɔ wo wɔ anankoroma, nye aserɛ do a worunntum wo eduyɛ, efi w'ananadze ekodu w'etsifipampamu.
    36. EWURADZE dze ɔwo, nye wo hen a ibesi no wo do no, bɔkɔ ɔman a ɔwo nye w'egyanom nnyim no mu; na hɔ na ebɔsom enyame fofor, ndua nye abo.
    37. Na ebɛdan ahodwuruwdze, akasabɛbu, nye ifen, wɔ aman a EWURADZE bɛma akɔ no nyinara mu.
    38. Ebɔsoa aba pii akɔ haban mu, na kakrabaabi na ebɔboa ano aba fie; na mbɛbɛw bedzi.
    39. Ebɛtsetsɛw wenyin nture na esiesie mu, na ne nsa dze eronnom, na erenntsew esikyireamba no so; na mbowaba bedzi.
    40. Ibenya ngodua wɔ wo ntoado nyinara, na ngo no dze erennsera woho bi; na wo ngodua no bɔporow n'aba.
    41. Ebɔwo mbabanyin nye mbabaa, na wɔrennyɛ wodze; na wɔbɔkɔ ndɔmmum mu.
    42. Wo ndua nyinara nye w'asaase do aba no mbɛbɛw bɛfa.
    43. Ɔhɔho a ɔwɔ wo mu bɛkyɛn wo akɔ sorsorsor; na ɔwo dze ibesian aba famufamu.
    44. Ɔno na ɔdze bɛfɛm wo, na ɔwo dze, irinnya mmfɛm no: ɔno na ɔbɔkɔ kan, na ɔwo aka ekyir.
    45. Na mpaa yi nyinara bɛba wo do, na woetsiw wo, na wɔamen wo, ekodu dɛ ebɛsɛɛ; osiandɛ enntsie EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ndze, dɛ ibedzi ne mbransɛm nye n'ahyɛdze a ɔhyɛ wo no do:
    46. na ɔbɛyɛ asɛnkyerɛdze nye anwanwadze, wɔ ɔwo nye w'asefo do daa:
    47. osiandɛ ammfa enyigye, nye akoma pa annsom EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn, wɔ adze nyinara no dodow mu ntsi:
    48. dɛm ntsi ebɔsom w'atamfo a EWURADZE bɔsoma hɔn ma wɔaaba wo do no, wɔ ɔkɔm, nsukɔm, adagyaw, nye ndzɛmba nyinara ho hia mu: na ɔdze dadze kɔndua bɔto wo kɔn do, ekodu dɛ ɔbɛsɛɛ wo.
    49. EWURADZE dze ɔman bi befi ekyirekyir efi asaase ano aba wo do, dɛ mbrɛ ɔkɔr tu; ɔman a enntse hɔn kasaa;
    50. ɔman a hɔn enyim yɛ hu a wɔmmfɛr akɔdaa enyim, na wɔnnkyerɛ enyimtsewee wɔ aberantsɛ do so:
    51. na obedzi wo fiembowa mu aba, na w'asaase do aba, ekodu dɛ wɔbɛsɛɛ wo: eso orinngya wo eburow, nsa, anaa ngo, w'anantwi mu ndɔree, anaa wo nguankuw mu mburwa, ekodu dɛ ɔbɛma asɛe.
    52. Na ɔbɔkɔ wo ntua wɔ wo nkuropon nyinara mu, ekodu dɛ w'afasu atsentsen a ɔyɛ dzen no a edze wo werɛ hyɛ mu no bodwuruw egu famu, wɔ w'asaase nyina do: na ɔbɔkɔ wo ntua wɔ wo nkuropon nyinara a ɔwɔ w'asaase a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze ama wo no nyinara do.
    53. Na ibedzi woara wo honam mu aba, wo mbabanyin nye wo mbabaa a EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze ama wo no hɔn nam; wɔ ntua nye ahokyer a w'atamfo bɛma wo ho akyer wo no mu.
    54. Ɔbanyin a ɔdwe na ɔwɔ tsema wɔ hom mu no n'enyiwa bɛyɛ bɔn wɔ no nua, nye ne yer a ɔda no bo mu, nye ne mba nkaa a wɔama wɔaka no ho:
    55. dɛ ɔremma hɔn mu biara ne mba nam a oridzi no bi, osiandɛ biribiara annka amma no; wɔ ntua nye ahokyer a wo tamfo bɛma wo ho akyer wo wɔ wo nkuropon nyinara mu no mu.
    56. Hom mu ɔbaa so a ɔdwe na ɔsonsɔn no ho na ɔnam mberɛwyɛ ntsi ɔnnsɔe nnhwɛɛ pɛn dɛ ɔdze n'ananadze bɛka famu no, bɛyɛ nyimpa bɔn wɔ no kun a ɔda no bo mu, nye ne babanyin, nye ne babaa ho;
    57. na ɔbɔwe ne mba a ɔbɔwo hɔn no nye ekyiradze no wɔ nsumaa mu: wɔ ahohia a ofi ntua nye ahokyer a wo tamfo bɛma aba wo do wɔ wo nkuropon mu no ntsi.
    58. Sɛ eronndwen do nnyɛ mbra yi mu nsɛm nyinara a wɔakyerɛw no wɔ nwoma yi mu, na ennsuro enyimnyam dzin a no ho yɛ hu yi, EWURADZE WO NYANKOPƆN no a, DEU.28.59, ɔno EWURADZE bɛma wo yardɔm, nye w'asefo yardɔm ayɛ nwanwa; mekyerɛ ɔyardɔm akɛse a ɔkyɛr, nye yarba bɔn a ɔkyɛr.
    59. Na ɔdze Egypt yarba no a isuro no nyinara bɛba wo do bio; na ɔabɛfemfam wo ho.
    60. Eso yarba biara, nye ɔyardɔm biara a wɔnnkyerɛɛw no wɔ mbra yi no nwoma mu no, ɔno na EWURADZE dze bɛba wo do, ekodu dɛ wɔbɛsɛɛ wo.
    61. Na ɔbɛka hom nyimpa kakrabaabi, naaso nna nkyɛ hom dodow tse dɛ ɔsor nworaba; osiandɛ hom enntsie EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ne ndze.
    62. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE n'enyi gye hom ho dɛ ɔbɛyɛ hom yie, na ɔama hom adɔr no; dɛmara so na EWURADZE n'enyi bɛgye hom ho dɛ ɔbɛma hom ase etu, na ɔasɛɛ hom: na wɔbɛpempan hom efi asaase a hom rokɔ do akɛfa no do.
    63. Na EWURADZE bɔbɔ wo apetse aman nyinara mu, efi asaase ano kor araa ekodu asaase ano kor so; na hɔ na ebɔsom enyame fofor a ɔwo nye w'egyanom nnyim hɔn, mekyerɛ ndua nye abo.
    64. Na amanaman iyinom mu irinnya ahotɔ, na w'ananadze rinnya ahomgye: na EWURADZE bɛma wo ahopopo akoma, nye enyiwa a ayɛ siamoo, nye ɔkra a etwintwam wɔ hɔ:
    65. na wo nkwa bɛsɛn ehihim wɔ w'enyim; na ibosuro adzesaa nye adzekyee, na irinnya wo nkwa ho gyedzi biara:
    66. anapa ebɛse dɛ, Kɛtseankyɛ ewimbir a: na ewimbir so ebɛse dɛ, Kɛtseankyɛ anapa a: wɔ w'akoma mu suro a ibosuro, nye w'enyiwa do adze a ibohu no ntsi.
    67. Na EWURADZE dze ahɛn dze wo bɛsan akɔ Egypt, wɔ ɔkwan a mekaa wo ho asɛm dɛ irunnhu bio no do: na hɔ na hom bɔtontɔn homho ama hom atamfo ayɛ nkowaa nye mfonabaa, na nyimpa biara ronntɔ hom.

    Tsir 29

    1. Iyi nye ahyɛmu a EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses dɛ ɔnye Israelmba ndzi wɔ Moab asaase do, nka ahyɛmu a ɔnye hɔn dzii wɔ Horeb no ho no, no mu nsɛm.
    2. Na Moses frɛɛ Israel nyinara, na ɔsee hɔn dɛ, Hom ehu adze nyinara a EWURADZE dze yɛɛ Farao, nye no nkowaa nyinara, nye n'asaase nyinara, wɔ hom enyiwa mu, wɔ Egypt asaase do;
    3. nsɔhwɛ akɛse a w'enyiwa hunii, nsɛnkyerɛdze, nye anwanwadze akɛse no:
    4. naaso obodu ndɛ da yi, EWURADZE mmaa wo dɛm akoma a onyim dze, enyiwa a wɔdze hwɛ, nye asowa a wɔdze tsie.
    5. Na medzi hom enyim ma hom anantsew mfe eduanan wɔ sar no do; hom ntar nnsuwii wɔ hom ho, na wo mpaboa nntsetsewee wɔ w'ananadze.
    6. Hom nndzii paanoo, nkyii so hom nnoom wenyin anaa nsa dzendzen: ama hom eehu dɛ emi nye EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn.
    7. Na aber a hom dur bea yi do no, Hesbonhen Sihon, nye Basenhen Ɔge, fiir edzi baa hɛn do nye hɛn bɔkoe, na yekum hɔn;
    8. na yɛfaa hɔn asaase, na yɛdze maa Reubenfo, nye Gadfo, nye Manaasɛfo ebusuakuw ɔfa no, dɛ egyapadze.
    9. Dɛm ntsi hom ndzi ahyɛmu yi mu nsɛm do, na hom nyɛ, na hom aayɛ prɔmprɔm wɔ dza hom yɛ nyinara mu.
    10. Ndɛ da yi hom nyinara hom ngyina EWURADZE hom Nyankopɔn enyim; hom etsitsir, hom ebusuakuw ebusuakuw, hom mpanyimfo, nye hom ahwɛdofo, mekyerɛ Israel mbanyin nyinara, DEU.29.11, hom mbofraba, hom yernom, nye wo hɔhoe a ɔwɔ wo nseraban mu, ofi wo duatwafo do dze kodu wo nsusawfo do:
    11. ama aahɛn EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn n'ahyɛmu, nye ne nsew a Ɔnye wo ridzi ndɛ da yi mu:
    12. ama ɔdze wo eesi hɔ ndɛ da yi dɛ ɔman ama noho, na ɔno so aayɛ Nyankopɔn ama wo, dɛ mbrɛ ɔkaa kyerɛɛ wo, na dɛ mbrɛ ɔkaa ntam kyerɛɛ w'egyanom, Abraham, Isaak, nye Jakob no.
    13. Nkyii so nnyɛ hom nko na menye hom ridzi ahyɛmu yi nye nsew yi:
    14. mbom menye nyia ɔnye hɛn gyina EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn enyim ha ndɛ da yi, na eso menye nyia ɔnnye hɛn nnyi ha ndɛ da yi:
    15. (na hom nyim mbrɛ yesii tsenaa Egypt asaase do; na mbrɛ yesii twaa mu wɔ amanaman a hom faa hɔn mu baa no mu;
    16. na hom ehu hɔn ekyiwadze, na hɔn abosom, ndua nye abo, dwetɛ nye sika a ɔwɔ hɔn mu no:)
    17. Na hom nhwɛ yie amma hom mu ɔbanyin bi, anaa ɔbaa bi, anaa ebusua bi, anaa ebusuakuw bi n'akoma anndan ndɛ da yi emmfi EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn ho, dɛ ɔrokɔ akɔsom amanaman no hɔn enyame no: na ndwow bi aammba hom mu a ɔsow bɔr nye ɔbɔwen;
    18. na aammba dɛ, sɛ ɔtse mpaa yi mu nsɛm a, oeennhyira noho wɔ n'akoma mu, dɛ, mibenya asomdwee, kaansa menam m'akoma no sorow mu mpo a, amma wɔaannsɛɛ dza afɔw annka dza awow ho.
    19. EWURADZE remmfa nnkyɛ no, na mbom EWURADZE n'ebufuw nye ne nyinkuntwe bopun nyimpa no, na mpaa a wɔakyerɛw no wɔ nwoma yi mu no nyinara bodwir no do, na EWURADZE bɛpepa ne dzin efi asaase do.
    20. Na EWURADZE beyi no efi Israel ebusuakuw nyinara mu esi hɔ ama mbusu, dɛ mbrɛ ahyɛmu a wɔakyerɛw no wɔ mbra nwoma yi mu mpaa no tse nyinara.
    21. Na ebusuasantsen a wɔbɛba no, hom mba a wɔbɔsoɛr edzi hom ekyir no, nye ɔnanaben a ofi ekyirekyir asaase do bɛba no, sɛ wohu asaase no do yardɔm, nye nyarbanyarba a EWURADZE dze ama wɔayar no a, wɔbɛse dɛ;
    22. n'asaase nyinara ayɛ sɔɔfee nye nkyen, nye nkyewee, wonndua do adze, ɔnnsow, nkyɛkyɛr mpo mmfir do, ɔtse dɛ Sodom nye Gomora, Adma nye Zeboim mbutuwii a EWURADZE nam n'ebufuw nye n'ebufuhyew mu butuwii no:
    23. mekyerɛ amanaman nyinara bɛse dɛ, Ebɛnadze ntsi na EWURADZE ayɛ asaase yi dɛm yi? Ebufuw kɛse hyeehyew yi ase nye dɛn?
    24. Nna nkorɔfo bɛse dɛ, Osiandɛ wɔpoow EWURADZE, hɔn egyanom Nyankopɔn ahyɛmu a oyii hɔn wɔ Egypt asaase do no ɔnye hɔn dzii no;
    25. na wɔkɔree kɔsoom enyame fofor, na wɔsɔɔr hɔn, enyame a wonnyim hɔn, na ɔammfa hɔn amma hɔn:
    26. dɛm ntsi na EWURADZE ebufuw dzɛwee wɔ asaase yi do, ma ɔdze mpaa a wɔakyerɛw no wɔ nwoma yi mu nyinara aba do;
    27. na EWURADZE tutuu hɔn ase fii hɔn asaase do wɔ ebufuw, nye ebufuhyew, nye ahohyehye kɛse mu, na wɔatow hɔn egu asaase bi so do, dɛ mbrɛ ɔtse ndɛ da yi.
    28. Ndzɛmba a osuma yɛ EWURADZE hɛn Nyankopɔn dze: na ndzɛmba a wɔada no edzi no dze ɔyɛ hɛn nye hɛn mba dze daa, ama yeedzi mbra yi mu nsɛm nyinara do.

    Tsir 30

    1. Na ɔbɛba dɛ, sɛ ndzɛmba iyinom nyinara ba wo do, nhyira nye mpaa a, medze esi w'enyim yi, na ekaakaa no wɔ amanaman nyinara mu mbrɛ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn apam wo akɔ no, DEU.30.2, na sɛ ɔwo, nye wo mba san ba EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn nkyɛn, na edze w'akoma nyinara, na ɔnye wo kra nyinara tsie ne ndze na eyɛ dza merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi nyinara a;
    2. ɔno na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛdan wo ndɔmmumkɔ no, na oenya ayamuhyehye ama wo, na ɔasan aboa w'ano efi aman a ɔnoara EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn abɔ wo apetse mu no nyinara mu.
    3. Sɛ wo nyimpa a wɔapam hɔn no bi wɔ ɔsor ano no ho mpo a, ofitsi hɔ na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɔboa w'ano, na hɔ na ɔbɛfa wo efi:
    4. na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze wo bɛba asaase a w'egyanom nyaa no do, na aafa; na ɔbɛyɛ wo yie, na ɔama adɔr akyɛn w'egyanom.
    5. Na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛma ɔwo nye w'asefo akoma, a ɔdwe, na edze w'akoma nye wo kra nyinara, aadɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn, na eenya nkwa.
    6. Na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn dze mpaa yi nyinara bogu w'atamfo, nye hɔn a wokyir wo a wohiahia wo ho no do.
    7. Na ɔwo dze, ebɛsan ebetsie EWURADZE ndze, na edzi ne mbransɛm a merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi nyinara do.
    8. Na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn bɛma yie ebu wo do wɔ wo nsaano edwuma nyinara mu, wɔ wo honam mu aba mu, nye wo fiembowa aba mu, nye w'asaase do aba mu, wɔ wo yieyɛ ntsi: na EWURADZE n'enyi bɛsan agye wo ho wɔ wo yieyɛ ntsi, dɛ mbrɛ n'enyi gyee w'egyanom ho no:
    9. sɛ ibetsie EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ndze, dɛ ibedzi ne mbransɛm nye n'ahyɛdze a wɔakyerɛw no wɔ mbra nwoma yi mu no do, dɛ edze w'akoma nyinara, na ɔnye wo kra nyinara bɛsan aba EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn nkyɛn a.
    10. Na mbrasɛm yi a merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi, ɔnnyɛ dzendzen dodow mma wo, nkyii so onnyi ekyirekyir.
    11. Onnyi sor, na aase dɛ, Woana na ɔbɔfow akɔ sor ama hɛn, na ɔakɛfa abrɛ hɛn, na ɔama yɛatse, na yɛayɛ.
    12. Nkyii so onnyi po n'egya, na aase dɛ, Woana na obotwa po ama hɛn, na ɔakɛfa abrɛ hɛn, na ɔama yɛatse, ma yaayɛ.
    13. Na asɛm no bɛn wo dodoodow, ɔwɔ w'anomu, na ɔwɔ w'akoma mu, ama aayɛ.
    14. Hwɛ, ndɛ da yi medze nkwa nye papa, nye owu nye bɔn, esi w'enyim;
    15. wɔ dɛ merehyɛ wo ndɛ da yi dɛ dɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn, nantsew n'akwan do, na dzi ne mbransɛm nye n'ahyɛdze nye ne ntsɛmbu do, na aatsena ase na aadɔr, na EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn ehyira wo wɔ asaase a erokɔ do akɛfa no do.
    16. Mbom sɛ w'akoma dan, na enntsie, na ema wɔtwe wo kɔ, na ekɔsɔr enyame fofor, na esom hɔn a;
    17. mepaa mu meka kyerɛ hom ndɛ da yi, dɛ ɔsɛɛ na hom bɛsɛɛ; na hom remma hom nda nnwar wɔ asaase mbrɛ irutwa Jordan akɔ do akɛfa no do.
    18. Mefrɛ ɔsor nye asaase dɛ wondzi hom dase ndɛ da yi, dɛ medze nkwa nye owu, nhyira nye mpaa esi w'enyim: dɛm ntsi fa nkwa, ma ɔwo, nye w'asefo nnya nkwa:
    19. na dɔ EWURADZE wo Nyankopɔn, tsie ne ndze, na takyer no ho: na ɔno nye wo nkwa, nye wo nda ne tsentsen: ama aatsena asaase a EWURADZE kaa ho ntam kyerɛɛ w'egyanom, Abraham, Isaak, nye Jakob dɛ ɔdze bɛma hɔn no do.

    Tsir 31

    1. Na Moses kɔree kɛkaa asɛm yi kyerɛɛ Israel nyinara.

    Tsir 34

    1. Na Moses fii Moab tɛtɛr mu fowee kɔr bepɔw Nebo do, wɔ Pisga etsifi a ɔwɔ Jeriko tsentsendo. Na EWURADZE kyerɛɛ no Gilead asaase nyinara, ɔdze kodu Dan:
    2. nye Naftali nyinara, nye Efraim nye Manaasɛ asaase, nye Juda asaase nyinara, ɔdze kodu ekyirekyir epo no ho;
    3. nye Epomu, nye Tɛtɛrmu, nkyiresua kurow Jeriko ɔbɔn no, ɔdze kodu Zoar.
    4. Na EWURADZE see no dɛ, Iyi nye asaase a mekaa ho ntam mekyerɛɛ Abraham, Isaak, nye Jakob dɛ, W'asefo na medze mebɛma hɔn no: mama wo kwan dɛ fa w'enyiwa hwɛ, na hɔ dze eronnkɔ.
    5. Nna EWURADZE n'akowaa Moses wuu hɔ wɔ Moab asaase do, dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE n'asɛm tse.
    6. Na osiee no wɔ ɔbɔn no mu wɔ Moab asaase do wɔ Bɛt-peor tsentsendo na n'adakamona dze nyimpa biara nnyim dze bodu ndɛ da yi.
    7. Na aber a Moses wui no na oedzi mfe ɔha eduonu: n'enyiwa annyɛ kusukusu, na n'ahoɔdzen so do ennhuan.
    8. Na Israelmba sui maa Moses wɔ Moab tɛtɛr mu adaduasa: nna Moab n'eyiyɛ mu osu nda no bɔɔ adze.
    9. Na nyansa sunsum hyɛɛ Nun ne babanyin Josua ma: na Israelmba tsiee no, na wɔyɛɛ dɛ mbrɛ EWURADZE hyɛɛ Moses no.
    10. Na ofitsi aber no nkɔnhyɛnyi biara nnsoɛree wɔ Israel mu bio a ɔtse dɛ Moses a EWURADZE nye no nyaa nkitahodzi a no mu dɔ yie
    11. Dɛm Moses yi ara so na EWURADZE somaa no wɔ nsa a ɔyɛ dzen do kɔr Egypt ma ɔkɛyɛɛ nsɛnkyerɛdze nye anwanwadze pii wɔ Farao nye no nkowaa nye n'asaase nyinara do no.
    12. Afei so, ɔnam Moses do na ndzɛmba akɛse a ɔyɛ hu na tum wɔ mu daa edzi wɔ Israel nyinara enyim.
